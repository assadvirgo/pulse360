## Scotland's T20 World Cup Campaign Concludes with Narrow Defeat to Sri Lanka

In a gripping encounter at Old Trafford, Scotland's T20 World Cup journey came to an end as they faced a narrow defeat against Sri Lanka. The match, which was marked by intense competition, saw Scotland post a respectable total of 151 runs for the loss of six wickets, largely thanks to an impressive performance by Sarah Bryce.

Batting first, Scotland struggled to find their rhythm early in the innings. However, Sarah Bryce emerged as a standout performer, anchoring the innings with a commendable unbeaten score of 47 runs. Her innings included several well-timed boundaries that helped Scotland recover from a shaky start. The team’s total of 151 was seen as a competitive score, especially on a pitch that offered assistance to bowlers.

### Sri Lanka's Response

The match reached a thrilling climax as Sri Lanka edged closer to the target in the final overs. With the game hanging in the balance, Sri Lanka managed to secure victory by three wickets, sealing their win in the final over. This result not only eliminated Scotland from the tournament but also highlighted the competitive nature of the T20 World Cup.
