## Fernandes Breaks Premier League Record as Manchester United Triumph Over Brighton

In a thrilling conclusion to the Premier League season, Manchester United secured a decisive victory against Brighton & Hove Albion, with a final score of 3-1. This match not only marked the end of the league campaign but also saw United's captain, Bruno Fernandes, etch his name into the record books by breaking the Premier League record for the most assists in a single season.

### A Historic Achievement

Bruno Fernandes, who has been a pivotal figure for Manchester United since his arrival in January 2020, showcased his exceptional playmaking abilities throughout the season. With his assist in the match against Brighton, Fernandes surpassed the previous record held by Kevin De Bruyne, who set the benchmark with 20 assists during the 2017-2018 season. Fernandes concluded the season with a remarkable total of 21 assists, a testament to his vision, creativity, and consistency on the pitch.

### Match Overview

The match at Old Trafford saw Manchester United take control early on, with Fernandes playing a crucial role in orchestrating the attack. The home side opened the scoring in the first half, with a well-placed shot from Marcus Rashford. Brighton responded with a goal of their own, leveling the match before halftime. However, United's resilience shone through as they regained the lead shortly after the break, with Fernandes providing a key assist for Anthony Martial's goal.

As the match progressed, United continued to apply pressure, ultimately sealing their victory with a third goal. The win not only secured a strong finish in the league standings but also allowed the team to celebrate Fernandes's historic achievement in front of their home crowd.
