## Ollie Watkins' Remarkable Return to Form

Aston Villa forward Ollie Watkins has made headlines recently for his impressive performances on the pitch, leading to renewed discussions about his role in the England national team. Following his omission from the England squad in March, Watkins has demonstrated exceptional skill and determination, compelling both fans and pundits to take notice.

## A Stellar Comeback

Since being left out of the England squad, Watkins has been on a remarkable scoring streak, showcasing his ability to find the back of the net consistently. His performances have drawn praise from notable figures in the football community, including Match of the Day pundits Alan Shearer and Wayne Rooney. Both have lauded Watkins for his "unbelievable" form, highlighting how he has transformed his game and returned to the forefront of discussions regarding national team selection.

Watkins' resurgence is characterized by his agility, sharp finishing, and an innate ability to create opportunities not just for himself but for his teammates as well. His contributions have been pivotal in Aston Villa's recent successes in the Premier League, solidifying his status as one of the league's most exciting forwards.

## The Impact of Exclusion

The decision to exclude Watkins from the England squad earlier this year was met with some surprise, given his previous contributions to the team. However, this setback appears to have served as a catalyst for his improvement. In the months following his omission, Watkins has displayed an unwavering work ethic and a renewed focus on his game, which has translated into tangible results on the pitch.

The psychological aspect of being left out of the national team cannot be understated. For many players, such moments can either hinder their performance or motivate them to elevate their game. In Watkins' case, it seems to have propelled him to new heights, as he has not only increased his goal tally but also enhanced his overall gameplay, making him a more rounded player.

## National Team Considerations

With the upcoming international fixtures on the horizon, the question of Watkins' inclusion in the England squad is becoming increasingly relevant. England manager Thomas Tuchel will undoubtedly be evaluating his options carefully, particularly in light of Watkins' recent form. The competition for places in the national team is fierce, but Watkins' current trajectory suggests he could be a valuable asset for the squad.

As the footballing landscape continues to evolve, players like Watkins who demonstrate resilience and adaptability are essential for any national team aiming for success on the international stage. His ability to respond positively to setbacks may not only secure him a place in the England squad but also serve as an inspiration for other players facing similar challenges.

## Conclusion

Ollie Watkins' journey from exclusion to excellence is a testament to his dedication and talent. As he continues to shine for Aston Villa, the football community will be watching closely to see if his efforts will earn him a return to the England squad. With the support of pundits and fans alike, Watkins is poised to make a significant impact, both at the club level and potentially on the international stage.
