## South Africa Advances to Round of 32 After Narrow Victory Over South Korea

In a tightly contested match held in Monterrey, South Africa secured a crucial 1-0 victory against South Korea, ensuring their progression to the knockout stage of the World Cup. The decisive moment came in the second half, when Thapelo Maseko found the back of the net, propelling his team into the round of 32.

The match, characterized by its intensity and strategic play, saw both teams vying for control. South Africa entered the game with determination, knowing that a win was essential for their advancement in the tournament. The first half ended in a stalemate, with both sides creating opportunities but failing to convert.

As the second half unfolded, South Africa's persistence paid off. In the 67th minute, Maseko capitalized on a defensive lapse from South Korea, striking a well-placed shot that eluded the goalkeeper. This goal not only marked a significant moment for Maseko but also served to energize the South African squad, who rallied to maintain their lead.
