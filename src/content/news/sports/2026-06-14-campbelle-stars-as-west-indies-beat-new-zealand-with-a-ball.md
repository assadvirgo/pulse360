## Campbelle Shines as West Indies Defeat New Zealand in T20 World Cup Opener

In a thrilling encounter at the ICC Women's T20 World Cup, the West Indies secured a remarkable victory over the defending champions, New Zealand, by seven wickets. The match, held in Southampton, marked the opening game of the tournament, showcasing the competitive spirit and skill of both teams.

Shemaine Campbelle emerged as the standout performer for the West Indies, finishing the match unbeaten on 90 runs. Her innings was pivotal in guiding the team to chase down a target of 163 runs, demonstrating both resilience and finesse under pressure. Campbelle's performance not only highlighted her individual talent but also underscored the strength of the West Indies batting lineup.

New Zealand, batting first, set a challenging total of 162 runs, thanks to a solid batting performance. However, the West Indies bowlers kept the pressure on, limiting the scoring opportunities and ensuring that the match remained competitive. The New Zealand team, known for their formidable presence in women's cricket, faced a determined West Indies side that was eager to make a statement in the tournament.

Campbelle's composure and experience shone through as she guided her team home with a ball to spare, sealing a memorable win that will be remembered as a significant achievement in the tournament.
