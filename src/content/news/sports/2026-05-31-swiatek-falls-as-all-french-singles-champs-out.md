In a surprising turn of events at the French Open, four-time champion Iga Swiatek has been eliminated from the tournament, marking a significant moment in the competition. Swiatek, who has dominated the clay courts of Roland Garros in recent years, was considered a strong contender for this year's title. However, Kostyuk's performance showcased her resilience and skill, as she managed to overcome the reigning champion in a match that will be remembered for its intensity and high stakes. The final score reflected the competitive nature of the match, with Kostyuk prevailing in straight sets.

This defeat not only signifies a pivotal moment for Kostyuk but also highlights the unpredictable nature of the tournament. With Swiatek's exit, all previous French Open singles champions have now been eliminated, paving the way for a new champion to emerge.
