## Men's March Madness Live Updates: Previews and Key Matchups for Sunday’s Games

As the NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament progresses, Sunday promises to deliver thrilling matchups that could shape the trajectory of the tournament. ### Purdue vs. Miami: A Clash of Styles

Purdue, known for its powerful frontcourt and disciplined play, faces off against Miami, a team that thrives on speed and perimeter shooting. The Boilermakers will rely on their size advantage, particularly in the paint, where players like Zach Edey can dominate. For Purdue to secure a victory, it will be crucial to establish an early lead and control the tempo of the game.

On the other hand, Miami's success hinges on its ability to create open shots from beyond the arc. The Hurricanes have shown resilience and adaptability throughout the tournament, often turning games in their favor with explosive scoring runs. If Miami can effectively transition from defense to offense and hit their three-point shots, they could pose a significant challenge to Purdue's defensive schemes.

### Alabama vs. Texas Tech: Defensive Battle Expected

In another highly anticipated matchup, Alabama takes on Texas Tech in what is expected to be a defensive showdown. Alabama's fast-paced offense, led by their dynamic guards, will be tested against Texas Tech's renowned defensive prowess. The Red Raiders are known for their physicality and ability to disrupt opposing offenses, making it essential for Alabama to maintain composure and execute their plays efficiently.

For Texas Tech, the key will be to limit Alabama's transition opportunities and force them into a half-court game. If they can contain Alabama's perimeter shooting and capitalize on turnovers, they stand a strong chance of advancing further in the tournament. Both teams will need to focus on rebounding and limiting second-chance points, as these factors could be pivotal in a closely contested game.
