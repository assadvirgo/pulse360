## Outtara's Double Secures Draw for Brentford Against Crystal Palace

In an exciting encounter at the Gtech Community Stadium, Brentford managed to secure a 2-2 draw against Crystal Palace, thanks to a remarkable performance from Dango Outtara, who scored both goals for the home side. The match, part of the Premier League fixture list, showcased the competitive spirit of both teams as they continue their quests for success this season.

The match started with a palpable intensity, as both teams sought to establish dominance early on. Crystal Palace, known for their organized defense and quick counter-attacks, struck first. In the 23rd minute, a well-placed cross found its way to Palace's forward, who made no mistake in converting the opportunity, putting the visitors ahead 1-0. 

Brentford, however, did not let the early setback deter their spirits. They gradually began to find their rhythm and created several chances, but Palace's defense held firm. The first half concluded with the Eagles leading, leaving Brentford with the challenge of overturning the deficit in the second half.

### Second Half: Outtara Shines

The second half witnessed a revitalized Brentford side, eager to level the score. Dango Outtara, who had been a constant threat throughout the match, finally found the back of the net in the 57th minute. Just when it seemed that Brentford might seize control of the match, Crystal Palace responded with determination. In the 68th minute, they regained the lead with a swift counter-attack, showcasing their attacking prowess. The score was now 2-1 in favor of the visitors, putting Brentford in a precarious position as time began to run out.

### Late Drama: Outtara's Equalizer

With the clock ticking down, Brentford pushed forward in search of another equalizer. Their persistence paid off when Outtara struck again in the 82nd minute. The stadium erupted as the score leveled at 2-2, leaving both teams with a point to show for their efforts.
