## Mid-Race Seizure to Marathon Mission: Jess Warner-Judd's Second Chance

Great Britain's Jess Warner-Judd is set to make her much-anticipated London Marathon debut, marking a significant milestone in her athletic career. This moment comes less than two years after a harrowing incident during a race that left her grappling with fears that her running career might be over.

### A Turning Point in Warner-Judd's Career

In 2022, Warner-Judd experienced a seizure mid-race, an event that not only shocked her but also raised serious concerns about her health and future in athletics. The incident occurred during a competitive event, where she had been performing well until the unexpected seizure halted her progress. Following this traumatic experience, Warner-Judd faced a challenging recovery period, both physically and mentally.

The seizure prompted extensive medical evaluations and a reassessment of her training regimen. Warner-Judd's determination and resilience were put to the test as she navigated through the uncertainties of her condition. The support from her family, coaches, and the wider athletic community played a crucial role in her recovery journey.

### Overcoming Adversity

Warner-Judd's return to competitive running has been marked by a series of rigorous training sessions and a renewed focus on her health. She has worked closely with medical professionals to ensure that she is fit to compete at the highest levels. The process has not been easy; however, her dedication to the sport and her desire to overcome the challenges she faced have been pivotal in her comeback.

As she prepares for the London Marathon, Warner-Judd reflects on the lessons learned during her recovery. "Every step I take now feels like a victory," she stated in a recent interview. "I have a new appreciation for the sport and the opportunities it presents. I am excited to be back on the starting line."

### The London Marathon: A New Beginning

The London Marathon is one of the most prestigious long-distance running events in the world, attracting elite athletes and amateur runners alike. For Warner-Judd, participating in this iconic race represents not just a return to form but also a celebration of resilience and determination. 

Her journey serves as an inspiration to many, illustrating how athletes can confront and overcome significant setbacks. The marathon will provide Warner-Judd with a platform to showcase her skills and share her story with a global audience. 

### Looking Ahead

As the race day approaches, Warner-Judd is focused on her training and mental preparation. She aims to not only complete the marathon but also to achieve a personal best time. Her experience has instilled in her a sense of purpose and a desire to advocate for health awareness among athletes.

Warner-Judd's story is a testament to the strength of the human spirit and the power of perseverance. As she takes her place among the runners in the London Marathon, she embodies the message that challenges can lead to new beginnings and that every setback can be a setup for a comeback. 

The London Marathon will take place on April 23, 2023, and Warner-Judd is ready to embrace the challenge ahead, eager to make her mark on this celebrated event.
