The second Test commenced with high expectations, especially after England's previous encounters with New Zealand. However, the match quickly turned in favor of the visitors, who showcased a dominant performance throughout. New Zealand's bowlers exploited the conditions effectively, leading to a series of dismissals that left England reeling.
