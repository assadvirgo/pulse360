## The Hundred 2026: Squads, Captains, and Injury Replacements Announced

As anticipation builds for the 2026 editions of The Hundred, both men's and women's competitions, the official squads have been unveiled, showcasing a blend of seasoned players and emerging talent. The announcement has generated significant interest among fans and analysts alike, as teams finalize their rosters ahead of the tournament.

### Overview of The Hundred

The Hundred, a unique format introduced by the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB) in 2021, has quickly gained popularity for its fast-paced matches and innovative structure. Each team consists of 100 balls per innings, creating a dynamic and engaging viewing experience. The competition features eight men's and eight women's teams, each representing major cities across England and Wales.

### Key Highlights from the 2026 Squads

The 2026 squads reflect strategic choices made by team management, emphasizing a balance between experienced players and young prospects. Notable captains have been named, each bringing leadership qualities and extensive experience to their respective teams. 

Among the standout captains is Ben Stokes, who will lead the Northern Superchargers. Stokes, known for his explosive batting and all-round capabilities, is expected to play a crucial role in guiding his team through the tournament. Similarly, the Southern Brave have appointed Heather Knight, the England women's captain, to spearhead their campaign. Knight's tactical acumen and experience in high-pressure situations make her an invaluable asset.
