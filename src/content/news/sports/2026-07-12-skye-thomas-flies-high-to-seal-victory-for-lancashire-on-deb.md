In a remarkable debut performance, Skye Thomas guided Lancashire to a narrow victory against Durham in a low-scoring contest. Thomas's unbeaten half-century proved pivotal, as Lancashire chased down a modest target, securing a win that showcased the young talent's potential on the cricket field.
