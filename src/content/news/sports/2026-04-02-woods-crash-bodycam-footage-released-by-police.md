## Bodycam Footage of Tiger Woods Released Following Car Accident

Authorities in Florida have released body camera footage from the incident involving professional golfer Tiger Woods, who was involved in a car crash last month. The footage captures the moments immediately following the accident, which occurred when Woods's vehicle collided with a truck and subsequently rolled over.

### Incident Overview

The accident took place on a roadway in Florida, where Woods's vehicle was reported to have struck a truck before flipping. Fortunately, Woods sustained non-life-threatening injuries and was able to exit the vehicle on his own. The footage reveals the golfer in a visibly shaken state, speaking with officers who arrived at the scene shortly after the crash.

### Police Response

In the released footage, police officers can be seen assessing the situation and speaking with Woods. The officers inquire about his well-being and the circumstances leading up to the crash. Woods, who was cooperative, expressed his surprise at the accident, stating that he did not recall the moments leading up to the collision.

### Investigation Findings

The Florida Highway Patrol is currently conducting an investigation into the accident. Preliminary reports indicate that Woods was driving at a high rate of speed when he clipped the truck. However, authorities have not yet determined whether any impairment or mechanical failure contributed to the crash. The investigation is ongoing, and further details are expected to emerge as officials analyze the evidence.

### Public Reaction

The release of the body camera footage has garnered significant media attention, as Woods is one of the most recognizable figures in professional sports. Fans and fellow athletes have expressed their relief that he was not seriously injured in the crash. Many have taken to social media to share their support for Woods, wishing him a swift recovery.
