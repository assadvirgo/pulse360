## Dani Gibson Appointed Captain of Sunrisers Leeds in Women's Hundred

In a significant development for the Sunrisers Leeds, England allrounder Dani Gibson has been named the captain for the upcoming season of the Women’s Hundred. This announcement follows her record-setting signing at the recent Hundred auction, where she was acquired for a remarkable £190,000, making her the most expensive player in the tournament's history.

Gibson's appointment comes at a pivotal time for the Sunrisers, who are looking to enhance their competitiveness in the Women's Hundred. The franchise has faced challenges in previous seasons and is keen to leverage Gibson's leadership skills and on-field experience to turn their fortunes around. As captain, Gibson will not only lead the team but also serve as a vital link between the players and the coaching staff, fostering a cohesive team environment.
