## O'Neill Hails Celtic Spirit as They Stay in Title Hunt

In a crucial match that could determine the trajectory of the Scottish Premiership title race, Celtic's interim manager Martin O'Neill praised his team's resilience following a hard-fought victory against Dundee. The win not only bolstered Celtic's position in the league but also showcased the fighting spirit that O'Neill believes is essential for success in the closing stages of the season.

### A Hard-Fought Victory

Celtic faced Dundee in a match that tested their mettle. Despite the challenges posed by their opponents, the team managed to secure a vital three points, keeping their title aspirations alive. O'Neill's comments post-match reflected a sense of pride in his players, who demonstrated determination and teamwork throughout the encounter.

“The spirit shown by the team today was commendable,” O'Neill remarked. “In moments where we could have faltered, we stood strong and fought for every ball. This victory is a testament to our character and our commitment to the cause.”

### The Importance of Team Spirit

O'Neill's emphasis on team spirit highlights a critical component of Celtic's success in the league. With the season entering its final stretch, maintaining morale and unity within the squad becomes increasingly important. The interim manager noted that the players' ability to rally together under pressure has been a defining factor in their recent performances.

“Every player contributed to this win, and it is this collective effort that will carry us forward,” he added. “As we approach the final matches of the season, it is crucial that we continue to support one another and build on this momentum.”

### Title Race Implications

Celtic's victory over Dundee not only serves to enhance their standing in the league but also sends a message to their title rivals. With each match becoming a potential turning point, the stakes are high, and the pressure is palpable. O'Neill's leadership during this pivotal time will be closely scrutinized as the team aims to reclaim their position at the top of the table.

The Scottish Premiership is known for its competitive nature, and with several teams vying for the title, every point gained is invaluable. O'Neill's ability to instill a winning mentality within the squad could prove to be the difference as the season approaches its climax.

### Looking Ahead

As Celtic prepares for their upcoming fixtures, the focus will remain on maintaining the spirit and determination that characterized their recent performance. O'Neill's interim tenure has already made an impact, and the club will be looking to capitalize on this victory to build further momentum in the league.

In conclusion, Martin O'Neill's commendation of his team's spirit following their victory against Dundee underscores the importance of unity and resilience in the pursuit of the Scottish Premiership title. With the season drawing to a close, Celtic will aim to harness this energy and continue their quest for glory.
