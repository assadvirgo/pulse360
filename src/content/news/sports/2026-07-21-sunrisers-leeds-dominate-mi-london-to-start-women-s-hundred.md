Phoebe Litchfield emerged as the standout performer for Sunrisers Leeds, scoring an impressive 43 runs off just 26 balls. Her aggressive batting style not only laid a solid foundation for the team's chase but also entertained the crowd with a series of well-timed boundaries. Litchfield's innings included five fours and two sixes, demonstrating her ability to find the gaps and accelerate the scoring when needed.
