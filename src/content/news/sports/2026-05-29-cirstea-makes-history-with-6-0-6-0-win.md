## Sorana Cirstea Achieves Historic 6-0, 6-0 Victory at French Open

In a stunning display of skill and determination, Romanian tennis player Sorana Cirstea triumphed over Solana Sierra with a commanding 6-0, 6-0 victory in the third round of the French Open. This remarkable win not only underscores Cirstea's exceptional form but also marks a significant milestone in her career as she advances to the fourth round of this prestigious Grand Slam tournament.

### A Dominant Performance

Cirstea's performance against Sierra was nothing short of extraordinary. The match, played on the clay courts of Roland Garros, showcased her powerful serve and precise groundstrokes, leaving her opponent with little opportunity to mount a challenge. The straight-sets victory, often referred to as a "double bagel" in tennis terminology, reflects the one-sided nature of the contest, with Cirstea dictating play from the outset.

### Career Highlights

At 33 years old, Cirstea is enjoying a resurgence in her career, having previously reached the quarterfinals of the French Open in 2009. This latest achievement not only highlights her longevity in the sport but also her ability to compete at a high level against younger opponents. Cirstea's journey through the tournament thus far has been marked by resilience and skill, making her a player to watch as the competition progresses.
