Audi Crooks, who has garnered attention for her impressive skills on the court, is expected to make an immediate impact at Oklahoma State. Known for her scoring ability and versatility, Crooks has been a standout player at her previous institution, where she showcased her potential as a future star in women's basketball. Her commitment is seen as a strategic move for Oklahoma State, aiming to bolster their competitive edge in the Big 12 Conference.
