## Prasidh Five-for and Jaiswal Century Propel India to 3-0 Series Sweep Against Afghanistan

In a commanding display of cricket, India secured a comprehensive 3-0 series victory over Afghanistan in their One Day International (ODI) series, with standout performances from bowler Prasidh Krishna and batsman Yashasvi Jaiswal. The final match of the series, held at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru, showcased India's dominance in the format, further solidifying their status as a formidable force in international cricket.

### Prasidh Krishna's Bowling Brilliance

Prasidh Krishna emerged as the star of the match, claiming five wickets for just 43 runs in his ten overs. His ability to consistently trouble the Afghan batsmen with pace and precision was instrumental in dismantling their batting lineup. Krishna's wickets included key players who could have posed a significant threat to India's chances of victory. His performance not only earned him the Player of the Match award but also highlighted his growing reputation as a reliable fast bowler in the Indian cricket setup.

### Jaiswal's Maiden ODI Century

Yashasvi Jaiswal also made headlines with his remarkable performance, scoring a brilliant century that contributed significantly to India's total. Jaiswal's innings of 104 runs came off 109 balls, featuring a mix of aggressive strokes and calculated defense. His century was pivotal in setting a challenging target for Afghanistan, showcasing his talent and composure under pressure. This match marked Jaiswal's first ODI century, further establishing him as a key player in India's batting lineup.

### Afghanistan's Response

Despite India's strong performance, Afghanistan's captain Hashmatullah Shahidi provided a glimmer of hope for his team with his maiden ODI century. Shahidi's innings of 102 runs was a testament to his skill and determination, as he fought valiantly against a relentless Indian bowling attack. However, his efforts were ultimately in vain, as the rest of the Afghan batting order struggled to build partnerships, leading to their defeat by a significant margin.

### Series Overview

The series concluded with India showcasing their depth in both batting and bowling departments. The first match saw India win convincingly by seven wickets, while the second match concluded with a 50-run victory for the hosts. The final match further emphasized India's dominance, with a comprehensive win that highlighted their preparation and execution in the lead-up to upcoming international tournaments.

### Looking Ahead

As both teams look to the future, India will take confidence from this series as they prepare for their next set of challenges, including the World Cup. Meanwhile, Afghanistan will seek to regroup and analyze their performances to improve in future series. The experience gained from competing against a top-tier team like India will be invaluable for the Afghan players as they continue to develop their cricketing prowess on the international stage.

In conclusion, India's 3-0 series sweep over Afghanistan not only reflects their current form but also sets the stage for exciting cricket in the months to come. The performances of players like Prasidh Krishna and Yashasvi Jaiswal will be pivotal as India aims for success in upcoming competitions.
