The highlight of the day was an impressive innings from Leus du Plooy, who scored 85 runs, showcasing his skill and determination on the field.

Middlesex began their innings with optimism, looking to set a solid foundation against Worcestershire's bowling attack. Du Plooy, in particular, was instrumental in stabilizing the innings after an early loss of wickets. His composed performance included a series of boundaries that not only boosted the team's score but also lifted the morale of his teammates.
