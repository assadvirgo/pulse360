## Uncapped Striker Cora Chambers Named in Northern Ireland's Squad for World Cup Qualifiers

This marks McArdle's first selection since taking over the managerial role, as the team prepares for a crucial double-header against Malta in the World Cup qualifying campaign scheduled for April.
