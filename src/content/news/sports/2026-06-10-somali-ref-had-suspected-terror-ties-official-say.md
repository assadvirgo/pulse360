In a significant development ahead of the upcoming World Cup, Omar Artan, a referee from Somalia, was denied entry into the United States due to alleged ties to suspected members of terrorist organizations. A U.S. official confirmed this information on Tuesday night, shedding light on the reasons behind the decision, which has sparked discussions within the sports community and beyond.

Omar Artan is recognized for his role in officiating various international football matches.
