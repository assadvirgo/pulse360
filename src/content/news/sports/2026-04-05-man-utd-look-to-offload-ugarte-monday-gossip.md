## Manchester United's Transfer Strategy: Uruguayan Midfielder Ugarte on the Market

As the January transfer window approaches, Manchester United is reportedly looking to offload Uruguayan midfielder Manuel Ugarte. The 22-year-old, who joined the club in the summer, has struggled to secure a consistent starting position, leading the management to consider potential buyers.

### Ugarte's Current Situation at Manchester United

Since his arrival, Ugarte has shown flashes of talent but has not been able to establish himself as a key player in Erik ten Hag's squad. With competition for places intensifying, the club appears to be reassessing its midfield options. Sources indicate that United is actively seeking to move Ugarte on loan or through a permanent transfer, depending on the interest from other clubs.

### Interest from Other Clubs

While specific clubs have not yet been confirmed, it is understood that several teams across Europe are monitoring Ugarte's situation. His performances in the Uruguayan national team and previous club experience have kept him on the radar of scouts from various leagues. A successful loan spell could provide Ugarte with the opportunity to gain valuable playing time and experience, which may ultimately benefit both him and Manchester United.
