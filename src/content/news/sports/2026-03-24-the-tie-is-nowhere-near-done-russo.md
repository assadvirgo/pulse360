## Alessia Russo Reflects on Arsenal's Victory Over Chelsea in Women's Champions League

In a thrilling encounter that showcased the growing competitiveness of women's football, Arsenal secured a decisive 3–1 victory against Chelsea in the first leg of the Women's Champions League quarter-finals. Following the match, Arsenal forward Alessia Russo shared her thoughts on her standout performance and the significance of the result.

### A Moment to Remember

Alessia Russo, who played a pivotal role in the match, scored a remarkable goal that not only contributed to her team's victory but also highlighted her individual talent. The goal, described by many as a defining moment of the match, underscored Russo's ability to perform under pressure and her growing influence within the team.

"I think it was a great team performance," Russo stated in a post-match interview. "We managed to create a lot of chances, and I'm just happy to have contributed with a goal. But the tie is nowhere near done; we have to remain focused for the second leg."

Russo's comments reflect the team's collective mindset as they prepare for the return leg against Chelsea. While the victory provides a strong advantage, the forward is keenly aware that the competition is far from over. The match itself was a testament to the high stakes of the Women's Champions League, with both teams demonstrating their skill and determination. Arsenal's performance was marked by strategic play and effective communication on the field, elements that Russo emphasized as crucial to their success.

### Preferred Playing Role

When asked about her preferred position on the field, Russo expressed her versatility and willingness to adapt. "I enjoy playing in different roles, but I feel most comfortable when I can be involved in the build-up and also get into goal-scoring positions," she explained. This adaptability has made her a valuable asset to Arsenal, as she can seamlessly integrate into various tactical setups.
