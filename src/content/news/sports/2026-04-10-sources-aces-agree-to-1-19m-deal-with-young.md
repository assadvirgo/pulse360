## Aces Finalize $1.19 Million Deal with Jackie Young

The Las Vegas Aces are reportedly finalizing a significant agreement with four-time All-Star guard Jackie Young, securing her return to the team for the upcoming season. According to sources familiar with the negotiations, the deal is valued at approximately $1.19 million.

### Aces' Commitment to Young

Jackie Young, who has been a pivotal player for the Aces since being drafted first overall in 2019, has established herself as a cornerstone of the franchise. Her contributions on both ends of the court have been instrumental in the Aces' recent successes, including their championship run in 2022. The decision to retain Young reflects the team's commitment to maintaining a competitive roster as they aim for continued success in the Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA).

### Young's Impact on the Team

Young's versatility and skill set make her one of the most valuable guards in the league. Over her career, she has averaged over 15 points per game, showcasing her scoring ability while also contributing in assists and rebounds. Her defensive prowess further enhances her value, allowing her to guard multiple positions effectively. The Aces' coaching staff has consistently praised her work ethic and leadership qualities, which have helped foster a strong team culture.

### The Aces' Offseason Strategy

The Aces have been active in the offseason, focusing on retaining key players while also exploring opportunities to enhance their roster. By securing Young's services, the team is not only retaining a proven talent but also sending a message to the league about their ambitions for the upcoming season. The Aces are determined to build upon their recent successes and aim for another championship title.

### Looking Ahead

As the WNBA offseason progresses, the Aces will continue to strategize around their roster and potential acquisitions. With Jackie Young back in the fold, the team is poised to remain a formidable force in the league. Fans and analysts alike will be watching closely as the Aces prepare for the next chapter in their pursuit of excellence.
