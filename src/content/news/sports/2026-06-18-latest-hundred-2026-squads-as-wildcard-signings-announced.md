### Overview of the Hundred Format

The Hundred is a unique cricket tournament that features eight city-based franchises competing in a 100-ball format. Launched in 2021, the tournament has quickly gained popularity, drawing in large crowds and significant viewership. Each franchise is allowed to sign a mix of domestic and international players, with the aim of creating a balanced and competitive squad.
