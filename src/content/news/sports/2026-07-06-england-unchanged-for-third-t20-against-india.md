The decision to maintain the same squad reflects the team's confidence in their current players and strategy. England's coach and selectors believe that the consistency in selection will provide the team with a stable foundation as they aim to secure a series victory against a formidable Indian side. The unchanged lineup indicates that the players have demonstrated the necessary skills and teamwork required to compete at this level.
