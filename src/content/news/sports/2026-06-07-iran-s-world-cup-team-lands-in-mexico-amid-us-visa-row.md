The Iranian squad faces unique challenges due to the current geopolitical climate. While they are set to compete in the World Cup, their group games are scheduled to be held in the United States. However, the players and support staff will not be able to stay in the U.S. for extended periods due to visa restrictions. Instead, they will have to fly in and out of the country on match days, a logistical hurdle that could impact their performance.
