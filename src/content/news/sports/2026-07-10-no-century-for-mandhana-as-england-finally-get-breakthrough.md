## Smriti Mandhana Falls Short of Century as India Faces England in One-Off Test

In a highly anticipated one-off Test match at Lord's, India captain Smriti Mandhana narrowly missed out on a historic century, scoring 83 runs before being dismissed by England's Issy Wong. This pivotal moment occurred shortly after the lunch break, marking a significant breakthrough for the English side as India fell to 190-4 on the opening day of the match.

She played a series of impressive strokes, demonstrating her skill and composure under pressure. Despite her efforts, Mandhana's dismissal came as a disappointment for the Indian side, which had hoped she would convert her promising start into a century. The catch taken by Wong not only ended Mandhana's innings but also shifted the momentum in favor of the English team.
