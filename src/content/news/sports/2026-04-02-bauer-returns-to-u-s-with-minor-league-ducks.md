## Trevor Bauer Signs with Long Island Ducks in Return to U.S. Baseball

Former Cy Young Award winner Trevor Bauer is set to make his return to professional baseball in the United States after signing with the Long Island Ducks of the Atlantic League. The announcement was made recently, with Bauer scheduled to pitch on opening night, April 21.

Bauer, who won the prestigious Cy Young Award in 2020 while playing for the Cincinnati Reds, has had a tumultuous few years both on and off the field. ### The Long Island Ducks

The Long Island Ducks are a professional baseball team based in Central Islip, New York, and are part of the Atlantic League of Professional Baseball. Established in 2000, the Ducks have built a reputation for being a competitive team in the independent league circuit. They have won multiple championships and are known for their community engagement and family-friendly atmosphere at their games.

Bauer's addition to the team is expected to draw significant attention, not only due to his past accolades but also because of the circumstances surrounding his departure from Major League Baseball.
