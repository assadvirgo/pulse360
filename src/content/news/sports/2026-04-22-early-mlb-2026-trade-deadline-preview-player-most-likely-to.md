## Early MLB 2026 Trade Deadline Preview: Potential Trade Candidates for All 30 Teams

As Major League Baseball (MLB) gears up for the 2026 trade deadline, discussions surrounding potential trades are intensifying. Teams are evaluating their rosters, assessing their playoff aspirations, and identifying players who could be made available for trade. This article provides an overview of the most likely candidates from each of the 30 teams, ranging from established stars to promising prospects.

### American League East

**Baltimore Orioles**: The Orioles may consider trading outfielder Cedric Mullins, especially if they are in a rebuilding phase. His speed and defensive skills make him an attractive option for teams looking for an impact player.

**Boston Red Sox**: With the Red Sox potentially facing a tough season, veteran pitcher Chris Sale could be on the move. His experience and talent might entice teams looking for a playoff push.

**New York Yankees**: The Yankees could explore trading infielder Gleyber Torres if they decide to shift their focus toward younger talent. His offensive capabilities remain appealing to many contenders.

**Tampa Bay Rays**: The Rays might part with pitcher Shane McClanahan, especially if they receive a lucrative offer. His ability to dominate on the mound could be a game-changer for any team.

**Toronto Blue Jays**: The Blue Jays may look to trade outfielder Teoscar Hernández, particularly if they need to bolster their pitching staff. His power bat would be a valuable asset for many teams.

### American League Central

**Chicago White Sox**: The White Sox could consider trading first baseman José Abreu if they find themselves out of contention. His veteran presence and hitting prowess would attract interest.

**Cleveland Guardians**: The Guardians might look to move pitcher Shane Bieber, especially if they are unable to secure a playoff spot. His status as an ace makes him a coveted target.

**Detroit Tigers**: The Tigers could trade outfielder Riley Greene if they are in a rebuilding mode. His potential and youth could draw significant interest.

**Kansas City Royals**: The Royals may consider moving pitcher Brady Singer, who has shown flashes of brilliance. A team in need of pitching depth might find him appealing.

**Minnesota Twins**: The Twins could trade infielder Jorge Polanco, particularly if they aim to revamp their roster. His versatility and experience could benefit many teams.

### American League West

**Houston Astros**: The Astros might look to trade infielder Alex Bregman if they seek to refresh their lineup. His offensive skills would be a strong asset to any contender.

**Los Angeles Angels**: The Angels could consider moving pitcher Shohei Ohtani, especially if they find themselves out of playoff contention. His unique talent as a two-way player would generate massive interest.

**Oakland Athletics**: The Athletics may look to trade catcher Sean Murphy, whose defensive skills and offensive potential could enhance a playoff-bound team.

**Seattle Mariners**: The Mariners could explore trading pitcher Luis Castillo if they are unable to compete. His strong arm makes him a valuable trade piece.

**Texas Rangers**: The Rangers might consider moving outfielder Adolis García if they decide to focus on younger talent. His power and speed would attract attention from various teams.

### National League East
