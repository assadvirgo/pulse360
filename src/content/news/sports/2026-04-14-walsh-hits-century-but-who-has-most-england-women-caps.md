## Keira Walsh Reaches Century Milestone in England Women's Football

In a notable achievement for both Keira Walsh and the England Women’s national football team, Walsh reached her 100th cap during a recent match against Spain. This milestone not only highlights her individual talent and dedication but also brings attention to the broader context of appearances within the national team.

### Walsh's Journey to 100 Caps

Keira Walsh, a key player in the England squad, has been instrumental in the team's performances over the years. Her journey to 100 caps is a testament to her skill, resilience, and commitment to the sport. Since making her debut, Walsh has established herself as a vital component of the midfield, contributing both defensively and offensively. 

Her century of appearances places her among an elite group of players who have represented England at the highest level. As fans and analysts reflect on her career, it is evident that Walsh's influence extends beyond the pitch, inspiring younger players and contributing to the growth of women's football in England.

### Most Capped Players in England Women's History

As Walsh celebrates her milestone, it raises the question of who holds the record for the most appearances for the England Women's team. Currently, the record is held by Fara Williams, who amassed an impressive 172 caps during her career. Williams, a midfield stalwart, played a pivotal role in the development of women's football in England and is widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport.

Following Williams, other notable players include Rachel Daly and Jill Scott, both of whom have made significant contributions to the national team and have been key figures in various international tournaments. Their experiences and performances have helped shape the current landscape of women's football in England.
