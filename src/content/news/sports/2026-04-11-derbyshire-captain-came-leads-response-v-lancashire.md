The day was notably affected by rain, which limited play and added an element of unpredictability to the contest.

Despite these challenges, Came's performance stood out as a beacon of determination for his side. His innings was marked by a blend of patience and aggression, crucial in setting a solid foundation for Derbyshire's response.
