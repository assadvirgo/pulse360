### BPI Projections

The Basketball Power Index (BPI) serves as a predictive tool that estimates a team's strength and likelihood of winning based on various metrics. For Sunday’s games, BPI projections indicate that Team A has a 65% chance of defeating Team B, while Team C is projected to have a commanding 75% chance against Team D.

These projections take into account not only the teams' win-loss records but also their performance against common opponents, player statistics, and other advanced metrics. As such, BPI serves as a comprehensive resource for understanding the dynamics of each matchup.
