## Harry Kane Sets New England World Cup Goals Record

In a remarkable display of skill and determination, Harry Kane has etched his name in the annals of football history by becoming England's all-time top scorer in the FIFA World Cup. During England's final Group L match against Panama, Kane netted his 11th World Cup goal, surpassing the previous record held by Gary Lineker.

### A Historic Moment

The milestone moment occurred as Kane doubled England's lead in the match, showcasing his exceptional striking ability. The goal not only solidified England's position in the game but also marked a significant personal achievement for Kane, who has rapidly become one of the nation's most celebrated footballers.

Kane's journey to this record began in 2018 when he made a significant impact during the World Cup in Russia, where he finished as the tournament's top scorer. Since then, he has continued to demonstrate his prowess on the international stage, contributing to England's successes in various competitions.

As England faced Panama, the stakes were high, with the team aiming to secure a strong position heading into the knockout stages of the tournament. Kane's goal came at a crucial time, providing the team with a psychological boost and further asserting their dominance in the match.
