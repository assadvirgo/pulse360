## Prosecutors Seek Pretrial Detention for Lions' Terrion Arnold

In a significant legal development, Terrion Arnold, a cornerback for the Detroit Lions, has been ordered to remain in custody without bail following a court hearing in Tampa, Florida. Arnold faces serious felony charges, including armed robbery and kidnapping, which have prompted prosecutors to argue for his continued detention until his trial.

### Court Proceedings and Charges

During the hearing held on Thursday, the presiding judge agreed to hold Arnold without bail, reflecting the gravity of the charges against him. A pretrial detention hearing has been scheduled for Monday morning, where the court will further evaluate the circumstances surrounding the case and determine whether Arnold should remain incarcerated until his trial begins.

The charges of armed robbery and kidnapping are particularly severe, carrying significant legal ramifications. If convicted, Arnold could face substantial prison time, which underscores the importance of the upcoming pretrial hearing.
