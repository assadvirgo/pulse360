## USMNT's Johnny Cardoso to Undergo Ankle Surgery, World Cup Participation in Doubt

In a significant development for the United States Men's National Team (USMNT), Atlético Madrid has confirmed that midfielder Johnny Cardoso will undergo surgery to address an injury to his right ankle. The announcement, made on Monday, raises concerns about Cardoso's availability for the upcoming FIFA World Cup.

### Injury Details

Cardoso, who has been a key player for both his club and the national team, sustained the ankle injury during a recent training session. The nature of the injury was not disclosed in detail, but it has been serious enough to necessitate surgical intervention. The procedure is expected to take place in the coming days, with the club expressing optimism regarding his recovery timeline.

### Implications for the USMNT

The timing of Cardoso's surgery is particularly concerning as the World Cup is set to commence in less than two months. The midfielder has been a crucial part of the USMNT's plans, contributing both defensively and offensively. His absence could significantly impact the team's strategy and depth, especially considering the competitive nature of the tournament.

Head Coach Gregg Berhalter will need to assess his options carefully in light of Cardoso's potential unavailability. The USMNT has a history of overcoming injuries, but losing a player of Cardoso's caliber poses a substantial challenge.

### Recovery Timeline

While specific details regarding the expected recovery timeline have not been released, surgical procedures on ankle injuries can vary widely in terms of rehabilitation duration. Depending on the severity of the damage, Cardoso could face a recovery period ranging from several weeks to a few months. This uncertainty adds to the urgency for the USMNT as they prepare for the World Cup.

### The Road Ahead

As the USMNT gears up for the tournament, the coaching staff will likely explore alternative midfield options. Players such as Weston McKennie and Tyler Adams may need to step up to fill the potential void left by Cardoso. The team is scheduled to finalize its roster soon, and every decision will be critical as they aim to make a strong showing on the world stage.

### Conclusion

Johnny Cardoso's upcoming surgery is a setback for both Atlético Madrid and the USMNT, with significant implications for the team's World Cup aspirations. As fans and analysts closely monitor his recovery, the focus will also shift to how the national team adapts to this unexpected challenge. The next few weeks will be crucial for Cardoso's rehabilitation and the USMNT's preparations as they aim to compete effectively in the global tournament.
