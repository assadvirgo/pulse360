During the match, Manchester United found the back of the net with what was initially celebrated as a pivotal goal. However, Cann's analysis suggests that the goal was marred by an infringement that went unnoticed by the officiating team. According to Cann, the goal should have been ruled out due to a violation of the laws of the game, raising questions about the effectiveness of VAR (Video Assistant Referee) technology in such instances.

### Cann's Analysis

In his commentary, Cann emphasized the importance of adhering to the rules that govern football. He pointed out that the officiating team, including the VAR, failed to recognize a key incident leading up to the goal. "There was a clear infringement that should have been caught," Cann noted, suggesting that the oversight undermined the integrity of the match. 

Cann's perspective is particularly significant given his extensive background in officiating. Cann's comments highlight ongoing frustrations regarding the technology, as many believe it should enhance the accuracy of decisions rather than complicate them.
