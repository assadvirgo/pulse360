## Nidar Dar Makes Herself Available for Pakistan Selection After Year-Long Hiatus

Nidar Dar, the talented all-rounder, has announced her readiness to return to the Pakistan national cricket team after a year-long break. Dar, who last represented Pakistan in October 2024, took a hiatus from the sport in April 2025 to prioritize her mental health. 

### Background of the Hiatus

Dar's decision to step away from cricket came at a time when mental health awareness was gaining significant traction within the sports community. The pressures of professional sports can be immense, and Dar's choice to focus on her well-being reflects a growing recognition of the importance of mental health among athletes. During her time away, she engaged in various activities aimed at self-care and recovery, which she has described as essential for her personal growth.

### Return to Competitive Cricket

In a recent statement, Dar expressed her enthusiasm about rejoining the national squad. "I feel rejuvenated and ready to contribute to the team once again," she said. "Taking time off was necessary for me to regain my focus and passion for the game." Her return is eagerly anticipated by fans and teammates alike, as she brings a unique skill set and experience to the field.

Dar's last appearance for Pakistan was marked by her impressive performances, which included vital contributions with both bat and ball. Her versatility as an all-rounder has made her an invaluable asset to the team, and her absence over the past year has been felt deeply in the squad.

### Implications for the National Team

With the upcoming series and tournaments on the horizon, Dar's return could significantly bolster Pakistan's chances of success. The national team has been working to build a strong lineup, and Dar's experience will be crucial in providing stability and leadership, especially for younger players. 

Head coach and selectors are likely to consider her for upcoming matches, given her track record and the skills she brings to the game. The team has been undergoing a transitional phase, and the inclusion of a seasoned player like Dar could enhance their performance and morale.

### The Importance of Mental Health in Sports

Dar's journey highlights an essential conversation about mental health in sports. Athletes often face intense scrutiny and pressure, which can lead to burnout and other mental health challenges. By openly discussing her struggles and prioritizing her well-being, Dar sets a positive example for others in the sporting community. 

Her return not only signifies a personal triumph but also serves as a reminder that mental health is a critical aspect of an athlete's career. As more athletes speak out about their experiences, the stigma surrounding mental health issues continues to diminish, paving the way for a more supportive environment in sports.

### Conclusion

Nidar Dar's decision to make herself available for selection is a promising development for Pakistan cricket. As she prepares to re-enter the competitive arena, her focus on mental health and personal well-being serves as an inspiring narrative for athletes everywhere. The cricketing community looks forward to witnessing her contributions on the field in the near future.
