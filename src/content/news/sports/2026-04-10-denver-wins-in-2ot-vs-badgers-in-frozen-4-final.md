In a gripping display of skill and endurance, the University of Denver Pioneers triumphed over the University of Michigan Wolverines in a double-overtime showdown, securing a 4-3 victory in the semifinals of the Frozen Four. The match began with both teams showcasing their offensive prowess. The Wolverines struck first, taking an early lead that set the tone for a back-and-forth contest. However, the Pioneers responded quickly, demonstrating their resilience as they fought to equalize. The first period ended with a 1-1 tie, setting the stage for an electrifying second period.

As the game progressed, both teams exchanged goals, with Denver's Kent Anderson emerging as a key player.
