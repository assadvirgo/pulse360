## GM Clarifies Myles Garrett Contract Changes Are Not Linked to Trade Speculation

In a recent statement, Cleveland Browns general manager Andrew Berry addressed concerns regarding modifications made to Myles Garrett's contract, emphasizing that these changes do not indicate an impending trade for the NFL's reigning Defensive Player of the Year.

### Context of the Contract Modification

The adjustments to Garrett's contract have sparked discussions among fans and analysts alike, leading to speculation about the team's future plans regarding the star defensive end. However, Berry was quick to clarify that the alterations were strictly contractual and not a precursor to any potential trade discussions. 

Garrett, who has been a cornerstone of the Browns' defense since being drafted first overall in 2017, has consistently demonstrated elite performance on the field. His accolades, including being named the NFL Defensive Player of the Year, have solidified his status as one of the league's premier defensive talents. 

### Berry's Assurance

In his statement, Berry reiterated the organization's commitment to Garrett, stating, "The changes we made to Myles' contract are purely procedural and should not be interpreted as anything more than that. We value Myles as a key player for our team and have no intention of trading him." This declaration aims to quell any rumors that may disrupt the team's focus as they prepare for the upcoming season.

### Implications for the Team

The clarification from Berry comes at a critical time as the Browns gear up for the 2023 NFL season. With heightened expectations surrounding the team, maintaining a stable roster is essential for their success. Garrett's presence on the field is vital not only for his individual contributions but also for the leadership he provides to the defense.

The Browns have made significant investments in their roster, and retaining top talent like Garrett is crucial for their aspirations of competing in a challenging AFC North division. The team's management appears to be focused on building a cohesive unit capable of contending for a playoff spot, and keeping Garrett is a key component of that strategy.

### Conclusion

As the NFL landscape continues to evolve, the Browns' decision to modify Myles Garrett's contract without any trade implications reflects a broader strategy of stability and commitment to their core players. Berry's reassurances serve to reinforce the organization's dedication to building a competitive team while keeping fans informed and engaged. 

With the season approaching, all eyes will be on the Browns as they look to leverage their talent and make a significant impact in the league.
