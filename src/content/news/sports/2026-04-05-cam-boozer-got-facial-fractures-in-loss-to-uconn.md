The injury occurred as the game intensified, ultimately contributing to Duke's defeat.

The injury took place in the second half of the game, a critical juncture where both teams were vying for a spot in the Final Four. Boozer, known for his skillful play and leadership on the court, was involved in a collision that resulted in the facial fractures. The specifics of the incident highlight the physical nature of college basketball, where players often face significant risks as they compete at high levels of intensity.
