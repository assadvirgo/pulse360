The recent dismissal of John Phelan from his position as the Secretary of the Navy has raised significant questions about the current state of military leadership in the United States. His firing, reportedly executed by Pete Hegseth, has occurred against a backdrop of heightened tensions in the Middle East, particularly concerning the ongoing conflict between Israel and Iran.

## Background on John Phelan

John Phelan was appointed as the Secretary of the Navy in early 2022, bringing with him a wealth of experience in military and defense matters. Prior to his appointment, Phelan held various roles within the Department of Defense, where he was recognized for his strategic insights and commitment to naval modernization. His tenure was marked by a focus on enhancing naval capabilities and addressing emerging threats in an increasingly complex global security environment.
