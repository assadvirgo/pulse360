## Indian Activist Sonam Wangchuk Urged to End Hunger Strike Amid Exam Paper Leak Protests

In a significant development in Indian civil society, prominent activist Sonam Wangchuk has been on a hunger strike in New Delhi since June 28, 2023, to protest against the recent leaks of examination papers that have sparked widespread outrage among students and parents alike. Wangchuk's fast is intended to draw attention to the issues surrounding educational integrity and the need for systemic reforms in the examination process.

### Background of the Protest

Wangchuk, a well-known advocate for educational reform and environmental sustainability, has aligned himself with the Cockroach Janta Party, a grassroots movement led by Generation Z activists. The party has been vocal about various issues affecting the youth, including educational inequities and the lack of accountability in the examination system. The recent leaks of examination papers have intensified calls for transparency and justice, with many students expressing concern over the fairness of the evaluation process.

The Cockroach Janta Party has gained traction on social media, mobilizing young people to voice their frustrations and demand change. Wangchuk's participation in the hunger strike serves to amplify these demands, highlighting the urgency of addressing the systemic failures within the education sector.

### Wangchuk's Message and Support

During his hunger strike, Wangchuk has emphasized the importance of integrity in education, arguing that the future of the youth is at stake. He has called for immediate action from the government to investigate the leaks and implement measures that ensure the sanctity of examinations. His fast has garnered support from various quarters, including fellow activists, students, and educational reform advocates who share his concerns.

Despite the growing support for his cause, there are increasing calls for Wangchuk to end his hunger strike for health reasons. Friends and colleagues have expressed concern for his well-being, urging him to consider alternative forms of protest that do not jeopardize his health. They argue that while his intentions are noble, his physical state is crucial for sustaining the momentum of the movement he represents.

### Government Response

As the situation unfolds, the Indian government has faced mounting pressure to address the issues raised by Wangchuk and the Cockroach Janta Party. Officials have acknowledged the need for a thorough investigation into the examination paper leaks and have indicated that measures will be taken to prevent such incidents in the future. However, critics argue that these responses have been slow and insufficient, calling for more decisive action.

### The Broader Implications

Wangchuk's hunger strike is not just a protest against examination paper leaks; it symbolizes a broader struggle for educational reform in India. The incident has sparked conversations about the quality of education, the fairness of assessment methods, and the need for a more equitable system that serves all students. As the youth of India increasingly assert their voices, the implications of this movement could extend beyond the immediate concerns of examination integrity, potentially influencing policy changes in the long term.

As Sonam Wangchuk continues his fast, the nation watches closely, with many hoping that his actions will lead to meaningful dialogue and reform in the education sector.
