In a thrilling day of competition at the World Indoor Athletics Championships in Poland, Great Britain's Georgia Hunter Bell secured her place in the final of the women's 1500 meters, while her compatriot Jemma Reekie narrowly missed advancing after finishing fourth in her heat.

Georgia Hunter Bell showcased her talent and determination in the heats, delivering a strong performance that saw her cross the finish line first in her heat.
