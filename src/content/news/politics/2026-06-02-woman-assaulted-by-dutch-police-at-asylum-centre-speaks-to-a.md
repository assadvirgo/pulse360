## Incident Involving Pregnant Woman at Dutch Asylum Centre

In a recent incident that has drawn significant attention, Malak Mahmoud, a pregnant woman, was filmed being forcibly restrained by a police officer at an asylum centre in the Netherlands. The footage, which has circulated widely on social media, shows Mahmoud being thrown to the ground, prompting widespread outrage and calls for accountability.

## Context of the Incident

The incident occurred at an asylum centre where Mahmoud was reportedly seeking refuge. The circumstances leading up to the confrontation remain unclear, but the video footage has raised serious questions about the treatment of asylum seekers in the Netherlands. Advocates for refugee rights have expressed concern over the use of force by law enforcement in such sensitive environments.

In an exclusive interview with Al Jazeera, Mahmoud recounted her experience and the impact it has had on her well-being and that of her unborn child.
