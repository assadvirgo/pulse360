Fortunately, no fatalities have been reported, although several individuals sustained injuries and were transported to nearby hospitals for treatment.

The police apprehended the pensioner without incident, and he is currently in custody as investigations continue. Authorities have not released the names of the victims or the specific location of the shooting, citing ongoing investigations and the need to protect the privacy of those involved.
