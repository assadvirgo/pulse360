## Greek Politician's Mother Dies Following Arson Attack

In a tragic turn of events, the mother of a prominent Greek politician has succumbed to injuries sustained during a series of violent arson attacks in Thessaloniki. The incidents, which occurred in the early hours of Wednesday, involved the use of petrol bombs and other improvised incendiary devices.

### Details of the Attacks

The attacks took place in various locations across Thessaloniki, Greece's second-largest city, which has a history of political unrest and violence. Local authorities reported that multiple petrol bombs were thrown at buildings, resulting in significant damage and injuries. The police have launched an investigation into the incidents, seeking to identify the perpetrators and the motivations behind the attacks.

### Victim's Background

The victim, who has not been publicly named, was the mother of a well-known political figure in Greece. Her death has sent shockwaves through the political community and raised concerns over the safety of public figures and their families. The politician's office released a statement expressing profound sorrow over the loss, highlighting the impact of political violence on innocent lives.
