## World Cup Half-Time Show Features Star-Studded Lineup

In a vibrant celebration of music and culture, the World Cup half-time show captivated audiences with performances from some of the biggest names in the entertainment industry. The half-time show, which took place during one of the tournament's key matches, was designed to entertain a diverse audience, blending various musical styles and cultural influences. Shakira, known for her energetic performances and international hits, brought her signature flair to the stage, while Madonna's appearance added a touch of pop royalty to the event. Justin Bieber, a global pop sensation, further energized the crowd with his chart-topping hits, and BTS, the South Korean boy band that has taken the world by storm, delivered a performance that resonated with fans across the globe.

In addition to these major acts, Robbie Williams and Jennifer Hudson also graced the stage, contributing to a dynamic lineup that appealed to fans of all ages. Their performances were marked by elaborate stage designs and choreography, enhancing the festive atmosphere of the World Cup.
