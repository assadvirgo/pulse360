### The Methodology Behind the Predictions

The zoo's staff have devised a method for the animals to "predict" the outcomes of the World Cup matches. Various animals are presented with two or more options, typically represented by food items, toys, or other stimuli that correspond to the competing teams. The choice made by the animal is then interpreted as a prediction for which team is likely to win.

This method is reminiscent of previous animal predictions made during international sporting events, where creatures such as octopuses and elephants have gained fame for their seemingly accurate forecasts. The Guadalajara Zoo's initiative aims to foster interest in the World Cup while providing a fun spectacle for visitors.

The zoo's animal predictions also serve a dual purpose by promoting community engagement and education.
