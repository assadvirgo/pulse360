## Neymar's Fitness Crucial for World Cup Selection, Says Ancelotti

As Brazil gears up for the upcoming World Cup, the national team's coach, Carlo Ancelotti, has emphasized the importance of Neymar's fitness in determining his selection for the squad. Ancelotti stated that the star forward has a two-month window to prove that he can regain full fitness ahead of the tournament, which is set to take place later this year.

### Neymar's Injury History

Neymar, who plays for Al-Hilal in Saudi Arabia, has faced a series of injuries in recent years that have raised concerns about his ability to perform at the highest level. His last significant injury occurred during the 2022 FIFA World Cup, where he was sidelined for crucial matches. Ancelotti's comments come as a reminder of the physical demands placed on athletes competing in such a prestigious event.

### Ancelotti's Assessment

In a recent press conference, Ancelotti expressed optimism about Neymar's potential return to form. "If Neymar can achieve full fitness in the next two months, he will certainly be in contention for a spot on the World Cup squad," Ancelotti remarked. He acknowledged the player's exceptional talent but reiterated that fitness would be the deciding factor in the selection process.

### The Road Ahead

With the World Cup fast approaching, Neymar's rehabilitation will be closely monitored by both the coaching staff and fans. The Brazilian forward has been an integral part of the national team, known for his skill, creativity, and ability to change the course of a match. His absence from the squad would not only be a significant loss for Brazil but could also impact the overall dynamics of the team.

### Support from Fans and Teammates

Neymar's journey to recovery is being followed with great interest by supporters and fellow players alike. Many have taken to social media to express their encouragement and belief in his ability to bounce back. Teammates have also voiced their support, emphasizing the importance of having Neymar on the field for Brazil's chances in the tournament.

### Conclusion

As the countdown to the World Cup continues, Neymar's fitness will remain a focal point for the Brazilian national team. Ancelotti's assertion underscores the delicate balance between talent and physical readiness in elite sports. With just two months to go, all eyes will be on Neymar as he strives to reclaim his place in one of the world's most competitive football squads. The coming weeks will be critical, not only for Neymar but for Brazil's ambitions on the global stage.
