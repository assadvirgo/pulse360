In a momentous achievement for Turkish football, Turkiye has qualified for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, marking the nation's return to the global stage after a 24-year hiatus. Turkiye's last appearance in the World Cup was in 2002, where they finished in a commendable third place. Since then, the national team has faced numerous challenges, including changes in coaching staff, player development issues, and fluctuating performances in international competitions. The long wait for qualification has been a source of frustration for fans and players alike, making this achievement particularly sweet.
