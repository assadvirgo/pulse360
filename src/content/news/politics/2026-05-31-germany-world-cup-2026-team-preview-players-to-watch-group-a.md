## Germany's World Cup 2026 Team Preview: A New Era Under Julian Nagelsmann

As the footballing world turns its attention to the 2026 FIFA World Cup, Germany is poised to make a significant comeback after a disappointing performance in the last two tournaments. The 2014 World Cup champions, who exited in the group stages in both 2018 and 2022, are now under the guidance of Julian Nagelsmann, a coach known for his innovative tactics and ability to develop young talent.

## A Fresh Start with Nagelsmann

Julian Nagelsmann took over the national team following a tumultuous period marked by underperformance. His appointment brings a fresh perspective and a renewed sense of optimism to the squad. Nagelsmann's coaching philosophy emphasizes possession-based football and high pressing, which could reinvigorate the German style of play that has historically been characterized by efficiency and teamwork.

Under Nagelsmann, Germany has already shown signs of improvement in recent friendlies and qualifying matches. His ability to adapt strategies based on the opponent and his focus on nurturing young players could be pivotal in shaping a competitive squad for the World Cup.

- **Joshua Kimmich**: A stalwart in the German lineup, Kimmich's leadership and experience are vital. His ability to control the tempo of the game and contribute defensively will be essential for the team's success.
