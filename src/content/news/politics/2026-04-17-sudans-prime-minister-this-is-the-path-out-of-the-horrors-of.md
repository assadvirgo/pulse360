## Sudan’s Prime Minister: This is the Path Out of the Horrors of War

In a recent address, Sudan's Prime Minister Kamil Idris outlined a comprehensive vision aimed at ending the ongoing conflict that has plagued the nation for years. With the country facing severe humanitarian crises and political instability, Idris emphasized the necessity of restoring civilian rule and rebuilding the war-torn nation.

## The Current Situation

Sudan has been embroiled in conflict since the overthrow of former President Omar al-Bashir in 2019, which led to a fragile transition towards democracy. However, this transition has been marred by military coups and escalating violence, particularly in regions like Darfur and South Kordofan. The humanitarian situation has deteriorated significantly, with millions displaced and in urgent need of assistance.

## Vision for Peace

In his address, Idris articulated a multi-faceted approach to achieving peace and stability. He called for an immediate ceasefire and urged all parties involved in the conflict to engage in dialogue. "The path to peace is not merely the absence of war; it is the presence of justice, equality, and opportunity for all Sudanese citizens," he stated.

Idris emphasized the importance of inclusive governance, asserting that all ethnic and political groups must have a voice in the reconstruction process. "We cannot build a new Sudan without the participation of every segment of our society," he remarked, highlighting the need for a national reconciliation process.

## Rebuilding the Nation

The Prime Minister outlined a strategic plan for rebuilding the nation’s infrastructure and economy, which have been severely impacted by years of conflict. He proposed initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and economic development, aiming to create jobs and improve living conditions for the Sudanese people. "Our focus will be on sustainable development that empowers our youth and revitalizes our communities," Idris noted.

Additionally, Idris called for international support to aid in these efforts. He acknowledged that while Sudan must take the lead in its recovery, external assistance will be crucial in addressing the immediate humanitarian needs and facilitating long-term development.

## Restoring Civilian Rule

A significant aspect of Idris's vision is the restoration of civilian rule, which he believes is essential for lasting peace. He reiterated his commitment to a democratic transition and urged the international community to hold all parties accountable for their actions during the conflict. "We must ensure that those who commit atrocities are brought to justice, as this is fundamental to rebuilding trust among our people," he stated.

## Conclusion

Kamil Idris's address marks a pivotal moment in Sudan's ongoing struggle for peace and stability. His vision for a reconciled, democratic nation reflects a deep understanding of the complexities involved in ending the conflict and rebuilding the country. As Sudan navigates this challenging path, the commitment to inclusive governance, sustainable development, and the restoration of civilian rule will be critical in shaping its future. The international community's role in supporting these efforts will also be vital in ensuring that Sudan can emerge from the shadows of war into a brighter, more hopeful future.
