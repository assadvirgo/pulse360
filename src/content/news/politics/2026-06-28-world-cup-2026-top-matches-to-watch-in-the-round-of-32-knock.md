## World Cup 2026: Key Matches to Anticipate in the Round of 32 Knockout Stage

As the 2026 FIFA World Cup progresses, football enthusiasts around the globe are gearing up for the highly anticipated knockout stage. With the tournament expanding to include 48 teams, the Round of 32 promises to deliver thrilling matchups that will capture the attention of fans and analysts alike. This article highlights some of the top matches to watch as teams vie for a place in the next round.

## The Format of the Knockout Stage

The knockout stage of the World Cup is a defining moment in the tournament, where teams must win to advance. Unlike the group stage, where teams can afford to lose or draw, each match in the knockout phase is a do-or-die affair. This format often leads to heightened tension and dramatic moments on the pitch, making it a focal point for viewers.

## Notable Matchups to Watch

### 1. USA vs. Mexico

One of the most anticipated matches in the Round of 32 is the clash between the United States and Mexico. Known for their fierce rivalry, both teams have a rich history in international football. The match is expected to attract significant attention, not only due to the competitive nature of the teams but also because of the cultural significance of the encounter. Fans from both nations are expected to fill the stadium, creating an electric atmosphere.

### 2. Brazil vs. Argentina

Another match that promises to be a spectacle is the potential showdown between Brazil and Argentina. These two South American powerhouses have a storied rivalry, and their encounters are often marked by high stakes and memorable moments. With both teams boasting world-class talent, this match could be a highlight of the knockout stage, showcasing the best of what international football has to offer.

### 3. Germany vs. France

The matchup between Germany and France is another key highlight to watch. Both teams have a history of success in the World Cup, with Germany being four-time champions and France having lifted the trophy twice. Their tactical prowess and skilled players make this encounter one that could go down to the wire, with fans eagerly anticipating a display of high-level football.

### 4. Spain vs. Portugal

The Iberian rivalry between Spain and Portugal is set to add another chapter in this World Cup. Both teams have shown strong performances in the group stage, and their encounter in the knockout phase is expected to be a tactical battle. With star players on both sides, this match is likely to be a showcase of skill and strategy, drawing in viewers from around the world.
