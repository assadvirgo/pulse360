In contrast, Atletico Madrid, known for its passionate supporters and resilient spirit, has carved out its own identity and success in recent years.

Historically, the matches between these two teams have been closely contested, with both clubs having their share of victories. The Madrid derby is not just a game; it is a cultural event that captivates fans and garners attention from around the globe.
