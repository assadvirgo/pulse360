## Mbappe Matches Messi's Goal Record as France Advances in World Cup 2026

In a tightly contested match, France secured a narrow 1-0 victory over Paraguay in the Round of 16 at the World Cup 2026, setting the stage for a quarter-final clash against Morocco. The match proved pivotal for Kylian Mbappe, who scored the only goal of the game, bringing his total to seven goals in the tournament, thereby equaling the record set by Lionel Messi.

### A Game of Few Chances

The encounter, held at a packed stadium, saw both teams struggle to find their rhythm in the first half. France, known for its attacking prowess, faced a resilient Paraguayan defense that thwarted several attempts on goal. Paraguay, on the other hand, aimed to capitalize on counter-attacks but found it challenging to penetrate the French backline.

The breakthrough came in the second half when Mbappe showcased his exceptional skill and composure. In the 67th minute, he received a well-placed pass from Antoine Griezmann, maneuvered past a defender, and struck a low shot that found the back of the net. This goal not only propelled France into the next round but also marked a significant milestone for the young forward, as he matched Messi's tally in the tournament.

### Mbappe's Rising Star

Kylian Mbappe's performance throughout the tournament has been nothing short of remarkable. At just 24 years old, he has consistently demonstrated his ability to perform on the biggest stage, further solidifying his status as one of the world's elite players. His achievement in matching Messi's goal-scoring record highlights his potential to become one of football's all-time greats.

Post-match, Mbappe expressed his gratitude towards his teammates and emphasized the importance of teamwork in achieving success. "Scoring goals is great, but it's a collective effort that gets us to this stage," he stated, reflecting on the team's journey thus far.

### Looking Ahead: France vs. Morocco

As France prepares for their upcoming quarter-final match against Morocco, the team is filled with confidence. Morocco's impressive run in the tournament has captured the attention of fans worldwide, and the clash promises to be an exciting encounter. France's coach, Didier Deschamps, acknowledged the challenge posed by Morocco but remained optimistic about his team's chances.

"We respect every opponent, and Morocco has shown they are a formidable side. However, we will focus on our strengths and play our game," Deschamps remarked during the post-match press conference.

### Conclusion

With the victory over Paraguay, France not only advances to the quarter-finals but also sets a new narrative for the tournament. The match highlighted the brilliance of Kylian Mbappe, who continues to rise as a key figure in international football.
