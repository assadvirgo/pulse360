In a significant disciplinary action following a heated encounter in La Liga 2, Esteban Andrada, the goalkeeper for Real Zaragoza, has been handed a 13-match suspension. This decision comes after a brawl erupted during a recent derby match, which has drawn considerable attention from fans and officials alike.

In addition to Andrada's lengthy ban, his teammate, Dani Tasende, has been suspended for two matches for his involvement in the fracas.
