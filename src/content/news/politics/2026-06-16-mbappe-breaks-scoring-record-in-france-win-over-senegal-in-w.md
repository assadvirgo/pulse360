## Kylian Mbappe Becomes France's All-Time Top Scorer

In a remarkable display of talent and determination, Kylian Mbappe has etched his name into the annals of French football history by becoming the all-time top scorer for the national team. This milestone was achieved during France's recent victory over Senegal in the ongoing FIFA World Cup, where Mbappe scored two goals, bringing his total to 58 international goals.

## A Historic Achievement

Mbappe's achievement is not just a personal triumph; it reflects his significant contributions to the French national team since his debut in 2017. The 24-year-old forward has consistently demonstrated his exceptional skill set, agility, and goal-scoring prowess, which have made him a pivotal figure in France's football landscape. With this latest performance, he surpasses the previous record held by Thierry Henry, who scored 51 goals for France.

The match against Senegal showcased not only Mbappe's scoring ability but also his leadership on the pitch. His goals came at crucial moments, helping to secure a 3-1 victory for France, which bolsters their position in the tournament. The win is vital as teams strive to advance to the knockout stages of the World Cup, and Mbappe's contributions are expected to be instrumental in France's quest for a second consecutive title.

## Climbing the All-Time Scoring Ladder

While Mbappe has now set a new benchmark for French football, he is also closing in on the all-time World Cup scoring record. He currently stands just two goals shy of Miroslav Klose's record of 24 goals, a feat that adds an exciting layer to his ongoing World Cup campaign. With several matches still to play, the possibility of Mbappe surpassing Klose's record is a tantalizing prospect for fans and analysts alike.

Mbappe's rise as a football icon has been meteoric, and his ability to perform under pressure has earned him accolades from players and coaches around the world.
