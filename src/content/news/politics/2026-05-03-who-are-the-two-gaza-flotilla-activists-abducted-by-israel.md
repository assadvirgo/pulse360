## Background on the Gaza Flotilla

The Gaza flotilla refers to a series of maritime missions aimed at breaking the Israeli blockade of the Gaza Strip, which has been in place since 2007. These missions typically involve ships carrying humanitarian aid, medical supplies, and construction materials. The flotilla has garnered support from various international activists and organizations advocating for Palestinian rights, as well as criticism from the Israeli government, which views these missions as provocations.
