## Scaloni Praises Messi's Enduring Excellence at 39

As the FIFA World Cup progresses, Argentine coach Lionel Scaloni has expressed his admiration for Lionel Messi, who continues to demonstrate remarkable performance on the world stage despite being 39 years old. Scaloni's comments come in light of a recent muscle strain that briefly raised concerns about Messi's ability to compete effectively in the tournament.

### Messi's Resilience

Messi, often regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, has had a storied career filled with accolades and achievements. His recent muscle strain, which could have jeopardized his participation in the World Cup, has not deterred him from showcasing his skills. Scaloni, who has closely observed Messi's training and gameplay, remarked, "He’s a machine." This statement underscores Scaloni's belief in Messi's unparalleled work ethic and resilience, traits that have contributed to his longevity in a sport that demands peak physical condition.

At 39, Messi's continued presence in high-stakes matches raises questions about age and performance in professional sports.
