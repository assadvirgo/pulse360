## 2026 FIFA World Cup Knockout Stage: Teams Qualified for the Round of 32

As the excitement surrounding the 2026 FIFA World Cup builds, teams from across the globe are gearing up for the knockout stage. ### Overview of the Knockout Stage Format

The knockout stage of the World Cup will feature 32 teams, which will be determined through a series of group matches in the initial phase of the tournament. The top two teams from each of the 16 groups will advance to the knockout round. This new format not only increases the number of participating nations but also introduces a more dynamic and competitive atmosphere, as teams vie for a chance to progress through the rounds.

### Qualification Criteria

To qualify for the knockout stage, teams must perform well in their respective groups. The criteria for advancing include:

1. **Points Accumulation**: Teams earn three points for a win, one point for a draw, and no points for a loss. The total points accumulated will determine the standings within each group.
   
2. **Goal Difference**: In the event that teams are tied on points, goal difference will be the next determining factor. This is calculated by subtracting the total goals conceded from the total goals scored.

3. **Goals Scored**: If teams are still tied after considering points and goal difference, the total number of goals scored will be evaluated.

4. **Head-to-Head Results**: Should teams remain tied, the results of the matches played between the tied teams will be reviewed.

5. **Fair Play Points**: In rare cases where teams cannot be separated by the previous criteria, fair play points may be used as a tiebreaker.
