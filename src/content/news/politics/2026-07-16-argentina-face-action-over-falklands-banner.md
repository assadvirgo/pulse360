## Argentina Faces FIFA Disciplinary Action Over Falklands Banner

Argentina is bracing for potential disciplinary measures from FIFA following an incident during the World Cup semi-final match against England. The Argentine national team celebrated their victory with a controversial banner that expressed support for the country's longstanding claims to the Falkland Islands, a territory that has been a point of contention between Argentina and the United Kingdom for decades.

### Background on the Falkland Islands Dispute

The Falkland Islands, located in the South Atlantic Ocean, have been a source of conflict since the early 19th century. The sovereignty of the islands was contested in 1982, leading to a brief but intense war between Argentina and the UK. Although the UK maintains control over the islands, Argentina continues to assert its claims, viewing the territory as a vital part of its national identity.
