## Tyson Fury Targets Anthony Joshua After Upcoming Fight

Tyson Fury, the reigning heavyweight boxing champion, has expressed a strong desire to face fellow British boxer Anthony Joshua following his scheduled comeback fight against Arslanbek Makhmudov this Saturday. Fury, known for his charismatic personality and exceptional skills in the ring, aims to reignite a long-anticipated clash with Joshua, a matchup that has been the subject of much speculation and fan interest.

### Upcoming Fight Against Makhmudov

Fury's return to the ring comes after a period of inactivity, during which he has remained a prominent figure in boxing discussions. His opponent, Makhmudov, is an undefeated fighter with a reputation for his knockout power. This matchup is seen as a significant challenge for Fury, who will need to demonstrate his readiness to reclaim his position at the top of the heavyweight division.

In the lead-up to the fight, Fury has been vocal about his ambitions, stating that a victory against Makhmudov is essential for setting the stage for a showdown with Joshua. "I want to make a statement on Saturday and then turn my attention to Anthony Joshua," Fury remarked in a recent interview. "That’s the fight everyone wants to see, and I’m ready to give it to them."

### The Joshua Showdown

The potential bout between Fury and Joshua has been highly anticipated by boxing fans around the world. Both fighters have held world titles and are considered two of the best heavyweights of their generation. Their rivalry has been fueled by their contrasting styles and personalities, making a clash between them one of the most sought-after events in modern boxing.

Joshua, who has faced his own challenges in recent years, including a couple of high-profile losses, has also expressed interest in facing Fury. The two have exchanged words in the media, further heightening the anticipation for a potential fight. As Fury prepares for Makhmudov, the boxing community is keenly watching to see if he can secure a victory that would pave the way for the much-desired encounter with Joshua.

### Implications for the Heavyweight Division

A fight between Fury and Joshua would not only be a significant event for both fighters but could also have substantial implications for the heavyweight division as a whole. The outcome of such a bout could determine the future landscape of heavyweight boxing, influencing rankings, title fights, and the careers of both athletes.

Fury's willingness to engage with Joshua post-fight reflects a broader trend in boxing where fighters are increasingly willing to take on high-stakes challenges to satisfy fan demand. This shift could lead to more exciting matchups in the future, revitalizing interest in the sport.

### Conclusion

As Tyson Fury prepares to step back into the ring against Arslanbek Makhmudov, the boxing world is abuzz with speculation regarding a potential fight with Anthony Joshua. Both fighters have the skill and charisma to draw significant attention, and a matchup between them would undoubtedly be a highlight in the sport's history. Fury's focus on Joshua after his comeback fight indicates his ambition to solidify his legacy and entertain fans worldwide. The coming weeks will be crucial in determining whether this long-awaited clash will finally come to fruition.
