## Ronaldo's Performance Takes Center Stage Ahead of World Cup

As the countdown to the FIFA World Cup continues, Portugal's head coach, Roberto Martinez, has emphasized that Cristiano Ronaldo's selection for the national team will be based on his current form rather than his age. This statement comes as Ronaldo prepares to participate in his fifth World Cup, a remarkable achievement that underscores his longevity in the sport.

### A Record-Breaking Career

Cristiano Ronaldo, who holds the record for the most goals scored at the international level with 143 goals for Portugal, has been a pivotal figure in international football for nearly two decades. His ability to perform at the highest level, even as he approaches the later stages of his career, has been a topic of discussion among fans and analysts alike. With his extensive experience and proven track record, Ronaldo's presence in the squad is seen as both a strategic advantage and a source of inspiration for younger players.

### Focus on Current Form

Roberto Martinez, who took over as Portugal's head coach earlier this year, has made it clear that performance on the pitch will be the primary criterion for selection. "We are looking at players based on their current form and contributions to the team," Martinez stated in a recent press conference. This approach reflects a broader trend in modern football, where age is increasingly viewed as less relevant compared to a player's ability to deliver results.

Martinez's comments are particularly significant given the scrutiny that Ronaldo has faced in recent years regarding his fitness and effectiveness on the field. Critics have pointed to his age as a potential liability, but the coach's stance suggests that Ronaldo's experience and skill set still hold considerable value for the national team.

### Preparing for the World Stage

As Portugal gears up for the World Cup, the team is focused on building a cohesive unit that can compete against the world's best. The inclusion of Ronaldo, alongside a talented roster of emerging stars, creates a dynamic that could prove advantageous in high-pressure situations. The blend of experience and youth is crucial as the team aims to advance deep into the tournament.

The upcoming World Cup presents an opportunity for Ronaldo to further cement his legacy in the sport. Should he perform well, it could not only enhance his standing as one of the greatest players of all time but also significantly impact Portugal's chances of success in the tournament.

### Conclusion

As the World Cup approaches, all eyes will be on Cristiano Ronaldo and the Portuguese national team. With Roberto Martinez's commitment to selecting players based on form, Ronaldo's performance in the lead-up to the tournament will be critical. Fans and analysts alike will be watching closely to see if the veteran forward can once again rise to the occasion and contribute to Portugal's quest for glory on the world stage.
