## Explosives Discovered Near Key Gas Pipeline in Serbia

In a concerning development, Serbian President Aleksandar Vučić announced the discovery of explosives described as having "devastating power" located near a pipeline that supplies Russian gas to Hungary and Serbia. This revelation raises significant security concerns for the region, which relies heavily on this energy source.

### Context of the Pipeline

The pipeline in question is a critical infrastructure component for both Hungary and Serbia, facilitating the transportation of Russian gas to these countries. Given the ongoing geopolitical tensions surrounding energy supplies in Europe, particularly in the context of the Russian invasion of Ukraine, the security of such pipelines has become paramount.

### Details of the Discovery

While specific details regarding the location and circumstances of the explosives' discovery have not been disclosed, the Serbian authorities are treating the situation with urgency. President Vučić emphasized the potential risks associated with the explosives, indicating that they could pose a significant threat not only to the pipeline itself but also to the broader regional stability.
