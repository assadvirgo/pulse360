## UK's Unconventional Entry in Eurovision 2023

As the Eurovision Song Contest approaches, the United Kingdom is making headlines with its bold choice of entry for this year's competition. ## A History of Eurovision Challenges

The United Kingdom has a storied history in the Eurovision Song Contest, having won the competition five times since its inception in 1956. However, the last victory came in 1997 with Katrina and the Waves' "Love Shine a Light." Since then, the UK has struggled to find success, often finishing in the lower ranks of the competition. This long-standing drought has prompted a reevaluation of the UK's approach to the contest, leading to this year's unconventional selection.
