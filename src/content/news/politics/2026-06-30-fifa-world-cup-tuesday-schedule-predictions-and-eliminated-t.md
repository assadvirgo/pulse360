These matches are not just about points; they represent the culmination of months of preparation and the hopes of nations.

As analysts and fans speculate on the outcomes, several factors come into play. France, the reigning champions, are expected to leverage their deep squad and experience. Mexico's clash with South Korea is particularly intriguing. Both teams have shown inconsistency, and the outcome may hinge on which side can capitalize on their opportunities. Predictions suggest a closely contested match, with the potential for late drama.
