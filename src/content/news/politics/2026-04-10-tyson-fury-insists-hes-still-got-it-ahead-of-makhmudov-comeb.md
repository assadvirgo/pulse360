## Tyson Fury Prepares for Comeback Fight Against Makhmudov

In the world of heavyweight boxing, few names resonate as powerfully as Tyson Fury. The British boxing champion, known for his charismatic personality and remarkable skills inside the ring, has recently expressed confidence in his abilities ahead of an upcoming fight against Russian-born heavyweight Arslanbek Makhmudov. This bout is set to take place before Fury potentially faces off against fellow heavyweight star Anthony Joshua, a matchup that has long been anticipated by boxing fans around the globe.

### Fury's Confidence Ahead of the Fight

Tyson Fury, who has held multiple heavyweight titles, has made headlines not only for his achievements but also for his larger-than-life persona. As he gears up for the fight against Makhmudov, Fury has stated emphatically that he "still got it," indicating his readiness to compete at the highest level. The 35-year-old champion has faced numerous challenges throughout his career, including personal struggles and formidable opponents, yet he remains a formidable force in the ring.

Makhmudov, known for his impressive physicality and unorthodox training methods — including wrestling bears for fun — presents a unique challenge for Fury. The Russian fighter has garnered attention for his knockout power and has been on an impressive winning streak, making this matchup particularly intriguing. Fury's ability to adapt and strategize will be tested as he faces an opponent who combines strength with an unconventional approach to training.

### The Road to a Potential Joshua Fight

While the upcoming bout with Makhmudov is Fury's immediate focus, the prospect of a fight against Anthony Joshua looms large on the horizon. Both fighters have expressed interest in facing each other, and a clash between the two British heavyweights would be a significant event in the boxing calendar. For Fury, a victory over Makhmudov would not only solidify his status as a top contender but also pave the way for a highly anticipated showdown with Joshua.

The rivalry between Fury and Joshua has been a topic of discussion among boxing enthusiasts for years. Both fighters have their unique styles and strengths, making a potential matchup a compelling narrative in the sport. As Fury prepares for Makhmudov, the boxing community is eager to see how this fight will influence the prospects of a future encounter with Joshua.

### Conclusion

As Tyson Fury prepares to step back into the ring, his confidence and determination are palpable. The upcoming fight against Arslanbek Makhmudov will be a critical moment in his career, not only for the immediate outcome but also for its implications on future matchups, particularly with Anthony Joshua. Boxing fans worldwide will be watching closely, eager to see if Fury can indeed prove that he "still got it" and maintain his position as one of the sport's elite heavyweights.
