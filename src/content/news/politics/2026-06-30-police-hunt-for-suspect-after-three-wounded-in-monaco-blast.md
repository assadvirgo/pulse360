The explosion reportedly happened in the early hours of the morning, shaking the quiet streets of Monaco. Emergency services were quickly dispatched to the scene, where they found three people injured. Among the injured is a prominent Ukrainian businessman, whose identity has not yet been officially confirmed. The extent of the injuries sustained by the victims remains unclear, but they were transported to a nearby hospital for treatment.
