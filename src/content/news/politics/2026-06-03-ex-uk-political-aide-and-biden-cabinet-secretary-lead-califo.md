In the ongoing primary elections for the California governor's race, two prominent figures have emerged as frontrunners: a former political aide from the United Kingdom and a current cabinet secretary in President Biden's administration. Their ability to connect with a diverse electorate has played a crucial role in their rise within the primary race.

In close contention is the Biden cabinet secretary, whose experience in federal government positions has positioned them as a strong candidate.
