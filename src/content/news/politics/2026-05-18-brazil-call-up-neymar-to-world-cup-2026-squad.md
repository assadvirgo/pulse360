## Brazil Calls Up Neymar for World Cup 2026 Squad

In a significant move for the Brazilian national football team, Neymar has been officially called up to participate in the 2026 FIFA World Cup. This announcement marks the first time that head coach Carlo Ancelotti has included the all-time leading scorer in Brazil's history in his squad for the upcoming tournament.

Neymar, who has been a pivotal figure in Brazilian football since his debut, holds the record for the most goals scored for the national team. His skill, agility, and experience make him a valuable asset as Brazil aims to secure its sixth World Cup title. The forward has previously showcased his talent on the world stage, contributing significantly to Brazil's success in international competitions.

Carlo Ancelotti, who took over as the head coach of the Brazilian national team, has emphasized the importance of blending experience with youthful energy. By calling up Neymar, Ancelotti appears to be banking on the forward's vast experience in high-pressure situations. The decision also signals a shift in strategy as Brazil prepares for a competitive World Cup, where they will face formidable opponents.

As the tournament approaches, Brazil will be focusing on building a cohesive team that can perform at its best.
