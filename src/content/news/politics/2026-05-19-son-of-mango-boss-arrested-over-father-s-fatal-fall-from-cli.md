In a shocking development, authorities have arrested the son of Isak, the late CEO of the Spanish fashion brand Mango, in connection with the circumstances surrounding his father's tragic death. The incident occurred during a hike in the picturesque Montserrat mountains, a popular destination known for its stunning views and challenging trails. Isak reportedly lost his footing and fell from a significant height, leading to his untimely death. Initial reports suggested that the fall was accidental, but subsequent investigations have raised questions about the circumstances leading to the incident.
