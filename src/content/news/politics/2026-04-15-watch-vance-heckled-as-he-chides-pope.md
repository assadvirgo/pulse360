The Vice President's statement appeared to challenge the Pope's authority on theological matters, which some audience members found provocative. This incident underscores the growing trend of political figures engaging directly with religious authorities, often leading to public confrontations or disagreements.

As Vance delivered his remarks, he was met with vocal disapproval from some attendees, who interrupted him with heckles.
