The pivotal moment of the match came in the 50th minute when Mexican midfielder Luis Romo found the back of the net. His goal, a result of a well-executed play, not only marked a significant personal milestone as his 50th career goal but also solidified Mexico's position as the first team to reach the knockout phase of the tournament.
