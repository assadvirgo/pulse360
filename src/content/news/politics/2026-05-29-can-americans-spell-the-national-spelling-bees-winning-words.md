## Shrey Parikh Crowned Champion of National Spelling Bee

In a remarkable display of linguistic prowess, 14-year-old Shrey Parikh emerged as the champion of this year's National Spelling Bee, a competition that has captivated audiences across the United States for decades. Parikh's victory was solidified during a tense 90-second lightning round tiebreaker, where he successfully spelled 32 words, showcasing not only his spelling abilities but also his composure under pressure.
