## Lamine Yamal Fit to Start on Bench for Spain vs Cape Verde at World Cup

In a significant update ahead of the World Cup match against Cape Verde, Spanish national team coach Luis de la Fuente has confirmed that young talent Lamine Yamal is fit to be included in the squad. Although he is expected to start on the bench, his availability is seen as a positive development for the team.

### Player Fitness and Team Updates

De la Fuente expressed confidence in the fitness levels of his players, stating that he has no concerns regarding their physical condition as they prepare for the upcoming match. Alongside Yamal, Victor Munoz has also been cleared to play, adding depth to the squad as Spain aims to secure a crucial victory in the tournament.

Yamal, who has garnered attention for his impressive performances in recent matches, represents a new generation of talent for Spain. His inclusion, even if initially as a substitute, highlights the coaching staff's commitment to integrating young players into the national setup.

The decision to include Yamal on the bench allows De la Fuente to maintain flexibility in his tactical approach against Cape Verde. The young forward's ability to change the dynamics of a game could be invaluable, particularly if Spain finds itself in a challenging situation during the match.
