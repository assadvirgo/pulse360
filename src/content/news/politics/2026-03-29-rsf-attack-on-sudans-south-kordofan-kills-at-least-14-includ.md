## RSF Attack in South Kordofan Claims Lives of 14, Including Children

In a tragic escalation of violence in Sudan, a recent attack by the Rapid Support Forces (RSF) in South Kordofan has left at least 14 individuals dead, among them children. The assault occurred in the city of Dilling, where the RSF, alongside allied forces, reportedly shelled residential areas for several hours, according to the Sudan Doctors Network.

### Details of the Attack

The bombardment targeted densely populated neighborhoods, raising concerns about the safety of civilians amid ongoing conflicts in the region. Eyewitness accounts describe scenes of chaos as families sought shelter from the relentless shelling. The RSF, a paramilitary group that has been involved in various conflicts across Sudan, has faced increasing scrutiny for its actions, particularly regarding civilian casualties.

### Humanitarian Impact

The Sudan Doctors Network has emphasized the urgent need for humanitarian assistance in the aftermath of the attack. Hospitals in Dilling are reportedly overwhelmed with casualties, and medical supplies are dwindling. The situation has prompted calls from local and international organizations for immediate intervention to address the humanitarian crisis exacerbated by the violence.

### Context of the Conflict

The recent violence in South Kordofan is part of a broader conflict that has plagued Sudan for years, particularly following the ousting of former President Omar al-Bashir in 2019. The power struggle between various military factions, including the RSF and the Sudanese Armed Forces, has led to widespread instability and suffering among the civilian population. The region of South Kordofan has been a hotspot for clashes, with numerous reports of human rights abuses and attacks on civilians.

### International Response

The international community has expressed deep concern regarding the ongoing violence in Sudan. Human rights organizations have called for accountability for those responsible for the attacks on civilians and have urged the Sudanese government to take immediate steps to protect its citizens. Diplomatic efforts to mediate the conflict have been ongoing, but progress remains slow as violence continues to erupt in various parts of the country.

### Conclusion

The attack in Dilling serves as a stark reminder of the precarious situation in Sudan, where civilians continue to bear the brunt of political and military conflicts. As the death toll rises and humanitarian needs grow, the call for peace and stability in the region becomes increasingly urgent. The international community must remain vigilant and responsive to the needs of those affected by this tragic violence, ensuring that humanitarian assistance reaches those in desperate need.
