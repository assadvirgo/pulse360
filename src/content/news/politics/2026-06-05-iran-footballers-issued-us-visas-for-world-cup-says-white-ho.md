## Iran Footballers Granted U.S. Visas Ahead of World Cup

In a significant development for the Iranian national football team, the White House has confirmed that the players have been issued visas to participate in the upcoming World Cup. This announcement comes just ten days before their opening match against New Zealand, scheduled for June 15 in Los Angeles.

The issuance of visas to the Iranian footballers is a notable event, particularly given the complex political relations between Iran and the United States. The visa approvals are seen as a gesture of goodwill, allowing the players to compete on an international stage amidst ongoing geopolitical tensions.
