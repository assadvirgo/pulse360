## Judge Dismisses Most Claims in Blake Lively's Harassment Suit Against Justin Baldoni

In a significant ruling, a judge has dismissed the majority of the claims made by actress Blake Lively in her harassment lawsuit against actor and director Justin Baldoni. This decision paves the way for a civil trial that is scheduled to take place next month, as three allegations against Baldoni remain intact.

The legal battle between Lively and Baldoni has garnered considerable attention, particularly given the high profiles of both individuals in the entertainment industry. Blake Lively, known for her roles in popular television series and films, accused Baldoni of inappropriate behavior that she claims created a hostile environment. The specifics of the allegations have not been disclosed in detail, but they have raised questions about workplace conduct and accountability within Hollywood.

### Court's Ruling

The judge's ruling to dismiss most of Lively's claims indicates a careful examination of the evidence presented. While the details of the dismissed claims remain confidential, the retention of three allegations suggests that there may be sufficient grounds for further legal scrutiny. These remaining claims will be central to the upcoming civil trial, where both parties will have the opportunity to present their cases in a court setting.
