According to PSG's medical team, the injury occurred during a recent training session. The semitendinosus muscle is one of the hamstring muscles, and injuries to this area can vary in severity, often requiring several days to weeks of recovery. While the club has not disclosed the exact timeline for Mbappe's return, they have indicated that he will undergo further evaluations to determine the extent of the injury.
