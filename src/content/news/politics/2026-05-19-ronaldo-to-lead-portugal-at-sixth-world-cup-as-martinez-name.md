## Ronaldo to Lead Portugal at Sixth World Cup as Martinez Names Squad

In a significant announcement for Portuguese football, national team coach Roberto Martinez has officially named his squad for the upcoming FIFA World Cup 2026. Among the notable inclusions is Cristiano Ronaldo, who will be making his sixth appearance in the prestigious tournament, further solidifying his status as one of the sport's all-time greats.

### Ronaldo's Continued Legacy

At 38 years old, Ronaldo's selection is a testament to his enduring talent and influence on the field. The forward, who has had a storied career with clubs such as Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus, continues to be a pivotal player for the national team. His leadership and experience will be invaluable as Portugal aims to make a deep run in the tournament.

Martinez expressed confidence in Ronaldo's ability to perform at the highest level, stating, "Cristiano is a player who brings not only skill but also a wealth of experience to our squad. His presence will be crucial as we prepare for the challenges ahead."

### Diogo Jota's Honorary Inclusion

In a unique move, Diogo Jota has been named as an honorary 'plus-one' player in the squad. This decision allows for additional flexibility in squad management, particularly in light of potential injuries or tactical adjustments as the tournament progresses. Jota, who plays for Liverpool in the English Premier League, has been a key contributor to the national team and his inclusion, even in an honorary capacity, highlights his importance to the squad.

### The Road to the World Cup

Portugal's journey to the World Cup has been marked by strong performances in the qualifying rounds, showcasing a blend of experienced players and emerging talents. The squad reflects a strategic approach by Martinez, who aims to balance veteran leadership with youthful energy. This combination is expected to enhance the team's competitiveness on the world stage.

As the tournament approaches, fans and analysts alike are keen to see how Portugal's tactics will evolve under Martinez's guidance. The coach has been known for his adaptable strategies, and his ability to harness the strengths of his players will be closely scrutinized.

### Looking Ahead

The FIFA World Cup 2026, co-hosted by the United States, Canada, and Mexico, promises to be a landmark event in football history. With an expanded format featuring more teams and matches, the tournament is set to capture the attention of millions around the globe. Portugal, with its rich footballing heritage and a squad filled with talent, will be looking to make a significant impact.

As Ronaldo prepares for what could be his final World Cup, the footballing world will be watching closely. His legacy, alongside that of his teammates, will be on the line as they strive to bring home the coveted trophy. The upcoming tournament is not just a chance for individual accolades but also an opportunity for Portugal to solidify its place among the elite in international football.
