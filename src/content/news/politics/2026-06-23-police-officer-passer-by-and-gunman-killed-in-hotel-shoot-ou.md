## Tragic Shooting Incident at Canadian Hotel Claims Three Lives

A shooting incident at a hotel in Canada has resulted in the deaths of a police officer, a civilian, and the suspected gunman. The event has raised significant concerns regarding public safety and the risks faced by law enforcement officers in the line of duty.

The shooting occurred in the early hours of the morning at a hotel located in a busy urban area. According to eyewitness accounts, the situation escalated quickly, leading to a confrontation between law enforcement and the suspect. The police were reportedly responding to a call about a disturbance when the gunfire erupted.

Local authorities have confirmed that a police officer, whose identity has not yet been released, was fatally wounded during the exchange. A civilian, who was in the vicinity at the time, also lost their life. The suspected gunman was killed in the shoot-out, although details regarding the circumstances of his death remain under investigation.
