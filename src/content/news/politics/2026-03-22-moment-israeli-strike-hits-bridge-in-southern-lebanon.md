## Moment Israeli Strike Hits Bridge in Southern Lebanon

In a significant escalation of military tensions in the region, Israeli jets conducted an airstrike on the Qasmiyeh Bridge, located over the Litani River in southern Lebanon. The incident, which was captured on video, highlights the ongoing conflict and the precarious security situation along the Israel-Lebanon border.

The airstrike comes amid heightened hostilities between Israel and various militant groups in Lebanon, including Hezbollah. The Qasmiyeh Bridge serves as a critical infrastructure point, facilitating transportation and commerce in the region. Its destruction raises concerns about the impact on local communities and the potential for further escalation in military actions.

Footage of the strike, which has circulated widely on social media, shows the moment the Israeli jets targeted the bridge, resulting in a significant explosion. The visual evidence underscores the intensity of the conflict and the precision of the military operation. While the video provides a stark illustration of the strike, it also raises questions about the broader implications for civilian safety and infrastructure in southern Lebanon.

The airstrike on the Qasmiyeh Bridge is indicative of the fragile state of affairs in the region.
