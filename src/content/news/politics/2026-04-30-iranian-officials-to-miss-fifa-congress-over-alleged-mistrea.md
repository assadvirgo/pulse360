## Iranian Delegation Abandons FIFA Congress Amid Immigration Dispute in Canada

In a significant diplomatic incident, the Iranian delegation led by Mehdi Taj, President of the Football Federation of the Islamic Republic of Iran, has turned back from the Toronto airport, resulting in their absence from the FIFA Congress scheduled to take place in Canada. This decision follows allegations of "unacceptable behavior" by Canadian immigration officials towards the Iranian representatives.

## Incident at Toronto Airport

Upon arrival in Toronto, the Iranian officials encountered what they described as mistreatment by immigration authorities. Specific details regarding the nature of the alleged behavior have not been disclosed, but the Iranian delegation deemed the circumstances intolerable, prompting them to withdraw from the event. This incident has raised concerns regarding diplomatic relations and the treatment of foreign officials in Canada.

The FIFA Congress is a crucial event for the global football community, where key decisions regarding the sport are made, including governance and policy changes.
