The plea comes after Mugabe was arrested in February following an altercation that resulted in the shooting of a 23-year-old man at his residence in Johannesburg.

### Incident Overview

The incident took place at Mugabe's home in Johannesburg, where he reportedly pointed a firearm at the victim during a dispute.
