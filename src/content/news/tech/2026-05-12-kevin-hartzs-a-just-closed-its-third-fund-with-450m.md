## Kevin Hartz’s A* Capital Closes $450 Million Third Fund

A* Capital, co-founded by entrepreneur Kevin Hartz, has successfully closed its third fund with a total of $450 million. This significant financial milestone marks a continued commitment to supporting innovative startups across various sectors, including artificial intelligence (AI), fintech, healthcare, and security.

### A Generalist Investment Approach

The firm adopts a generalist investment strategy, allowing it to diversify its portfolio by backing companies in multiple categories. This approach not only mitigates risk but also positions A* Capital to capitalize on emerging trends and technologies across different industries. By focusing on a broad spectrum of sectors, the firm aims to identify and nurture startups that demonstrate strong growth potential and innovative solutions.

### Investment Strategy and Goals

With the newly raised capital, A* Capital plans to make investments with an average check size ranging from $3 million to $5 million. The firm has set an ambitious goal to support at least 30 startups in this funding cycle. This strategy reflects a belief in the power of early-stage investments to drive significant returns, particularly in sectors poised for rapid growth.

### Focus on Emerging Technologies

The emphasis on sectors such as AI, fintech, healthcare, and security highlights A* Capital's recognition of the transformative potential of these industries. AI applications are increasingly becoming integral to various business operations, while fintech continues to disrupt traditional banking and financial services. Healthcare technology is also witnessing a surge in demand, particularly in light of the ongoing global health challenges. Additionally, the security sector remains crucial as businesses and consumers alike seek robust solutions to protect their data and privacy.

### The Role of Kevin Hartz

Kevin Hartz, known for his entrepreneurial ventures and leadership in the tech space, plays a pivotal role in guiding A* Capital's vision and strategy. His experience in building successful companies and understanding market dynamics positions him as a key figure in identifying promising startups that align with the firm’s investment philosophy.

### Conclusion

The closing of A* Capital's third fund with $450 million underscores the growing interest in venture capital investment, particularly in technology-driven sectors. As the firm embarks on this new investment journey, it aims to foster innovation and support the next generation of startups that are poised to make significant impacts in their respective fields. With a clear focus on diverse sectors and a robust investment strategy, A* Capital is well-positioned to navigate the evolving landscape of technology and entrepreneurship.
