## Schlage’s UWB-Enabled Smart Lock Set to Launch This Month

After a year of anticipation, Schlage is poised to release its first smart lock featuring ultra-wideband (UWB) technology. The Schlage Sense Pro deadbolt lock will officially be available for purchase starting June 29th, 2023, at a retail price of $399 in the United States.

### Advancements in Smart Lock Technology

The introduction of UWB technology marks a significant advancement in the functionality of smart locks. Unlike traditional Bluetooth or Wi-Fi connections, UWB provides precise location tracking, allowing users to unlock their doors simply by approaching them. This feature enhances convenience and security, as it eliminates the need for users to fumble for their keys or smartphones.

UWB technology operates by transmitting signals that can determine the distance between devices with high accuracy. This capability enables the Schlage Sense Pro to recognize when a user is nearby, automatically unlocking the door without any physical interaction. Such innovations are expected to appeal to tech-savvy homeowners looking for seamless integration of smart home devices.

### Features of the Schlage Sense Pro

In addition to its UWB capabilities, the Schlage Sense Pro offers a range of features designed to enhance security and user experience. The lock can be controlled via a smartphone app, allowing users to manage access remotely. This is particularly beneficial for homeowners who wish to grant temporary access to guests or service providers.

The lock also includes a keypad for traditional keyless entry, ensuring that users have multiple options for accessing their homes. Furthermore, the Schlage Sense Pro is designed to withstand various weather conditions, making it suitable for outdoor installation.

### Market Impact and Consumer Reception

The launch of the Schlage Sense Pro is anticipated to have a notable impact on the smart lock market, which has seen significant growth in recent years. As consumers increasingly prioritize home automation and security, products that offer advanced technology and ease of use are likely to gain traction.

Industry experts suggest that UWB technology could set a new standard for smart locks, potentially prompting other manufacturers to adopt similar features in their products. The integration of UWB into consumer devices is still in its early stages, and Schlage's entry into this space may encourage further innovation.

### Conclusion

With the Schlage Sense Pro set to hit the market later this month, consumers can look forward to a new era of smart locking solutions that prioritize both convenience and security. As the demand for smart home technology continues to grow, Schlage's latest offering may well become a benchmark for future developments in the industry. The combination of UWB technology and user-friendly features positions the Schlage Sense Pro as a compelling option for homeowners seeking to enhance their home security systems.
