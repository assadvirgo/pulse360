## Framework Adjusts RAM Configurations for Upcoming Laptop 13 Pro

In a notable development within the tech industry, Framework, a company known for its commitment to modular and repairable laptops, has announced a significant pricing update concerning its upcoming premium Laptop 13 Pro. This update, which affects both new buyers and those who have preordered the device, comes in response to substantial cost increases from the company's memory supplier.

### RAM Configuration Changes

Framework revealed that the memory configurations for the Laptop 13 Pro have been altered due to what the company described as a "major and unfortunate" pricing update. The adjustments to the RAM specifications are a direct result of cost updates from their supplier, which Framework indicated are "more than double" previous rates. This shift has prompted the company to reassess the configurations available to consumers, ultimately leading to a reduction in the amount of RAM offered in the new laptop model.

### Impact on Preorders and Pricing

The changes in RAM configurations will not only affect new customers but also those who have already placed preorders for the Laptop 13 Pro. Framework has stated that it will communicate directly with affected customers regarding the adjustments, ensuring that they are informed about the new specifications and pricing structures. This proactive approach aims to maintain transparency and customer trust amid the challenging circumstances.

### Industry Context

The announcement from Framework comes at a time when the tech industry is grappling with various supply chain challenges and rising component costs. The phenomenon dubbed "RAMageddon" reflects broader trends affecting memory prices, which have seen significant fluctuations in recent months. As demand for high-performance computing continues to grow, particularly in sectors such as gaming and content creation, the strain on memory supply chains has become increasingly apparent.

Framework's decision to adjust its product offering is indicative of the larger pressures facing manufacturers in the current market. Companies are often forced to make difficult choices regarding specifications and pricing to remain viable while still delivering quality products to consumers.

### Looking Ahead

As Framework navigates these challenges, the company remains committed to its core principles of sustainability and user empowerment. The Laptop 13 Pro is designed with a focus on modularity, allowing users to upgrade components easily, which aligns with Framework's vision of reducing electronic waste and promoting longevity in technology.

While the adjustments to RAM configurations may be disappointing for some prospective buyers, Framework's dedication to transparency and customer communication will be crucial as they move forward. The company aims to deliver a product that meets the needs of its users while adapting to the realities of the current market landscape.

In conclusion, Framework's announcement serves as a reminder of the complexities involved in tech manufacturing today. As the industry continues to evolve, companies like Framework will need to balance innovation with the economic realities of component sourcing and pricing.
