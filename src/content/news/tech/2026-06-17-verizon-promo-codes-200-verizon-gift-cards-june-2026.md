The promotional offer is designed to incentivize both new and existing customers to explore Verizon's extensive range of service plans. By applying the promotional codes during the sign-up or upgrade process, customers can unlock a $200 gift card, which can be used towards future purchases or services within the Verizon ecosystem. This initiative reflects Verizon's ongoing commitment to providing value to its customers, especially in a competitive telecommunications market.

To participate in this promotion, customers need to visit the Verizon website or authorized retail outlets. Upon selecting a service plan, they will be prompted to enter the promotional code at checkout. The specific codes and eligible plans are expected to be detailed on Verizon's promotional page, ensuring transparency and ease of access for users.
