## Moto Tag 2 Tracker Launches in the US at Special Price

Motorola has officially launched its latest Bluetooth tracker, the Moto Tag 2, in the United States. Announced during the Consumer Electronics Show (CES) 2026, the device is now available for a limited time at a promotional price of $19.99, down from its original retail price of $29.99.

## Enhanced Features for Improved Tracking

The Moto Tag 2 comes equipped with several enhancements over its predecessor, making it a noteworthy option for consumers seeking reliable tracking solutions. One of the standout features is its improved battery life, which promises longer usage between charges. Additionally, the device incorporates Ultra-Wideband (UWB) sensing technology, allowing for more accurate tracking capabilities. This advancement is particularly beneficial for users who require precise location data for their belongings.

## Competitive Market for Bluetooth Trackers

The introduction of the Moto Tag 2 adds to a growing market of Bluetooth trackers available for Android users. With various options currently on the market, consumers are encouraged to consider the unique features and pricing of each device before making a purchase. The Moto Tag 2's competitive pricing, combined with its advanced tracking technology, positions it as a strong contender in this space.

## Availability and Purchase Options

The Moto Tag 2 can be purchased directly from Motorola's official website. The current promotional price is part of a limited-time offer, which may attract early adopters looking to take advantage of the reduced cost. As Bluetooth trackers continue to gain popularity, especially among those who frequently misplace personal items, Motorola's latest offering is poised to appeal to a wide audience.

## Conclusion

With the launch of the Moto Tag 2, Motorola aims to provide consumers with a reliable and efficient tracking solution at an accessible price point. The combination of enhanced battery life and advanced tracking technology could make the Moto Tag 2 a popular choice for those in need of a Bluetooth tracker. As the market for such devices continues to evolve, it will be interesting to see how this latest offering from Motorola performs against its competitors.
