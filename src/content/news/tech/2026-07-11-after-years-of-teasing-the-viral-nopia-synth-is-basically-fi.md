This news comes after years of teasing and speculation surrounding the innovative instrument, which first captured the attention of music enthusiasts back in 2023.

The Nopia synthesizer has been a topic of conversation among musicians and gear aficionados since its initial reveal. Grieco and Gal have dedicated significant time and effort to refine the instrument, ensuring that it meets the high expectations of its future users. The duo recently showcased the Nopia at the MusicRadar offices, providing an in-depth look at its features and capabilities.

During the demonstration, Grieco and Gal highlighted the unique aspects of the Nopia that set it apart from other synthesizers on the market. With a focus on user-friendly design and innovative sound generation techniques, the Nopia aims to cater to both novice musicians and seasoned professionals alike.
