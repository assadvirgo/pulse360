## Humanoid 'Lightning' Robot Sets New Half-Marathon Record in Beijing

In a groundbreaking display of technological advancement, an autonomous humanoid robot named "Lightning" has set a new record for completing a half-marathon. The event took place in Beijing on Sunday, where Lightning finished the 13-mile race in an impressive time of 50 minutes and 26 seconds. This remarkable achievement is less than half the time of last year's fastest robot, which completed the same distance in two hours and 40 minutes.

### The Development of Lightning

Lightning was developed by a team of engineers at Honor, a prominent smartphone company in China. Du Xiaodi, one of the lead engineers behind the project, expressed enthusiasm about the robot's capabilities and the implications of its performance. "We aimed to push the boundaries of robotics and artificial intelligence," Du stated. The development of Lightning is seen as a significant milestone in the field of robotics, showcasing not only the technological prowess of the team but also the potential for future applications in various sectors.
