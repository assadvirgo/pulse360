## Teenage Engineering Launches the KO Sidekick Mixer

Teenage Engineering, a Swedish company renowned for its innovative music technology, has unveiled its latest product, the EP-136 KO Sidekick. This stylish two-channel mixer is designed primarily for live performances and DJ sets, offering a seamless connection for users of the company's KO-II samplers, as well as compatibility with a variety of other audio sources.

## Design and Features

The KO Sidekick stands out with its sleek design, which is characteristic of Teenage Engineering's aesthetic. The mixer is compact and portable, making it an ideal choice for musicians and DJs who require mobility without sacrificing functionality. It features two channels, allowing users to mix and manipulate audio from two separate sources simultaneously.

While the mixer is optimized for use with Teenage Engineering's KO-II samplers, its versatility extends beyond proprietary devices. Users can connect any two audio sources, making the KO Sidekick a flexible tool for a wide range of audio applications. This adaptability is particularly appealing to live performers who may want to integrate various instruments or audio devices into their setup.
