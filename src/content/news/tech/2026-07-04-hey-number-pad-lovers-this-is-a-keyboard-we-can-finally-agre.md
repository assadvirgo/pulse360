## Epomaker RT98: A Mechanical Keyboard for Number Pad Enthusiasts

In the ever-evolving landscape of computer peripherals, the introduction of the Epomaker RT98 mechanical keyboard has sparked interest among both casual users and dedicated number pad enthusiasts. This innovative keyboard combines functionality with a nostalgic design, catering to a specific demographic that has long advocated for the inclusion of number pads in modern keyboards.

### A Nostalgic Design with Modern Features

The Epomaker RT98 stands out with its retro aesthetic, reminiscent of classic computing devices. Its design is complemented by a unique CRT-like screen that adds a playful touch to the overall appearance. This combination of vintage charm and contemporary functionality is appealing to users who appreciate both style and substance in their tech accessories.

### Enhanced Typing Experience

One of the most significant features of the RT98 is its mechanical key switches, which provide a satisfying tactile feedback that many users find enhances their typing experience. The keyboard's layout includes a dedicated number pad, which is often a point of contention among keyboard users. While some prefer a compact design without a number pad, others swear by its utility for data entry and numerical tasks. The RT98 aims to bridge this gap, offering a solution that satisfies both camps.

### Compatibility and Customization

The RT98 is equipped with VIA compatibility, allowing users to customize their keyboard settings easily. This feature is particularly beneficial for those who want to tailor their keyboard layout to suit specific workflows or personal preferences. The ability to remap keys and create macros can significantly enhance productivity, making the RT98 a versatile tool for various tasks.

### User Reception and Market Impact

Initial impressions of the Epomaker RT98 have been positive, particularly among those who have long advocated for the inclusion of number pads in keyboard designs. Users have noted the comfortable typing feel and the keyboard's overall build quality. This reception may indicate a shift in market trends, as manufacturers increasingly recognize the importance of catering to diverse user preferences.

### Conclusion

The Epomaker RT98 mechanical keyboard represents a thoughtful approach to keyboard design, merging nostalgic aesthetics with modern functionality. Its dedicated number pad, customizable features, and satisfying typing experience position it as a compelling choice for both number pad lovers and casual users alike. As the demand for personalized and functional computer peripherals continues to grow, the RT98 may set a new standard in the keyboard market, appealing to a wide range of consumers seeking both style and utility in their typing experience.
