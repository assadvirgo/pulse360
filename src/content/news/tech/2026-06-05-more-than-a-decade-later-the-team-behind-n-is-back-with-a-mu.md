## Metanet Studios Returns with Multiplayer Sequel to N++

After a decade-long hiatus, Metanet Studios, the Canadian indie game development team known for their challenging platformers, has announced a new multiplayer sequel to their critically acclaimed title, N++. The original game, released in 2015, was the culmination of ten years of development and built upon the legacy of earlier iterations, starting from the freeware Flash game N.

### A Legacy of Challenge

N++ was praised for its precise controls, minimalist design, and punishing difficulty, which resonated with both casual gamers and hardcore platforming enthusiasts. The game featured a variety of levels that demanded quick reflexes and strategic thinking, making it a staple in the indie gaming community. It was a significant milestone for Metanet, co-founded by Raigan Burns and Mare Sheppard, who had long been advocates for the platforming genre.

In a statement from 2015, Burns expressed hope that the team would not take another decade to create a new game, saying, "We hope it's not another 10 years before we come back." With the announcement of the multiplayer sequel, it appears that the team is ready to fulfill that promise.

### New Features and Multiplayer Dynamics

The upcoming sequel is expected to introduce multiplayer functionality, a significant evolution from the single-player focus of its predecessor. This shift aims to enhance the gameplay experience by allowing players to collaborate or compete against one another in a shared environment. While specific details about the new game mechanics and level designs remain under wraps, the inclusion of multiplayer is anticipated to add a new layer of complexity and engagement to the already challenging gameplay.

### The Indie Game Landscape

The return of Metanet Studios comes at a time when the indie game scene is thriving, with many developers experimenting with innovative gameplay mechanics and storytelling techniques. The success of titles like N++ has paved the way for other indie developers to explore niche genres and create games that prioritize player experience over commercial viability.

As the gaming community eagerly awaits more information about the sequel, the excitement surrounding Metanet's return highlights the enduring appeal of well-crafted indie games. The combination of nostalgia for the original N++ and the promise of new multiplayer experiences is likely to attract both long-time fans and new players alike.

### Conclusion

Metanet Studios’ announcement of a multiplayer sequel to N++ marks an important moment in the indie gaming world. As the team prepares to unveil more details, the anticipation builds for what promises to be another challenging and rewarding experience for players. The legacy of N++ continues, and the gaming community watches closely as Metanet embarks on this new chapter.
