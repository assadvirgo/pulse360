## Features and Benefits of Shokz OpenRun Pro

The headphones utilize bone conduction technology, which transmits sound through the cheekbones, bypassing the eardrums. The Shokz OpenRun Pro headphones are lightweight and designed for an active lifestyle, making them suitable for running, cycling, and other outdoor activities.
