## Significant Discounts on Art TVs This Weekend

In a notable sale event, Woot is offering substantial discounts on two popular art televisions, making them more accessible to consumers. The promotion runs through June 26th and features significant price reductions on Samsung's 2025 Frame TV and Hisense's 2025 CanvasTV.

### Price Reductions

The 65-inch Hisense CanvasTV is currently priced at $779.99, reflecting a discount of $520, which marks its best price of the year. This television is designed to blend seamlessly into home decor, allowing users to display art when the TV is not in use. The CanvasTV offers a unique combination of functionality and aesthetic appeal, catering to those who value both entertainment and design.

Similarly, Samsung's 2025 Frame TV, which is also part of this sale, has garnered attention for its innovative approach to television design. While specific pricing details for the Frame TV were not disclosed in the initial announcement, it is known for its ability to transform into a piece of art, displaying a variety of artworks and photographs when not in use. This feature has made it a popular choice among consumers looking to enhance their living spaces.

### The Appeal of Art TVs

Art TVs have gained traction in recent years as they offer more than just traditional viewing experiences. These televisions are equipped with technology that allows them to display high-quality images, making them suitable for showcasing artwork, family photos, or even digital displays. This dual functionality appeals to consumers who wish to maintain a stylish home environment without sacrificing entertainment options.

The current discounts on these models come at a time when many consumers are looking to upgrade their home entertainment systems, especially as more people continue to spend time at home. The ability to purchase high-quality art TVs at reduced prices may encourage more individuals to consider these options for their living spaces.

### Conclusion

Woot's sale on the Samsung Frame TV and Hisense CanvasTV presents an opportunity for consumers to invest in stylish and functional home entertainment solutions at significantly reduced prices. As the sale continues until June 26th, interested buyers are encouraged to take advantage of these offers while they last. The trend of art TVs reflects a growing desire for technology that complements modern living spaces, merging aesthetics with functionality in a way that appeals to diverse consumer preferences.
