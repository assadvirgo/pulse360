## Shokz Launches Mother’s Day Promotion on OpenRun Pro 2 Headphones

In a timely initiative for the upcoming Mother’s Day, Shokz, a prominent name in bone conduction audio technology, has announced a special promotion on its flagship OpenRun Pro 2 headphones. This limited-time offer, which runs through May 10th, allows consumers to purchase the headphones at a discounted price of approximately $139.95, reflecting a savings of $40.

### Overview of the OpenRun Pro 2 Headphones

The OpenRun Pro 2 headphones are designed to provide an open-ear listening experience, making them suitable for users who prioritize situational awareness while enjoying their audio. Utilizing bone conduction technology, these headphones transmit sound through the cheekbones, allowing users to hear their surroundings even while listening to music or taking calls. This feature is particularly beneficial for outdoor activities such as running or cycling, where awareness of the environment is crucial for safety.

### Promotional Details

The Mother’s Day promotion is available through multiple retail channels, including Amazon, Best Buy, and Shokz's official website. The promotional pricing is part of Shokz's broader strategy to attract consumers looking for thoughtful gift options for their mothers or themselves during this celebratory season.
