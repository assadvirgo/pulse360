## Squarespace Offers Discounts for July 2026

### Promotional Details

The primary offer allows customers to enjoy a 20% discount on their next website purchase. This promotion is designed to incentivize individuals and businesses to create or revamp their online platforms, which is particularly relevant as more entities recognize the importance of having a robust digital presence.

In addition to the 20% discount, Squarespace is also providing an exclusive discount code that grants users an additional 10% off their purchases. This dual-tier discount system aims to maximize savings for customers, making it an attractive option for those considering website development or redesign.

Furthermore, users can take advantage of a substantial 50% off on select plans. This significant reduction in pricing is expected to appeal to a wide range of users, from small businesses to individual entrepreneurs, who may be looking for cost-effective solutions to establish their websites.
