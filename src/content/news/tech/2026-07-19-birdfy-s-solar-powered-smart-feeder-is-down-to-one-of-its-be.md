## Birdfy Launches Midyear Sale on Smart Bird Feeders

In a notable move within the tech and outdoor living sectors, Birdfy has initiated a midyear sale, offering discounts of up to 40% on its innovative range of smart bird feeders. This promotion highlights the growing interest in smart home technology and its integration into everyday outdoor activities.

### Significant Price Reduction on Popular Models

Among the standout deals is the Netvue Birdfy Feeder, an AI-powered smart bird feeder equipped with a camera. This particular model has seen its price reduced to $149.99, marking it as one of the best offers available to date. The Birdfy Feeder not only attracts a variety of birds but also allows users to observe and identify different species through its integrated camera, enhancing the birdwatching experience.

### Features and Benefits of the Birdfy Feeder

The Birdfy Feeder is designed with several features that appeal to both casual birdwatchers and avid ornithologists. The AI technology enables the feeder to recognize over 600 species of birds, providing users with valuable insights into the avian visitors to their gardens. Additionally, the camera functionality allows for real-time streaming and recording, making it easier for users to capture moments in nature.

The feeder is powered by solar energy, which aligns with the growing trend towards sustainable and eco-friendly products. This feature not only reduces the need for battery replacements but also ensures that the feeder remains operational during various weather conditions, making it a reliable choice for bird enthusiasts.
