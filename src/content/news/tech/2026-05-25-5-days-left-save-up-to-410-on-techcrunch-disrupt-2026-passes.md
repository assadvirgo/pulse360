## Early Bird Registration for TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 Closes Soon

As the countdown to TechCrunch Disrupt 2026 continues, potential attendees are reminded that early bird registration is set to end soon. Those interested in participating in this prominent technology conference in San Francisco have until May 29 at 11:59 p.m. PT to secure their passes at a reduced rate.

### Significant Savings Available

TechCrunch Disrupt has established itself as a pivotal event in the technology sector, attracting innovators, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders from around the globe. This year, attendees can save up to $410 on their registration fees by taking advantage of the early bird pricing. The savings are a compelling incentive for those looking to immerse themselves in the latest trends and developments within the tech landscape.

### Event Highlights

Scheduled for September 2026, TechCrunch Disrupt will feature a diverse lineup of speakers, workshops, and networking opportunities. The conference is renowned for its Startup Battlefield competition, where emerging companies pitch their ideas to a panel of judges and a live audience, vying for investment and recognition. Additionally, attendees will have access to expert-led sessions covering a wide range of topics, from artificial intelligence to venture capital trends.

### Importance of Timely Registration

With only five days remaining for early bird registration, potential attendees are encouraged to act swiftly. The transition to standard pricing could significantly impact budget-conscious participants, particularly startups and small businesses that may rely on savings to attend such events. Early registration not only secures a lower price but also guarantees a spot at one of the year’s most anticipated technology gatherings.
