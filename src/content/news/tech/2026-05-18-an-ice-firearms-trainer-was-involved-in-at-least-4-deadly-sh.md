Norman's career in law enforcement has been marked by a controversial reputation. He has openly referred to himself as “a fucking savage,” a statement that raises questions about his approach to policing and training. His transition from a police officer to a firearms trainer for federal agencies highlights a significant shift in his professional focus, moving from direct engagement in law enforcement to shaping the tactics and strategies of those who serve in high-pressure situations.
