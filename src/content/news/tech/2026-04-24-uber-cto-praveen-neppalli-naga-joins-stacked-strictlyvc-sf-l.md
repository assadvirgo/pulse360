## Uber CTO Joins StrictlyVC San Francisco Event

In a notable addition to the upcoming StrictlyVC San Francisco event, Uber's Chief Technology Officer, Praveen Neppalli Naga, has been announced as a speaker. The event is set to take place on April 30 at the Sentro Filipino Cultural Center and marks the beginning of this year’s events lineup for TechCrunch.

### A Focus on AI and Scaling Operations

Praveen Neppalli Naga will join an impressive roster of speakers to discuss the challenges and opportunities of operating at scale in the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence (AI). As the CTO of Uber, Naga brings a wealth of experience in technology and innovation, particularly in the context of large-scale operations. His insights are expected to resonate with attendees who are keen on understanding how AI can be leveraged to enhance efficiency and drive growth in various sectors.

### The Significance of the Event

StrictlyVC is known for its focus on the intersection of technology and venture capital, providing a platform for industry leaders and innovators to share their knowledge and experiences. The inclusion of Naga in the speaker lineup underscores the event's commitment to addressing current trends in technology, particularly the integration of AI into business practices.

The event will also feature discussions on various topics relevant to the tech community, making it a valuable opportunity for networking and learning. Attendees can look forward to engaging with experts and thought leaders who are shaping the future of technology.

### Venue and Logistics

The Sentro Filipino Cultural Center, chosen as the venue for this event, is expected to provide an inviting atmosphere for attendees. With its cultural significance and community-oriented space, the center aligns with the event's goal of fostering collaboration and dialogue among technology professionals.

As the tech industry continues to evolve, events like StrictlyVC San Francisco play a crucial role in bringing together thought leaders to discuss pressing issues and share innovative ideas.
