## Lovable in Talks to Double Valuation to $13.2 Billion

In a significant development within the technology sector, Lovable, a prominent player in its field, is reportedly engaged in discussions to secure a new funding round that could double its current valuation to an impressive $13.2 billion. This news comes as the company aims to attract further investment to bolster its growth and expand its market presence.

According to reports from Sifted, the anticipated funding round is expected to raise approximately $300 million. The round is likely to be led by Menlo Ventures, a well-known venture capital firm that has a history of backing innovative technology companies. If successful, this funding would not only enhance Lovable's financial standing but also position it for further strategic initiatives.

Doubling its valuation to $13.2 billion would mark a significant milestone for Lovable, reflecting strong investor confidence in the company's business model and growth trajectory.
