## Diego Luna Joins Disney’s Live-Action Adaptation of ‘Tangled’

In a significant development for fans of Disney's cinematic universe, actor Diego Luna has been confirmed as one of the stars of the upcoming live-action adaptation of the beloved animated film "Tangled." This announcement marks another step in Luna's expanding collaboration with Disney, following his notable performances in various projects within the Star Wars franchise, including the acclaimed series "Andor."

### A New Chapter for Disney's Live-Action Projects

"Tangled," originally released in 2010, received critical acclaim and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Animated Feature. The film is based on the classic fairy tale of Rapunzel, featuring a spirited princess with magical long hair who embarks on an adventure of self-discovery and romance. The live-action version is expected to retain the charm and essence of the animated original while introducing new elements and perspectives.

While specific character details regarding Luna's role have not yet been disclosed, his involvement suggests that Disney is aiming to bring a fresh and dynamic approach to the story. Luna's previous work in the Star Wars universe has showcased his versatility as an actor, making him a fitting choice for a project that blends fantasy and adventure.

### Diego Luna's Rising Star

Diego Luna has garnered a reputation as a talented and versatile actor, known for his ability to navigate a wide range of genres. His portrayal of Cassian Andor in "Rogue One: A Star Wars Story" and the subsequent series "Andor" has been particularly well-received, earning him praise for his nuanced performance and depth of character. With his involvement in "Tangled," Luna continues to solidify his status as a prominent figure in contemporary cinema.

### Anticipation for the Live-Action ‘Tangled’

The announcement of a live-action "Tangled" has generated excitement among fans of the original film and newcomers alike. Disney's recent trend of adapting animated classics into live-action films has seen varying degrees of success, with titles such as "The Lion King" and "Aladdin" drawing significant attention and box office success. The studio aims to recapture the magic of its animated heritage while appealing to modern audiences.

As production details remain under wraps, anticipation continues to build around the project. The casting of Luna, alongside previously announced actress Teagan Croft, suggests that Disney is committed to assembling a talented ensemble that can bring the beloved characters to life in a new format.

### Conclusion

As Diego Luna steps into the world of Disney's live-action adaptations, fans eagerly await further announcements regarding the film's plot, additional cast members, and release dates. With a rich legacy of storytelling and a commitment to innovation, Disney's "Tangled" promises to be a captivating addition to its portfolio of live-action films, blending nostalgia with contemporary cinematic artistry.
