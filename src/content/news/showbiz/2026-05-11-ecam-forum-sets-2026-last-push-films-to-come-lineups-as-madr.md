## ECAM Forum Announces Lineup for 2026 Edition in Madrid

The ECAM Forum, an emerging industry event in Madrid, Spain, is set to take place from June 9-11, 2026, at the Matadero cultural center. In just two editions, the forum has established itself as a vital platform for auteur cinema and international co-production, highlighting Spain's growing influence in the global film industry.

### A Growing Platform for Cinema

Organized by the prestigious ECAM film school, the forum has quickly garnered attention for its commitment to fostering new talent and innovative storytelling. The event aims to connect filmmakers, producers, and industry professionals, facilitating discussions and collaborations that can lead to the development of unique cinematic works.

The ECAM Forum's rapid ascent in the film festival landscape reflects a broader trend in Spain, where the film industry is increasingly recognized for its contributions to international cinema. The forum serves as a launchpad for emerging filmmakers, providing them with the necessary resources and networking opportunities to bring their projects to fruition.

### Unveiling the 2026 Project Lineup

As anticipation builds for its third edition, the ECAM Forum has unveiled its first major project lineup. While specific details about the films and projects have yet to be disclosed, the forum is expected to showcase a diverse range of works that reflect the artistic vision of participating filmmakers. This lineup is anticipated to include both local and international projects, further emphasizing the forum's commitment to global collaboration in the film industry.

The 2026 edition promises to build on the successes of previous years, with a focus on not only showcasing films but also facilitating discussions around the future of cinema. The event will likely include panels, workshops, and networking sessions designed to engage participants and encourage dialogue about the evolving landscape of film production and distribution.

### Impact on the Film Industry

The ECAM Forum's emphasis on auteur cinema aligns with a growing appreciation for unique and personal storytelling in the film industry. By providing a platform for filmmakers to present their work and connect with industry professionals, the forum plays a crucial role in nurturing creativity and innovation within the sector.

Moreover, the forum's location in Madrid, a city known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant arts scene, adds to its appeal. The Matadero, a former slaughterhouse turned cultural hub, serves as an ideal backdrop for the event, offering a space that fosters creativity and collaboration.

### Looking Ahead

As the ECAM Forum prepares for its 2026 edition, it is clear that the event is poised to make a significant impact on the film industry. With its focus on auteur cinema and international co-production, the forum not only highlights the talents of emerging filmmakers but also reinforces Spain's position as a key player in the global film landscape.

As the date approaches, industry professionals and film enthusiasts alike will be eager to see the lineup of projects and participate in the discussions that will shape the future of cinema. The ECAM Forum stands as a testament to the power of collaboration and innovation in the arts, promising an exciting future for the film community in Spain and beyond.
