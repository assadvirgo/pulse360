In a poignant final interview, actor Eric Dane shared insights about his experience filming a particularly challenging scene for the acclaimed HBO series "Euphoria." The actor, who portrayed the character Cal Jacobs, discussed the complexities of shooting a wedding scene while grappling with the realities of his battle with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS). Dane passed away on February 19, leaving behind a legacy of memorable performances.

### The Wedding Scene in "Euphoria"

In the interview, Dane described the wedding scene as one of the most intense moments in the series. He noted that the scene required him to embody a character who was "drunk" and emotionally charged, making it a demanding task both physically and mentally. The actor's commitment to authenticity in his performance was evident, as he sought to portray the raw emotions of his character in a way that resonated with viewers.

### A Personal Battle

Dane's discussion of the filming process was underscored by his personal struggle with ALS, a progressive neurodegenerative disease that affects nerve cells in the brain and spinal cord. Despite the challenges posed by his condition, Dane remained dedicated to his craft, showcasing his resilience and passion for acting. He expressed gratitude for the opportunity to work on "Euphoria," a series known for its bold storytelling and complex characters.
