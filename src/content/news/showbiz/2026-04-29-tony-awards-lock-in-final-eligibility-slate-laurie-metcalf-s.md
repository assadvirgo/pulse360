## Tony Awards Final Eligibility Slate Announced for 2025-2026 Season

The Tony Awards Administration Committee has concluded its final eligibility deliberations for the 2025-2026 Broadway season, marking a significant milestone in the lead-up to one of the most anticipated events in the theater world. This announcement comes as the committee prepares for the nominations, which are set to be revealed on May 5, 2026.

### Key Productions and Performers

During its fourth and final meeting, the committee reviewed 13 productions, determining their eligibility and category placements. This year's deliberations have highlighted a number of prominent performances, ensuring a competitive landscape in various categories. Notable names among the eligible performers include Laurie Metcalf, Stephanie Hsu, Don Cheadle, and Luke Evans, all of whom have garnered significant acclaim for their work on stage.

The decisions made by the Tony Awards Administration Committee play a crucial role in shaping the awards landscape.
