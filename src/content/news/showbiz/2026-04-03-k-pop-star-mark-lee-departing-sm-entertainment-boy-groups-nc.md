## K-pop Star Mark Lee to Depart SM Entertainment and NCT Groups

In a surprising announcement that has sent ripples through the K-pop community, Mark Lee, a prominent member of the globally renowned boy group NCT and its subunits NCT 127 and NCT Dream, is set to leave SM Entertainment. The 26-year-old artist, who has been a foundational figure in the group since its inception, will officially exit next week.

### Background on Mark Lee and NCT

Mark Lee debuted with NCT in 2016 as a part of its unique rotational structure, which allows for a diverse lineup of members across various subunits. NCT has gained international acclaim for its innovative approach to music and performance, with songs that often blend various genres and showcase the talents of its members. Mark, known for his rap skills and charismatic stage presence, has played a significant role in the group's success, contributing to hit tracks and participating in numerous concerts and tours.

### Implications of His Departure

Mark's departure marks a significant moment for both NCT and SM Entertainment, a company that has been instrumental in shaping the K-pop industry. His exit raises questions about the future direction of NCT and its subunits, particularly given the group's ongoing popularity and upcoming projects.
