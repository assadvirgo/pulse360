## Trump to Appear on '60 Minutes' Following Recent Shooting Incident

In a significant development, President Donald Trump is set to appear on CBS's "60 Minutes" this Sunday night. The interview, conducted by Norah O'Donnell, comes in the wake of a shooting incident that occurred near the White House Correspondents' Dinner on Saturday night.

### Context of the Incident

The shooting unfolded outside the Washington Hilton, where the annual White House Correspondents' Dinner was taking place. Law enforcement officers acted swiftly to prevent a potential tragedy, intercepting a gunman who attempted to rush the ballroom where the event was being held. Fortunately, no injuries were reported, and the situation was brought under control without further escalation.

This incident has raised concerns regarding security at high-profile events in the capital, particularly those attended by prominent political figures and media representatives. The White House Correspondents' Dinner, which celebrates the relationship between the press and the presidency, has historically drawn significant attention and attendance from both political and media elites.
