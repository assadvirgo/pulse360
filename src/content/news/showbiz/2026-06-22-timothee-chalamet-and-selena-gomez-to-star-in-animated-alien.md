## Timothée Chalamet and Selena Gomez to Star in Animated Film 'Not Alone'

In a significant development in the realm of animated cinema, acclaimed actors Timothée Chalamet and Selena Gomez are set to collaborate on a new film titled "Not Alone." This project is being produced by Illumination, the studio renowned for its successful franchises such as "Despicable Me" and "Super Mario Bros."

### A New Venture for Chalamet

This film marks Timothée Chalamet's debut in the world of animation. He will lend his voice to the character Joe, described as an "introverted rocket mechanic who lives a quiet life alone." This role presents a unique opportunity for Chalamet to explore a different facet of his acting capabilities, showcasing his versatility beyond live-action performances.

Chalamet has garnered critical acclaim for his performances in films such as "Call Me by Your Name," "Dune," and "Little Women." His transition to voice acting in an animated feature is highly anticipated, as audiences are eager to see how he brings the character of Joe to life.

### Selena Gomez's Role

Joining Chalamet is Selena Gomez, a multi-talented artist known for her work in both music and acting. While specific details about her character have yet to be disclosed, Gomez's involvement adds star power to the project and promises to attract a diverse audience.
