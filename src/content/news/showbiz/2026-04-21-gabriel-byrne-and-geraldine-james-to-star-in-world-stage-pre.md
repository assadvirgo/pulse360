## Gabriel Byrne and Geraldine James to Star in World Stage Premiere of '45 Years'

The world stage premiere of "45 Years," a theatrical adaptation of Andrew Haigh's acclaimed 2015 film, is set to take place at the Minerva Theatre in Chichester, U.K. The production will feature renowned actors Gabriel Byrne and Geraldine James in leading roles, with direction by Prasanna Puwanarajah. The performances are scheduled to run from June 12 to July 11, 2024.

### A Compelling Narrative

"45 Years" explores the complexities of a long-term relationship, focusing on a couple as they prepare to celebrate their anniversary. The narrative delves into themes of love, memory, and the impact of the past on present relationships. The adaptation promises to capture the emotional depth of the original film while bringing a fresh perspective to the stage.

### Notable Cast and Crew

Gabriel Byrne, an acclaimed Irish actor known for his diverse roles in film and television, brings a wealth of experience to the production. He has received critical acclaim for his performances in works such as "The Usual Suspects" and "In Treatment." Geraldine James, a distinguished British actress, is celebrated for her versatility and has appeared in numerous stage and screen productions, including "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" and "The Secret Garden."

The direction by Prasanna Puwanarajah, who has garnered praise for his innovative approach to storytelling, is expected to enhance the emotional resonance of the play. Puwanarajah's previous work has demonstrated a keen ability to engage audiences, making him a fitting choice for this poignant adaptation.
