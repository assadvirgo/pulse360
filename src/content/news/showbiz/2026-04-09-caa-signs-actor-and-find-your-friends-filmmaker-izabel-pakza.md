## CAA Signs Filmmaker Izabel Pakzad for Representation

Creative Artists Agency (CAA) has announced the signing of Iranian-Greek American filmmaker, writer, and actress Izabel Pakzad. This decision comes as Pakzad prepares for the release of her feature directorial debut, "Find Your Friends," which is set to premiere on the streaming platform Shudder on June 12.

### A Rising Talent in Filmmaking

Izabel Pakzad is making waves in the film industry, showcasing her multifaceted talents as both a director and actress. Her upcoming film, "Find Your Friends," is a thriller that explores the dynamics of friendship through a gripping narrative. The film features an ensemble cast that includes notable names such as Helena Howard, Bella Thorne, Chloe Cherry, Zion Moreno, Sophia Ali, and Pakzad herself. 

With this signing, CAA aims to leverage Pakzad's unique voice and vision within the industry, positioning her for future projects that could further her career in filmmaking. The agency has a reputation for representing some of the most influential figures in entertainment, and this partnership is expected to open new avenues for Pakzad's creative endeavors.

### "Find Your Friends": A Synopsis

"Find Your Friends" revolves around a group of friends whose bond is tested when they find themselves in a series of unexpected and dangerous situations. The film delves into themes of loyalty, trust, and the complexities of modern friendships, set against a backdrop of suspense and intrigue. As the release date approaches, anticipation is building among audiences eager to see how Pakzad's directorial vision translates on screen.

### The Significance of Representation

Pakzad's signing with CAA underscores the growing recognition of diverse voices in Hollywood. This move not only highlights her individual talent but also signals a broader shift towards embracing filmmakers from varied backgrounds.
