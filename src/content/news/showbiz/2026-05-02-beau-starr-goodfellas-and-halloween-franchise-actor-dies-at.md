## Beau Starr, Notable Actor from ‘Goodfellas’ and ‘Halloween’ Franchise, Passes Away at 81

Beau Starr, the American actor renowned for his roles in iconic films and television series, has passed away at the age of 81. His death was confirmed by his younger brother, Mike Starr, who reported that Beau died of natural causes at his home in Vancouver.

Beau Starr is perhaps best remembered for his portrayal of Sheriff Ben Meeker in the horror classics "Halloween 4: The Return of Michael Myers" and "Halloween 5: The Revenge of Michael Myers." His performances in these films contributed significantly to the enduring legacy of the Halloween franchise, which has captivated audiences for decades.

In addition to his work in horror, Starr had a notable role in Martin Scorsese's critically acclaimed film "Goodfellas," where he showcased his versatility as an actor. His career spanned several decades, during which he appeared in various television series and films, leaving an indelible mark on the entertainment industry.
