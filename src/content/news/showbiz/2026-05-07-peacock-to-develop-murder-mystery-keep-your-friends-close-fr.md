## Peacock Announces Development of Murder Mystery Series "Keep Your Friends Close"

Peacock, the streaming service operated by NBCUniversal, has officially announced the development of a new murder mystery series titled "Keep Your Friends Close." The project is being spearheaded by writer and executive producer Leo Richardson, who is known for his engaging storytelling and intricate plot development.

### Executive Producers and Production Team

In addition to Richardson, the series will feature Garcelle Beauvais as an executive producer. Beauvais, a prominent actress and television personality, has garnered attention for her roles in various television series and her impactful presence in the entertainment industry. The series will be produced in collaboration with FilmNation, a well-regarded production company known for its diverse portfolio of films and television projects. Universal Television will serve as the studio behind the production, ensuring a robust support system for the series.

### Plot Overview

While specific plot details remain under wraps, the logline for "Keep Your Friends Close" hints at an intriguing premise. The story revolves around the president of the West Hollywood Mom’s Club, suggesting a mix of community dynamics and suspense. The juxtaposition of a seemingly benign social group with the dark undertones of a murder mystery promises to deliver a captivating narrative that explores themes of friendship, trust, and betrayal.
