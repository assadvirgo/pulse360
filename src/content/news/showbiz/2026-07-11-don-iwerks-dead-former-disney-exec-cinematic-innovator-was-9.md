## Don Iwerks, Former Disney Executive and Cinematic Innovator, Passes Away at 96

Don Iwerks, a notable figure in the world of animation and a former executive at The Walt Disney Company, has passed away at the age of 96. His family announced that he died peacefully on Thursday at the Ojai Community Memorial Care Center, surrounded by loved ones.

### A Legacy of Innovation

Iwerks was instrumental in advancing cinematic technology throughout his career. He co-founded Iwerks Entertainment, a company that specialized in creating innovative attractions and experiences for theme parks and entertainment venues. His contributions to the industry have left an indelible mark, particularly in the realms of animation and special effects.

During his tenure at Disney, Iwerks played a crucial role in the development of various groundbreaking projects. He was known for his work on the Disney theme parks, where he helped to integrate cutting-edge technology into attractions that captivated millions of visitors. His vision and creativity were key in enhancing the immersive experiences that Disney is renowned for today.

### Remembering a Visionary

Those who knew Don Iwerks remember him not only for his professional achievements but also for his kindness and generosity. Friends and colleagues have expressed their condolences and shared anecdotes highlighting his passion for storytelling and innovation. His ability to inspire others and foster creativity in those around him was a hallmark of his character.

Iwerks' influence extended beyond his immediate projects; he was a mentor to many in the industry, encouraging the next generation of animators and filmmakers to push the boundaries of their craft. His legacy will continue to inspire future innovators in the world of animation and entertainment.

### A Life Well-Lived

Born in 1927, Iwerks grew up during a transformative era in American cinema. He developed a fascination with animation early on, which led him to pursue a career in the industry. His work at Disney began in the 1950s, where he quickly became known for his technical expertise and creative vision.

Over the decades, Iwerks received numerous accolades for his contributions to the field, including awards recognizing his achievements in animation and technology. His work not only helped to shape the Disney brand but also influenced the broader landscape of entertainment.

### Conclusion

Don Iwerks leaves behind a rich legacy that will be remembered by those who had the privilege of working with him and by countless fans of Disney and animation. As the industry reflects on his contributions, it is clear that his impact will resonate for years to come. His passing marks the end of an era, but his innovations and spirit will continue to inspire future generations in the world of entertainment.
