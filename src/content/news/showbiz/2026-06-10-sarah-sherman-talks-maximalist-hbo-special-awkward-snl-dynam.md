## Sarah Sherman Unveils Maximalist Comedy Special Amid Unique ‘SNL’ Dynamics

In an engaging discussion on the "Comedy Means Business" podcast, Sarah Sherman, known for her role on "Saturday Night Live" (SNL), shared insights into her debut comedy special, "Sarah Squirm: Live + in the Flesh." The special, which has garnered attention for its bold and unconventional style, reflects Sherman's unique comedic voice and her experiences within the competitive landscape of late-night television.

### A New Direction in Comedy

Sherman’s special, produced by Josh Safdie and Ronald Bronstein, is marked by a clear directive from its executive producers: to embrace the absurd and the unconventional. Sherman recounted their approach, stating, “They were like, ‘Cut the f*cking RFK Jr. jokes, bro, Cut the airplane jokes. Just freak sh*t only.’” This mandate set the tone for a performance that is unapologetically maximalist, showcasing Sherman's penchant for pushing boundaries in her comedic narratives.

### The Challenge of SNL Dynamics

As a cast member of SNL, Sherman has navigated the intricate dynamics of a show that often balances traditional sketch comedy with contemporary social commentary. Her experiences on the show have shaped her comedic style, allowing her to explore themes that resonate with a diverse audience. However, the environment can be challenging, as Sherman noted the complexities of collaborating with a large ensemble cast while maintaining her individual voice.

### Embracing the Absurd

"Sarah Squirm: Live + in the Flesh" is not just a reflection of Sherman's comedic style but also a commentary on the current state of comedy. With a focus on the bizarre and the surreal, Sherman invites viewers to engage with humor that defies conventional expectations. This approach aligns with a growing trend in comedy where artists seek to challenge norms and provoke thought through their performances.

### The Impact of the Special

Critics and audiences alike have responded positively to Sherman’s special, praising its originality and fearless exploration of unconventional themes. By prioritizing creativity over mainstream appeal, Sherman has carved out a niche that resonates with those who appreciate a more avant-garde approach to comedy. The special serves as a testament to the evolving landscape of entertainment, where unique voices are increasingly celebrated.

### Conclusion

Sarah Sherman’s "Sarah Squirm: Live + in the Flesh" marks a significant milestone in her career, showcasing her ability to blend personal experience with a broader commentary on the art of comedy. As she continues to navigate the complexities of SNL and beyond, Sherman remains committed to pushing the boundaries of humor, inviting audiences to embrace the unexpected. Her journey reflects a broader shift in the comedy landscape, where authenticity and creativity are paramount.
