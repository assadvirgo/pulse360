## NBCU Television Chairman and Apple Services VP to Speak at Variety's Entertainment Marketing Summit

Variety has announced an exciting lineup of speakers for its upcoming Entertainment Marketing Summit, which is set to take place on April 22, 2026, in Los Angeles. The event, presented by Deloitte, will feature prominent figures from the entertainment and media industries, including Pearlena Igbokwe, Chairman of NBCU Television, and Oliver Schusser, Vice President of Music, TV, Sports, Podcasts, and Beats at Apple.

### Keynote Speakers and Their Contributions

Pearlena Igbokwe, who oversees NBC Entertainment and Peacock Scripted, will engage in a keynote conversation with Cynthia Littleton, a respected figure in entertainment journalism. Igbokwe's extensive experience in television production and her leadership role at NBCU positions her as a significant voice in discussions regarding the future of entertainment marketing and the evolving landscape of media consumption.

Oliver Schusser, representing Apple, will also take the stage to share insights from his role in overseeing various content platforms. His expertise in music, television, and sports aligns with the summit's focus on innovative marketing strategies in an increasingly competitive environment. Schusser's participation underscores Apple's commitment to engaging with industry leaders and fostering discussions that shape the future of entertainment.

### Additional Notable Speakers

The summit will also feature Ashley Graham, a supermodel and entrepreneur known for her advocacy in body positivity and inclusivity. Graham's involvement brings a unique perspective on the intersection of marketing and social issues, highlighting the importance of representation in media.

Alain Tascan, President of Netflix Games, will round out the list of speakers. With the gaming industry rapidly growing and becoming an integral part of entertainment, Tascan's insights are expected to provide valuable context on how gaming intersects with traditional media and marketing strategies.

### The Importance of the Entertainment Marketing Summit

The Entertainment Marketing Summit serves as a platform for industry leaders to discuss trends, challenges, and opportunities within the entertainment sector. As media consumption habits continue to evolve, the insights shared at this summit will be crucial for marketers, producers, and executives looking to adapt and thrive in a dynamic landscape.

With a focus on collaboration and innovation, the event aims to foster discussions that will not only address current challenges but also explore future possibilities in entertainment marketing. Attendees can expect to gain valuable knowledge and networking opportunities that can enhance their understanding of the industry's direction.

As the date approaches, anticipation builds for the insights and discussions that will emerge from this gathering of influential voices in the entertainment world. The 2026 Entertainment Marketing Summit promises to be a significant event for professionals seeking to navigate the complexities of modern media and marketing.
