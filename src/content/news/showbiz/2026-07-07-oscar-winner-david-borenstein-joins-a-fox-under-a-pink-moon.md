## Oscar Winner David Borenstein Joins ‘A Fox Under A Pink Moon’ as Executive Producer

In a significant development for the documentary film landscape, Oscar-winning director David Borenstein has announced his involvement as an executive producer for the upcoming film *A Fox Under A Pink Moon*. This documentary, directed by Mehrdad Oskouei and Soraya Akhlaghi, is already generating buzz as a contender for the next Academy Awards.

### A Bold Narrative

* A Fox Under A Pink Moon* is poised to tell the story of a courageous Afghan heroine, a narrative that Borenstein describes as “punk rock.” This characterization reflects not only the film's themes of resilience and defiance but also highlights the unique storytelling approach that the filmmakers are employing. The film aims to shed light on the struggles and triumphs of women in Afghanistan, particularly in the context of the ongoing socio-political challenges faced by the country.

### Borenstein's Vision

David Borenstein is no stranger to tackling complex and contentious subjects. His previous work, *Mr. Nobody Against Putin*, earned him an Oscar and established him as a filmmaker dedicated to exploring pressing global issues through a nuanced lens. His involvement in *A Fox Under A Pink Moon* is expected to bring a wealth of experience and a distinctive perspective to the project, enhancing its potential impact.

In a statement regarding his new role, Borenstein expressed his admiration for the film's subject matter and the bravery of its central character. "This film is not just about one woman's journey; it’s a representation of the spirit of countless individuals who resist oppression and fight for their rights," he remarked. His commitment to amplifying underrepresented voices in cinema aligns with the film's mission to highlight the resilience of Afghan women.

### Awards and Recognition

The film has already garnered attention in various film festivals, winning accolades that underscore its artistic and narrative strength. With Borenstein's involvement, *A Fox Under A Pink Moon* is expected to gain even more visibility as it approaches the awards circuit. The combination of Oskouei and Akhlaghi's directorial vision, alongside Borenstein's executive production, positions the documentary as a strong contender in the upcoming Academy Awards.

### The Broader Context

As global audiences become increasingly aware of the challenges faced by women in Afghanistan, documentaries like *A Fox Under A Pink Moon* play a crucial role in fostering understanding and empathy. The film not only aims to entertain but also to educate viewers about the realities of life in a country that has been in the spotlight for decades due to conflict and humanitarian crises.

In conclusion, David Borenstein's addition to *A Fox Under A Pink Moon* as executive producer signals a promising trajectory for the film. As it prepares for its release and potential awards season, the documentary stands as a testament to the power of storytelling in advocating for social change and highlighting the indomitable spirit of those who dare to challenge the status quo.
