## Dermot Mulroney Confirms Completion of 'The Hunting Wives' Season 2 and Preparation for Season 3

In an exciting update for fans of Netflix's popular series "The Hunting Wives," actor Dermot Mulroney has announced that the production of Season 2 has officially wrapped. The show, which has garnered attention for its unique blend of sapphic themes and murder mystery, continues to captivate audiences with its engaging storylines and complex characters.

### Production Milestones

Mulroney, who portrays the character Jed Banks, shared this news during a recent interview, indicating that the cast and crew have successfully completed filming for the second season. This announcement comes as a relief to fans who have been eagerly awaiting the continuation of the series, which has been praised for its innovative storytelling and representation.

### Looking Ahead to Season 3

In addition to confirming the completion of Season 2, Mulroney hinted at the preparation for a third season.
