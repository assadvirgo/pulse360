In a notable return to television, Emmy-winning actor Judd Hirsch is set to feature in the two-part Season 2 finale of Fox's medical drama, *Doc*. At 91 years old, Hirsch's involvement adds a significant layer of anticipation to the episodes titled "Stuck" and "Happy Birthday," which promise to deliver high-stakes drama reminiscent of popular series such as *Grey's Anatomy*.

The finale, which will air back-to-back, is expected to present a cataclysmic event that places the lives of major characters in jeopardy. This narrative choice aligns with the trend seen in many successful medical dramas, where pivotal moments often lead to intense emotional and ethical dilemmas. The stakes have never been higher for the show's characters, as viewers will be left on the edge of their seats, wondering who will survive the impending crisis.

Judd Hirsch is a celebrated figure in the entertainment industry, known for his extensive work in both television and film.
