## ACM Awards Expand Performer Lineup for Upcoming Telecast

The Academy of Country Music (ACM) has unveiled an exciting lineup of performers set to take the stage at this year's ACM Awards, scheduled to be held in Las Vegas. The announcement, made on Friday, highlights a diverse array of talent, including some of the top nominees for the prestigious awards.

Among the notable performers are Ella Langley and Zach Top, both of whom have garnered significant recognition within the country music community. Their inclusion in the lineup underscores the ACM's commitment to showcasing emerging artists alongside established names in the industry.

## A Diverse Lineup of Talent

The full list of performers announced by the ACM features a total of 11 artists, reflecting a blend of both rising stars and seasoned performers. Alongside Langley and Top, the lineup includes popular country music figures such as Jordan Davis, Thomas Rhett, and the Red Clay Strays. This mix of talent is expected to create an engaging and dynamic show, appealing to a wide range of country music fans.

The ACM Awards have a long-standing tradition of celebrating excellence in country music, and this year's ceremony promises to continue that legacy. The inclusion of both established artists and newcomers is indicative of the ACM's efforts to foster growth and innovation within the genre.
