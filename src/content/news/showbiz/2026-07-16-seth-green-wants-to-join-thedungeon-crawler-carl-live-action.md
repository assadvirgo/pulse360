## Seth Green Expresses Interest in Joining 'Dungeon Crawler Carl' Live-Action Series

Seth Green, the well-known actor and producer recognized for his role in "Family Guy," has publicly expressed his enthusiasm for the upcoming live-action adaptation of "Dungeon Crawler Carl." The series, which was recently ordered to production by Peacock, has garnered attention for its unique premise and dedicated fanbase. Green, a self-proclaimed fan of the source material, has indicated his desire to join the cast, specifically eyeing the role of Mordecai.

### A Fan's Passion

In a recent interview, Green shared his admiration for "Dungeon Crawler Carl," a popular webcomic and novel series created by Matt Dinniman. The story follows Carl, a man who finds himself in a video game-like world filled with challenges and monsters. As a fan of the narrative, Green's enthusiasm for the project is evident. He believes he would be a fitting choice for the character of Mordecai, a significant figure in the storyline.

This approach reflects a growing trend among actors who prioritize authenticity and fairness in the entertainment industry.
