## FilmNation Acquires Absurdist Action-Thriller Spec from Gabe Delahaye

In a notable development within the entertainment industry, FilmNation Entertainment has secured the rights to the absurdist action-thriller screenplay titled *I’d Like to Speak to Your Manager*, written by emerging talent Gabe Delahaye. This acquisition comes amidst a highly competitive landscape, underscoring the increasing interest in unique narratives that blend humor with action.

### The Rising Star: Gabe Delahaye

Gabe Delahaye has been making waves in Hollywood, with his career gaining significant momentum following the successful sale of his previous action-comedy screenplay. His ability to craft engaging and original stories has caught the attention of several production companies, leading to this latest deal with FilmNation. Delahaye's work is characterized by a distinctive blend of absurdity and action, which seems to resonate well with contemporary audiences looking for fresh content.

### FilmNation's Strategic Move

FilmNation, known for its commitment to producing high-quality films across various genres, has been actively expanding its portfolio. The acquisition of *I’d Like to Speak to Your Manager* aligns with the company’s strategy to invest in innovative projects that challenge conventional storytelling. The involvement of Fortunate Jack as a co-producer further strengthens the project's potential, as both companies aim to bring a unique vision to life.
