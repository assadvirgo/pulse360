## Diego Luna Joins Disney's Live Action Adaptation of 'Tangled'

In a significant casting announcement, Diego Luna, best known for his role in the critically acclaimed series "Andor," is set to join the live-action adaptation of Disney's beloved animated feature "Tangled." This transition marks Luna's continued collaboration with the Disney brand, following his successful portrayal of Cassian Andor in the "Star Wars" universe.

### A New Chapter for Diego Luna

Having recently concluded his work on "Andor," Luna is now poised to take on a new character in the upcoming adaptation of "Tangled." The animated film, originally released in 2010, tells the story of Rapunzel, a young woman with magical long hair who embarks on an adventurous journey of self-discovery. The live-action remake aims to bring a fresh perspective to this classic tale while retaining the charm that made the original a favorite among audiences.

### The Ensemble Cast

Luna will be joined by a talented ensemble cast that includes Teagan Croft, known for her role in "Titans," Milo Manheim from "Zombies," and Kathryn Hahn, who has garnered acclaim for her performances in various projects, including "The Studio." The combination of these actors is expected to bring a dynamic energy to the film, appealing to both longtime fans of the original and new audiences alike.

### Anticipation for the Adaptation

Disney's decision to adapt "Tangled" into a live-action format follows a broader trend within the studio, which has successfully reimagined several animated classics in recent years. The live-action adaptations of films such as "The Lion King," "Aladdin," and "Mulan" have proven to be commercially successful, and "Tangled" is anticipated to follow suit.

The original animated film was praised for its animation, music, and storytelling, and it remains a significant part of Disney's animated canon. With the involvement of a diverse and skilled cast, there is considerable excitement surrounding how the live-action version will interpret the beloved story and its characters.

### Conclusion

As Diego Luna steps into this new role, fans are eager to see how he will contribute to the narrative of "Tangled." With a rich background in both film and television, combined with his recent success in the "Star Wars" franchise, Luna's participation is expected to add depth to the film. As production progresses, further details about the storyline and character dynamics are likely to emerge, keeping audiences engaged and looking forward to this latest offering from Disney.
