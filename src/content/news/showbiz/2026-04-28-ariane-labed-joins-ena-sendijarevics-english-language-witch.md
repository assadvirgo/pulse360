French actress Ariane Labed, acclaimed for her performance in Athina Rachel Tsangari’s film "Attenberg," has been cast in the upcoming drama "The Possessed." This film is the third feature from Bosnian-Dutch writer-director Ena Sendijarević and marks her debut in English-language cinema.

Ariane Labed gained significant recognition after winning the Best Actress award at the Venice Film Festival for her role in "Attenberg," a film that helped establish her as a prominent figure in international cinema.
