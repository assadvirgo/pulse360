## Director Jon Erwin Discusses the Role of AI in 'Young Washington'

In an exclusive interview, Jon Erwin, the director of the upcoming historical drama "Young Washington," shared insights into how artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed the filmmaking process, making it both safer and more affordable. The film, which is set to release nationwide on July 3, portrays a young George Washington during the pre-Revolutionary War era, with actor William Franklyn-Miller taking on the titular role.

### Embracing Technology in Filmmaking

Erwin, known for his innovative approach to storytelling, emphasized the importance of integrating modern technology into the production of "Young Washington." He noted that while advanced visual effects tools can enhance many aspects of filmmaking, they still face limitations when it comes to replicating the complexities of natural elements, such as water. This acknowledgment highlights the ongoing challenge filmmakers face in balancing realism with technological capabilities.

“The physics of water is incredibly complex,” Erwin remarked, pointing out that even the most sophisticated visual effects cannot fully capture its unpredictable nature. This realization led the production team to explore alternative methods to ensure the authenticity of the scenes involving water, particularly those depicting perilous situations for the characters.

### AI as a Game Changer

Erwin explained that the use of AI in various stages of production has significantly streamlined processes that were once labor-intensive. From pre-visualization to post-production, AI tools have enabled the team to visualize scenes more effectively and make informed decisions on how to approach challenging sequences. This not only enhances the creative process but also reduces costs, making high-quality filmmaking accessible to a broader range of creators.

“AI has made production safer and more affordable,” Erwin stated, underscoring how the technology allows filmmakers to experiment with different scenarios without the risks associated with live-action shoots. By leveraging AI, the team was able to plan and execute scenes that might have otherwise posed safety concerns, particularly those involving water dynamics.

### The Importance of Historical Accuracy

As "Young Washington" aims to depict a pivotal moment in American history, Erwin is committed to maintaining historical accuracy while also engaging modern audiences. The film seeks to explore the formative years of Washington, providing insight into the experiences that shaped him into a leader. This balance of authenticity and entertainment is central to Erwin's vision for the film.

In addition to the technological advancements, the director emphasized the importance of a talented cast and crew in bringing the story to life. The collaboration among skilled professionals has been instrumental in ensuring that the film resonates with viewers, both emotionally and intellectually.

### Looking Ahead

As the film's release date approaches, anticipation is building among audiences eager to see how "Young Washington" combines historical narrative with cutting-edge technology. Erwin's insights into the use of AI reflect a broader trend in the film industry, where innovation continues to reshape the landscape of storytelling.

In conclusion, "Young Washington" not only aims to entertain but also to educate viewers about the early life of one of America's most iconic figures. With the integration of AI and a commitment to authenticity, Jon Erwin is poised to deliver a compelling cinematic experience that honors history while embracing the future of filmmaking.
