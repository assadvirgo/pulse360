## Owen and Teddy Exit ‘Grey’s Anatomy’: A Reflection on Their Journey

As the dust settles on the Season 22 finale of ABC's long-running medical drama "Grey's Anatomy," fans are coming to terms with the departure of two beloved characters, Owen Hunt and Teddy Altman. Portrayed by Kevin McKidd and Kim Raver, respectively, these characters have been integral to the series and their exit marks the end of an era for Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital.

### A Farewell to Long-Standing Characters

In the finale titled "Bridge Over Troubled Water," viewers witnessed Owen and Teddy's journey culminate in a heartfelt conclusion. The episode not only served as a farewell to the characters but also provided a moment of reflection on their growth throughout the series. Kevin McKidd and Kim Raver shared insights into their characters' arcs and the significance of their departure in a recent interview.

McKidd noted that Owen's journey has been one of resilience and transformation. From a trauma surgeon grappling with the scars of war to a devoted partner and father, Owen's character has evolved significantly. "I think Owen has earned his place as endgame," McKidd stated, emphasizing the importance of the character's development and the relationships he has built over the years.

### Teddy's Role in Owen's Life

Kim Raver echoed these sentiments, highlighting Teddy's role in Owen's life and the impact of their relationship. As the "Cardio God," Teddy has navigated her own challenges, including professional hurdles and personal losses. Raver explained that Teddy's journey has been about finding her strength and identity, both as a surgeon and as a partner to Owen. "They've both been through so much individually and together, and it feels like they’ve finally found their way," she remarked.

Their relationship has been a focal point of the series, showcasing the complexities of love, trust, and healing. The finale encapsulated this theme, presenting a resolution that felt both earned and satisfying for viewers who have followed their story over the years.

### The Impact of Their Departure

The exit of Owen and Teddy raises questions about the future of "Grey's Anatomy." With the show now entering its 23rd season, fans are left wondering how the narrative will evolve without these pivotal characters. The series has a history of introducing new characters and storylines, and the departure of long-standing figures often paves the way for fresh dynamics.

Both McKidd and Raver expressed gratitude for their time on the show, acknowledging the profound impact it has had on their careers and personal lives. The actors emphasized the importance of the fans, who have supported them throughout their journey. "It's been an incredible ride, and we hope the audience feels the love we put into these characters," Raver said.

### Conclusion

As Owen Hunt and Teddy Altman bid farewell to Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital, their legacy will undoubtedly linger in the hearts of fans. The characters' journeys have been marked by triumphs and tribulations, ultimately leading to a resolution that reflects their growth and resilience. With the future of "Grey's Anatomy" uncertain, the impact of Owen and Teddy's departure will be felt for seasons to come, leaving viewers to ponder what lies ahead in the ever-evolving landscape of the series.
