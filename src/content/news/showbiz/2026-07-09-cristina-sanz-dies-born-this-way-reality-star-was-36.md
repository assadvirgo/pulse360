## Cristina Sanz, Star of 'Born This Way,' Passes Away at 36

Cristina Sanz, a beloved figure known for her role in the A&E reality series "Born This Way," has died at the age of 36. Her passing occurred on July 6, following a cardiac arrest in Long Beach, California. The news was shared by her parents, Mariano and Beatriz Sanz, through an emotional announcement on Instagram.

### A Life Celebrated

"Born This Way" premiered in 2015 and ran for four seasons, concluding in 2019. The series was notable for its portrayal of adults living with Down syndrome, highlighting their aspirations, challenges, and triumphs. Cristina Sanz was a central figure in the show, captivating audiences with her vibrant personality and resilience.

Her parents expressed their grief and pride in their daughter's achievements, stating, “Cristina was a light in our lives, and her spirit will continue to inspire us every day.” The announcement has prompted an outpouring of tributes from fans and fellow cast members, who have shared their memories and the impact Cristina had on their lives.
