## Women in Animation Celebrates a Decade of the WIA World Summit in Annecy

Women in Animation (WIA) has announced an exciting lineup for the upcoming WIA World Summit, set to take place in Annecy, France. This year marks a significant milestone as the summit celebrates its 10th anniversary. Themed “Frame by Frame: A Global Celebration of Women and Nonbinary Creators,” the event aims to highlight the contributions of diverse voices in the animation industry.

### A Platform for Diverse Voices

The WIA World Summit has established itself as a crucial platform for discussing the challenges and opportunities faced by women and nonbinary creators in animation. The theme for this year's summit reflects a commitment to inclusivity and recognition of the varied experiences that shape the animation landscape.

WIA’s mission has always been to promote gender equality and representation within the animation sector. This year’s celebration not only commemorates a decade of progress but also serves as a call to action for future advancements in diversity within the industry.

### Notable Guests and Key Discussions

One of the highlights of this year’s summit will be a fireside chat featuring Bonnie Arnold, a renowned producer known for her work on acclaimed animated films. Arnold's participation underscores the importance of mentorship and shared experiences in fostering the next generation of creators. Her insights are expected to resonate with attendees, providing inspiration and guidance as they navigate their careers in animation.

The summit will also include a series of panels, workshops, and networking opportunities designed to engage participants in meaningful discussions about the future of animation. Topics will range from storytelling techniques to the evolving role of technology in animation production, all while emphasizing the importance of diverse perspectives.

### Looking Ahead

As WIA celebrates its 10th anniversary, the organization is also taking the opportunity to reflect on the progress made over the past decade. The summit will pose the critical question: What comes next for women and nonbinary creators in the animation industry? This inquiry aims to inspire dialogue about the ongoing challenges and potential pathways for growth and innovation.

The WIA World Summit in Annecy is not only a celebration of past achievements but also a forward-looking event that seeks to chart a course for the future. By fostering collaboration and dialogue among creators, industry leaders, and advocates, WIA aims to ensure that the animation industry continues to evolve in a way that is inclusive and equitable.

### Conclusion

The WIA World Summit at Annecy promises to be a significant event for those involved in the animation industry. As it marks a decade of advocacy and celebration, the summit will serve as a reminder of the vital role that women and nonbinary creators play in shaping the future of animation. With a strong lineup of speakers and a commitment to fostering inclusivity, this year's summit is poised to inspire and empower attendees as they continue to push the boundaries of creativity in animation.
