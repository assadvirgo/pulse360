## Dan Levy Reflects on a Potential 'Schitt's Creek' Sequel Following Catherine O'Hara's Passing

In a recent interview, Dan Levy, the co-creator and star of the acclaimed Canadian television series *Schitt's Creek*, revealed that he had contemplated the idea of a sequel to the beloved show. However, following the death of Catherine O'Hara, who played the iconic character Moira Rose, Levy has decided against pursuing the project.

### A Fond Remembrance

Levy's reflections came during an interview with CBS Sunday Mornings, where he discussed his upcoming Netflix comedy series, *Big Mistakes*. While he initially entertained the thought of revisiting the world of *Schitt's Creek*, the loss of O'Hara has profoundly impacted his perspective. "No, not now," Levy stated when asked about the possibility of a sequel. "You can’t," he added, highlighting the deep emotional connection he shared with his co-star and friend.

O'Hara's portrayal of Moira Rose earned her widespread acclaim and contributed significantly to the show's success, which ran for six seasons from 2015 to 2020. The series garnered numerous awards, including multiple Primetime Emmy Awards, and has since become a cultural touchstone, celebrated for its humor, heart, and representation of LGBTQ+ characters.

### The Legacy of *Schitt's Creek*

*Schitt's Creek* tells the story of the wealthy Rose family, who find themselves broke and forced to relocate to a small town they once bought as a joke. The show is lauded not only for its comedic elements but also for its exploration of family dynamics, love, and acceptance. The chemistry among the cast, particularly between Levy and O'Hara, was a significant factor in the show's charm and appeal.

Levy's decision to refrain from a sequel reflects the profound impact that O'Hara's passing has had on him and the *Schitt's Creek* community. The series concluded on a high note, with a heartfelt finale that left fans satisfied yet yearning for more. The show's legacy continues to resonate, and Levy's acknowledgment of O'Hara's contribution underscores the importance of their collaborative work.

### Looking Ahead

As Levy moves forward with his new project, *Big Mistakes*, he aims to capture a different narrative while honoring the spirit of storytelling that *Schitt's Creek* embodied. The new series is expected to showcase Levy's signature wit and insight, exploring themes of personal growth and the comedic pitfalls of life.

While the dream of a *Schitt's Creek* sequel may remain unfulfilled, Levy's commitment to creating meaningful content continues. The impact of O'Hara's role in the series will undoubtedly linger, influencing both Levy's future projects and the hearts of fans who cherished the original series.

In summary, Dan Levy's reflections on a potential *Schitt's Creek* sequel reveal not only his respect for the show's legacy but also the profound emotional weight that comes with the loss of a beloved co-star. As he embarks on new creative endeavors, the memory of O'Hara and the essence of *Schitt's Creek* will continue to inspire and resonate within the entertainment landscape.
