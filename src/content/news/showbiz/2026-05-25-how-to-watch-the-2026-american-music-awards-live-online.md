## How to Watch the 2026 American Music Awards Live Online

The anticipation for the 2026 American Music Awards (AMAs) is building, with fans eager to see their favorite artists recognized for their contributions to the music industry. Scheduled to air live on May 25, 2026, at 8 p.m. ET / 5 p.m. PT, the event promises to be a highlight in the entertainment calendar.

### Broadcast Details

The American Music Awards will be broadcast live on CBS, a long-standing partner of the event. For those who prefer streaming options, the ceremony will also be available on Paramount+. This provides a convenient way for viewers to catch the show from the comfort of their homes or on the go.

### Streaming Options for Cord-Cutters

As the trend of cable-cutting continues, the AMAs are accessible to a broader audience through various streaming platforms. Viewers can stream the ceremony live on DirecTV and Hulu + Live TV, both of which offer access to local CBS channels. This flexibility allows fans to choose the platform that best suits their viewing preferences.

### Free Trials Available

For those who have yet to subscribe to these streaming services, both DirecTV and Hulu + Live TV are currently offering free trials. This presents an excellent opportunity for new users to enjoy the American Music Awards without any initial cost. However, it is advisable to check the terms of the free trials to ensure a seamless viewing experience.

### Conclusion

The 2026 American Music Awards is set to be an exciting event, showcasing the best in music and entertainment. With multiple viewing options available, fans have the opportunity to enjoy the ceremony live, whether through traditional cable or modern streaming services. As the date approaches, viewers are encouraged to mark their calendars and prepare for a night of celebration in the music industry.
