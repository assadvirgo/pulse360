## The Role of Intimacy Coordinators

Intimacy coordinators have become increasingly important in the entertainment industry, particularly in projects that explore sensitive themes. Their primary role is to ensure that all actors feel comfortable and secure during intimate scenes, which can often be emotionally charged. Aasiya Shah highlighted the significance of having a "safe bubble" on set, allowing actors to focus on their performances without the added stress of navigating potentially uncomfortable situations.
