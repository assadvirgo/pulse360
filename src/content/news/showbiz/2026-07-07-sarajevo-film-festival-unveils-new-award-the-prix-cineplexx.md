## Sarajevo Film Festival Introduces Prix Cineplexx to Promote Theatrical Releases

The Sarajevo Film Festival (SFF), a prominent cultural event in the Balkans, has announced the introduction of a new award named the Prix Cineplexx. This initiative aims to underscore the importance of theatrical releases in an era increasingly dominated by digital streaming platforms.

### Audience-Driven Recognition

The Prix Cineplexx will be unique in its voting process, as it will be determined entirely by the festival audience. This approach not only engages festival-goers but also empowers them to play a significant role in recognizing films that resonate with viewers. The award carries a total value of 20,000 euros (approximately $22,855), which will be allocated to support a marketing campaign for the winning film's theatrical distribution.

### Supporting Theatrical Distribution

The introduction of this award comes at a crucial time when the film industry is grappling with the challenges posed by the rise of streaming services. The SFF aims to highlight the necessity of theatrical releases, which provide a communal viewing experience and foster a deeper connection between filmmakers and audiences. By supporting the marketing of the winning film, the Prix Cineplexx seeks to encourage more films to reach cinemas, thereby enhancing the cultural landscape of the region.

### A Commitment to Cinema

The festival organizers expressed their commitment to preserving the theatrical experience. “In an age where digital consumption is prevalent, we believe it is essential to remind audiences of the magic of cinema in a theater setting,” said a spokesperson for the SFF. The initiative reflects a broader trend within the film industry to advocate for theatrical releases as a vital component of film distribution.

### A Platform for Emerging Talent

The Sarajevo Film Festival has long been a platform for showcasing emerging talent from the region and beyond. The introduction of the Prix Cineplexx aligns with the festival's mission to support filmmakers and promote diverse storytelling. By incentivizing theatrical releases, the award aims to provide filmmakers with the resources necessary to bring their stories to a wider audience.

### Conclusion

As the film industry continues to evolve, the Prix Cineplexx represents a proactive step towards ensuring that theatrical releases remain a vital part of the cinematic experience. The Sarajevo Film Festival's initiative not only recognizes the audience's role in film appreciation but also emphasizes the importance of community engagement in the arts. With this new award, the SFF reaffirms its dedication to celebrating cinema and fostering a vibrant film culture in the region.
