*The Northumbria Mysteries* features Robson Green, known for his role in the popular series *Grantchester*, as the lead character, Joe Ruby. Ruby is portrayed as a resourceful jack-of-all-trades who finds himself embroiled in a series of intriguing criminal investigations. The show promises to blend elements of mystery and drama, drawing viewers into the unique cultural and geographical landscape of Northumberland.
