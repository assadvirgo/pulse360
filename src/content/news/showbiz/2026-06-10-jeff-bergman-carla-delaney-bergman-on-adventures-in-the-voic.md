## Exploring the Voiceover Industry with Jeff and Carla Delaney Bergman

In a recent episode of the Crew Call podcast, hosts engaged in an insightful conversation with husband-and-wife duo Jeff Bergman and Carla Delaney Bergman, both prominent figures in the voiceover industry. The couple shared their unique journeys, experiences, and the evolving landscape of voice acting, from iconic projects like "Looney Tunes" to the upcoming "Toy Story 5."

### A Journey into Voiceover

Jeff Bergman's foray into voiceover work began in an unexpected way. During his college years, he had the rare opportunity to meet Mel Blanc, the legendary voice behind many beloved Looney Tunes characters. This encounter sparked Jeff's interest in the craft, leading him to pursue a career in voice acting. His passion and dedication eventually paid off, as he became known for his ability to embody classic characters and create new ones.

Carla Delaney Bergman, also an accomplished voiceover artist, shared her own path into the industry. While she initially took a different route, her talent and enthusiasm for voice acting led her to collaborate with Jeff on various projects. Together, they have carved out a niche in a competitive field, demonstrating the importance of perseverance and adaptability.

### Insights into the Industry

During the podcast, the Bergmans discussed the challenges and rewards of working in voiceover. They emphasized the significance of versatility, as voice actors often need to adapt their skills to fit a wide range of characters and styles. The couple also highlighted the importance of networking and building relationships within the industry, noting that many opportunities arise from connections made over time.

Jeff and Carla also touched upon the impact of technology on voiceover work. With advancements in recording equipment and software, the landscape has changed dramatically, allowing for greater accessibility and flexibility. However, they cautioned that this also means increased competition, making it essential for aspiring voice actors to continuously hone their craft.

### Looking Ahead

As the conversation shifted towards future projects, the Bergmans expressed excitement about their involvement in "Toy Story 5." This highly anticipated installment in the beloved franchise promises to deliver new adventures and characters, and the couple is eager to contribute their voices to the film.

The Bergmans' journey serves as an inspiration to many aspiring voice actors. Their story underscores the importance of passion, dedication, and the willingness to embrace change in a rapidly evolving industry. As they continue to navigate their careers, Jeff and Carla remain committed to sharing their experiences and insights with others, fostering a sense of community within the voiceover world.

### Conclusion

The Crew Call podcast episode featuring Jeff and Carla Delaney Bergman offers a compelling glimpse into the voiceover industry, showcasing the couple's remarkable journey and the lessons they have learned along the way. Their experiences serve as a testament to the power of perseverance and creativity in a field that continues to evolve, providing valuable insights for both seasoned professionals and newcomers alike.
