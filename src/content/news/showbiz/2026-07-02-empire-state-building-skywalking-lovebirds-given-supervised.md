## Empire State Building Skywalking Couple Granted Supervised Release

In a notable development from Manhattan Criminal Court, Ivan “Vanya” Beerkus and Angela Nikolau, who gained attention for their daring ascent to the top of the Empire State Building, have been granted supervised release following their recent arrest. This incident, which was part of a publicity stunt linked to their upcoming Netflix documentary titled *Skywalkers: A Love Story*, has captured the interest of both the media and the public.

### The Incident

On a Wednesday in late June, Beerkus and Nikolau climbed to the iconic needle-top of the Empire State Building, an act that not only raised eyebrows but also prompted a swift response from law enforcement. The couple reportedly undertook this climb as part of a betrothal celebration, intending to showcase their adventurous spirits and love for heights. However, their actions were met with legal consequences, leading to their arrest and subsequent court appearance.

### Court Proceedings

During their brief appearance in court, the couple faced charges related to their unauthorized climb. The judge decided to grant them supervised release, allowing them to avoid immediate incarceration while awaiting further legal proceedings. This decision reflects a recognition of the couple's unique circumstances, though it underscores the seriousness of their actions.

### Public Reaction and Media Attention

The couple's climb has sparked a mix of admiration and criticism. While some view their actions as a bold expression of love and adventure, others raise concerns about safety and the legality of such stunts. The incident has also drawn significant media coverage, particularly in light of the upcoming documentary that chronicles their experiences as urban climbers. *Skywalkers: A Love Story* is expected to delve into their motivations and the risks associated with their lifestyle.

### Next Steps

As part of their supervised release, Beerkus and Nikolau will have to adhere to specific conditions set by the court. A new court date has been scheduled, where further proceedings will take place to address the charges against them. The couple's situation serves as a reminder of the legal implications that can arise from high-profile stunts, regardless of the intentions behind them.
