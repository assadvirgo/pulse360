## Katherine LaNasa Joins Hulu's 'Count My Lies' as Shailene Woodley's Mother

Katherine LaNasa, an Emmy Award-winning actress known for her role in the acclaimed series "The Pitt," has been announced as part of the cast for Hulu's upcoming limited series "Count My Lies." This project is being developed by former "This Is Us" executive producers and co-showrunners Isaac Aptaker and Elizabeth Berger.

### Role Details

In "Count My Lies," LaNasa will portray the character Tracy, who is the mother of Sloane, played by actress Shailene Woodley. The series is anticipated to explore complex family dynamics and the challenges faced by its characters, with LaNasa's role likely to be pivotal in shaping Sloane's narrative arc.
