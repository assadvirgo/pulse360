## Aubrey Plaza’s ‘Kevin’ Unveils New Trailer Featuring a Star-Studded Cast

Prime Video has recently released the official trailer for its upcoming adult animated comedy series, **Kevin**, co-created by Aubrey Plaza and Joe Wengert. The series centers around the life of a neurotic housecat named Kevin, who embarks on a journey of self-discovery after breaking up with his owners. 

### A Unique Concept

The premise of **Kevin** offers a fresh perspective on the common trope of pet narratives, presenting the story through the eyes of a housecat grappling with complex emotions and relationships. This unique angle is expected to resonate with audiences who appreciate humor intertwined with relatable themes of independence and personal growth.

### Star-Studded Guest Appearances

The trailer has generated excitement not only for its intriguing storyline but also for its impressive lineup of guest stars. Among those featured are social media sensation **Addison Rae**, actor **Joe Locke**, and comedian **Nicole Byer**. The inclusion of such diverse talents suggests that the series will blend various comedic styles, appealing to a broad audience.

Other notable guest appearances include **Cary Elwes**, **Charles Melton**, **Ben Rodgers**, **Carl Tart**, **Debby Ryan**, **Eugene Cordero**, and **Jim O’Heir**. The ensemble cast hints at a rich tapestry of characters that will contribute to the series' humor and narrative depth.

### The Creative Team Behind Kevin

Aubrey Plaza, known for her distinctive comedic style and roles in shows like *Parks and Recreation*, brings her unique voice to the project. Co-creator Joe Wengert, who has a background in comedy writing and performance, is expected to complement Plaza's vision, resulting in a series that balances wit with emotional resonance.

### Anticipation and Release Date

As anticipation builds for the series, fans are eager to see how **Kevin** will differentiate itself in the crowded landscape of animated comedies. The combination of Plaza's comedic prowess and the innovative premise is likely to attract viewers looking for fresh content in the genre.

While a specific release date has yet to be announced, the trailer provides a tantalizing glimpse into what audiences can expect. With its blend of humor, heart, and a star-studded cast, **Kevin** is poised to make a significant impact upon its debut on Prime Video.

### Conclusion

In a landscape where animated series are increasingly popular, **Kevin** stands out with its unique premise and talented cast. As viewers await its release, the trailer serves as a promising introduction to what could become a beloved addition to the genre. With Aubrey Plaza at the helm, expectations are high for a series that aims to entertain while exploring the complexities of both human and feline relationships.
