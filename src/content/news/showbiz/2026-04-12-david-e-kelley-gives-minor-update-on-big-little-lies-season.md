David E. Kelley, the acclaimed creator of the HBO drama series "Big Little Lies," recently shared a brief update regarding the much-anticipated third season of the show. In an interview with People, Kelley, who has won 11 Emmy Awards throughout his career, indicated that while he is restricted from disclosing specific details about the upcoming season, fans can look forward to a reunion of the beloved characters.

Since its debut, "Big Little Lies" has garnered a dedicated fan base and critical acclaim, making the prospect of a third season highly anticipated.
