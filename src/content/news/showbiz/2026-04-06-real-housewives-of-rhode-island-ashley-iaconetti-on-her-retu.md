Ashley Iaconetti, a familiar face to fans of reality television, is set to make her return to the small screen as one of the seven women featured in Bravo's new series, "The Real Housewives of Rhode Island." Known for her appearances on multiple seasons of "The Bachelor" franchise, Iaconetti's involvement in this new venture has generated significant interest among viewers and fans alike.

Iaconetti first gained prominence as a contestant on "The Bachelor" in 2015, where her emotional journey and relatable personality resonated with audiences. Since then, she has participated in various spin-offs, including "Bachelor in Paradise," showcasing her growth and evolution as a reality star. Her return to television with "The Real Housewives of Rhode Island" marks a new chapter in her career, as she transitions from the dating-focused narrative of "The Bachelor" to the more dynamic and often dramatic world of the "Real Housewives" franchise.
