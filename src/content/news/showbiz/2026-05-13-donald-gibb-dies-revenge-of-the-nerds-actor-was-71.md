## Donald Gibb, Iconic 'Revenge of the Nerds' Actor, Passes Away at 71

Donald Gibb, widely recognized for his role as "Ogre" in the beloved film franchise "Revenge of the Nerds," has passed away at the age of 71. His son, Travis Gibb, confirmed the news to TMZ, revealing that Donald died on Tuesday evening due to health complications at his home in Texas, surrounded by family.

### A Legacy in Film

Gibb's portrayal of the intimidating yet ultimately lovable character "Ogre" in the "Revenge of the Nerds" series left an indelible mark on popular culture. The franchise, which began with the original film in 1984, became a cult classic, celebrated for its humorous take on college life and the struggles faced by socially awkward individuals. Gibb's character, known for his imposing physique and comedic antics, resonated with audiences and contributed to the films' enduring popularity.

Beyond "Revenge of the Nerds," Gibb's career spanned several decades and included various roles in television and film. He appeared in popular series such as "The A-Team" and "Knight Rider," showcasing his versatility as an actor. Gibb's larger-than-life personality and charm made him a memorable figure in the entertainment industry.
