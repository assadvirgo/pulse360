## Isabelle Huppert to Star in Yann Demange's Thriller 'Lineage'

French screen legend Isabelle Huppert is poised to make her mark in British cinema with her upcoming role in "Lineage," a highly anticipated thriller directed by acclaimed filmmaker Yann Demange. Known for his work on projects such as "Top Boy" and the historical drama "71," Demange's latest venture promises to deliver a gripping narrative set against a backdrop of crime and intrigue.

### A Stellar Cast

Joining Huppert in this exciting project are rising stars Dali Benssalah and Adam Bessa, alongside award-winning global recording artist Raye, who is set to make her breakout appearance on the big screen. The ensemble cast reflects a blend of seasoned talent and fresh faces, creating a dynamic that is likely to resonate with audiences.

### The Project's Significance

"Lineage" marks a significant milestone for Huppert, as it is her first foray into British film. The actress, who has garnered critical acclaim and numerous awards throughout her career, is expected to bring her unparalleled depth and intensity to the role. The film's exploration of complex themes within the crime genre aligns well with Huppert's artistic sensibilities.

### International Sales Launch at Cannes

The project is generating considerable buzz ahead of its international sales launch at the Cannes Film Market. This high-profile event serves as a platform for filmmakers and producers to showcase their work to potential buyers and distributors. With the involvement of CAA Media Finance, a prominent player in the entertainment industry, "Lineage" is positioned to attract significant attention from global markets.

### A Look Ahead

As the film industry continues to evolve, "Lineage" stands out as a project that not only highlights the talents of its cast but also underscores the collaborative spirit of international cinema. With Demange at the helm, audiences can expect a film that is not only thrilling but also rich in character development and storytelling.

In conclusion, "Lineage" is shaping up to be a noteworthy addition to the crime drama genre, and the combination of Huppert's experience, the fresh talent of Benssalah, Bessa, and Raye, along with Demange's directorial vision, makes it a project to watch in the coming months.
