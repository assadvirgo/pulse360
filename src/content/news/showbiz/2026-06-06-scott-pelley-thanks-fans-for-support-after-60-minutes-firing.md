## Scott Pelley Expresses Gratitude to Supporters Following Departure from '60 Minutes'

Former "60 Minutes" correspondent Scott Pelley has publicly thanked his fans for their unwavering support following his recent dismissal from the long-running news program. In an emotional post on Instagram, Pelley reflected on the outpouring of kindness he has received, stating, "To all of you who have been so kind, you are the wind in my sails."

### Background on the Dismissal

Pelley's departure from "60 Minutes" comes after a reported confrontation with the program's new executive producer, Nick Bilton. While details surrounding the incident remain sparse, it has been characterized as "explosive," suggesting a significant clash of perspectives regarding the show's direction and editorial choices. Pelley, who has been a prominent figure on the program for years, is known for his in-depth reporting and engaging storytelling, which has earned him a loyal following.

In the wake of his firing, fans and colleagues alike have expressed their support for Pelley.
