## Sabrina Impacciatore and Alessandro Borghi to Star in New Psychological Thriller

Sabrina Impacciatore, known for her role in the acclaimed series "The White Lotus," is set to take on a challenging new role in the upcoming Italian psychological thriller "Un'Altra Madre" ("Another Mother"). The series will also feature Alessandro Borghi, a prominent figure in Italian cinema, recognized for his performances in "The Eight Mountains" and "Supersex."

### Project Overview

"Un'Altra Madre" is being produced as a Prime Original and is currently in the midst of filming. The announcement was made during Prime Video’s Italy upfront presentation held in Rome, where the streaming service unveiled its upcoming projects aimed at the Italian audience. The film is directed by Francesco Carrozzini, who has previously worked on "Supersex," indicating a collaboration that could blend his directorial vision with the talents of the cast.

### The Storyline

While specific plot details remain under wraps, the title "Un'Altra Madre" suggests themes of motherhood and psychological complexity. Impacciatore is set to portray a tormented therapist, a role that promises to showcase her dramatic range and depth as an actress. The character's struggles may reflect broader themes of mental health and emotional turmoil, resonating with contemporary societal issues.

Sabrina Impacciatore gained international recognition for her performance in "The White Lotus," where she captivated audiences with her nuanced portrayal of complex characters. Her involvement in "Un'Altra Madre" marks a significant step in her career, as she transitions from supporting roles to leading ones in high-profile projects.

Alessandro Borghi, on the other hand, has established himself as a versatile actor in Italian cinema. His previous works, including "The Eight Mountains," have earned critical acclaim, and his participation in this thriller is expected to add a compelling dynamic to the film.
