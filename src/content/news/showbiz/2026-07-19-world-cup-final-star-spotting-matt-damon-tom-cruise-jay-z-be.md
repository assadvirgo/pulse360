The recent World Cup final held at the New York-New Jersey Stadium attracted a star-studded lineup of celebrities from various entertainment sectors. Damon, known for his versatile roles in films such as "Good Will Hunting" and the "Bourne" series, was seen enjoying the electrifying atmosphere of the stadium. Meanwhile, Cruise, celebrated for his performances in blockbuster franchises like "Mission: Impossible," added to the glamour of the event.
