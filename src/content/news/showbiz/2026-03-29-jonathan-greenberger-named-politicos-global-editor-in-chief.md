## Jonathan Greenberger Appointed as Politico's Global Editor in Chief

In a significant leadership transition, Politico has announced the appointment of Jonathan Greenberger as its new global editor in chief, effective May 1, 2024. Greenberger will succeed John Harris, who co-founded the political news outlet and will transition to the role of chairman.

### Background of Jonathan Greenberger

Jonathan Greenberger brings a wealth of experience to his new position. He joined Politico in 2024 as executive vice president, where he played a pivotal role in shaping the outlet's editorial direction and expanding its global reach. Prior to his tenure at Politico, Greenberger served as the Washington bureau chief for ABC News for a decade, where he was instrumental in covering major political events and shaping the network's political reporting.

His extensive background in journalism and deep understanding of political landscapes are expected to guide Politico as it navigates the complexities of global political reporting in an increasingly interconnected world.

### Transition of Leadership

John Harris, the outgoing editor in chief, has been a foundational figure at Politico since its inception. Under his leadership, the outlet has grown into a prominent source of political news and analysis, recognized for its in-depth reporting and innovative storytelling. As he steps into the role of chairman, Harris will continue to influence the strategic direction of the organization.

In a statement regarding the transition, Harris expressed confidence in Greenberger's capabilities, stating, "Jonathan has a remarkable ability to connect with audiences and a keen insight into the evolving political landscape. I am excited to see how he will lead Politico into its next chapter."

### The Future of Politico

Greenberger's appointment comes at a crucial time for media organizations, particularly those focused on political reporting. The landscape is rapidly changing, with audiences increasingly seeking reliable information amidst a backdrop of misinformation and polarized narratives. As global editor in chief, Greenberger will be tasked with enhancing Politico's credibility and expanding its international presence.

His vision for the future of Politico will likely focus on leveraging digital platforms to reach a wider audience while maintaining the high standards of journalism that the outlet is known for. This includes fostering a diverse range of voices and perspectives in political discourse, which is essential in today's globalized society.

### Conclusion

As Jonathan Greenberger prepares to take the helm as global editor in chief of Politico, the media landscape will be watching closely. His leadership is anticipated to usher in a new era for the outlet, one that embraces the challenges and opportunities of modern political journalism. With the foundation laid by John Harris, Greenberger's experience and vision may well position Politico for continued success in the years to come.
