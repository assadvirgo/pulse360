## HBO Max Unveils Trailer Featuring New Footage from ‘Gilded Age’ Season 4 and Upcoming Titles

HBO Max has recently released a trailer that showcases a variety of new footage from both returning series and highly anticipated upcoming titles. Among the highlights is a first look at the fourth season of the critically acclaimed series “Gilded Age,” which has garnered significant attention since its debut.

### Renewal and Anticipation

In July 2025, HBO Max announced the renewal of “Gilded Age” for a fourth season, a decision that reflects the show's popularity and the strong viewership it has maintained. The series, set in New York City during the late 19th century, delves into the lives of the wealthy elite and the social dynamics of the era. With its rich storytelling and elaborate production design, “Gilded Age” has captivated audiences and critics alike.

Francesca Orsi, the executive vice president of HBO programming, expressed enthusiasm about the continuation of the series. “The Gilded Age has proven to be a remarkable exploration of the complexities of wealth and class in America, and we are excited to bring more stories to our viewers,” Orsi stated.

### Highlights from the Trailer

The newly released trailer offers glimpses of what fans can expect in the upcoming season. Although specific plot details remain under wraps, the visuals suggest a continuation of the intricate relationships and societal challenges that have defined the series thus far. The trailer also includes snippets from other popular HBO Max titles, creating a sense of excitement for the platform's diverse offerings.

In addition to “Gilded Age,” the trailer teases new footage from the much-anticipated “Harry Potter” series, which has been a topic of discussion among fans of the franchise. The inclusion of these beloved titles in the trailer indicates HBO Max's strategy to leverage its existing intellectual properties while introducing new content to attract a wide audience.

### Conclusion

As HBO Max prepares for the release of “Gilded Age” Season 4 and other upcoming projects, the anticipation among viewers continues to build. The trailer serves as a reminder of the platform's commitment to delivering high-quality programming that resonates with its audience. With the combination of returning favorites and new adventures, HBO Max aims to solidify its position as a leading provider of premium entertainment in the streaming landscape.
