4. **Navigate to the ACMs**: Once logged in, search for the Academy of Country Music Awards in the Prime Video library. The show will be prominently featured, making it easy to find.

5. **Enjoy the Show**: Tune in at the scheduled time to watch the live broadcast of the ACMs.
