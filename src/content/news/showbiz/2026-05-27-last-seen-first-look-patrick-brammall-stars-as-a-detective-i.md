## Apple TV Unveils First Look at Australian Thriller 'Last Seen'

Apple TV has recently announced the upcoming release of its new six-episode thriller series, "Last Seen," which is set to premiere on September 9. The show, previously titled "The Dispatcher," features Australian actor Patrick Brammall in the lead role as Detective Ian Ridley. 

## Plot Overview

"Last Seen" delves into the complexities of a police detective's life as he navigates a personal and professional crisis. The storyline focuses on Ridley, whose life has unraveled due to circumstances surrounding his work. The series promises to explore themes of loss, redemption, and the psychological toll of crime investigation, drawing viewers into the intricacies of the detective's journey.

## Release Schedule

The series will debut with the first two episodes available for streaming on September 9, followed by a weekly release of new episodes every Wednesday. This structured rollout aims to build anticipation and engagement among viewers, allowing them to immerse themselves in the unfolding narrative.

## Cast and Crew

In addition to Brammall, the series features a talented ensemble cast, although specific details about other actors have yet to be disclosed. The production team behind "Last Seen" is noted for its commitment to high-quality storytelling, a hallmark of Apple TV's growing portfolio of original content.

"Last Seen" represents a significant addition to the Australian television landscape, showcasing local talent and narratives. The series not only highlights the capabilities of Australian filmmakers but also aims to reach a broader international audience through the global platform of Apple TV.
