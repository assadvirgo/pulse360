## ‘For All Mankind’ Star Joel Kinnaman Discusses Character's Death in Season 5

### A Poignant Coincidence

The episode aired on the same day as the historic Artemis II mission, which marked the first crewed mission to orbit the moon in fifty years. This coincidence has not gone unnoticed, as it highlights the ongoing themes of exploration and sacrifice that are central to "For All Mankind." The creators aimed to draw parallels between the show's fictional narrative and real advancements in space travel, making the character's demise even more impactful.

The decision to write such a pivotal moment was not taken lightly. The creative team spent considerable time deliberating on how the character's departure would affect the overall narrative arc of the series. Kinnaman noted that the character had been a cornerstone of the show, making the loss feel personal for both the cast and the viewers.
