## Monia Chokri to Lead Jury for Cannes Caméra d’Or Prize

Monia Chokri, the acclaimed Québécois actress, director, and screenwriter, has been appointed to preside over the jury for the prestigious Caméra d’Or prize at the Cannes Film Festival. This recognition underscores her significant contributions to the film industry and highlights her growing influence on the international stage.

### The Caméra d’Or Prize

The Caméra d’Or is awarded annually at the Cannes Film Festival to the best first feature film presented in the official selection, as well as in the Critics’ Week and Directors’ Fortnight sections. Established in 1978, the award aims to promote new talent and encourage innovative storytelling in cinema. It serves as a crucial platform for emerging filmmakers, providing them with visibility and recognition within the global film community.

### Monia Chokri's Career

Chokri gained prominence in the film industry with her breakout role in Denys Arcand’s critically acclaimed film, "The Age of Ignorance." Since then, she has established herself not only as a talented actress but also as a skilled director and screenwriter. Her work often explores complex themes and character dynamics, resonating with audiences and critics alike.

In addition to her acting career, Chokri has directed several short films and feature-length projects, showcasing her versatility and commitment to the craft. Her appointment as jury president reflects her deep understanding of cinema and her dedication to nurturing the next generation of filmmakers.

### Significance of the Appointment

Chokri's leadership of the Caméra d’Or jury is particularly significant given the ongoing discussions about diversity and representation in the film industry. Her role as a female filmmaker in a prominent position at one of the world’s most prestigious film festivals marks a step toward greater inclusivity in the cinematic landscape.

As the jury president, Chokri will have the opportunity to influence the selection process and highlight films that not only demonstrate artistic excellence but also bring fresh perspectives to the forefront. Her insights and experiences will be invaluable in evaluating the submissions and recognizing outstanding talent.
