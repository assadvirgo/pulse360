The Las Culturistas Culture Awards, a highly anticipated event in the entertainment calendar, returned for its fifth iteration on Wednesday, showcasing a blend of humor, music, and cultural commentary. ### A Star-Studded Evening

This year’s ceremony was notable not only for its celebratory atmosphere but also for its impressive lineup of performances and appearances. One of the standout moments came when actress and singer Rachel Zegler delivered a captivating rendition of "Fame Is a Gun." Her performance was met with enthusiastic applause, highlighting her growing prominence in the entertainment industry.

### Lisa Kudrow Takes the Stage

Another highlight of the evening was the appearance of beloved actress Lisa Kudrow, known for her iconic role in the television series "Friends." Kudrow's presence added a nostalgic touch to the event, as she engaged with the audience and shared insights into her career.
