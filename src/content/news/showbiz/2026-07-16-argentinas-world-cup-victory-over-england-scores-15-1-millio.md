## Argentina's World Cup Victory Over England Sets Viewership Record

In a remarkable display of sporting prowess and fan engagement, the FIFA World Cup 2026 semifinal match between Argentina and England garnered an average of 15.063 million viewers on Fox Sports. This figure not only signifies a monumental achievement for the network but also establishes the match as the most-watched FIFA Men's World Cup semifinal in English-language history.

### Record-Breaking Viewership

The match, held on Wednesday, showcased a thrilling contest that captivated audiences across the United States. The viewership numbers surpassed those of the previous day's semifinal, where Spain faced France, which itself had set a high bar with impressive ratings. The back-to-back record-breaking telecasts highlight the growing popularity of soccer in the U.S., particularly during major international tournaments.

Argentina's victory over England was not just a sporting event; it was a cultural phenomenon that drew in fans from diverse backgrounds.
