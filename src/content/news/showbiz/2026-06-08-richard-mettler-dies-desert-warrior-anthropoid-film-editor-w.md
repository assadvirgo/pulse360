## Richard Mettler, Acclaimed Film Editor, Passes Away at 56

Richard Mettler, a notable film editor recognized for his work on acclaimed films such as *Anthropoid* and the upcoming *Desert Warrior*, has died at the age of 56. Mettler passed away on June 1 at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center in New York City, following an eight-year battle with cancer.

Mettler began his career in the film industry editing music videos, where he honed his skills in storytelling and visual pacing. His transition to feature films marked a significant shift in his career, allowing him to collaborate with various directors and contribute to projects that garnered both critical and commercial success.

His editing work on *Anthropoid* (2016), a historical thriller starring Jamie Dornan and Cillian Murphy, showcased his ability to craft suspenseful narratives. The film, which tells the story of the assassination of Nazi leader Reinhard Heydrich during World War II, received praise for its gripping storytelling and meticulous editing, elements that were integral to its impact.
