## Lauren Graham, Patti LuPone, and Dan Bucatinsky Join Nick Borenstein in Indie Comedy 'August'

In an exciting development for the independent film scene, acclaimed actors Lauren Graham, Patti LuPone, and Dan Bucatinsky are set to star alongside Nick Borenstein in the upcoming comedy 'August.' This film marks Borenstein's debut as a feature director, screenwriter, and actor, and is currently in production in New York City.

### A Fresh Perspective in Indie Film

'August' tells the poignant story of a young, gay New Yorker, played by Borenstein himself, who grapples with the sudden death of a loved one. The narrative promises to explore themes of love, loss, and the complexities of navigating life in a bustling metropolis. The film aims to blend humor with heartfelt moments, reflecting the unique challenges faced by its protagonist.

### Notable Cast Contributions

Lauren Graham, known for her roles in popular television series such as 'Gilmore Girls' and 'Parenthood,' brings her signature charm and depth to the project. Her involvement is expected to add a layer of emotional resonance to the film, enhancing its exploration of personal relationships and grief.

Patti LuPone, a Tony Award-winning actress celebrated for her powerful performances on stage and screen, joins the cast, bringing her formidable talent and experience to the project. LuPone's participation is particularly noteworthy, as she has a history of choosing roles that resonate deeply with audiences, often tackling complex characters with grace and intensity.

Dan Bucatinsky, recognized for his work in 'The Comeback' and as an Emmy Award-winning producer, rounds out the cast. His experience in both acting and production will likely contribute to the film's overall quality, ensuring that the story is told with authenticity and care.

### The Vision Behind 'August'

Nick Borenstein's transition from actor to director and screenwriter is a significant step in his career. His vision for 'August' reflects a personal connection to the material, as he seeks to portray the realities of modern life through the lens of his own experiences. The film aims to resonate with audiences who have faced similar challenges, offering both humor and solace in the face of adversity.

### Production and Release Information

Currently filming in New York City, 'August' is anticipated to capture the vibrant essence of the city, serving as both a backdrop and a character in its own right. While a specific release date has yet to be announced, the film is generating buzz within the indie film community, with many eager to see how Borenstein's debut unfolds.

As the project progresses, it will be interesting to observe how the collaboration between such talented individuals shapes the final product. With a blend of humor and heartfelt storytelling, 'August' has the potential to make a significant impact in the indie film landscape.

In conclusion, the combination of Graham, LuPone, and Bucatinsky alongside Borenstein promises a compelling cinematic experience that could resonate with a wide audience. As production continues, anticipation builds for what could be a noteworthy addition to the genre of independent comedy.
