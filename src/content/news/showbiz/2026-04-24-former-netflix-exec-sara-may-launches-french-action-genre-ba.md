Sara May, a prominent figure in the film industry and former executive at Netflix, has announced the launch of her new production company, Sonderworld. The initiative marks May's foray into the action and genre film sector, a space she aims to invigorate with innovative storytelling and original intellectual properties.

### Backing from Goodfellas

Sonderworld has secured backing from Goodfellas, a well-known player in the film financing arena. This partnership is expected to provide the necessary resources and expertise to support the development of a diverse slate of projects. The collaboration underscores a shared vision of creating elevated, globally-minded action and genre films that resonate with audiences both in France and internationally.

### Focus on Original IP and Franchises

With Sonderworld, May intends to focus on building original intellectual properties and franchises. This strategic direction aligns with current trends in the film industry, where unique narratives and characters are increasingly sought after to captivate viewers. By emphasizing originality, Sonderworld aims to carve out a niche in the competitive landscape of action and genre cinema.

Sara May's transition from Netflix to establishing her own production company reflects a broader trend among industry veterans seeking to leverage their experience in new ventures.
