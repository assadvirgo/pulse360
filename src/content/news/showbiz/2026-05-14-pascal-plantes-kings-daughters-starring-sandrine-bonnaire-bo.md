## WTFilms Acquires Worldwide Rights to Pascal Plante’s ‘King’s Daughters’

WTFilms, a prominent player in the global film distribution landscape, has secured the worldwide rights to Pascal Plante’s latest feature film, “King’s Daughters.” This historical survival drama, which incorporates elements of folk horror, showcases a talented cast led by acclaimed French actress Sandrine Bonnaire, alongside Laurie Babin, Lauréna Thellier, Zoé Adjani, and newcomer Marguerite Champoux.

### A Glimpse into the Film

Set in the year 1663, “King’s Daughters” delves into a tumultuous period in history, chronicling the experiences of women who were sent from France to New France (now Canada) as part of a royal initiative to populate the colonies. These women, known as the "King's Daughters," faced numerous challenges, including harsh living conditions and the complexities of survival in an unfamiliar land. The film promises to intertwine historical accuracy with imaginative storytelling, offering audiences a unique perspective on the struggles and resilience of these women.

### Pascal Plante's Directorial Vision

Pascal Plante, known for his previous works such as the cyber-thriller “Red Rooms,” the punk rom-com “Fake Tattoos,” and the sports drama “Nadia, Butterfly,” brings his distinctive narrative style to “King’s Daughters.” His ability to blend genres and explore human experiences through a nuanced lens has garnered critical acclaim, and this latest project is anticipated to further showcase his artistic vision.

### The Cast

The film features a diverse ensemble cast, with Sandrine Bonnaire, a veteran of French cinema, leading the charge. Bonnaire’s extensive career includes notable performances in films such as “Vagabond” and “The Ceremony,” and her involvement adds significant weight to the project. The inclusion of rising talents like Lauréna Thellier and Marguerite Champoux suggests that “King’s Daughters” aims to not only tell a compelling story but also to highlight the next generation of actors in the industry.

### Distribution and Expectations

With WTFilms at the helm of distribution, expectations are high for “King’s Daughters.” The company has a reputation for promoting innovative and thought-provoking films, and its acquisition of this title indicates a commitment to bringing unique narratives to a global audience. The film’s blend of historical context and folk horror elements may resonate well with viewers seeking depth and intrigue in their cinematic experiences.

### Conclusion

As “King’s Daughters” moves forward in its production and distribution phases, it stands as a testament to the evolving landscape of historical dramas in cinema. With a skilled director, a strong cast, and the backing of a reputable distributor, the film is poised to make a significant impact upon its release. Audiences can look forward to a gripping portrayal of resilience and survival, set against the backdrop of a pivotal moment in history.
