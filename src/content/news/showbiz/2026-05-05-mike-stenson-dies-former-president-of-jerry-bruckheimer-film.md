## Mike Stenson, Former President of Jerry Bruckheimer Films, Passes Away at 65

Mike Stenson, a prominent figure in the film industry and the former president of Jerry Bruckheimer Films, has died at the age of 65. Stenson's passing occurred on April 26, 2023, although the cause of death has not been disclosed.

His tenure included significant projects such as "Top Gun: Maverick," which marked his final film before his untimely death, and the iconic "Coyote Ugly." Under his leadership, the studio became synonymous with blockbuster hits and high-octane storytelling.
