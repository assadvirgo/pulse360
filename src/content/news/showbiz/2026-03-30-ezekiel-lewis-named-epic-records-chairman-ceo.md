## Ezekiel Lewis Appointed Chairman and CEO of Epic Records

Sony Music has officially announced the appointment of Ezekiel “Zeke” Lewis as the new chairman and chief executive officer of Epic Records. This decision, made public on Monday, marks a significant milestone in Lewis's career, as he transitions from his previous role as president of the label, a position he has held since 2023.

### Background and Experience

Ezekiel Lewis has been a pivotal figure within Epic Records, particularly in the areas of artist and repertoire (A&R) management. His leadership has been instrumental in shaping the label's strategic direction and fostering relationships with artists. Under his guidance, the A&R division has seen considerable growth and innovation, contributing to the label's reputation for nurturing diverse musical talent.

Prior to his role at Epic, Lewis gained extensive experience in the music industry, where he developed a keen understanding of market dynamics and artist development. His ascent to the role of chairman and CEO follows the departure of Sylvia Rhone, who had a long tenure leading the label. Rhone's exit left a significant void, and Lewis, alongside General Manager Rick Sackheim, has been managing the day-to-day operations during this transitional period.

### Vision for the Future

In his new role, Lewis is expected to leverage his extensive experience to further enhance Epic Records' position in the competitive music landscape. His vision includes a focus on expanding the label's roster of artists and exploring new avenues for music distribution and promotion. Lewis has expressed a commitment to fostering an inclusive environment that supports creativity and innovation, aiming to attract a wide array of talent across various genres.

### Industry Reactions

The announcement of Lewis's appointment has been met with positive reactions from industry insiders. Many see his elevation as a natural progression, given his track record of success at Epic. His deep understanding of the evolving music industry landscape is viewed as a vital asset that will help guide the label through the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

### Conclusion

Ezekiel Lewis's appointment as chairman and CEO of Epic Records signifies a new chapter for the label as it seeks to navigate the complexities of the modern music industry. With his experience and vision, Lewis is poised to lead Epic into a new era, continuing its legacy of artistic excellence while adapting to the changing dynamics of music consumption and production. As he steps into this prominent role, the industry will be watching closely to see how his leadership shapes the future of Epic Records.
