With the Emmy nominations round voting set to begin on June 11, 2026, Paramount+ is ramping up its promotional efforts. This timeline places "The Madison" in a prime position to capture the attention of voters and viewers alike as the awards season unfolds.

Paramount+ has devised a series of themed promotions designed to resonate with audiences and critics.
