## Filmmakers Use Generative AI to Reclaim Black History in Revolutionary War Documentary

As the United States approaches its 250th anniversary, a new documentary titled *Declarations: Black Americans and the Revolutionary War* is set to premiere on June 29. Directed by Stacey Holman and co-directed by Maya Tepler, the film seeks to illuminate the often-overlooked contributions of Black Americans during the Revolutionary War. In a bold move, the filmmakers have turned to generative artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance their storytelling and "give our historical subjects agency."

### Reclaiming Historical Narratives

The Revolutionary War is a pivotal event in American history, but the narratives surrounding it have frequently marginalized the roles played by Black Americans. Holman and Tepler aim to rectify this oversight by using modern technology to bring these historical figures to life in a more dynamic and engaging manner. The filmmakers believe that employing generative AI can help create a more nuanced portrayal of Black Americans who fought for their freedom and contributed to the nation’s founding ideals.

In an interview, Holman emphasized the importance of agency in historical storytelling. "We wanted to ensure that the voices of Black Americans are not just heard but are given the prominence they deserve," she stated. The use of AI allows the filmmakers to reconstruct dialogues, create immersive visuals, and even simulate historical scenarios that highlight the struggles and triumphs of Black individuals during this era.

### The Role of Generative AI

Generative AI has emerged as a powerful tool in various fields, including film and media. In the context of *Declarations*, it enables the filmmakers to explore creative avenues that were previously limited by traditional documentary techniques. By harnessing AI, Holman and Tepler can generate realistic representations of historical events, thereby inviting viewers to engage with history in a more interactive way.

Tepler noted that the technology serves not only to enhance visual storytelling but also to foster a deeper emotional connection with the audience. "We want viewers to feel the weight of these historical moments," she explained. "Generative AI helps us create an experience that resonates on a personal level."

### Aiming for Impact

The documentary is part of a broader movement to recognize and celebrate the contributions of marginalized groups in American history. By focusing on Black Americans in the Revolutionary War, Holman and Tepler hope to inspire discussions about race, identity, and the ongoing struggle for equality in the United States. The filmmakers believe that understanding the past is crucial for shaping a more inclusive future.

As the premiere date approaches, anticipation is building around how *Declarations* will challenge conventional narratives and offer fresh perspectives on the Revolutionary War. The filmmakers are hopeful that their innovative approach will not only educate viewers but also encourage them to reflect on the complexities of American history.

### Conclusion

*Declarations: Black Americans and the Revolutionary War* represents a significant step toward reclaiming and reinterpreting history through the lens of those who have been historically marginalized. By integrating generative AI into their storytelling, Stacey Holman and Maya Tepler aim to give voice and agency to Black Americans who played a crucial role in the founding of the United States. As the nation approaches its 250th birthday, this documentary serves as a timely reminder of the diverse narratives that have shaped the American experience.
