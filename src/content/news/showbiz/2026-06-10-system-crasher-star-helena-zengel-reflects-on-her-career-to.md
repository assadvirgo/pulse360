## Helena Zengel Reflects on Her Career as She Transitions to Adulthood in ‘Westend Girl’

Helena Zengel, the Golden Globe-nominated actress who gained international acclaim for her role in the critically acclaimed film *System Crasher*, is now stepping into adulthood with her latest project, *Westend Girl*. As she embarks on this new chapter in her career, Zengel has taken the opportunity to reflect on her journey thus far.

Zengel first captured the attention of audiences worldwide with her powerful performance in *System Crasher*, directed by Nora Fingscheidt. The film, which tells the story of a troubled young girl navigating the complexities of the foster care system, showcased Zengel's remarkable talent at a young age. Her portrayal earned her accolades and established her as one of the most promising young actors in the industry.

In a recent interview with Deadline, Zengel expressed her thoughts on her early career, stating, “I watched *System Crasher* the other day and was thinking it’s crazy what such a young child did.” This reflection highlights not only her recognition of her past achievements but also the maturity she has gained as she transitions into more complex roles.

As Zengel prepares for her role in *Westend Girl*, she acknowledges the challenges and responsibilities that come with growing up in the public eye. The film, which marks her first adult role, presents a significant shift in her career trajectory. Zengel is eager to embrace the new opportunities that lie ahead while remaining grounded in her experiences as a child actor.
