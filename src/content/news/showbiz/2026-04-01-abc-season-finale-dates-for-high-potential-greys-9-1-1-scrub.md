## ABC Announces Season-Finale Dates for Popular Shows

As the 2025-26 television year approaches its conclusion, ABC has unveiled the season-finale dates for several of its highly anticipated dramas and comedies. This announcement comes as fans prepare for the climactic endings of their favorite series, including "High Potential," "Grey’s Anatomy," "9-1-1," "The Rookie," and the rebooted "Scrubs."

### Key Finale Dates

ABC's lineup features a variety of genres, from gripping dramas to lighthearted comedies. The season finales are set to air in the coming weeks, providing viewers with a chance to see how various storylines will wrap up. Notable finale dates include:

- **High Potential**: This new drama has garnered attention for its engaging narrative and character development. The finale is expected to deliver a thrilling conclusion to its inaugural season.
- **Grey’s Anatomy**: A stalwart of ABC's programming, this long-running medical drama continues to captivate audiences. The finale promises to address lingering plot threads and character arcs that have developed over the years.
- **9-1-1**: Known for its intense scenarios and emotional storytelling, this series will also conclude its season with high stakes and dramatic revelations.
- **The Rookie**: This police procedural has built a loyal following, and its season finale is anticipated to provide a satisfying resolution to its current storylines.
- **Scrubs**: The reboot of this beloved sitcom has been met with enthusiasm, and fans are eager to see how the season will conclude.

In addition to these dramas, ABC will also wrap up several popular sitcoms, including "Abbott Elementary," which has received critical acclaim for its humor and heartfelt moments.

### Unscripted Programming

ABC is not only focusing on scripted content; the network will also conclude several long-running unscripted shows. Among these is "American Idol," which has been a staple of ABC's programming for years. The finale is expected to showcase the talent and emotional journeys of the contestants, culminating in a highly anticipated winner announcement.
