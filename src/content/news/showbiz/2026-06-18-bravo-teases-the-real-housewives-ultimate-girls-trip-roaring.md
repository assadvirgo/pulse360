## Bravo Celebrates 20 Years of 'The Real Housewives' Franchise with New Special

Bravo has announced a special event to commemorate the 20th anniversary of its iconic franchise, 'The Real Housewives.' Titled 'The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip: Roaring 20th,' the new series aims to celebrate the legacy and impact of the show that has captivated audiences for two decades.

### A Look Back at Two Decades

The announcement was made during the 2026 Las Culturistas Culture Awards, where Bravo unveiled the first promotional video for the upcoming special. This initiative reflects the network's commitment to honoring the franchise that has not only reshaped reality television but has also become a cultural phenomenon.

Since its inception in 2006 with 'The Real Housewives of Orange County,' the franchise has expanded to include numerous spin-offs and adaptations, featuring a diverse array of women from different backgrounds and lifestyles. The series has sparked conversations about wealth, relationships, and the complexities of modern womanhood, making it a staple in reality TV.

### Featured Cast Members

The promotional video highlights several prominent figures from the franchise, including Vicki Gunvalson, Kyle Richards, Porsha Williams, Lisa Barlow, and Teresa Giudice. Each of these women has left an indelible mark on the series, bringing their unique personalities and stories to the forefront. Their participation in this special is expected to draw significant interest from fans who have followed their journeys over the years.

### What to Expect from 'Roaring 20th'

While specific plot details remain under wraps, 'The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip: Roaring 20th' is anticipated to blend nostalgia with fresh content. The special is likely to feature memorable moments, behind-the-scenes insights, and reflections from cast members on their experiences within the franchise. This format has been successful in previous iterations of the 'Ultimate Girls Trip' series, which has garnered a positive response from viewers.

### The Cultural Impact of 'The Real Housewives'

Over the years, 'The Real Housewives' franchise has not only entertained but has also influenced popular culture. It has spawned countless memes, merchandise, and discussions about societal norms and expectations. The show's ability to blend drama with humor has resonated with audiences, making it a topic of conversation across various platforms.

As Bravo prepares to launch 'The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip: Roaring 20th,' fans are eager to see how the network will honor the legacy of the franchise while continuing to engage its audience in new and exciting ways.

### Conclusion

Bravo's upcoming special promises to be a celebration of two decades of entertainment, drama, and memorable moments. As the franchise continues to evolve, it remains a significant part of the reality television landscape, and 'Roaring 20th' is set to be a highlight in its storied history. Fans can look forward to a nostalgic trip down memory lane, coupled with the excitement of new adventures with their favorite housewives.
