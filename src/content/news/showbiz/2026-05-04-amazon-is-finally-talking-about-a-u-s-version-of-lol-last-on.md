## Amazon Considers U.S. Adaptation of 'LOL: Last One Laughing'

Amazon's popular comedy format, 'LOL: Last One Laughing,' may soon find its way to American audiences. The show, which has been successfully adapted in over 25 countries, has yet to make its debut in the United States. However, recent comments from Amazon’s Head of Nonfiction Series, Jenn Levy, suggest that this could change in the near future.

### A Global Phenomenon

Originally launched in 2017, 'LOL: Last One Laughing' challenges contestants to keep a straight face while trying to make each other laugh. The format has proven to be a hit across various international markets, with adaptations in countries such as Canada, Australia, and Germany. Each version brings its own cultural flair, contributing to the show's global appeal.

Levy, who took on her role earlier this year, revealed that the question she receives most frequently is regarding the potential for a U.S. version of the series. This feedback highlights the anticipation and interest among American audiences for the unique comedic format.

### The Road Ahead

While Levy has not provided a specific timeline for the U.S. adaptation, her acknowledgment of the demand indicates that Amazon is seriously considering the project. The success of similar reality and competition shows in the U.S. market suggests that 'LOL: Last One Laughing' could resonate well with American viewers.

The adaptation process will likely involve selecting a host and a diverse group of contestants who can bring their own comedic styles to the show. Given the popularity of comedy in American entertainment, the potential for a successful U.S. version appears promising.

### The Impact of Reality Television

Reality television continues to thrive, with networks and streaming platforms constantly on the lookout for fresh formats that can engage viewers. The success of 'LOL: Last One Laughing' in other countries demonstrates the universal appeal of humor and competition, making it a strong candidate for adaptation.

As Amazon explores this opportunity, it will be interesting to see how the show is tailored to fit American sensibilities while maintaining the essence that has made it a global success. The potential for celebrity guest appearances and the incorporation of social media elements could further enhance its appeal.

### Conclusion

As discussions continue regarding the U.S. version of 'LOL: Last One Laughing,' viewers are left to speculate about the potential cast and format changes. With Jenn Levy at the helm, Amazon appears poised to make a significant move into the reality comedy space. If successful, this adaptation could not only entertain but also reinvigorate interest in comedy competitions in the U.S. market. 

As more details emerge, fans of the original format and newcomers alike will be watching closely to see how Amazon brings this international hit to American screens.
