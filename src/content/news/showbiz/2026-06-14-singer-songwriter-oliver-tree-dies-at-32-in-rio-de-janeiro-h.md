In a tragic incident that has shocked fans and the music industry alike, singer-songwriter Oliver Tree has died at the age of 32 following a helicopter crash in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The Civil Police of Rio de Janeiro confirmed that Tree was among six individuals who lost their lives in the accident.

Oliver Tree, known for his eclectic style and genre-blending sound, first gained widespread recognition in 2018 with his collaboration on Whethan's viral alternative pop track "When I’m Down." His unique approach to music, characterized by a fusion of pop, electronic, and alternative elements, quickly garnered a dedicated fanbase. Tree's debut album, "Ugly Is Beautiful," released in 2020, showcased his distinctive voice and artistic vision, earning him critical acclaim and commercial success.
