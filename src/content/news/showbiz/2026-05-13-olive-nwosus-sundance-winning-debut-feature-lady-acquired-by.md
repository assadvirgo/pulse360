## Olive Nwosu’s Sundance-Winning Debut Feature ‘Lady’ Acquired by Cohen Media Group

Cohen Media Group has secured the U.S. distribution rights to "Lady," the debut feature film from Nigerian-born writer-director Olive Nwosu. The acquisition comes on the heels of the film's successful world premiere at the Sundance Film Festival, where it competed in the World Dramatic Cinema Competition and garnered the Special Jury Award for Best Ensemble Cast.

### A Promising Debut

"Lady" marks a significant milestone in Nwosu's career, showcasing her talent and vision as a filmmaker. The film has been well-received for its compelling narrative and strong performances, which contributed to its recognition at Sundance. The Special Jury Award for Best Ensemble Cast highlights the collaborative effort of the actors involved, reflecting Nwosu's ability to bring together a diverse group of performers to tell a resonant story.

Cohen Media Group, known for its commitment to distributing independent and foreign films, has a history of supporting filmmakers who bring unique stories to the forefront.
