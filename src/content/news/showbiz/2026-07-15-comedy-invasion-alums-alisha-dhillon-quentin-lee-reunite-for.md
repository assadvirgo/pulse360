## Alisha Dhillon and Quentin Lee Team Up for New Series 'Unleeshed'

In an exciting development for fans of comedy and Asian American storytelling, comedian Alisha Dhillon and creator Quentin Lee are reuniting for a new vertical series titled "Unleeshed." Both Dhillon and Lee are well-known for their contributions to the Canadian Screen Award-winning series "Comedy Invasion," and their collaboration on "Unleeshed" promises to bring fresh perspectives to the world of standup comedy.

### A New Venture in Comedy

"Unleeshed" is set to be broadcast worldwide on Asian American Movies (AAM.tv) in time for Thanksgiving 2026. The series will showcase the vibrant and often challenging landscape of standup comedy in Los Angeles, a city known for its rich comedic history and diverse talent pool. Dhillon, who not only stars in the series but also serves as the writer, aims to explore the nuances of the comedy scene while infusing her unique voice and experiences into the narrative.

### The Creative Partnership

The reunion of Dhillon and Lee marks a significant moment for both creators, who have previously collaborated on projects that highlight Asian American experiences. Their work has often been characterized by a blend of humor and poignant social commentary, making it relatable to a wide audience. This new series is expected to continue that tradition, offering insights into the lives of comedians as they navigate their careers and personal challenges.

### The Importance of Representation

The announcement of "Unleeshed" comes at a time when representation in media is more crucial than ever. With a growing demand for diverse stories in the entertainment industry, Dhillon and Lee's project is poised to contribute to the ongoing conversation about visibility and authenticity in comedy. By centering Asian American narratives, the series aims to provide a platform for underrepresented voices in the industry.

### Looking Ahead

As the release date approaches, anticipation is building for "Unleeshed." The series promises to not only entertain but also provoke thought about the complexities of identity, culture, and the pursuit of dreams in the competitive world of standup comedy. With Dhillon's comedic talent and Lee's creative vision, viewers can expect a series that is both engaging and meaningful.

In summary, the collaboration between Alisha Dhillon and Quentin Lee on "Unleeshed" represents a significant step forward in the representation of Asian American stories in mainstream media. As they prepare for the series' debut on AAM.tv in 2026, audiences are eager to see how their shared experiences and insights will shape this new comedic venture.
