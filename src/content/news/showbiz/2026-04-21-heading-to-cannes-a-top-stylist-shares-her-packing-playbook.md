## Heading to Cannes? A Top Stylist Shares Her Packing Playbook for the Croisette

As the prestigious Cannes Film Festival approaches, fashion enthusiasts and attendees alike are gearing up for one of the most glamorous events in the film industry. Renowned stylist Maryam Malakpour, who is celebrated for her work with A-list celebrities, including the Academy Award-winning actress Halle Berry, has shared her insights on how to curate the perfect wardrobe for the festival. 

### Styling for the Stars

Maryam Malakpour's expertise was notably showcased when she designed a series of stunning looks for Halle Berry during her role as a juror at the 2025 Cannes Film Festival. The collaboration not only highlighted Berry's elegance but also underscored Malakpour's understanding of the unique demands of festival fashion. 

In planning Berry's wardrobe, Malakpour emphasized the importance of balancing sophistication with comfort, a crucial aspect when attending multiple screenings and events throughout the festival. "Cannes is about making a statement, but it’s also about feeling good in what you wear," Malakpour explained. 

### Key Packing Tips for Festival Attendees

As attendees prepare for this year’s festival, Malakpour has shared her top packing tips to ensure that everyone can navigate the Croisette with style and ease.

1. **Versatile Wardrobe Choices**: Malakpour recommends packing a selection of versatile pieces that can be mixed and matched. "Choose outfits that can transition from day to night, such as a chic dress that can be dressed down with flats for daytime events and dressed up with heels for evening galas," she advises.

2. **Statement Accessories**: Accessories play a pivotal role in elevating any outfit. Malakpour suggests bringing a variety of statement jewelry and bags to add a personal touch to each look. "A bold necklace or a unique handbag can transform a simple outfit into something extraordinary," she notes.

3. **Comfortable Footwear**: Given the extensive walking involved in navigating the festival, comfortable yet stylish footwear is a must. "Opt for shoes that offer both support and style. You want to look fabulous while also being able to enjoy the festival without discomfort," Malakpour emphasizes.

4. **Weather Considerations**: The weather in Cannes can be unpredictable, so Malakpour advises packing layers. "A lightweight jacket or wrap can be a lifesaver for cooler evenings," she says, highlighting the importance of being prepared for varying temperatures.

5. **Personal Touch**: Finally, Malakpour encourages attendees to infuse their personal style into their festival wardrobe. "Cannes is a celebration of creativity and individuality. Don’t shy away from showcasing your unique fashion sense," she concludes.

### Conclusion

As the Cannes Film Festival draws near, Maryam Malakpour's expert packing advice serves as a valuable resource for those looking to make a lasting impression on the Croisette. By focusing on versatility, comfort, and personal style, attendees can ensure they are not only fashionable but also fully prepared to enjoy the festivities that Cannes has to offer. Whether walking the red carpet or attending exclusive parties, the right wardrobe can enhance the overall experience of this iconic event.
