## Nepali Drama ‘Shakti’ to Premiere on Prime Video in U.S. and U.K.

The Nepali film industry is set to gain international visibility with the upcoming release of "Shakti," a narrative feature that marks the directorial debut of filmmaker Nani Sahra Walker. The film is scheduled to premiere on Prime Video on May 22, 2024, across both the United States and the United Kingdom.

### Overview of 'Shakti'

"Shakti" is a poignant drama that unfolds in the vibrant city of Kathmandu. The narrative centers around a mother’s desperate efforts to protect her nine-year-old daughter from unforeseen challenges. While specific plot details remain under wraps, the film is expected to explore themes of resilience, familial bonds, and the complexities of motherhood in a rapidly changing society.

### Distribution and Marketing

The film's distribution is being managed by Buffalo 8, a company known for its focus on independent films. Ahead of the launch, a trailer for "Shakti" has been released, providing a glimpse into the film's emotional depth and visual storytelling. The trailer aims to generate interest and anticipation among audiences in both the U.S. and U.K., highlighting the film's unique cultural perspective.

### Significance of the Release

The debut of "Shakti" on a global platform like Prime Video signifies a growing trend of international recognition for Nepali cinema. This film not only showcases the talent of local filmmakers but also offers viewers outside Nepal an opportunity to engage with stories that reflect the country's rich cultural tapestry.

As streaming platforms continue to expand their offerings, films like "Shakti" play a crucial role in diversifying content and promoting cross-cultural understanding. The film industry in Nepal has been evolving, and this release could pave the way for more Nepali films to reach wider audiences.
