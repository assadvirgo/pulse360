## Nia Long and Jaafar Jackson Celebrate Success of 'Michael' at 2026 BET Awards

At the recently concluded 2026 BET Awards, actor Nia Long and rising star Jaafar Jackson took to the stage to present the first award of the evening, marking a significant moment in their careers as they celebrated the success of their film, "Michael." The film, which chronicles the life of the legendary pop icon Michael Jackson, has garnered considerable attention and acclaim since its release.

Jaafar Jackson, the nephew of Michael Jackson, expressed his gratitude to the audience for their overwhelming support. "We’re so grateful for the love you all showed to our movie," he stated, eliciting a warm round of applause from attendees. His heartfelt acknowledgment of the film's success resonated with many, reflecting the strong connection and legacy that the Jackson family holds in the entertainment industry.

Nia Long, an acclaimed actress known for her versatile roles, praised Jaafar for his remarkable performance in the film. She remarked, "It was a joy watching you transform into your uncle right before our eyes." This statement not only highlights Jaafar's dedication to embodying the essence of his famous uncle but also underscores the emotional weight carried by the project. The film's portrayal of Michael Jackson's life aims to provide a nuanced perspective on both his artistry and personal struggles, making it a significant cinematic endeavor.

As Jaafar Jackson continues to carve out his own identity as an artist, his performance in "Michael" has been a pivotal moment in his career. With Nia Long's support and mentorship, he is poised to make a significant mark in the industry.

As the night progressed, the atmosphere at the BET Awards was filled with celebration and appreciation for the arts. The success of "Michael" reflects not only the enduring legacy of the Jackson family but also the importance of storytelling in capturing the complexities of human experience.
