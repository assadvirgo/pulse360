This recognition comes as Efira continues to gain acclaim for her performances, particularly following her recent appearances at the Cannes Film Festival.

Virginie Efira, who was born in Belgium and is now based in France, has made significant strides in the film industry over the past few years. The films in question are Ryusuke Hamaguchi's "All of a Sudden" and Asghar Farhadi's "Parallel Tales," both of which have received critical acclaim.
