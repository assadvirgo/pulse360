## ‘Baywatch’ Reboot Welcomes Back Erika Eleniak as Shauni McClain

The iconic television series "Baywatch," known for its dramatic lifeguard rescues and beachside camaraderie, is set to make a return on Fox, and it has just announced a familiar face from its original run. Erika Eleniak, who portrayed the beloved character Shauni McClain in the show's early seasons, will reprise her role in a guest-starring capacity.

### A Nostalgic Return

Eleniak's involvement in the reboot is a significant nod to the show's legacy, as she played Shauni McClain during the first two seasons of "Baywatch," which originally aired from 1989 to 1990. Her character was a fan favorite, known for her dedication to lifeguarding and her relationships with fellow lifeguards. After her departure at the beginning of Season 3, Eleniak's character became a part of the show's history, leaving a lasting impression on viewers.

In the upcoming reboot, Shauni McClain has transitioned from the sandy shores of lifeguarding to the political arena, now serving as a city councilwoman in Santa Monica. This character evolution reflects a broader trend in television, where characters are often reimagined to fit contemporary narratives and societal roles.
