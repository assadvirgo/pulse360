## SAG-AFTRA Launches ‘I Am An Actor’ Podcast Featuring Prominent Guests

The Screen Actors Guild-American Federation of Television and Radio Artists (SAG-AFTRA) has officially launched a new podcast titled "I Am An Actor," aiming to provide a platform for actors to share their personal stories and experiences within the industry. The inaugural episodes feature a lineup of well-known personalities, including Quinta Brunson, Kate Hudson, Mariska Hargitay, and Kerry Washington, who will delve into their journeys and the challenges they have faced throughout their careers.

### A New Avenue for Storytelling

"I Am An Actor" seeks to capture the essence of the actor's journey, offering listeners an intimate look at the lives of those who work in the entertainment industry. The podcast is inspired by a tradition at the SAG Awards, where actors share their origin stories from their seats during the ceremony. These speeches, often filled with humor, emotion, and personal anecdotes, have become a cherished part of the awards experience. The podcast aims to extend this tradition, allowing for deeper conversations and reflections on the craft.

### Engaging Conversations with Industry Icons

The first episodes of "I Am An Actor" will feature conversations with notable figures in the acting community. Quinta Brunson, known for her work on the hit series "Abbott Elementary," will share her unique perspective on breaking into the industry and her rise to fame. Kate Hudson, an Academy Award-nominated actress, will discuss her family legacy in Hollywood and the impact of her upbringing on her career choices.

Mariska Hargitay, celebrated for her long-running role on "Law & Order: Special Victims Unit," will provide insights into her advocacy work and the importance of representation in the media. Kerry Washington, an acclaimed actress and producer, will reflect on her experiences navigating the complexities of the entertainment landscape and her commitment to social justice.

### A Commitment to Authenticity

The podcast is designed to foster authentic conversations, allowing actors to express their true selves and share the realities of their profession. SAG-AFTRA emphasizes the importance of storytelling in the acting community, and "I Am An Actor" aims to highlight the diverse experiences of its members. By providing a platform for these discussions, the podcast hopes to inspire both aspiring actors and audiences alike.

### Looking Ahead

As the entertainment industry continues to evolve, "I Am An Actor" represents a significant step in creating a dialogue around the challenges and triumphs faced by actors. The podcast not only serves as a source of inspiration but also as a reminder of the shared experiences that unite those in the profession.

Listeners can expect new episodes to be released regularly, featuring a variety of guests from different backgrounds and genres within the acting world. The initiative reflects SAG-AFTRA's ongoing commitment to supporting its members and fostering a sense of community within the industry.

With its engaging content and focus on personal narratives, "I Am An Actor" is poised to become a valuable resource for both actors and fans, celebrating the art of acting and the stories that shape it.
