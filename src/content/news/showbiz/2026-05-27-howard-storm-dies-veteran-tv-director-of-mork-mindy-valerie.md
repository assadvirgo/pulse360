## Howard Storm, Veteran TV Director, Passes Away at 94

Howard Storm, a distinguished television director known for his extensive work on iconic shows such as *Mork & Mindy*, *Rhoda*, and *Valerie*, has passed away at the age of 94. His death occurred on May 26, marking the end of a prolific career that spanned several decades in the entertainment industry.

The show, which aired from 1978 to 1982, became a cultural phenomenon and is often credited with helping to launch Williams' illustrious career. Storm's direction contributed significantly to the show's unique blend of comedy and heartfelt moments, establishing it as a classic in American television history.

In addition to *Mork & Mindy*, Storm directed episodes of several other popular series, including *ALF*, *Full House*, *Head of the Class*, *Major Dad*, and *Perfect Strangers*. His versatility and ability to work across various genres made him a sought-after director during the peak of his career.
