## Mubi Acquires North American Rights to Lukas Dhont’s ‘Coward’

In a significant move within the film industry, Mubi has secured the North American distribution rights for Lukas Dhont's latest film, "Coward." The acquisition comes on the heels of the film's world premiere at the prestigious Cannes Film Festival, where it received an impressive 13-minute standing ovation from the audience.

## Overview of ‘Coward’

"Coward" is a queer romantic drama that explores the complexities of identity and courage through the eyes of its protagonist, Pierre. The narrative centers around Pierre, a soldier who has just arrived at the frontlines and is eager to establish his place among his peers. The film delves into themes of bravery, vulnerability, and the search for acceptance, making it a poignant addition to contemporary cinema.

Lukas Dhont, known for his previous works that often explore LGBTQ+ themes, has garnered critical acclaim for his ability to convey deep emotional narratives. His directorial style combines a keen understanding of character development with a visually striking aesthetic, which has resonated with audiences and critics alike.

## Reception at Cannes

The film's premiere at Cannes was met with enthusiasm, highlighted by the lengthy standing ovation that underscored the audience's appreciation for Dhont's storytelling. Such a response at one of the world's most prestigious film festivals is indicative of the film's potential impact and relevance in today's cultural landscape.

## Mubi's Role in Independent Cinema

Mubi, a global streaming service and film distributor, has made a name for itself by championing independent and international cinema. The platform's focus on curating a diverse selection of films aligns with the themes presented in "Coward," making it a fitting choice for its North American release. Mubi's acquisition of the film reflects its commitment to bringing unique and thought-provoking stories to a broader audience.

While specific release plans for "Coward" in North America have yet to be announced, the anticipation surrounding the film is palpable.
