## USMNT Sets FIFA World Cup English-Language Telecast Record

In a historic moment for American soccer, the U.S. Men's National Team (USMNT) achieved a significant milestone during their opening match at the 2026 FIFA World Cup. The game, which took place at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles, attracted an impressive 15.986 million viewers across various platforms, including Fox, Fox One, and Tubi. This remarkable figure marks the highest viewership for any USMNT World Cup match broadcast in English.

### A New Benchmark for Soccer in the U.S.

The record-setting viewership underscores the growing popularity of soccer in the United States, particularly as the nation co-hosts the World Cup alongside Canada and Mexico. The match not only captivated fans but also highlighted the increasing engagement with the sport, which has seen a surge in interest over the past decade. 

Fox Sports, which aired the match, confirmed that the audience numbers reflect a significant achievement for soccer in the country. The previous record for a USMNT World Cup telecast was eclipsed, demonstrating the potential for soccer to draw large audiences in the U.S. sports landscape, traditionally dominated by American football, basketball, and baseball.
