## Winners Announced for 2026 Rockie Awards at Banff World Media Festival

The Banff World Media Festival has revealed the winners of the 2026 Rockie Awards International Program Competition, celebrating excellence in global television and digital content. The announcement was made on Tuesday during a ceremony hosted by Allana Harkin, known for her work on "Full Frontal With Samantha Bee," at the Fairmont Banff Springs Hotel in Banff, Alberta.

### Celebrating Diverse Talent

Among the notable winners this year are "The Pitt," "The Simpsons," "The Chair Company," and "Mountainhead." Each of these programs has made a significant impact in their respective genres, showcasing creativity and storytelling that resonates with audiences worldwide.

"The Pitt," a drama series that has garnered critical acclaim, continues to captivate viewers with its intricate plot and character development. Meanwhile, "The Simpsons," a long-standing animated series, remains a cultural touchstone, recognized for its satirical take on contemporary society. "The Chair Company," which has been praised for its innovative approach to storytelling, adds to the diverse lineup of winners this year. Lastly, "Mountainhead," a gripping narrative that explores themes of survival and human connection, has also been acknowledged for its artistic merit.
