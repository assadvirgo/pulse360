## Carrie Preston Discusses the Challenges of Playing Elsbeth in CBS Series

In a recent interview, actress Carrie Preston opened up about her role as Elsbeth on CBS's new series "Elsbeth," highlighting the character's unique quirk of carrying multiple bags to crime scenes. While these bags have become a defining characteristic of Elsbeth, they also pose a significant physical challenge for Preston.

### The Role of the Bags

Preston explained that the bags are not merely props; they are integral to the character's identity. Each bag serves a purpose, reflecting Elsbeth's personality and her approach to solving crimes. "The bags are key to the character," Preston stated during her appearance on Variety's Awards Circuit Podcast. She emphasized that they add depth to Elsbeth, showcasing her resourcefulness and quirky nature.

### Physical Toll on the Actress

However, the weight of the bags has become a point of concern for Preston. Carrying them around on set has led to some unexpected physical strain. "It's a bit of a heavy lift — pun intended," she remarked, indicating that the sheer number of bags and their contents can be cumbersome. Crew members have noted the toll it takes on her, and Preston humorously suggested that they might have some stories to tell about her struggles off-camera.

### Balancing Character and Comfort

Despite the physical challenges, Preston remains committed to bringing Elsbeth to life authentically. She understands that the bags contribute to the overall narrative and character development. As the series progresses, viewers can expect to see how Elsbeth's quirks, including her bags, play a role in her interactions and problem-solving abilities.

### Conclusion

Carrie Preston's insights into her character Elsbeth provide a fascinating glimpse into the creative process behind the series. The bags, while a source of physical difficulty, are emblematic of the character's complexity and charm. As "Elsbeth" continues to unfold, audiences will likely appreciate the layers that Preston brings to her role, despite the added weight on her shoulders.
