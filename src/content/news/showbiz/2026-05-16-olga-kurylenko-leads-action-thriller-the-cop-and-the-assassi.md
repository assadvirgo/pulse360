## Olga Kurylenko to Star in Action Thriller ‘The Cop and the Assassin’

All Rights Entertainment has announced its involvement in the international sales of the upcoming action thriller, "The Cop and the Assassin." The film, which features acclaimed actress Olga Kurylenko, is set to begin production in Thailand this November.

### A Promising Cast

Olga Kurylenko, known for her roles in major films such as "Black Widow," "Quantum of Solace," "Oblivion," and "Hitman," will lead the cast as a former SAS operative turned assassin. Her character is on a mission to track down a formidable crime boss, a role that promises to showcase Kurylenko's action-oriented talents and depth as an actress. 

Joining Kurylenko in this thrilling narrative is Nina Bergman, who is also set to deliver a compelling performance. Bergman's involvement adds to the film's appeal, as she has built a reputation for her roles in various action films and television series.

### Production Details

The choice of Thailand as the filming location is notable, given the country's diverse landscapes and vibrant urban settings, which can enhance the film's action sequences and overall aesthetic. The production team is expected to leverage Thailand's unique environment to create a visually engaging experience for audiences.

### Market Implications

The announcement of "The Cop and the Assassin" comes at a time when action thrillers are gaining traction in the global film market. With Kurylenko's established fan base and the film's promising premise, All Rights Entertainment is optimistic about the project's commercial potential. The involvement of experienced actors and the strategic choice of location suggest that the film aims to appeal to both domestic and international audiences.
