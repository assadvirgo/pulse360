## New Animated Feature 'Nina and the Goddess of Thunder' Secures French Distribution Rights

In a notable development for the animation sector, New Europe Film Sales has announced the commencement of presales for its upcoming 3D animated film, "Nina and the Goddess of Thunder." The film, which boasts a production budget of €7.5 million (approximately $8.77 million), is poised to capture the attention of family audiences with its engaging storyline and vibrant animation.

### French Rights Acquired by KMBO

In a significant move, KMBO, a prominent distributor known for its successful track record in the animation genre, has acquired the French distribution rights for "Nina and the Goddess of Thunder." KMBO has previously released several notable animated features, including "Ozi: Voice of the Forest" and "10 Lives," establishing itself as a key player in the market for family-friendly content in France.

The acquisition of "Nina and the Goddess of Thunder" aligns with KMBO's strategy to enhance its portfolio with high-quality animated films that appeal to both children and their families. The film's vibrant visuals and compelling narrative are expected to resonate well with audiences, further solidifying KMBO's position in the competitive animation distribution landscape.

### About the Film

"Nina and the Goddess of Thunder" is set to be a captivating journey that combines elements of adventure and fantasy. While specific plot details remain under wraps, the film is anticipated to explore themes of courage, friendship, and the importance of nature, making it not only entertaining but also educational for younger viewers.

The production team behind the film has emphasized a commitment to high-quality animation, aiming to deliver an experience that stands out in a crowded marketplace. With a budget of €7.5 million, the film is expected to leverage cutting-edge animation technology to create a visually stunning experience.
