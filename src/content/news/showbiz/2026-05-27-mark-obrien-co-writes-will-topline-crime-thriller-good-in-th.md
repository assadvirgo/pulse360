## Mark O’Brien to Star in Indie Crime Thriller 'Good in the Room'

In an exciting development for the independent film scene, actor Mark O’Brien is set to star in the upcoming crime thriller "Good in the Room." O’Brien, known for his role in the hit film "Ready or Not," has also co-written the screenplay for the film alongside Canadian filmmaker Pat Kiely, who will take the helm as director.

### A Promising Collaboration

The collaboration between O’Brien and Kiely marks a significant step in their respective careers. O’Brien's experience in front of the camera, combined with his burgeoning talent as a screenwriter, positions him as a versatile figure in the industry. Kiely, recognized for his unique storytelling style, is expected to bring a fresh perspective to the crime thriller genre.

### An Ensemble Cast

Joining O’Brien in "Good in the Room" is a talented ensemble cast that includes Laysla De Oliveira, Andrew "King Bach" Bachelor, and Eric Bruneau. De Oliveira, who has garnered attention for her role in the series "Lioness," is set to bring depth to the narrative. Bachelor, widely known for his comedic roles, is anticipated to add a unique flavor to the film, while Bruneau, recognized for his work in "Fanny," rounds out the cast with his compelling performances.

### The Storyline

While specific plot details remain under wraps, "Good in the Room" promises to delve into the intricate dynamics of crime and morality.
