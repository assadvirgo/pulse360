## Mariana van Zeller Transitions to New Ventures Following 'Trafficked'

Mariana van Zeller, the acclaimed journalist known for her groundbreaking work on the National Geographic series "Trafficked With Mariana van Zeller," has embarked on a new journey as she concludes her award-winning series. After a successful run that garnered multiple prestigious accolades, including an Emmy, Peabody, and DuPont Award, van Zeller is set to launch her independent production company, The Hidden Third.

### A New Chapter

The announcement of The Hidden Third comes as van Zeller reflects on her experiences during her tenure with "Trafficked," which concluded its final season in September 2023. The series, which explored the complexities of global trafficking networks, was praised for its in-depth reporting and compelling storytelling. It not only highlighted the darker sides of human trade but also brought to light the resilience of individuals affected by these issues.

Van Zeller's transition to an independent media company signifies her commitment to continue producing impactful journalism. The Hidden Third aims to focus on stories that often go untold, shining a light on marginalized communities and pressing social issues. In a statement, van Zeller expressed her enthusiasm for this new venture, emphasizing the importance of storytelling in fostering understanding and change.

### The Vision Behind The Hidden Third

The Hidden Third is envisioned as a platform for innovative storytelling that prioritizes authenticity and depth. Van Zeller aims to collaborate with a diverse range of creators and journalists, allowing for a multiplicity of voices and perspectives. This initiative reflects a growing trend in the media landscape, where independent production companies are increasingly seen as vital players in delivering quality content that resonates with audiences.

Muck Media, a company known for its support of independent creators, is partnering with van Zeller in this endeavor. The collaboration is expected to enhance the reach and impact of The Hidden Third, leveraging Muck Media's expertise in production and distribution.
