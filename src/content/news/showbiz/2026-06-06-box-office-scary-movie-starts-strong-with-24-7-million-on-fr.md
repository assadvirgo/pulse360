The latest installment of the beloved parody franchise, "Scary Movie 6," has made a significant impact at the box office, earning an impressive $24.7 million on its opening day, Friday. The film, produced by Paramount and Miramax, is the first to be directed by the Wayans brothers since 2001's "Scary Movie 2," bringing back the original comedic style that fans have come to love.
