The award ceremony was marked by a sense of excitement and anticipation, as filmmakers and audiences gathered to recognize outstanding contributions to the short film medium. This year, the festival awarded a total of $30,000 in cash prizes to honorees, reflecting its commitment to supporting emerging talent in the film industry.
