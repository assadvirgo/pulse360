## Evangeline Lilly Joins 'Farmacy of Light' as Narrator and Executive Producer

Evangeline Lilly, renowned for her roles in both film and television, has taken on a new venture in the documentary space. She will serve as the narrator and executive producer for the feature documentary titled *Farmacy of Light*. This announcement comes after the film's successful run at various film festivals last year, where it garnered critical acclaim and recognition.

### Overview of 'Farmacy of Light'

*Farmacy of Light* explores themes surrounding health, wellness, and the interconnectedness of nature and humanity. The documentary features Erik Cutter, known for his work in *The Need to Grow*, who is central to the narrative. The film aims to inspire viewers to consider alternative approaches to health and well-being, emphasizing the importance of natural remedies and holistic practices.

The documentary has been updated with Lilly's narration, which is expected to enhance the storytelling and provide a more engaging experience for the audience. Her involvement is seen as a significant boost for the film, given her popularity and the respect she commands in the entertainment industry.

### Release Details

The final version of *Farmacy of Light* will be available for global streaming from June 23 to June 27. This free streaming initiative aims to make the film accessible to a wider audience, allowing viewers from various backgrounds to engage with its message. The decision to offer the documentary at no cost reflects a commitment to promoting awareness around health and wellness topics that are often overlooked in mainstream media.

### Impact and Reception

The documentary's festival run last year was marked by several awards, indicating a positive reception from audiences and critics alike. Lilly's participation is anticipated to further elevate the film's profile, potentially attracting a larger viewership. The combination of her star power and the film's compelling subject matter may lead to meaningful discussions about health practices and the role of nature in our lives.

### Conclusion

Evangeline Lilly's role in *Farmacy of Light* represents a significant intersection of entertainment and advocacy, as the film seeks to educate and inspire viewers about holistic health approaches. With its upcoming release, the documentary promises to contribute to ongoing conversations about wellness and the importance of reconnecting with nature. As the streaming dates approach, audiences can look forward to an enlightening experience that blends Lilly's narrative talent with a powerful message.
