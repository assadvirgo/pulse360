## Rose Leslie, Andreas Pietschmann, and Daniel Zovatto Join Russell Crowe in ‘The Last Druid’

In an exciting development for cinema enthusiasts, actors Rose Leslie, Andreas Pietschmann, and Daniel Zovatto have been confirmed to join Oscar-winning actor Russell Crowe in the upcoming action-drama film, *The Last Druid*. The film is set to commence production on June 8, 2024, in scenic locations across Barcelona and the Canary Islands, Spain.

### A Star-Studded Cast

Rose Leslie, known for her iconic role as Ygritte in HBO's *Game of Thrones*, brings her talent and charisma to the project. Leslie has gained acclaim for her performances across various genres, making her a valuable addition to the cast. Similarly, Andreas Pietschmann, recognized for his role in the German series *Nuremberg*, and Daniel Zovatto, who has garnered attention for his performances in *Don’t Breathe* and other films, are expected to contribute significantly to the film's narrative.

Russell Crowe, who is no stranger to epic roles, is set to portray a character that once again takes him into the realm of historical drama. Crowe is best known for his portrayal of Maximus Decimus Meridius in Ridley Scott's *Gladiator*, a role that earned him an Academy Award and solidified his status as a leading actor in Hollywood. In *The Last Druid*, he will once again find himself at odds with the Roman Empire, a theme that resonates with audiences familiar with his earlier work.

### Production Details

*The Last Druid* is anticipated to combine elements of action and drama, weaving a narrative that explores themes of heroism, conflict, and mythology. The choice of filming locations in Spain offers a rich backdrop, with the historical architecture and picturesque landscapes providing an authentic setting for the story. The production is expected to attract significant attention at the Cannes Market, where it will be showcased to potential distributors and investors.
