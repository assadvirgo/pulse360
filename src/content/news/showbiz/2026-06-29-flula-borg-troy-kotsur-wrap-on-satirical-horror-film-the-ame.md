## Flula Borg and Troy Kotsur Complete Filming on ‘The American Winner’

Flula Borg, known for his role in the "Pitch Perfect" franchise, and Academy Award winner Troy Kotsur, celebrated for his performance in "CODA," have recently concluded filming for their latest project, "The American Winner." This satirical horror film marks the debut feature of Iranian writer-director Farbod Ardebili and was shot in Los Angeles.

## A Unique Narrative

"The American Winner" presents a compelling narrative centered around a megalomaniacal tech billionaire, portrayed by Borg, who manipulates financial markets from the confines of his lavish residence. The film promises to blend elements of satire and horror, offering a critical commentary on contemporary issues surrounding wealth, power, and technology. 

Farbod Ardebili, who has previously garnered attention for his work in short films, aims to explore the darker aspects of ambition and greed through this unique lens. The choice of a tech billionaire as the protagonist reflects a growing societal concern regarding the influence of powerful individuals in shaping economic landscapes and public opinion.

## A Promising Collaboration

The collaboration between Borg and Kotsur is particularly noteworthy. Borg's comedic background juxtaposed with Kotsur's dramatic prowess creates a dynamic that may enhance the film's exploration of its themes. Kotsur's Academy Award win for Best Supporting Actor has positioned him as a significant figure in the industry, and his involvement in this project is expected to draw attention and acclaim.

The film is produced by Bad Grey and Plain Text, two production companies known for their commitment to innovative storytelling. Their involvement suggests a focus on quality and creativity, which may resonate with audiences seeking fresh perspectives in the horror genre.

While specific details regarding the release date and distribution of "The American Winner" have yet to be announced, the film's unique premise and the talent involved have already generated considerable buzz within the industry.
