## Eleventh Hour Films Develops New Environmental Legal Drama

Eleventh Hour Films is set to expand its portfolio with a new legal drama inspired by the book "A Barrister for the Earth," authored by eco-lawyer Monica Feria-Tinta. The project will be developed by Victoria Asare-Archer, known for her work on popular Netflix series such as "Missing You" and "Stay Close."

### A Unique Perspective on Environmental Law

The upcoming series aims to shed light on the intersection of environmental issues and the legal system, drawing from Feria-Tinta's experiences and insights as an environmental barrister. The narrative will likely explore the complexities of legal battles surrounding climate change, conservation, and ecological justice, providing a platform for critical discussions on pressing environmental challenges.

### Victoria Asare-Archer's Proven Track Record

Victoria Asare-Archer brings a wealth of experience to the project. She served as the lead writer and executive producer for Netflix's "Missing You," which garnered attention for its engaging storytelling and character development. Her previous work on "Stay Close" and contributions to the Emmy-nominated VR game "Doctor Who: The Runaway" further establish her as a versatile and skilled writer in the entertainment industry.

Asare-Archer's involvement signals a commitment to creating content that not only entertains but also informs and inspires audiences about significant social issues. Her ability to weave compelling narratives with relevant themes will be crucial in bringing the story of environmental law to life.
