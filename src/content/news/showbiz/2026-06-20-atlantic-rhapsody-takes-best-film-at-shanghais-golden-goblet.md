## ‘Atlantic Rhapsody’ Wins Best Film at Shanghai’s Golden Goblet Awards

The 28th Golden Goblet Awards, held during the Shanghai International Film Festival, concluded on June 20, 2023, with China's "Atlantic Rhapsody" taking home the prestigious award for Best Feature Film. Directed by Zhong Kaifeng, this debut feature has garnered significant attention not only for its storytelling but also for its visual artistry, as it also secured the award for Best Cinematography, awarded to Hao Jiayue.

### Celebrating Global Cinema

The Golden Goblet Awards are known for their commitment to recognizing and celebrating cinematic achievements from around the world. This year’s ceremony showcased a diverse array of films, with entries spanning various genres and countries. The awards serve as a platform for filmmakers to gain international recognition and foster cultural exchange through the medium of film.

"Atlantic Rhapsody" stood out among the competition, impressing both the jury and the audience with its engaging narrative and stunning visuals.
