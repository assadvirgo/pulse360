## French Tech Entrepreneur Adrien Nussenbaum Launches Palatio Films at Cannes

Adrien Nussenbaum, a prominent figure in the French tech landscape, is making a significant transition from the world of e-commerce to film production. The co-founder of Mirakl, a leading e-commerce platform, has officially launched Palatio Films, a new production company that is set to make its debut at the prestigious Cannes Film Festival. 

### A New Chapter in Film Production

Palatio Films will premiere its first project, "When Night Falls," a World War II drama directed by acclaimed French actor Daniel Auteuil. This film marks a notable pivot in Nussenbaum's career, as he shifts his focus from technology to the arts. Known for his innovative contributions to the e-commerce sector, Nussenbaum's foray into the film industry reflects a broader trend of entrepreneurs diversifying their portfolios into creative fields.

### "When Night Falls" at Cannes

The film "When Night Falls" is anticipated to garner attention not only for its artistic merit but also for its historical significance. Set against the backdrop of World War II, the narrative promises to explore themes of resilience and human spirit during one of history's most tumultuous periods. Auteuil, who has a storied career in both acting and directing, brings a wealth of experience to this project, which is expected to resonate with audiences at Cannes.

### Nussenbaum's Vision for Palatio Films

In a statement regarding the launch of Palatio Films, Nussenbaum expressed his enthusiasm for storytelling and the potential of cinema to impact society. He emphasized the importance of creating films that not only entertain but also provoke thought and discussion. His vision for the company includes a commitment to producing high-quality content that reflects diverse narratives and perspectives.
