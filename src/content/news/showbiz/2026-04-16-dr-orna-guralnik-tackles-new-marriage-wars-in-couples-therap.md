In the upcoming season, Dr. Guralnik will guide these couples through their crises, offering insights and therapeutic techniques to help them navigate the complexities of their relationships. The show's premise revolves around the intimate and often raw discussions that occur during therapy sessions, providing viewers with a unique lens into the struggles and triumphs of modern love.

The new season promises to delve deeper into the emotional landscapes of the participants, showcasing not only their conflicts but also the potential for growth and healing. Each couple's journey will be documented, allowing audiences to witness the transformative power of therapy in real-time.
