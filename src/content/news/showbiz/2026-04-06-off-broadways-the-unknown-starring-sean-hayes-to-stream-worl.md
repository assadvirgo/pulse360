## Off Broadway’s ‘The Unknown’ Starring Sean Hayes to Stream Worldwide

In a significant development for theatre enthusiasts and fans of Sean Hayes, the Off Broadway thriller "The Unknown," written by David Cale, will be available for worldwide streaming during its final weekend performances. This decision comes on the heels of the production receiving a nomination for the prestigious Lucille Lortel Award, which recognizes outstanding achievements in Off Broadway theatre.

### Streaming Details

The live broadcasts of "The Unknown" will be captured and streamed at specific curtain times: Friday, April 10 at 8 PM and Saturday, April 11 at 2 PM. This initiative allows audiences who may not have the opportunity to attend the live performances in person to experience the gripping narrative and powerful performances from the comfort of their homes.

### About the Production

"The Unknown" features Sean Hayes, best known for his role in the television series "Will & Grace," in a captivating performance that has garnered critical acclaim. The play, penned by David Cale, is a thriller that explores complex themes and character dynamics, drawing audiences into a world of suspense and intrigue. Cale’s writing has been praised for its depth and ability to engage viewers, making this production a noteworthy addition to the Off Broadway scene.

### Recognition and Impact

The nomination for the Lucille Lortel Award is a testament to the quality and impact of "The Unknown." This award, named after the influential producer Lucille Lortel, celebrates excellence in Off Broadway theatre and serves to highlight productions that push boundaries and offer innovative storytelling. The recognition not only elevates the status of the play but also enhances the visibility of Off Broadway productions as a whole.

### Conclusion

As "The Unknown" prepares to conclude its run, the opportunity for global audiences to engage with the production through streaming is a significant step in making theatre more accessible. This initiative reflects a growing trend in the performing arts, where live performances are increasingly being shared with wider audiences through digital platforms. For those interested in experiencing a thrilling narrative brought to life by a talented cast, the upcoming streaming dates present a unique opportunity to witness this acclaimed production.
