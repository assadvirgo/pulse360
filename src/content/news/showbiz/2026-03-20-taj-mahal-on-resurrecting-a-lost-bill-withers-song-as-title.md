## Taj Mahal to Release New Album 'Time' Featuring a Resurrected Bill Withers Song

Taj Mahal, the renowned blues-folk-soul-reggae artist, has announced the release of his latest album titled "Time," set to debut on May 1. This album marks a significant milestone in Mahal's extensive career, which has spanned over six decades and includes more than 60 releases. 

### A Tribute to the Passage of Time

The title "Time" encapsulates a theme that resonates deeply with Mahal, who at 84 years old, reflects on his long and storied journey in the music industry. In an exclusive interview with Variety, Mahal described his career as "an ongoing freight train," highlighting the continuous evolution of his artistry and the experiences that have shaped him as a musician. 

Mahal's ability to address the concept of time is not just a reflection of his age but also a testament to the depth of his musical exploration. The album is expected to delve into the nuances of life, memory, and the passage of time, themes that are particularly poignant given Mahal's extensive experience in the industry.

### Reviving a Classic: A Bill Withers Song

One of the standout features of "Time" is the inclusion of a previously unreleased song by the late Bill Withers, a legendary figure in American music known for his soulful hits like "Lean on Me" and "Ain't No Sunshine." Mahal's decision to resurrect this song as the title track underscores his commitment to honoring the legacy of fellow musicians while also bringing fresh interpretations to classic works.

Mahal's collaboration with the Withers estate to bring this song to light demonstrates his respect for the craft and the importance of preserving musical history. This endeavor not only enriches Mahal's album but also serves to introduce Withers' timeless artistry to a new generation of listeners.

### A Career Built on Passion

Throughout his career, Taj Mahal has been celebrated for his eclectic blend of musical styles, drawing from blues, folk, soul, and reggae influences. His ability to weave these genres together has garnered him a diverse fan base and critical acclaim. Mahal's commitment to his craft is evident in his assertion that he is "in it for the duration," suggesting a dedication to continue creating and performing for as long as he is able.

As he prepares for the release of "Time," Mahal's reflections on his career and the music he has created resonate with both longtime fans and newcomers alike. His work serves as a reminder of the power of music to transcend time and connect generations.

### Looking Ahead

With the release of "Time" on the horizon, fans can anticipate a rich tapestry of sound and storytelling that reflects Mahal's unique perspective on life and art. As he continues to forge ahead in his musical journey, Taj Mahal remains a vital figure in the landscape of contemporary music, proving that the passage of time only enhances the depth and richness of artistic expression.

In conclusion, "Time" promises to be a significant addition to Taj Mahal's illustrious discography, celebrating both his legacy and the enduring influence of Bill Withers. As the album's release date approaches, anticipation builds for what is sure to be another remarkable chapter in Mahal's ongoing musical saga.
