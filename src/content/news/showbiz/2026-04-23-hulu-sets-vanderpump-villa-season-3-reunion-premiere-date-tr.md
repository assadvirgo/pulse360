## Hulu Announces Premiere Date for 'Vanderpump Villa' Season 3 Reunion

Hulu has officially announced the premiere date for the highly anticipated reunion special of "Vanderpump Villa" Season 3. Set to air on April 30, the reunion will delve into the various scandals and dramatic moments that have unfolded throughout the season. 

### Trailer Release and Highlights

In conjunction with the announcement, Hulu has released a trailer that hints at some of the key moments viewers can expect. One of the most intriguing elements teased in the trailer is a "secret recording," which suggests that revelations and confrontations are on the horizon. The trailer also features Hannah Fouch, who is seen walking off set in tears, indicating that the reunion may bring heightened emotions and intense discussions among the cast.

### Hosts and Format

Stassi Schroeder will return as the host for this season's reunion, joining franchise matriarch Lisa Vanderpump. Together, they will guide the conversation as cast members reflect on the season's events and address unresolved tensions. The reunion format promises a mix of candid conversations, confrontations, and moments of reflection, which have become hallmarks of the series.

### Availability for Viewers

The reunion special will be available for streaming on Hulu and for subscribers of the Hulu on Disney+ bundle in the United States. This accessibility aims to cater to the show's dedicated fan base, ensuring that viewers can easily engage with the latest developments in the Vanderpump universe.

### Anticipation Among Fans

The announcement of the reunion has generated excitement among fans of the series, many of whom have followed the cast's journey through various ups and downs. The combination of returning hosts, the promise of shocking revelations, and the emotional stakes highlighted in the trailer are likely to draw significant viewership when the special airs.

As the premiere date approaches, fans are left speculating about the implications of the "secret recording" and what it might reveal about the relationships and dynamics within the cast. The reunion is expected to not only address the controversies of the season but also set the stage for future storylines.

### Conclusion

With the reunion special just weeks away, "Vanderpump Villa" continues to solidify its place in the reality television landscape. As viewers prepare for what promises to be a compelling and dramatic reunion, the anticipation surrounding the special underscores the show's enduring popularity and its ability to engage audiences with its blend of drama, humor, and heartfelt moments.
