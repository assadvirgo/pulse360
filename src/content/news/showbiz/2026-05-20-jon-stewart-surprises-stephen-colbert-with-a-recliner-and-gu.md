Jon Stewart, who previously hosted "The Daily Show" and has been a significant figure in late-night television, made a surprise appearance on Colbert's final episode. Their reunion was marked by a light-hearted exchange that showcased their longstanding friendship and mutual respect. Stewart presented Colbert with a recliner, a humorous nod to the comfort and ease that comes with years of hosting late-night television. 

The gesture was not just a gift but a symbolic representation of the camaraderie that exists among late-night hosts, often characterized by playful teasing and shared experiences in the industry. Day's performance not only captivated viewers but also underscored the theme of support and encouragement that has been a hallmark of Colbert's tenure.
