## Bam Margera Reflects on 'Jackass' Reunion and Upcoming Film

Bam Margera, a prominent figure in the "Jackass" franchise, has recently shared his thoughts on the group's latest film, "Jackass: Best & Last." Despite past conflicts with the crew, Margera has expressed his intention to support the film, although he remains realistic about the prospects of a reunion with his former co-stars.

### Acknowledging the Past

In 2021, Margera's relationship with the "Jackass" team became strained, leading to a public fallout that raised concerns about his future involvement in the franchise. However, in a recent statement, he clarified that he holds no resentment towards his former colleagues. "I don’t have any bad blood," Margera noted, indicating a sense of closure regarding their past disagreements.

### The Future of 'Jackass'

While Margera plans to watch "Jackass: Best & Last," he has made it clear that a reunion of the original cast is unlikely. "It's not going to happen," he stated, emphasizing the reality of their current circumstances. This sentiment reflects not only his personal journey but also the evolution of the franchise itself, which has seen various changes in its lineup over the years.

### Support for the Crew

Despite his absence from the latest project, Margera's support for the "Jackass" crew remains strong. His comments suggest that he values the experiences and memories shared with his co-stars, even as he navigates his own path. The camaraderie that characterized the early days of "Jackass" continues to resonate with Margera, even if he is not part of the current iteration.

### Looking Ahead

As "Jackass: Best & Last" prepares for its release, fans of the franchise are eager to see how the film encapsulates the spirit of the original series. Margera's decision to watch the film indicates a willingness to engage with the legacy of "Jackass," despite the complexities of his relationship with the crew.

In conclusion, Bam Margera's reflections on the "Jackass" franchise highlight a blend of nostalgia and realism. While he acknowledges the challenges of the past, his lack of animosity towards his former co-stars suggests a hopeful outlook for both himself and the franchise. As the film approaches its premiere, it remains to be seen how audiences will respond to this final chapter in the "Jackass" saga.
