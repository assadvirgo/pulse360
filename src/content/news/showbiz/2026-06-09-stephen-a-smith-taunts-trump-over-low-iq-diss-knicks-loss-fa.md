Smith has taken to social media to address former President Donald Trump, following the New York Knicks' disappointing loss at home in Game 3 of the NBA Finals. The tension between Smith and Trump appears to have been ignited by a series of comments made by Trump regarding the Knicks' performance. Smith stated, asserting that he would "put my IQ up against yours any day of the week." This bold claim underscores Smith's confidence in his intellectual capabilities, while also suggesting a deeper critique of Trump's public persona.
