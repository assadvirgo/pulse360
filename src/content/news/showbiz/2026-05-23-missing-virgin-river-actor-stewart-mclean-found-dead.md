The announcement was made on Friday, bringing a somber conclusion to the search for the actor, who had been reported missing earlier.

Stewart McLean was known for his role in the popular Netflix series "Virgin River," where he captivated audiences with his performances. McLean's disappearance raised concerns among fans and colleagues alike, prompting an extensive search effort.
