## Tak Sakaguchi Joins Iko Uwais in ‘Pendekar: Warrior’ as Nemesis

In a significant development for the global action film genre, Japanese cult action star Tak Sakaguchi has been cast as the primary antagonist in the upcoming martial arts feature, "Pendekar: Warrior." This film will not only showcase Sakaguchi's talents but also marks his first major role outside of Japan, indicating a potential expansion of his career into international cinema.

### A New Chapter for Sakaguchi

Tak Sakaguchi has built a reputation as a formidable presence in the action film landscape, particularly noted for his roles in cult classics that have garnered a dedicated following. His inclusion in "Pendekar: Warrior" is a noteworthy step, as it introduces him to a broader audience and allows him to collaborate with other international stars, including Indonesian action hero Iko Uwais. Uwais is best known for his roles in films like "The Raid" and "Merantau," which have received critical acclaim for their choreography and intense action sequences.

### The Film's Production Timeline

"Pendekar: Warrior" is poised to begin principal photography in the fourth quarter of 2026. The film aims to blend traditional martial arts with contemporary storytelling, a combination that has proven successful in similar projects. The involvement of Sakaguchi and Uwais suggests a strong emphasis on high-octane action and intricate fight choreography, appealing to fans of the genre.

### Anticipation and Expectations

The announcement of Sakaguchi's role has generated excitement among action film enthusiasts and industry insiders alike. With his extensive background in martial arts cinema, expectations are high for the dynamic between Sakaguchi and Uwais. Their on-screen rivalry is anticipated to be a central element of the film, potentially elevating the narrative and action sequences to new heights.

### Conclusion

As "Pendekar: Warrior" prepares to enter production, the collaboration between Tak Sakaguchi and Iko Uwais represents a promising fusion of talent from different cultural backgrounds.
