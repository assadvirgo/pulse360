## Annabelle Gurwitch to Be Honored at Gilda Gala Benefitting Cancer Support Community Los Angeles

The upcoming Gilda Gala, set to take place on May 28, will honor actress and author Annabelle Gurwitch for her contributions to the arts and her advocacy in cancer support. The event, which aims to raise funds for the Cancer Support Community Los Angeles (CSCLA), will feature a special award presentation by renowned comedian and former "Saturday Night Live" cast member Laraine Newman.

### A Celebration of Resilience and Community

The Gilda Gala is named in memory of comedian Gilda Radner, who was one of the original cast members of "SNL" and a beloved figure in the comedy world. Radner's legacy is celebrated through the gala, which seeks to provide support and resources to individuals and families affected by cancer. The Cancer Support Community Los Angeles is dedicated to ensuring that no one faces cancer alone, offering a variety of programs including support groups, educational workshops, and wellness activities.

Annabelle Gurwitch, known for her roles in television and film as well as her written works, has been a vocal advocate for cancer awareness and support. Her involvement in the gala underscores her commitment to helping those impacted by the disease. Gurwitch's work often reflects themes of resilience and community, making her an ideal honoree for this event.

### A Night of Inspiration and Fundraising

The Gilda Gala will feature a range of activities designed to engage attendees and raise funds for the CSCLA. In addition to the award presentation, the evening will include live entertainment, a silent auction, and opportunities for guests to connect with others who share a commitment to cancer support. The event aims to foster a sense of community and solidarity among attendees, while also generating essential resources for the organization's programs.

Laraine Newman, who will present the award to Gurwitch, is celebrated for her comedic talent and her contributions to the entertainment industry. Her presence at the gala adds a layer of excitement and recognition to the event, as she brings her own experiences and insights to the cause.

### Supporting Those Affected by Cancer

The funds raised during the Gilda Gala will directly benefit the Cancer Support Community Los Angeles, which provides vital services to individuals facing cancer. The organization emphasizes the importance of emotional and social support, recognizing that cancer affects not only the patient but also their loved ones. By attending the gala, participants will not only celebrate Gurwitch's achievements but also contribute to a cause that impacts countless lives.

As the date of the gala approaches, anticipation is building within the community. The event promises to be a night of inspiration, laughter, and hope, honoring the spirit of Gilda Radner while supporting those who continue to face the challenges of cancer.

In conclusion, the Gilda Gala represents a significant opportunity to unite individuals in the fight against cancer, celebrating the contributions of those like Annabelle Gurwitch who strive to make a difference. The event serves as a reminder of the power of community and the importance of supporting one another through difficult times.
