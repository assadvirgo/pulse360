## Zendaya Discusses Relationship with Tom Holland on "Modern Love" Podcast

In a recent episode of The New York Times' "Modern Love" podcast, actress Zendaya opened up about her relationship with fellow actor Tom Holland, revealing the depth of their bond and the comfort he brings to her life. The conversation, which also featured her "The Drama" co-star Robert Pattinson, highlighted Zendaya's reflections on how their connection began during their time auditioning for the "Spider-Man" franchise.

### A Unique Connection

Zendaya described Holland as "my person," emphasizing that she feels a sense of calmness when she is around him. This sentiment is particularly significant given the pressures and public scrutiny that come with being an A-list celebrity. The actress noted that her initial experiences with Holland during auditions played a crucial role in establishing their rapport. "He did make me feel calm," she stated, suggesting that their chemistry was evident even in the early stages of their professional relationship.

### The Impact of Public Attention

The discussion comes amidst heightened media interest in Zendaya and Holland's relationship, especially following comments made by Zendaya's stylist, Law Roach, at the Actor Awards in March. Roach hinted at the possibility of a wedding, stirring speculation about the couple's future. While Zendaya did not directly address these rumors, her remarks on the podcast indicate a strong emotional foundation between the two.

### Navigating Fame Together

Both Zendaya and Holland have experienced the challenges of navigating fame, particularly as young actors in the spotlight. Their shared experiences in the entertainment industry may contribute to their understanding of one another, allowing for a supportive relationship amid the chaos of public life. Zendaya's candid reflections on their bond highlight the importance of having a partner who can provide solace and stability.

### Conclusion

As Zendaya and Holland continue to capture public interest, their relationship serves as a reminder of the human side of celebrity life. The actress's insights on the "Modern Love" podcast reveal not just the affection she has for Holland but also the significance of emotional support in a world that often feels overwhelming. Their journey together, marked by both professional collaboration and personal connection, resonates with many fans who admire their work and their relationship.
