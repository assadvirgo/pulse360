## Mindy Cohn Shares Battle with Cancer: A Second Fight for Life

Mindy Cohn, the beloved actress known for her role in the iconic television series "The Facts of Life," has revealed that she has been battling cancer for the second time. The Emmy-nominated star disclosed her recent health struggles in a candid update, expressing her determination and resilience in the face of adversity.

In a heartfelt message, Cohn shared that she underwent treatment for cancer and is currently recuperating in a hospital in Santa Monica, California. This announcement comes after her first successful battle with breast cancer in 2017, a journey that she had previously kept private. 

Cohn's latest health challenge has been met with the same fighting spirit she exhibited during her first diagnosis. In her statement, she emphasized her resolve, stating, "Had to go kick cancer's ass." This declaration not only reflects her personal strength but also serves as an inspiration to many who may be facing similar battles.
