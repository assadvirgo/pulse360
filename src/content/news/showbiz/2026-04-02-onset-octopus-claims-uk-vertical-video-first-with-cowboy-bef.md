## Onset Octopus Pioneers Vertical Video Production in the UK with 'Cowboy Before Dark'

In a significant development for the British film industry, Onset Octopus, a microdrama production company, has announced that it is the first independent British producer to finance and produce a vertical video series aimed at international distribution. The series, titled "Cowboy Before Dark," marks a notable shift in the way content is created and consumed in today's digital landscape.

### A New Format for Storytelling

"Cowboy Before Dark" is a queer love story that revolves around the reunion of two friends, exploring themes of love, identity, and connection. The series is penned by writer Octavia McKenzie, with direction from Elliot Eason, who is gaining recognition for his innovative approach to storytelling. The vertical video format, which is designed for mobile viewing, caters to the increasing number of viewers who consume content on their smartphones, making it a timely and relevant choice for modern audiences.

### The Rise of Microdrama

Onset Octopus specializes in microdrama, a genre characterized by short, impactful narratives that can be easily consumed in brief viewing sessions. This production model aligns with current viewing habits, particularly among younger demographics who favor quick, engaging content over traditional long-form storytelling. By embracing this format, Onset Octopus is not only appealing to contemporary audiences but also setting a precedent for future productions in the UK.

### Independent Financing and Distribution

The independent financing of "Cowboy Before Dark" is a noteworthy aspect of this project. In an industry often dominated by large studios and production companies, Onset Octopus's ability to secure funding independently showcases the potential for smaller entities to thrive in the competitive landscape of film and television. This approach allows for greater creative freedom and the opportunity to tell diverse stories that may not fit into mainstream narratives.

The international distribution of the series further underscores its potential impact. By targeting global audiences, Onset Octopus aims to reach viewers beyond the UK, contributing to a broader dialogue about queer narratives and representation in media. The series is expected to resonate with audiences who seek authentic and relatable stories, particularly those that reflect their own experiences.

### Looking Ahead

As "Cowboy Before Dark" prepares for its release, the industry will be watching closely to see how this innovative approach to vertical video production performs in the marketplace. Onset Octopus's pioneering efforts may inspire other producers in the UK and beyond to explore similar formats, potentially reshaping the landscape of digital storytelling.

In conclusion, Onset Octopus's claim to be the first British producer of an independently financed vertical video series marks a significant milestone in the UK film industry. With "Cowboy Before Dark," the company is not only pushing the boundaries of traditional storytelling but also paving the way for future innovations in content creation and distribution. As audiences continue to evolve in their viewing preferences, productions like this may play a crucial role in defining the future of media consumption.
