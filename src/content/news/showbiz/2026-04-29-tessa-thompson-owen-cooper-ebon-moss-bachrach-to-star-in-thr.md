## Tessa Thompson, Owen Cooper, and Ebon Moss-Bachrach to Star in Psychological Thriller 'Foxfinder'

In an exciting development for the film industry, acclaimed director Aoife McArdle is set to helm a new psychological thriller titled *Foxfinder*. The film will feature a star-studded cast, including BAFTA and Emmy award-nominee Tessa Thompson, Golden Globe and Emmy winner Owen Cooper, and two-time Emmy winner Ebon Moss-Bachrach. 

### A Promising Collaboration

Aoife McArdle, known for her work on the critically acclaimed series *Severance*, brings her unique vision and expertise to *Foxfinder*. This collaboration marks a significant step in her directorial career, as she transitions from television to feature films. The film is expected to showcase her ability to create tension and psychological depth, elements that have resonated with audiences in her previous projects.

The casting of Tessa Thompson, Owen Cooper, and Ebon Moss-Bachrach adds considerable weight to the film. Thompson, celebrated for her performances in *Creed* and *Westworld*, is known for her versatility and depth as an actress. Owen Cooper, recognized for his role in *Adolescence*, and Ebon Moss-Bachrach, who gained acclaim for his performance in *The Bear*, complete a trio of talented actors that promise to deliver compelling performances.

### Sales and Distribution

*Foxfinder* is poised to attract significant attention at the upcoming Cannes Market, where Cornerstone and Logical Pictures Group will co-represent international sales and distribution. The Cannes Film Festival is a prestigious platform that often serves as a launchpad for new films, making it an ideal venue for *Foxfinder* to generate buzz among international buyers and audiences.

The involvement of reputable sales agents such as WME Independent and Cornerstone indicates a strong belief in the film’s potential. Their expertise in navigating the competitive landscape of film distribution will be crucial in ensuring that *Foxfinder* reaches a wide audience.

### Thematic Elements

While specific plot details remain under wraps, the title *Foxfinder* suggests a narrative that may explore themes of deception, truth, and the psychological struggles of its characters. The genre of psychological thrillers often delves into the complexities of human emotions and relationships, and with McArdle at the helm, audiences can expect a thought-provoking experience.
