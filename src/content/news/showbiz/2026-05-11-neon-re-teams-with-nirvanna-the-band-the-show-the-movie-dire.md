The new project is set to feature rising star Finn Wolfhard in a leading role, and it draws inspiration from a real-life incident involving a Vice Canada editor implicated in a drug smuggling operation.

The film will explore the true events surrounding the bust of the Vice Canada editor, who was accused of recruiting young individuals to participate in a drug trafficking scheme. This narrative promises to delve into the complexities of youth involvement in illegal activities, the allure of quick money, and the moral dilemmas faced by those drawn into such operations.

Johnson's unique storytelling style, which often blends humor with poignant social commentary, is expected to bring a fresh perspective to the subject matter.
