## Christina Malach Appointed President of Scripted Series at Media Res

In a significant leadership change within the indie studio Media Res, Christina Malach has been promoted to President of Scripted Series. This move comes as Lindsey Springer, who has held the position for six years, steps down to pursue other opportunities while continuing her production work within the industry.

### Leadership Transition

Christina Malach's promotion was announced by Michael Ellenberg, the CEO and founder of Media Res, known for its acclaimed productions such as "The Morning Show" and "Pachinko." In her new role, Malach will be responsible for overseeing the studio's current and upcoming scripted projects, a task that will require a keen understanding of both creative and business aspects of television production.

Malach's ascent to this prominent position reflects her extensive experience and contributions to the studio. Prior to her promotion, she played a pivotal role in various projects that have garnered critical acclaim and audience engagement. Her deep understanding of narrative development and production processes positions her well to lead Media Res into its next chapter.
