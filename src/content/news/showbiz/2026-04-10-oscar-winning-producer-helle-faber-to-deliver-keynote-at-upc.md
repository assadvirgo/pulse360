## Oscar-Winning Producer Helle Faber to Deliver Keynote at Sunny Side of the Doc Market

The international documentary marketplace, Sunny Side of the Doc, is set to host its 37th edition in La Rochelle, France, from June 22 to June 24, 2026. This year’s event will feature a keynote address from Helle Faber, an acclaimed producer who recently garnered an Oscar for her work on the documentary "Mr. Nobody Against Putin."

### Helle Faber's Rising Profile

Helle Faber's recognition at the Academy Awards marks a significant milestone in her career, highlighting her contributions to the field of documentary filmmaking. "Mr. Nobody Against Putin" has been praised for its compelling narrative and insightful commentary on pressing global issues, particularly those surrounding political dynamics and human rights. Faber's expertise and perspective are expected to provide valuable insights during her keynote address at the upcoming event.

### Sunny Side of the Doc: A Platform for Documentary Filmmakers

Sunny Side of the Doc serves as a crucial platform for documentary filmmakers, producers, and industry professionals from around the world. The event facilitates networking opportunities, discussions, and collaborations, fostering a vibrant community dedicated to the art of documentary storytelling. With a diverse lineup of speakers and sessions, the marketplace aims to inspire creativity and innovation within the documentary genre.

### Expectations for the 2026 Edition

The 2026 edition of Sunny Side of the Doc promises to be an engaging and enlightening experience for attendees. Faber's keynote is anticipated to draw significant interest, as she shares her observations on international documentary production and the evolving landscape of storytelling. Her unique perspective, shaped by her recent experiences, is likely to resonate with both emerging and established filmmakers.

In addition to Faber, the event will feature a range of speakers and panel discussions that address current trends and challenges in the documentary industry. Participants will have the opportunity to engage with thought leaders and peers, discussing topics such as funding, distribution, and the impact of technology on documentary filmmaking.

As the documentary landscape continues to evolve, events like Sunny Side of the Doc play a vital role in nurturing talent and fostering dialogue among industry professionals.
