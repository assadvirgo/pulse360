## OpenAP Appoints Ryan Gould as Chairman

In a significant move within the advertising and media landscape, OpenAP has announced the appointment of Ryan Gould as its new chairman. Gould, who currently oversees U.S. ad sales for Warner Bros. Discovery, will succeed John Halley, the former head of ad sales for Paramount Skydance, who recently departed from the role.

### Background on OpenAP

OpenAP is a consortium of media and technology companies focused on enhancing the interoperability of digital ad-buying practices across various publishers. Established to address the complexities and challenges in the advertising ecosystem, OpenAP aims to streamline the process for advertisers and publishers alike, fostering a more efficient marketplace.

The consortium has been instrumental in promoting standardized practices that facilitate data-driven advertising, allowing brands to reach their target audiences more effectively. With the rapid evolution of digital media, the need for such collaborative efforts has become increasingly critical.

### Ryan Gould's Role and Vision

Ryan Gould brings a wealth of experience to his new position as chairman. His tenure at Warner Bros. Discovery has equipped him with a deep understanding of the advertising landscape, particularly in navigating the complexities of digital media. In his new role, Gould will be responsible for guiding OpenAP's strategic direction and fostering collaboration among its members.

In a statement regarding his appointment, Gould expressed enthusiasm for the opportunity to lead OpenAP during a pivotal time for the industry. He emphasized the importance of innovation and collaboration in adapting to the changing dynamics of digital advertising. "I look forward to working with our partners to develop solutions that benefit both advertisers and publishers, ultimately enhancing the advertising experience for consumers," he stated.

### Transition and Future Directions

Gould's appointment comes at a time when the advertising industry is facing numerous challenges, including the need for greater transparency and efficiency in ad buying. His predecessor, John Halley, played a vital role in establishing OpenAP's foundation and fostering relationships among its members. As Gould steps into this leadership role, he will aim to build on Halley's legacy while addressing the evolving needs of the marketplace.

The transition in leadership is expected to bring fresh perspectives and renewed energy to OpenAP's initiatives. Gould's vision for the consortium includes expanding its reach and influence within the advertising community, promoting best practices, and enhancing the overall effectiveness of digital advertising strategies.

### Conclusion

As OpenAP embarks on this new chapter with Ryan Gould at the helm, the consortium is poised to continue its mission of transforming the digital advertising landscape. With a focus on interoperability and collaboration, OpenAP aims to create a more streamlined and effective advertising ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders involved. The advertising industry will be watching closely as Gould implements his vision and drives the consortium forward in the coming months.
