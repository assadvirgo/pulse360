This year, Taylor Swift stands out as the frontrunner, leading the nominations with an impressive eight nods. For those wishing to catch the action live, the AMAs will be available for streaming online as well as on television. Here are the options for viewers:

- **Television Broadcast**: The awards show will be aired live on ABC, allowing viewers to enjoy the event from the comfort of their homes. Check local listings for the exact time and channel.
  
- **Online Streaming**: Fans can also watch the AMAs through various streaming platforms. ABC's website and app will provide live streaming options for viewers who prefer to watch on their devices. Additionally, services like Hulu + Live TV and YouTube TV may offer access to the event, depending on regional availability.
