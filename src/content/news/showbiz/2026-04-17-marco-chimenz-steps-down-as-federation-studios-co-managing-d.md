## Marco Chimenz Steps Down as Co-Managing Director of Federation Studios

In a notable shift within the European entertainment landscape, Marco Chimenz has announced his resignation as co-managing director of Federation Studios, a prominent production and sales company based in Paris. ### Background on Marco Chimenz

Chimenz, an experienced executive in the media industry, joined Federation Studios in 2023, bringing with him over two decades of expertise from his previous role at ITV-Cattleya, one of Italy's leading production companies. At Cattleya, he was instrumental in the success of various acclaimed projects, including the internationally recognized series "Gomorrah." His extensive background also includes serving as president of the European Producers Club, a position that underscores his influence and commitment to the European film and television sector.
