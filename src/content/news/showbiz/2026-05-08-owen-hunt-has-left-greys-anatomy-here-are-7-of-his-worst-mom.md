## Dr. Owen Hunt Departs 'Grey's Anatomy' After 18 Seasons

After an impressive 18-season run, Dr. Owen Hunt, portrayed by Kevin McKidd, has officially exited the beloved medical drama "Grey's Anatomy." The character's departure was marked during the Season 22 finale, which aired on May 7, 2023. Alongside McKidd, Kim Raver, who played Dr. Teddy Altman, also bid farewell to the series.

### A Long-Standing Role

Kevin McKidd joined "Grey's Anatomy" in its fifth season in 2008, quickly becoming a pivotal character within the ensemble cast. His portrayal of Dr. Hunt, a trauma surgeon with a complex background, resonated with audiences and contributed significantly to the show's enduring popularity. Over the years, McKidd not only acted but also directed 49 episodes, showcasing his multifaceted talent.
