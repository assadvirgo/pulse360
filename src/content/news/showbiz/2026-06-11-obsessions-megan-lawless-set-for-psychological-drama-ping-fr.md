## Megan Lawless to Star in Indie Psychological Drama 'Ping'

Megan Lawless, known for her role in the acclaimed series *Obsession*, is set to take on a new challenge in the upcoming indie psychological drama *Ping*. This film marks the feature debut of writer-director Richie Gordon and is scheduled to begin shooting in Utah this summer.

## Overview of 'Ping'

*Ping* centers around the character Annie, portrayed by Lawless, who finds herself at a crossroads in her career. After experiencing a professional setback, she joins a wellness startup led by a charismatic founder. This startup promises its members a renewed sense of purpose, status, and creative power. However, as the narrative unfolds, it becomes evident that the allure of the startup may come with unforeseen complications.

The film is expected to delve into themes of ambition, identity, and the psychological impacts of pursuing success in a competitive environment. Gordon's vision for *Ping* aims to explore the complexities of modern wellness culture and the pressures it places on individuals seeking validation and fulfillment.

## Megan Lawless: A Rising Star

Megan Lawless has garnered attention for her compelling performances, particularly in *Obsession*, which has contributed to her rising profile in the entertainment industry. Her transition from television to film with *Ping* signifies a pivotal moment in her career, showcasing her versatility as an actress.

Lawless's involvement in *Ping* is anticipated to bring depth to the character of Annie, allowing her to explore the psychological nuances of a woman navigating the challenges of a new professional landscape. 

## Richie Gordon's Directorial Debut

Richie Gordon, who steps into the role of both writer and director for *Ping*, is poised to make a significant impact with his first feature film. Known for his innovative storytelling and keen understanding of character development, Gordon's work is expected to resonate with audiences looking for thought-provoking cinema.

The choice of Utah as the filming location adds an intriguing backdrop to the story, potentially enhancing the film's exploration of the wellness industry, which often intersects with the natural beauty and tranquility associated with such settings.

## Conclusion

As *Ping* prepares to enter production, anticipation builds around both Megan Lawless's performance and Richie Gordon's directorial debut. The film's exploration of ambition and the psychological intricacies of modern wellness culture promises to offer a compelling narrative that resonates with contemporary audiences. With a talented cast and a thoughtful approach to storytelling, *Ping* is positioned to be a noteworthy addition to the indie film landscape.
