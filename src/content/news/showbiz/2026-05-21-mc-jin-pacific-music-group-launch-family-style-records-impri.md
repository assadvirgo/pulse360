## MC Jin and Pacific Music Group Launch Family Style Records

In a significant development for the music industry, Pacific Music Group has officially launched Family Style Records, a new Hip-Hop imprint spearheaded by renowned artist MC Jin. The announcement comes in conjunction with the release of Jin's latest single, "Humble Flex," which celebrates Asian American and Pacific Islander (AAPI) Heritage Month.

### A New Chapter in Hip-Hop

Family Style Records aims to provide a platform for diverse voices within the Hip-Hop genre, reflecting the rich tapestry of cultures that influence the music. The label's launch signifies a commitment to fostering talent from various backgrounds, particularly those within the AAPI community. MC Jin, who has been a trailblazer in the industry since his early days, expressed enthusiasm about this new venture, stating that the label will focus on authenticity and creativity.

### A Diverse Roster

The inaugural roster of Family Style Records includes a mix of established and emerging artists. Alongside MC Jin, the label features the Korean sibling duo Lil Cherry and Goldbuuda, who are known for their innovative sound and engaging performances. Additionally, the label has signed 13-year-old Indonesian rapper Nada Askana, who is already making waves in the music scene with her unique style and lyrical prowess.

This diverse lineup reflects the label's mission to represent a wide array of cultural influences and perspectives within Hip-Hop. By nurturing talent from different backgrounds, Family Style Records aims to contribute to the evolution of the genre while providing a platform for underrepresented voices.

### Celebrating AAPI Heritage Month

The launch of Family Style Records is particularly timely, coinciding with AAPI Heritage Month, a time dedicated to honoring the contributions and achievements of Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders. Through this initiative, MC Jin and Pacific Music Group hope to inspire a new generation of artists and fans, highlighting the importance of cultural representation in the music industry.

MC Jin, who has long been an advocate for AAPI representation, emphasized the significance of this moment. He stated, "It's about time we create spaces where our stories can be told and celebrated. Family Style Records is not just a label; it's a community that embraces our heritage and creativity."

### Looking Ahead

As Family Style Records embarks on its journey, the music community is watching closely. The label's commitment to diversity and authenticity in Hip-Hop could pave the way for a new era of music that resonates with a broader audience. With the launch of "Humble Flex" and the introduction of its talented roster, Family Style Records is poised to make a meaningful impact in the industry.

In conclusion, the establishment of Family Style Records underlines the importance of representation and innovation in music. As MC Jin leads this new initiative, it is clear that the future of Hip-Hop is not only about the beats and rhymes but also about the stories and cultures that shape them.
