## Oasis Wins Major Tour of the Year at Pollstar Awards

In a significant recognition of their much-anticipated return to the stage, British rock band Oasis has been awarded the Major Tour of the Year at the 2026 Pollstar Awards. The accolade was presented during a ceremony held on Wednesday night in Hollywood, which served as the highlight of the annual Pollstar Live! conference, a key event in the live music industry.

### A Triumphant Return

Oasis, known for their iconic hits and influential role in the Britpop movement of the 1990s, announced their reunion tour for 2025 earlier this year. The tour has generated substantial excitement among fans, marking a pivotal moment in the band's history after a prolonged hiatus. The Major Tour of the Year award underscores the band's enduring popularity and the high expectations surrounding their comeback.

### Other Notable Winners

The Pollstar Awards celebrated a variety of artists across different genres, with several notable winners recognized for their contributions to the music scene. In the rock category, Metallica took home the award for their recent tour, highlighting their continued relevance and strong fanbase. Additionally, Kendrick Lamar and SZA were acknowledged for their collaborative efforts, further showcasing the diversity of talent within the industry.

Emerging artist Benson Boone also received accolades, reflecting the growing trend of recognizing new voices alongside established acts. The Weeknd, known for his innovative approach to music and performance, was another standout winner, solidifying his status as a leading figure in contemporary music.

### The Significance of the Pollstar Awards

The Pollstar Awards are an essential part of the live music calendar, celebrating achievements in concert touring and live performances. This year's event not only highlighted the success of established artists but also emphasized the importance of new talent in shaping the future of the industry. As the live music scene continues to recover from the challenges posed by the pandemic, the recognition of these artists serves as a testament to the resilience and adaptability of the music community.

### Looking Ahead

As Oasis prepares for their reunion tour, the band is expected to draw large crowds, reigniting the passion of long-time fans while attracting a new generation of listeners. The Major Tour of the Year award is a promising indicator of the tour's potential success, setting the stage for a memorable series of performances.

With the Pollstar Awards shining a spotlight on both established and emerging artists, the live music industry appears poised for a vibrant future. As fans eagerly await the return of Oasis and other celebrated acts, the excitement surrounding live performances continues to build, promising a dynamic year ahead for music lovers worldwide.
