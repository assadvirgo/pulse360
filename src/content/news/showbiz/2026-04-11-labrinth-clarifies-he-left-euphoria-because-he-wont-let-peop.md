British musician and composer Labrinth has recently clarified his reasons for leaving the acclaimed HBO series 'Euphoria.' His comments come in response to remarks made by the show's creator, Sam Levinson, as anticipation builds for the premiere of Season 3, scheduled for Sunday at 9 PM ET/PT.

In a candid statement, Labrinth expressed his dissatisfaction with the treatment he received while working in Hollywood. He emphasized that he would not tolerate being treated poorly, stating, "I won’t let people treat me like sh*t." This declaration highlights a broader conversation about the dynamics of the entertainment industry, particularly regarding the treatment of artists and creators.
