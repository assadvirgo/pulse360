## Tom Francis Joins ‘The Off Weeks’ Limited Series on Apple TV

In a significant casting update, Tony Award nominee Tom Francis has been announced as a recurring character in the upcoming limited series "The Off Weeks," produced by Apple TV. The series, which is set to feature a star-studded lineup including Richard Gere, Jessica Chastain, and Ben Stiller, is directed by Michael Showalter and showcases the creative talents of showrunner Alissa Nutting.

### Overview of "The Off Weeks"

"The Off Weeks" is an eight-episode limited series that delves into the complexities of relationships and the emotional turmoil that can arise from significant life changes, such as divorce. The narrative promises to explore the intricacies of human connections and the challenges faced by individuals as they navigate through personal upheavals.

The involvement of high-profile actors such as Jessica Chastain and Richard Gere adds considerable weight to the project. Chastain, who also serves as an executive producer, is known for her compelling performances in films like "The Help" and "Zero Dark Thirty." Gere, celebrated for his roles in classics such as "Pretty Woman" and "Chicago," brings a wealth of experience to the series.

### Tom Francis's Rising Career

Tom Francis, recognized for his performances in productions like "Sunset Blvd." and "Jay Kelly," has garnered attention for his talent and versatility as an actor. His inclusion in "The Off Weeks" marks a notable step in his career, as he joins an ensemble cast that is expected to deliver a powerful narrative. 

The series is anticipated to resonate with audiences, given its relatable themes and the strong creative team behind it.
