"The Uprising" is set against the backdrop of medieval England during the tumultuous reign of King Richard II. The film's official logline describes Garfield's character as a legendary leader who spearheads a rebellion against the king's oppressive rule. As conflict engulfs the nation, he rallies an army of common people, uniting them in their fight for justice and freedom.
