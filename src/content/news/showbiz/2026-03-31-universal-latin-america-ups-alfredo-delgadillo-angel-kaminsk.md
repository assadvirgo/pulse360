## Universal Music Latin America Announces Key Strategic Appointments

In a significant move aimed at strengthening its leadership and strategic direction, Universal Music Latin America has announced the promotion of three executives under the guidance of Jesús López, chairman and CEO of Universal Music Latin America and the Iberian Peninsula. This restructuring is expected to enhance the company's operations across the region, particularly in the dynamic and rapidly evolving Latin music market.

### Alfredo Delgadillo Promoted to CEO & President

Alfredo Delgadillo has been elevated to the position of CEO and President of Universal Music Latin Entertainment. In this role, Delgadillo will oversee Universal Music Latino in the United States while continuing to lead Universal Music's operations in Mexico. His extensive experience and deep understanding of the Latin music landscape are anticipated to drive the company's growth and innovation in the industry.

Delgadillo's ascent reflects Universal Music's commitment to nurturing talent within its ranks and adapting to the unique demands of the Latin market. His leadership is expected to foster new opportunities for artists and enhance the company's engagement with diverse audiences.

### Angel Kaminsky and Daniel Luna Take on New Roles

In addition to Delgadillo's promotion, Angel Kaminsky has been appointed as the Executive Vice President of Universal Music Latin Entertainment. Kaminsky's new role will involve overseeing strategic initiatives and partnerships that align with the company's vision for expansion in the Latin music sector. His previous experience within the organization positions him well to drive collaborative projects and enhance the label's artist roster.

Daniel Luna has also been promoted to Vice President of Marketing for Universal Music Latin Entertainment. In this capacity, Luna will focus on developing innovative marketing strategies that resonate with Latin music fans and elevate the visibility of Universal's artists. His expertise in marketing and brand development is expected to play a crucial role in promoting new releases and initiatives.

### ASCAP Unveils Award Winners

In a related development within the music industry, the American Society of Composers, Authors, and Publishers (ASCAP) recently announced its award winners, celebrating the achievements of songwriters and composers across various genres. While specific details about the winners were not included in the initial announcement, the recognition from ASCAP underscores the importance of creative contributions in shaping the music landscape.

### Looking Ahead

These strategic appointments at Universal Music Latin America come at a time when the Latin music genre is experiencing unprecedented growth and global recognition. The company's leadership changes reflect a proactive approach to harnessing this momentum and expanding its influence in the market.

As the Latin music scene continues to evolve, the leadership of Delgadillo, Kaminsky, and Luna is poised to drive innovation and success at Universal Music Latin Entertainment. With a focus on collaboration and strategic partnerships, the company aims to further elevate the profiles of its artists and connect with audiences around the world.
