## Jimmy Kimmel Live! Announces Summer Guest Hosts

In a recent episode of "Jimmy Kimmel Live!", host Jimmy Kimmel unveiled the lineup of guest hosts who will take over the show during his traditional summer hiatus. The announcement, made on his last show before the break, has generated excitement among fans as various celebrities prepare to step into the hosting role.

### Tiffany Haddish Kicks Off the Summer Lineup

The summer guest hosting schedule will commence with comedian and actress Tiffany Haddish, who is set to take the reins during the week of July 6. Known for her vibrant personality and comedic talent, Haddish is expected to bring her unique flair to the show, promising an engaging experience for viewers.

### A Star-Studded Lineup

Following Haddish, several other notable personalities will join the lineup as guest hosts throughout the summer. While the complete list has not been disclosed, Kimmel hinted at a diverse range of talent, ensuring that each week will offer something fresh and entertaining for the audience. 

### Matt Damon’s Surprise Appearance

In a surprising twist, Kimmel's announcement also included a humorous segment featuring actor Matt Damon, who made a grand entrance in a Trojan horse. This playful stunt is characteristic of Kimmel's comedic style and adds an element of fun to the show's summer festivities. Damon's unexpected appearance is sure to delight fans, as he has frequently been a target of Kimmel's playful banter over the years.

### The Tradition Continues

The tradition of summer guest hosts on "Jimmy Kimmel Live!" has become a staple of the show, allowing Kimmel to take a break while still providing viewers with entertaining content. This practice not only showcases a variety of comedic styles but also allows different celebrities to connect with the audience in a late-night format. 

### Conclusion

As "Jimmy Kimmel Live!" gears up for its summer break, the announcement of guest hosts, starting with Tiffany Haddish, has set the stage for an exciting season ahead. With a mix of humor, celebrity appearances, and engaging content, fans can look forward to a summer filled with laughter and entertainment. The combination of Haddish's hosting debut and Damon's playful cameo promises to keep the spirit of the show alive during Kimmel's absence.
