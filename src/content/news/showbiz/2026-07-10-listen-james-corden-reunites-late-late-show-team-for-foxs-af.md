## James Corden Reunites ‘Late Late Show’ Team for Fox’s ‘After Hours’ World Cup Show

Three years after concluding his tenure on CBS's "The Late Late Show," James Corden has returned to the spotlight, this time as the host of Fox's "After Hours With James Corden," a post-World Cup show. The program marks a significant moment for Corden, who has long been an avid football fan, allowing him to combine his passion for the sport with his entertainment career.

### A New Chapter in Corden's Career

Corden's transition from late-night television to hosting a sports-centric program has been met with enthusiasm. "After Hours" provides a platform for Corden to engage with fellow football enthusiasts and share insights on the World Cup, a tournament that captivates millions around the globe. In a recent episode of the "Daily Variety" podcast, Corden expressed his joy in being able to indulge in his lifelong love of football, highlighting how the sport has been a significant part of his life.

### Reuniting with the ‘Late Late Show’ Team

In a nostalgic twist, Corden has brought back some familiar faces from "The Late Late Show," creating a sense of continuity and camaraderie. This reunion not only enhances the show's appeal but also allows fans to reconnect with the personalities they enjoyed during Corden's previous hosting stint. The chemistry among the team members is evident, contributing to a lively and engaging atmosphere that resonates with viewers.

### The Impact of Football on Corden's Life

Corden's deep-rooted passion for football is not merely a passing interest; it has been a constant throughout his life. Growing up in the UK, Corden developed a love for the sport at an early age, and this affection has only grown over the years. Hosting "After Hours" gives him the opportunity to share this enthusiasm with a broader audience, celebrating the sport's cultural significance and its ability to unite fans from diverse backgrounds.

### Looking Ahead

As the World Cup progresses, "After Hours With James Corden" is poised to become a staple for football fans seeking post-match analysis and entertainment. Corden's unique blend of humor and genuine passion for the game is expected to attract a wide viewership, making it a must-watch for both football aficionados and casual viewers alike.
