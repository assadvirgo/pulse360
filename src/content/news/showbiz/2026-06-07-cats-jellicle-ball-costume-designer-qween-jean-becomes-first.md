## Qween Jean Makes History as First Openly Trans Woman to Win a Tony Award

In a groundbreaking moment for the theater community, Qween Jean has become the first openly transgender woman to win a Tony Award. Her award-winning costume design for the production of *Cats: The Jellicle Ball* marks a significant milestone not only in her career but also in the history of the prestigious awards.

### A Trailblazer in Costume Design

Qween Jean's recognition at the Tony Awards highlights her innovative approach to costume design, particularly in a production as iconic as *Cats*. The musical, known for its elaborate costumes and unique feline characters, provided an ideal platform for Jean to showcase her creativity and vision. Her designs have been praised for their vibrancy and ability to enhance the storytelling of the performance.
