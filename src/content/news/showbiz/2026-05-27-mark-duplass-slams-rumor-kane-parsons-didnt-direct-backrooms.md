## Mark Duplass Defends Kane Parsons Against Directorial Rumors

In a recent statement, actor and producer Mark Duplass has come to the defense of filmmaker Kane Parsons, who has faced speculation regarding his directorial role in the upcoming feature film "Backrooms." Duplass, who stars in the film, emphasized that Parsons, at just 20 years old, was "100% in control" during the production, countering claims that he did not direct the movie.

### Background on "Backrooms"

"Backrooms" is a feature adaptation of Parsons' 2022 web series, which gained significant traction online for its unique storytelling and innovative approach to horror. The film is being produced by A24, a company known for its commitment to independent and artistic filmmaking. If successful, Parsons will become the youngest director in A24's history, marking a significant milestone in his burgeoning career.

### Addressing the Rumors

Duplass's remarks come in response to rumors circulating within the industry that questioned Parsons' involvement in the film's direction. While some cast and crew members reportedly experienced disorientation on set, Duplass clarified that this was not indicative of Parsons' leadership. "Kane was the one steering the ship," he stated, reinforcing his confidence in the young director's vision and capabilities.

### The Importance of Young Voices in Film

The discussion surrounding Parsons highlights a broader trend in the film industry, where young filmmakers are increasingly making their mark. With platforms like A24 supporting innovative narratives, there is a growing opportunity for fresh voices to emerge. Duplass's defense of Parsons not only underscores the importance of mentorship in the industry but also reflects a commitment to fostering new talent.

### Conclusion

As the release date for "Backrooms" approaches, the film is generating buzz not only for its content but also for the conversations it has sparked about the role of young directors in Hollywood. With Duplass's endorsement, Kane Parsons is poised to make a significant impact, and audiences will be watching closely to see how this young filmmaker navigates the complexities of the industry. The support from established figures like Duplass may pave the way for a new generation of storytellers, encouraging them to take bold creative risks.
