## Alma Pöysti, Valentina Bellè, and Priit Võigemast to Star in International Film 'Beatrice'

In an exciting development for the film industry, the acclaimed Finnish actress Alma Pöysti, alongside Valentina Bellè and Priit Võigemast, has been announced as the lead cast for the upcoming film "Beatrice." Directed by Vallo Toomla, this project marks a collaborative effort between Estonia, Lithuania, and Italy, showcasing a compelling international love story that promises to resonate with audiences across borders.

### A Talented Cast

Alma Pöysti, who gained significant recognition for her role in Aki Kaurismäki’s "Fallen Leaves," which was awarded the Jury Prize at the Cannes Film Festival, is set to bring her exceptional talent to "Beatrice." Her performance in "Fallen Leaves" earned her a nomination for a Golden Globe, solidifying her status as a rising star in the film industry. Joining her are Valentina Bellè, known for her versatile performances in both film and television, and Priit Võigemast, a prominent figure in the Estonian film scene.

### Plot Overview

"Beatrice" tells the poignant story of a woman who returns to her husband and child, navigating the complexities of love, family, and personal growth. The film's narrative promises to delve into the emotional intricacies of relationships, making it a relatable tale for many viewers. The collaboration between the three countries not only enhances the film's cultural depth but also reflects the growing trend of international co-productions in the cinematic landscape.
