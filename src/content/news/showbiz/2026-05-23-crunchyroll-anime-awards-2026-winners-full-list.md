## Crunchyroll Anime Awards 2026 Winners Announced

On Saturday, Crunchyroll celebrated the 10th edition of its prestigious Anime Awards in Tokyo, marking a significant milestone in recognizing excellence in the anime industry. This year, the awards ceremony showcased a diverse range of talent and creativity, with winners selected from an impressive pool of 73 million votes cast by fans worldwide.

### Highlights from the Ceremony

The event was hosted by popular figures Sally Amaki and Jon Kabira, who guided the audience through an evening filled with excitement and anticipation. The ceremony not only honored individual achievements but also celebrated the collective passion of the global anime community.

### Major Award Winners

The standout winner of the night was the final season of "My Hero Academia," which was awarded the coveted title of Anime of the Year. This recognition underscores the series' enduring popularity and its impact on the anime landscape over the years. 

In addition to Anime of the Year, several other categories were recognized, showcasing a variety of genres and styles that have captivated audiences.
