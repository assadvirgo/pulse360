## Prominent Leaders to Speak at Paley Center for Media’s International Council Summit

The Paley Center for Media is set to host its annual International Council Summit in Midtown Manhattan from October 19 to 21, 2023. This prestigious event will feature a lineup of influential figures from various sectors, including media, business, politics, and academia.

Among the notable speakers are John Malone, a prominent media mogul and chairman of Liberty Media; Hiroki Totoki, the chief executive officer of Sony Corporation; and Strauss Zelnick, the chairman and CEO of Take-Two Interactive. Their participation underscores the summit's commitment to fostering dialogue on the evolving landscape of media and entertainment.

### A Platform for Dialogue

The International Council Summit serves as a platform for leaders to engage in discussions about the future of media, technology, and their impact on society. The event aims to bring together diverse perspectives, highlighting the intersection of creativity and commerce in today’s fast-paced environment.

John Malone is well-known for his strategic vision in the media industry, having played a pivotal role in shaping cable and telecommunications. His insights into the future of media distribution and consumer engagement are anticipated to be a focal point of the discussions.

Hiroki Totoki, representing Sony, will likely address the challenges and opportunities facing the entertainment sector, particularly in light of technological advancements and changing consumer behaviors. Under his leadership, Sony has continued to innovate in various realms, including gaming, music, and film.

Strauss Zelnick, whose company is known for its successful video game franchises, will bring a unique perspective on the convergence of gaming and media. His experience in navigating the complexities of digital entertainment makes him a valuable contributor to the summit’s conversations.

### Networking and Collaboration Opportunities

The summit not only provides a venue for discussions but also facilitates networking among leaders across industries. Attendees will have the opportunity to share ideas, forge partnerships, and explore collaborative initiatives that can drive innovation and growth in the media landscape.

The Paley Center for Media, known for its dedication to preserving and celebrating the history of television and radio, continues to be a vital institution in the media sector. By hosting events like the International Council Summit, it reinforces its role as a hub for critical conversations that shape the future of media.

### Conclusion

As the media landscape continues to evolve, the Paley Center for Media’s International Council Summit promises to be a significant event for industry leaders. With a distinguished lineup of speakers and a focus on collaboration and innovation, the summit is poised to address the pressing challenges and opportunities that lie ahead for media and entertainment. Attendees can look forward to engaging discussions that will likely influence the trajectory of the industry in the coming years.
