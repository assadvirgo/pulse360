## Jennifer Garner and Judy Greer Reunite for Netflix's '13 Going on 30' Reboot

In a highly anticipated revival of a beloved classic, Jennifer Garner and Judy Greer have been spotted on the set of Netflix's reimagining of the iconic film "13 Going on 30." The original movie, which premiered in 2004, captured the hearts of audiences with its charming story of a young girl who magically transforms into a thirty-year-old woman. Garner, who portrayed the lead character Jenna Rink, is reprising her role in this new adaptation, alongside Greer, who played her best friend in the original film.

### A Nostalgic Return

The original "13 Going on 30" became a cultural touchstone, celebrated for its blend of fantasy and coming-of-age themes. Garner's portrayal of Jenna Rink, an awkward teenager navigating the complexities of adulthood, resonated with many viewers. The film's themes of self-discovery and the challenges of growing up remain relevant, making the reboot a timely project for both new audiences and fans of the original.

### New Faces in the Cast

In this reimagining, Garner and Greer will be joined by a fresh cast that includes Emily Bader and Logan Lerman. Bader is set to take on a significant role, likely as a new iteration of Jenna Rink, while Lerman's involvement adds an intriguing dynamic to the project. The inclusion of younger actors aims to introduce the story to a new generation while still honoring the essence of the original film.

### Filming and Production Insights

Recent sightings of Garner and Greer on set have sparked excitement among fans and film enthusiasts alike. The duo was seen filming a scene that hints at the film's playful and heartfelt tone. The production team is reportedly committed to maintaining the charm of the original while updating the narrative to reflect contemporary themes and sensibilities.

### The Impact of Reboots in Hollywood

The trend of rebooting classic films has become increasingly prevalent in Hollywood, as studios seek to capitalize on nostalgia while introducing timeless stories to new audiences. While reboots can often be met with skepticism, the involvement of original cast members like Garner and Greer lends credibility to the project and may enhance its appeal.

### Conclusion

As Netflix continues to expand its catalog of original films, the reboot of "13 Going on 30" stands out as a promising addition. With Jennifer Garner and Judy Greer returning to their iconic roles, the film is poised to evoke nostalgia while potentially captivating a new generation of viewers. Fans eagerly await further updates as production progresses, hoping to see how this beloved story will be reinterpreted for today's audience.
