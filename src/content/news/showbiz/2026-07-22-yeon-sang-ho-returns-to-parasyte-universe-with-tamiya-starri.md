## Yeon Sang-ho Returns to ‘Parasyte’ Universe with New Series ‘Tamiya’

Renowned South Korean director Yeon Sang-ho is set to expand the "Parasyte" universe with a new live-action series titled "Parasyte: Tamiya." The series, which will star Japanese actress Shioli Kutsuna, is scheduled to stream on Netflix in 2027. This announcement marks another significant collaboration between Yeon and the streaming giant, following his previous works such as "Hellbound" and "Parasyte: The Grey."

### A New Chapter in the "Parasyte" Franchise

"Parasyte: Tamiya" will delve deeper into the themes and narratives established in the original "Parasyte" series, which has garnered a dedicated following since its inception. The franchise, based on the acclaimed manga by Hitoshi Iwaaki, explores the complex relationship between humans and parasitic aliens that invade their bodies. Yeon Sang-ho, known for his unique storytelling and visual style, aims to bring a fresh perspective to this beloved universe.

In this new installment, Kutsuna will take on a pivotal role, although specific details about her character have yet to be revealed. The casting of Kutsuna, who has appeared in various international projects, suggests that the series may aim for a broader appeal, potentially attracting viewers beyond the traditional fanbase of the franchise.

### Collaboration and Creative Team

Yeon Sang-ho has co-written the script for "Parasyte: Tamiya" alongside Ryu Yong-jae, further solidifying their partnership in the creative process. Their previous collaborations have been well-received, indicating a promising synergy that could enhance the storytelling in this new series. 

The production will be handled by Wowpoint, the same company responsible for Yeon's earlier works. This continuity in production is expected to maintain the high-quality standards that fans have come to expect from the franchise.

### Anticipation and Expectations

As anticipation builds for "Parasyte: Tamiya," fans of the original series and newcomers alike are eager to see how Yeon will navigate the intricate themes of identity, survival, and the human condition that are central to the "Parasyte" narrative. The series is expected to blend thrilling action with thought-provoking commentary, a hallmark of Yeon's directorial style.

With Netflix's global reach, "Parasyte: Tamiya" has the potential to introduce the franchise to a wider audience, further expanding its influence in the realm of science fiction and horror. The release date in 2027 gives fans ample time to speculate and discuss the direction the series may take, as well as the implications of its themes in contemporary society.

### Conclusion

Yeon Sang-ho's return to the "Parasyte" universe with "Parasyte: Tamiya" promises to be an exciting development for both fans and new viewers. As the series approaches its release date, the creative team’s commitment to delivering a compelling story will be closely watched, with many hoping it will live up to the legacy of its predecessors.
