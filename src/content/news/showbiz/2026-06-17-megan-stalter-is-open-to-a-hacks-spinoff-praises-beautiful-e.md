## Megan Stalter Reflects on 'Hacks' Finale and Potential Spinoff

Megan Stalter, known for her role in the critically acclaimed HBO Max series "Hacks," recently shared her thoughts on the show's conclusion and the possibility of a spinoff. Stalter's comments came during her appearance at Bravo's Las Culturistas Culture Awards 2026, shortly after the series wrapped up its latest season.

### Emotional Farewell

Stalter expressed her mixed feelings about the finale, stating, "I feel very sick. I feel very sad." Her emotional response highlights the strong connection she formed with her character and the show's narrative. "Hacks" has garnered significant praise for its writing and performances, particularly for its exploration of the complex relationship between its lead characters, played by Jean Smart and Hannah Einbinder.

The series, which delves into the world of stand-up comedy, has resonated with audiences for its sharp humor and poignant moments. Stalter's character, a young aspiring comedian, has been a fan favorite, contributing to the show's success. As the series concluded, Stalter reflected on the journey, noting that the ending was beautifully crafted. "It couldn’t have ended any other way," she remarked, emphasizing her appreciation for the storytelling.

### Spinoff Possibilities

In addition to her reflections on the finale, Stalter expressed openness to the idea of a "Hacks" spinoff. The show's popularity and critical acclaim have sparked discussions about potential future projects that could expand on its universe. While no official announcements have been made regarding a spinoff, Stalter's enthusiasm suggests that there is a demand for more content related to the beloved series.

The prospect of a spinoff aligns with the trend in the entertainment industry where successful shows often lead to expanded narratives. Fans of "Hacks" have shown a strong desire to see more of the characters they have come to love, and Stalter's willingness to explore this avenue could pave the way for new creative opportunities.

### New Music Release

In conjunction with her acting career, Stalter recently released her new single, "Prettiest Girl in America." The song marks a significant step in her musical journey, showcasing her versatility as an artist. As she transitions from acting to music, Stalter continues to engage her audience through various creative outlets.

### Conclusion

Megan Stalter's reflections on the "Hacks" finale and her openness to a potential spinoff highlight the impact of the series on both its cast and its audience. As fans process the conclusion of the show, Stalter's comments provide a glimpse into the emotional connections formed during its run. With her new music venture and the possibility of further storytelling in the "Hacks" universe, Stalter remains a dynamic figure in the entertainment landscape.
