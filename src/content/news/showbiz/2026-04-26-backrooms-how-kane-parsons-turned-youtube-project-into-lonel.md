## Kane Parsons Transforms YouTube Project into A24 Horror Film "Backrooms"

In an impressive leap from digital content creation to feature filmmaking, Kane Parsons has brought his YouTube project "Backrooms" to life as a full-length horror film under the A24 banner. This transition marks a significant milestone for Parsons, who at just 24 years old has become the youngest feature director in the studio's history. 

### The Concept Behind "Backrooms"

"Backrooms" is inspired by an internet phenomenon that revolves around the concept of endless, surreal spaces that evoke feelings of isolation and dread. The original YouTube series gained traction for its unique take on horror, utilizing minimalistic settings and psychological tension to engage viewers. Parsons has successfully expanded this concept into a cinematic experience, promising to deliver a film that resonates with both fans of the original series and new audiences alike.

### A Grand Scale Production

The film’s production is notable not only for its ambitious concept but also for its extensive set design. The "Backrooms" film features a sprawling 30,000-square-foot set, meticulously crafted to replicate the eerie and disorienting environments that characterize the original YouTube series. This significant investment in set design reflects A24's commitment to creating an immersive experience for viewers, enhancing the film’s atmospheric tension.

### Star-Studded Cast

"Backrooms" boasts a talented cast, including acclaimed actors Chiwetel Ejiofor and Renate Reinsve. Ejiofor, known for his powerful performances in films such as "12 Years a Slave" and "Doctor Strange," brings a wealth of experience to the project, while Reinsve, who gained recognition for her role in "The Worst Person in the World," is expected to deliver a compelling performance that complements the film's unsettling narrative.

### Anticipation and Release

The film is set to hit theaters next month, and excitement is building among horror enthusiasts and fans of Parsons' original work. At the recent CCXP Mexico event, Parsons teased elements of the film, hinting at its psychological depth and the unique storytelling approach that has become his signature. The anticipation surrounding "Backrooms" is further fueled by A24's reputation for producing critically acclaimed films that often push the boundaries of traditional genres.

### Conclusion

Kane Parsons’ journey from YouTube creator to feature film director exemplifies the evolving landscape of the film industry, where digital platforms can serve as launching pads for innovative talent. As "Backrooms" prepares for its theatrical debut, it stands as a testament to Parsons' creative vision and A24's dedication to supporting fresh voices in cinema. With its intriguing premise, impressive production scale, and a strong cast, "Backrooms" is poised to make a significant impact in the horror genre and beyond.
