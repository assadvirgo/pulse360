During his remarks, Trump humorously noted that Melania “hates” when he dances, particularly to what he referred to as the “gay national anthem.” This comment, while intended to be jovial, reflects the complex interplay of politics and personal life that often characterizes Trump's public persona. Despite his wife's apparent disapproval, Trump proceeded to demonstrate his dance moves, eliciting laughter and cheers from the audience.

The “YMCA” has long been a staple at Trump’s rallies, where the upbeat tempo and catchy chorus encourage audience participation.
