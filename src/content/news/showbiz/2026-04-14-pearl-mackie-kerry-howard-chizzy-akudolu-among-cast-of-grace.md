## Pearl Mackie, Kerry Howard, and Chizzy Akudolu Join Cast of Grace Link's 'Locker Room Talk'

In an exciting development for the British television landscape, actress Pearl Mackie, known for her roles in "Doctor Who" and "The Diplomat," is set to star in the upcoming indie TV series "Locker Room Talk." Joining her are fellow actresses Chizzy Akudolu, recognized for her performances in "Tommy & Tuppence" and "Holby City," and Kerry Howard, celebrated for her work in "Him and Her" and "Bad Apples." 

### A Promising Ensemble

The casting of these talented actresses marks a significant milestone for the series, which is being helmed by Grace Link, a name that has garnered attention for her previous works, including "Geek Girl" and "Bulletproof." The ensemble cast is rounded out by three additional actresses, though their identities have yet to be disclosed. The anticipation surrounding the project is palpable, given the impressive credentials of the lead actors and the creative team behind it.
