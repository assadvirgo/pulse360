## Stacey Aglok MacDonald and Alethea Arnaquq-Baril to be Honored at The Hollywood Reporter's Women in Entertainment Canada Event

In a significant recognition of their contributions to the entertainment industry, Stacey Aglok MacDonald and Alethea Arnaquq-Baril are set to receive The Hollywood Reporter's Breakthrough Award. The award ceremony will take place on May 28 in Toronto, celebrating the achievements of women in the Canadian entertainment sector.

### Celebrating Indigenous Voices

Stacey Aglok MacDonald and Alethea Arnaquq-Baril are renowned for their work in the realm of indigenous comedy, bringing unique narratives and perspectives to the forefront of Canadian television. Their collaboration on the series "North of North" has not only garnered critical acclaim but has also played a pivotal role in amplifying indigenous stories and voices within the entertainment landscape.

The Breakthrough Award is a testament to the duo's innovative approach to storytelling and their commitment to fostering a more inclusive environment in the industry. As showrunners and creators, MacDonald and Arnaquq-Baril have demonstrated exceptional talent and resilience, paving the way for future generations of indigenous creators.
