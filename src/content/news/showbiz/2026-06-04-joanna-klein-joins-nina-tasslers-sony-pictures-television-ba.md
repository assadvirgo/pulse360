## Joanna Klein Joins Nina Tassler’s Kismet Creative Group as Senior Vice President

In a significant move within the television industry, Joanna Klein has been appointed as Senior Vice President at Kismet Creative Group, a production company led by veteran television executive Nina Tassler. The announcement comes as Kismet continues to expand its creative endeavors under its overall deal with Sony Pictures Television Studios.

### A New Chapter for Kismet Creative Group

Nina Tassler, who previously served as Chairman of CBS Entertainment, expressed her enthusiasm regarding Klein's appointment. In her statement, Tassler highlighted Klein's extensive experience in the television landscape, stating, “I am thrilled to have Joanna join Kismet Creative Group. As a producer, former executive, and an innovative thinker, her insights will be invaluable as we continue to develop compelling content.”

Klein's background includes a wealth of experience in various roles within the television industry, making her a fitting addition to Kismet’s leadership team. Her previous positions have equipped her with a deep understanding of both the creative and business aspects of television production, which will be instrumental as Kismet aims to produce high-quality programming.

### The Vision of Kismet Creative Group

Kismet Creative Group was founded by Nina Tassler to cultivate diverse storytelling and innovative programming. The company has already made strides in developing projects that resonate with a wide audience. With Klein on board, Kismet is poised to enhance its slate of projects, focusing on narratives that reflect a range of perspectives and experiences.

Klein's appointment is part of a broader trend in the industry where experienced executives transition into production roles, bringing their insights and expertise to the creative process. This shift is indicative of the evolving landscape of television, where collaboration between seasoned executives and creative talent is increasingly valued.
