## Frankie Shaw Joins Jeff Ward in Upcoming Crime Thriller 'Strangers'

In an exciting development for the film industry, actress Frankie Shaw is set to star alongside Jeff Ward in the upcoming romantic crime thriller titled *Strangers*. This project marks a significant collaboration, as Ward not only stars in the film but also takes on the role of director, bringing his vision to life from a script penned by Dan Schimpf.

### Plot Overview

*Strangers* delves into the lives of two lonely individuals who cross paths at a bar, setting the stage for an unexpected romance. As their relationship blossoms, the couple devises a plan to commit a robbery, intertwining themes of love and crime. This unique narrative promises to explore the complexities of human connection amidst morally ambiguous circumstances.

### Cast and Crew

Frankie Shaw, known for her acclaimed work in *SMILF*, brings her talent and depth to the role, complementing Ward's performance. Jeff Ward, recognized for his role in *Danny and the Deep Blue Sea*, adds another layer of intrigue to the film with his dual role as both actor and director. The collaboration between Shaw and Ward is anticipated to create a dynamic on-screen chemistry that enhances the film's emotional and dramatic stakes.
