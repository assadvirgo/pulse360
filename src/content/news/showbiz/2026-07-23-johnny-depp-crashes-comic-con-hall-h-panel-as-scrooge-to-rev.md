## Johnny Depp Surprises Comic-Con Attendees with 'Ebenezer' Trailer

In an unexpected turn of events at this year's Comic-Con, three-time Oscar nominee Johnny Depp made a memorable appearance as the character Scrooge, captivating fans and attendees alike. The event took place in San Diego, California, where Depp not only graced the streets of the Gaslamp district but also made a surprise entrance at a panel in Hall H, one of the convention's most coveted venues.

### A Surprise Appearance

Depp's surprise appearance came during Collider's "Directors on Directing" panel, where he revealed the trailer for his upcoming holiday film, "Ebenezer." Directed by Ti West, the movie is set to be released on November 13, 2023. This marks a significant return for Depp, who has been relatively low-profile in recent years due to various personal and professional challenges.

As the actor stepped onto the stage, dressed in character as Scrooge, the audience erupted in applause. His charismatic presence and enthusiasm for the project were palpable, as he expressed his excitement, stating, "It’s good to be back." This sentiment resonated with fans, many of whom have eagerly awaited his return to the big screen.

### The Trailer and Film Details

The trailer for "Ebenezer" offers a fresh take on the classic Charles Dickens tale, focusing on the transformation of the miserly Scrooge during the Christmas season. While details about the plot remain under wraps, the film is anticipated to blend elements of drama and holiday spirit, appealing to a broad audience.

Ti West, known for his work in the horror genre, is stepping into new territory with this film. His direction has generated curiosity, as fans are eager to see how his unique style will shape the narrative of such a beloved story. The collaboration between West and Depp has already garnered significant attention, with many speculating about the film's potential impact during the holiday season.

### Fan Reactions

The reaction from Comic-Con attendees has been overwhelmingly positive. Fans expressed their delight at seeing Depp return to a major film project, especially in a role that allows him to showcase his range as an actor. Social media platforms were abuzz with comments and reactions, as many shared their excitement about the trailer and Depp's engaging performance.

Depp's appearance at Comic-Con not only highlights his enduring popularity but also serves as a reminder of the actor's significant contributions to the film industry over the years. His ability to draw crowds and generate buzz is a testament to his status as a Hollywood icon.
