## Indie Feature Drama 'Friend Thing' Cast Announced

In an exciting development for the independent film sector, the upcoming feature drama titled **Friend Thing** has revealed its main cast. The film will star rising talents Alfie Williams and Ryan Kiera Armstrong, alongside seasoned actors Pete Holmes and Lewis Black, with Adam Ray also joining the ensemble.

### A Promising Cast

Alfie Williams, known for his roles in **28 Years Later** and **Bone Temple**, will take on a leading role in the film. His co-star, Ryan Kiera Armstrong, recognized for her performance in **Lowdown**, is expected to bring depth and nuance to her character. The combination of these emerging actors with established names like Pete Holmes and Lewis Black promises to create a dynamic and engaging narrative.

Pete Holmes, celebrated for his work in the series **Crashing** and **Woman of The Hour**, is known for his comedic timing and ability to tackle serious themes with a light touch. Lewis Black, a veteran comedian recognized for his sharp wit and incisive commentary, adds a layer of gravitas to the project. Adam Ray, known for his appearances in **The Adam Ray Show** and **Jackass: Best and Last**, rounds out the cast, bringing his unique comedic style to the film.
