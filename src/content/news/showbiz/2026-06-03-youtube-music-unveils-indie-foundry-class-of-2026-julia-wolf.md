## YouTube Music Introduces Indie ‘Foundry Class’ of 2026

YouTube Music has announced the latest cohort of its “Foundry Class,” a program designed to support and elevate independent artists. The 2026 class features 24 diverse talents from around the globe, including notable names such as Julia Wolf, RaiNao, and Kelela. This initiative, which has gained recognition over the past decade, aims to provide emerging artists with the resources and exposure necessary to thrive in the competitive music industry.

### A Global Representation

The 2026 Foundry Class showcases a remarkable array of artists hailing from various countries, including the United States, the United Kingdom, Poland, South Korea, Brazil, Morocco, and India. This international representation underscores YouTube Music's commitment to fostering a diverse musical landscape and highlighting unique voices from different cultural backgrounds.

### Notable Artists in the Class

Among the artists selected for this year's class, Julia Wolf has garnered attention for her innovative sound and compelling storytelling. RaiNao, known for her genre-blending style, and Kelela, a prominent figure in the R&B scene, are also set to make significant impacts in the coming years. The inclusion of these artists not only reflects their individual talents but also the broader trend of independent music gaining traction on global platforms.

### Support and Resources

The Foundry program offers a variety of resources to its participants, including marketing support, access to industry professionals, and opportunities for collaboration. These resources are designed to help artists navigate the complexities of the music business while allowing them to maintain their creative independence. By providing these tools, YouTube Music aims to empower artists to build sustainable careers.

### Impact on the Music Industry

The Foundry Class has become synonymous with quality in independent music, and its alumni have gone on to achieve significant success. The program not only highlights emerging talent but also serves as a barometer for trends in the music industry. As independent artists continue to gain prominence, initiatives like the Foundry Class play a crucial role in shaping the future of music.

### Conclusion

The announcement of the 2026 Foundry Class by YouTube Music marks another step in the platform's ongoing commitment to supporting independent artists. By spotlighting a diverse group of musicians from around the world, YouTube Music reinforces its position as a key player in the evolution of the music industry. As these artists embark on their journeys, the industry will be watching closely to see how they shape the sound of the future.
