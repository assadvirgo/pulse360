## Scott Aukerman's 'Comedy Bang! Bang!' Secures Multi-Year Deal with SiriusXM

In a significant development for the podcasting landscape, Scott Aukerman's renowned improvisational interview show, **Comedy Bang! Bang!**, has finalized a multi-year extension with SiriusXM. This agreement, which spans three years, ensures the continued production, distribution, and exclusive advertising sales of the show by SiriusXM, solidifying its status as a cornerstone of the platform.

### A Legacy in Podcasting

Since its inception, **Comedy Bang! Bang!** has established itself as one of the longest-running comedy podcasts globally. Launched in 2009, the show has garnered a dedicated following, blending improvisational comedy with engaging interviews featuring a wide array of guests from the entertainment industry. Aukerman, a writer and comedian known for his unique style, has been instrumental in shaping the podcast's distinctive voice and format.

### The Importance of the Deal

This new agreement not only underscores the show's popularity but also highlights SiriusXM's commitment to delivering high-quality content to its subscribers. With the podcast's blend of humor and spontaneity, it has attracted a diverse audience, making it an essential part of SiriusXM's programming lineup. The deal is expected to enhance the show's reach and provide fans with even more engaging content in the coming years.

### Future Prospects

As part of the agreement, **Comedy Bang! Bang!** will continue to explore new creative avenues, potentially expanding its format and guest roster. The partnership with SiriusXM opens up opportunities for innovative advertising strategies, which could further enhance the listener experience while supporting the show's continued growth.

In a statement, Aukerman expressed enthusiasm about the extension, emphasizing his excitement to continue producing content that resonates with fans. He noted the importance of collaboration with SiriusXM in bringing the show's unique brand of comedy to a broader audience.

### Conclusion

The multi-year deal between Scott Aukerman's **Comedy Bang! Bang!** and SiriusXM marks a pivotal moment in the podcasting realm, reinforcing the show's legacy and ensuring its presence in the ever-evolving media landscape. As the podcast continues to thrive, both Aukerman and SiriusXM are poised to explore new horizons, promising fans an exciting future filled with laughter and creativity.
