## France TV Distribution Acquires Rights to "Apocalypse Civilizations: Rome"

France TV Distribution has secured the international rights to the highly anticipated historical documentary "Apocalypse Civilizations: Rome." This new installment is part of the successful "Apocalypse" franchise, which has garnered significant acclaim since its inception in 2013. The series, written and directed by Thomas Marlier, is set to explore the pivotal Battle of Actium, a defining moment in the power struggle between Octavian and the duo of Mark Antony and Cleopatra.

### A New Chapter in the "Apocalypse" Franchise

The "Apocalypse" series has established itself as a leading documentary franchise, renowned for its engaging storytelling and meticulous historical detail. The original series captivated audiences worldwide, and the latest installment promises to continue this tradition. Thomas Marlier, known for his previous works such as "Sardinia, the Mysterious Civilization of the Nuraghi" and "Science Grand Format," brings his expertise to this new project, aiming to provide viewers with an in-depth understanding of one of history's most significant naval battles.

### The Battle of Actium: A Defining Moment

The Battle of Actium, fought in 31 BC, was a decisive confrontation that ultimately led to the rise of Octavian as the first Roman Emperor. The conflict saw the forces of Octavian clash with those of Mark Antony and Cleopatra, marking a turning point in Roman history. The documentary is expected to delve into the intricate political dynamics, military strategies, and personal rivalries that characterized this monumental event.

### Excitement Surrounds the Reboot

France TV Distribution expressed enthusiasm about the acquisition, highlighting the franchise's ability to attract viewers with its compelling narratives and high production values. The reboot of "Apocalypse" is anticipated to resonate with both history enthusiasts and a broader audience, as it combines educational content with cinematic storytelling.

### Global Reach and Impact

The "Apocalypse" series has been distributed internationally, reaching audiences in various countries and languages. With the addition of "Apocalypse Civilizations: Rome," France TV Distribution aims to further enhance the franchise's global footprint, ensuring that the stories of ancient civilizations continue to captivate and educate.
