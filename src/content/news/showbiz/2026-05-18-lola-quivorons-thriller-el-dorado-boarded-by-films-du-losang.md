## Lola Quivoron’s Thriller ‘El Dorado’ Secures International Sales Representation

Paris-based Films du Losange has announced its involvement in the international sales of "El Dorado," a psychological thriller directed by the emerging French filmmaker Lola Quivoron. The film recently concluded production in the picturesque landscapes of Southern France, further solidifying Quivoron's position as a notable voice in contemporary cinema.

### A Promising Follow-Up

"El Dorado" represents Quivoron's second feature film, following her critically acclaimed debut "Rodeo," which premiered in the Un Certain Regard section at the Cannes Film Festival in 2022. "Rodeo" garnered significant attention and acclaim, establishing Quivoron as a filmmaker to watch. The success of her first film has set high expectations for "El Dorado," which is anticipated to explore complex psychological themes through its narrative and character development.

### Notable Cast

The film boasts a talented cast, including Matthias Schoenaerts, known for his versatile performances in both French and international cinema, alongside rising stars Mallory Wanecque, Antonia Buresi, and Samuel Kircher. The ensemble is expected to bring depth and nuance to the film, enhancing the psychological tension that is central to the story.

### Themes and Expectations

While specific plot details remain under wraps, "El Dorado" is expected to delve into the intricacies of human emotion and the darker aspects of the human psyche. Quivoron's previous work has demonstrated her ability to blend gripping narratives with rich character studies, and audiences are eager to see how she continues to develop her cinematic voice in this new project.

### Distribution and Future Prospects

With Films du Losange on board for international sales, "El Dorado" is well-positioned for a global audience. The company has a strong reputation for distributing high-quality French cinema, and its involvement is likely to facilitate the film's reach beyond national borders. As the film moves into post-production, anticipation is building for its eventual release, which could further elevate Quivoron’s profile in the international film community.

As Lola Quivoron continues to carve her niche in the film industry, "El Dorado" stands as a testament to her evolving artistry and commitment to storytelling.
