## Comedy Mockumentary “Dave Vs. Hollywood” Set for World Premiere at TCL Chinese Theatre

The upcoming comedy mockumentary “Dave Vs. Hollywood,” directed by Brad Dickson and Daniel Katz, is poised to make its World Premiere at the iconic TCL Chinese Theatre on June 26. The film will be featured as part of the Dances With Films festival in Los Angeles, a platform known for showcasing independent filmmakers and their innovative works.

### Overview of the Film

“Dave Vs. Hollywood” follows the journey of a struggling actor, played by Preston Tyler Ward, who relocates to Los Angeles in pursuit of his dreams. The film promises to blend humor with the often harsh realities of the entertainment industry, offering a satirical look at the challenges faced by aspiring actors in Hollywood.

### Dances With Films Festival

The Dances With Films festival has gained recognition for its commitment to supporting independent cinema and providing a platform for emerging talent. This year’s festival will feature a diverse lineup of films, and “Dave Vs. Hollywood” is expected to be a highlight. The festival will take place at various venues in Los Angeles, with the TCL Chinese Theatre serving as a prominent location for premieres.

### Anticipation and Reception

As the premiere date approaches, anticipation is building around “Dave Vs. Hollywood.” The film's unique premise and comedic approach to the struggles of aspiring actors resonate with many, particularly those familiar with the challenges of the entertainment industry. The directors, Dickson and Katz, have expressed excitement about showcasing their work at such a prestigious venue, which has a rich history of hosting significant film events.

### Conclusion

“Dave Vs. Hollywood” represents not only a comedic exploration of the actor's journey but also underscores the importance of independent filmmaking in telling diverse stories. As it prepares for its premiere at the TCL Chinese Theatre, the film is set to engage audiences and contribute to the ongoing conversation about the realities of pursuing a career in Hollywood. The Dances With Films festival continues to be a vital platform for emerging filmmakers, and the inclusion of this mockumentary highlights the festival’s commitment to innovative storytelling.
