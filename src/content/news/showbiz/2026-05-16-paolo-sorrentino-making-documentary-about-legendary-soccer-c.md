## Paolo Sorrentino to Create Documentary on Soccer Icon Carlo Ancelotti

Oscar-winning Italian director Paolo Sorrentino is set to embark on an ambitious project that will delve into the life and career of renowned soccer coach Carlo Ancelotti. The documentary, which remains untitled, promises to offer an intimate look at Ancelotti's remarkable journey through the world of football, spanning over five decades.

### A Legendary Career

Carlo Ancelotti, celebrated for his tactical acumen and leadership, has made a significant impact on the sport, coaching some of the world's most prestigious clubs, including AC Milan, Chelsea, Real Madrid, and Bayern Munich. Ancelotti's career is marked by numerous accolades, including multiple UEFA Champions League titles, which have solidified his reputation as one of the greatest coaches in soccer history.

The documentary will not only highlight Ancelotti's professional achievements but also provide a personal glimpse into his life, showcasing his philosophy on coaching and the relationships he has built throughout his career. It is expected to feature interviews with players, colleagues, and sports analysts who have witnessed Ancelotti's evolution as a coach.

### Focus on the 2026 World Cup

One of the unique aspects of this documentary is its timing, as it will culminate in the upcoming 2026 FIFA World Cup, where Ancelotti will take on the role of head coach for Brazil's national team. This marks a significant chapter in Ancelotti's career, as he aims to lead Brazil to glory in a tournament that promises to be a showcase of talent and passion for the sport.

The 2026 World Cup will be co-hosted by the United States, Canada, and Mexico, making it a historic event in soccer history. Ancelotti's involvement with the Brazilian team adds an exciting layer to the documentary, as fans will be eager to see how his coaching strategies and experience influence one of the most storied national teams in the world.
