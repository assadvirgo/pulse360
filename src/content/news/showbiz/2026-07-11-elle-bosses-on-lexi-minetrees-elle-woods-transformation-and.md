## 'Elle' Showrunners Discuss Lexi Minetree's Transformation and Character Evolution

As the new series "Elle" streams on Amazon Prime Video, showrunners Laura Kittrell and Caroline Dries have opened up about the creative decisions that shaped the show, particularly the transformation of Lexi Minetree into a modern-day Elle Woods. The series, which has garnered attention for its fresh take on familiar themes, also pays homage to the late actor James Van Der Beek, whose character underwent significant changes during production.

### Lexi Minetree's Transformation

Lexi Minetree, portrayed by a rising star in the industry, has been central to discussions surrounding "Elle." Kittrell and Dries emphasized the importance of Minetree's character arc, which mirrors the original spirit of Elle Woods while also addressing contemporary issues faced by young women today. The showrunners aimed to create a character that resonates with modern audiences, reflecting both empowerment and vulnerability.

"The essence of Elle Woods is timeless, but we wanted to explore how that character would evolve in today's world," Kittrell explained. "Lexi's journey is about finding her voice and navigating the complexities of modern life, from career challenges to personal relationships."

### Navigating Character Development

The creative team faced the challenge of crafting a narrative that honors the legacy of the original "Legally Blonde" while establishing its own identity. Dries noted that the character development process involved extensive discussions about authenticity and relatability. "We wanted to ensure that Lexi's experiences felt genuine," she said. "Her struggles and triumphs are reflective of what many young women face today."

In addition to Minetree's growth, the series also explores themes of friendship, ambition, and self-discovery. The showrunners have been praised for their ability to weave these elements into a cohesive narrative that appeals to both fans of the original film and new viewers alike.

### A Tribute to James Van Der Beek

Another significant aspect of "Elle" is the inclusion of James Van Der Beek's character, whose storyline was notably altered following the actor's passing. Van Der Beek, known for his role in "Dawson's Creek," had been involved in the project prior to his death, and his contributions left a lasting impact on the series.

Kittrell and Dries shared that they had to navigate the emotional landscape of rewriting his character's arc. "It was important for us to honor James and the work he had done," Kittrell stated. "We wanted to ensure that his character's ending was respectful and meaningful."

The showrunners decided to incorporate elements that reflect Van Der Beek's spirit, allowing his character to serve as a guiding force for Lexi. This decision not only pays tribute to the late actor but also enriches the narrative, providing depth to the storyline.

### Conclusion

As "Elle" continues to unfold on Amazon Prime Video, the creative vision behind the series is becoming increasingly clear. With Lexi Minetree's transformation at the forefront and a heartfelt tribute to James Van Der Beek, Kittrell and Dries have crafted a show that resonates with audiences on multiple levels. The series not only entertains but also invites viewers to reflect on the challenges and triumphs of modern womanhood, ensuring that the legacy of Elle Woods lives on in a new and meaningful way.
