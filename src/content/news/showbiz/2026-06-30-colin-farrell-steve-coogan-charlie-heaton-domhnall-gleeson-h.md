## New Cast Announced for Netflix's 'Bad Bridgets'

Rich Peppiatt's upcoming Netflix-backed film, "Bad Bridgets," has garnered significant attention with the announcement of its expanding cast. The film, which is set to begin shooting in July, features an impressive lineup of actors, including Colin Farrell, Steve Coogan, Charlie Heaton, Domhnall Gleeson, Himesh Patel, Niamh Algar, and Simone Kirby.

### A Star-Studded Ensemble

Colin Farrell, known for his acclaimed performance in "The Banshees of Inisherin," brings a wealth of experience and critical acclaim to the project. His ability to portray complex characters is expected to add depth to the film's narrative. Steve Coogan, recognized for his work in "Philomena," is another seasoned actor whose comedic timing and dramatic skills will likely enhance the film's appeal.

Joining them is Charlie Heaton, who gained fame through his role in the popular series "Stranger Things." Heaton's rising star power is expected to attract a younger audience to the film. Domhnall Gleeson, known for his performances in "Ex Machina" and "Star Wars," adds further credibility to the ensemble with his versatile acting range.

Himesh Patel, who received praise for his lead role in "Yesterday," is also part of the cast, bringing his musical background and acting prowess to the film. Niamh Algar, recognized for her role in "The Iris Affair," and Simone Kirby, known for her work in "Kneecap," round out the cast with their impressive credentials.

### The Vision Behind 'Bad Bridgets'

"Bad Bridgets" is described as a period tale, although specific plot details remain under wraps. Rich Peppiatt, who has previously worked on projects that blend humor and drama, is expected to bring a unique perspective to this narrative. The film is anticipated to explore themes relevant to its historical setting, potentially addressing social issues through the lens of its characters.

The collaboration with Netflix signifies a commitment to high-quality storytelling and production values, which the streaming giant is known for. With a cast of this caliber, "Bad Bridgets" is poised to become a significant addition to Netflix's growing library of original films.
