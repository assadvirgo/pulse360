## Fandango Acquires International Sales Rights for 'Vittorio De Sica – Staging Life'

Fandango Sales, a prominent Italian sales agency, has secured the international sales rights for the documentary "Vittorio De Sica – Staging Life." This acquisition comes as the film prepares to launch at the Cannes Film Festival's prestigious Cannes Classics section, a platform known for showcasing significant cinematic works.

### A Tribute to a Cinematic Legend

Directed by Francesco Zippel, "Vittorio De Sica – Staging Life" delves into the life and career of the influential Italian filmmaker Vittorio De Sica, who is widely regarded as one of the pioneers of neorealism in cinema. De Sica's work has left an indelible mark on the film industry, with classics such as "Bicycle Thieves" and "Umberto D." earning him a place among the greats of world cinema.

Zippel, who previously showcased his documentary "Oscar Micheaux – The Superhero of Black Filmmaking" in the same Cannes section in 2021, brings his expertise in documentary filmmaking to this new project. His previous work has been well-received, indicating a strong potential for "Vittorio De Sica – Staging Life" to resonate with audiences and critics alike.

### Significance of the Cannes Film Festival

The Cannes Film Festival is one of the most prestigious film festivals globally, attracting filmmakers, actors, and industry professionals from around the world. The Cannes Classics section, in particular, is dedicated to celebrating classic films and documentaries that have made significant contributions to the art of cinema. The selection of "Vittorio De Sica – Staging Life" for this section underscores the documentary's importance and the enduring legacy of its subject.

Fandango Sales has established itself as a key player in the international film sales market, representing a diverse array of films from various genres and regions.
