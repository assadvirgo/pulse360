## Preview of 'The End of Oak Street' Showcases Thrilling 4DX Experience

A special preview of the upcoming sci-fi film "The End of Oak Street" was unveiled on Saturday night in Los Angeles, providing audiences with an exhilarating glimpse into a world where dinosaurs roam and danger lurks at every turn. Directed by David Robert Mitchell, known for his work on "It Follows," the film features notable performances by Anne Hathaway and Ewan McGregor, who find themselves in a fight for survival against prehistoric predators.

## The 4DX Experience

One of the most striking aspects of this preview was the incorporation of 4DX technology, which enhances the cinematic experience by engaging multiple senses. The 14-minute footage allowed viewers to not only see the action unfold on screen but also feel it through motion seats, environmental effects, and scents that simulate the film's thrilling atmosphere. This immersive approach aims to elevate the traditional movie-going experience, making it more interactive and engaging for audiences.

While specific plot details remain largely under wraps, "The End of Oak Street" is set in a world where dinosaurs have returned, creating a chaotic and dangerous environment.
