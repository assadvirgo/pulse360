## Laufey Signs New Deal with Warner Chappell Music

In a significant move within the music industry, Warner Chappell Music has announced the re-signing of Grammy-winning singer-songwriter Laufey to a global publishing contract. The terms of the deal have not been disclosed, but the partnership underscores Laufey's rising prominence in the music scene.

### Laufey's Ascendancy in Music

Laufey, known for her unique blend of jazz and pop influences, has captured the attention of audiences and critics alike with her distinctive sound and lyrical prowess. Her recent accolades, including a Grammy Award, highlight her growing influence and artistry. The re-signing with Warner Chappell is seen as a testament to her potential for further success in the industry.

### Warner Chappell's Commitment to Talent

Warner Chappell Music, a leading global music publishing company, has a history of nurturing and promoting talented artists. The decision to extend their partnership with Laufey reflects their commitment to supporting innovative voices in music. The company has expressed excitement about continuing to work with Laufey, emphasizing her creative vision and the impact of her work on contemporary music.

### ASCAP's Creative Voice Award

In addition to her new publishing deal, Laufey will also be honored with the Creative Voice Award from the American Society of Composers, Authors, and Publishers (ASCAP). This award recognizes her contributions to the music industry and her ability to connect with audiences through her songwriting and performances. The award ceremony will provide an opportunity for Laufey to showcase her talent and celebrate her achievements with fellow artists and industry professionals.

### Leadership Changes at Warner Records

In related news, Warner Records has announced the promotion of Miles Gersh to Executive Vice President (EVP). Gersh has been instrumental in the label's success, overseeing various projects and artist development initiatives. His promotion signals Warner Records' commitment to fostering leadership within the organization and enhancing its strategic direction in an evolving music landscape.

The recent developments surrounding Laufey and Warner Chappell Music, along with the leadership changes at Warner Records, highlight the dynamic nature of the music industry.
