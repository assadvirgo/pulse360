## Tony Leung Named Jury President of 2026 Shanghai Film Festival

Tony Leung, the acclaimed Hong Kong actor, has been appointed as the Jury President for the 28th edition of the Shanghai Film Festival, set to take place from June 12 to June 21, 2026. This prestigious role highlights Leung's significant contributions to the film industry and his status as a cultural icon in Asia and beyond.

### A Celebrated Career

Tony Leung is widely recognized for his remarkable performances in both Hong Kong cinema and international films. With a career spanning over three decades, he has garnered numerous awards and accolades, including the Best Actor award at the Cannes Film Festival for his role in "In the Mood for Love." His work has not only captivated audiences but has also influenced a generation of filmmakers and actors.

Leung's selection as Jury President is a testament to his artistic achievements and his influence in the film community. The Shanghai Film Festival, one of the most prominent film festivals in Asia, is known for showcasing a diverse array of films and promoting cultural exchange through cinema.

### The Shanghai Film Festival

The Shanghai Film Festival, established in 1993, has grown to become a significant platform for filmmakers from around the world. The festival features the Golden Goblet Awards, which honor outstanding films and talents in various categories. As Jury President, Leung will lead a panel of esteemed jurors in evaluating films submitted for competition, ensuring that the awards reflect excellence in filmmaking.

The festival is expected to attract a wide range of international filmmakers, industry professionals, and cinema enthusiasts, further enhancing Shanghai's reputation as a hub for film and culture. The 28th edition promises to be a celebration of creativity and storytelling, with Leung at the helm of the jury.

### Looking Ahead

As the festival approaches, anticipation is building among filmmakers and audiences alike. Leung's leadership is expected to bring a unique perspective to the jury's deliberations, given his extensive experience and deep understanding of the art of filmmaking. His involvement is likely to elevate the festival's profile and draw attention to emerging talents in the industry.
