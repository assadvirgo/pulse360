## Dwayne Johnson Delivers Raucous Roast at Kevin Hart's Netflix Special

Dwayne Johnson, widely known as "The Rock," made headlines recently for his appearance at the Netflix roast of fellow comedian and actor Kevin Hart. The event, which celebrated Hart's career and personal life, featured a series of comedic jabs and playful banter, with Johnson's performance standing out for its boldness and humor.

### A Dramatic Entrance

The roast commenced with Johnson making a dramatic entrance, accompanied by special effects and the iconic anthem "We Will Rock You" by Queen. This theatrical approach set the tone for what would be an evening filled with laughter and light-hearted ribbing. Johnson, who co-starred with Hart in the "Jumanji" films, took the opportunity to showcase his comedic chops while also paying homage to their longstanding friendship.

### The Focus on Family

Much of Johnson's set was centered around Hart's wife, Eniko Parrish. In a particularly memorable moment, Johnson quipped that Eniko deserves "an Academy Award for pretending she likes to f— you." This line, while certainly risqué, elicited laughter from the audience and highlighted the playful nature of the roast. The humor, albeit edgy, reflects the camaraderie between the two stars and their ability to poke fun at each other's personal lives.
