### Scheduling Conflicts and Personal Reflections

Musso explained that he was unable to attend the reunion due to a scheduling conflict. However, he emphasized that his absence was not simply a matter of availability. "It wasn’t the right thing for me to do," he stated, suggesting that there were deeper considerations influencing his decision. While he did not elaborate further, his comments reflect a thoughtful approach to the reunion, indicating that he weighed his participation carefully.
