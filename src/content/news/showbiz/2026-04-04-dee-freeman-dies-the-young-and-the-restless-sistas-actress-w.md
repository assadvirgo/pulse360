## Dee Freeman, Actress Known for 'The Young and the Restless' and 'Sistas', Passes Away at 66

Freeman's passing marks a significant loss for the entertainment industry, where she was recognized not only for her acting skills but also for her vibrant personality and dedication to her craft. Her family shared a heartfelt statement on her Instagram profile, describing her as a brave and fearless fighter throughout her illness.
