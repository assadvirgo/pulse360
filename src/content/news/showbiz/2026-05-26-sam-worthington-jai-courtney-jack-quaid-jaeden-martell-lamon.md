In an exciting development for the film industry, the upcoming Western drama *Blood On The Promontory* has officially cast a notable ensemble of actors. Sam Worthington, best known for his leading role in the *Avatar* franchise, brings significant star power to the project. His experience in high-profile films positions him as a central figure in the narrative. Jai Courtney, recognized for his performances in *Dutton Ranch* and other action-oriented roles, adds depth to the cast with his dynamic acting capabilities.

Jack Quaid, who has garnered attention for his role in the critically acclaimed series *The Boys*, joins the ensemble, promising to deliver a compelling performance. Jaeden Martell, known for his work in *The Boy in the Iron Box*, is another young talent whose involvement is anticipated to resonate with audiences.
