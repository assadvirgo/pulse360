## Jane Fonda Stars in Katie Camosy’s Directorial Debut ‘Gaslit’

In a remarkable collaboration, actress and activist Jane Fonda stars in "Gaslit," the feature directorial debut of Katie Camosy. This documentary, produced in partnership with Greenpeace, offers a poignant exploration of the environmental challenges faced by communities in the oil and gas fields of the Southern United States.

### A Unique Perspective on Environmental Issues

"Gaslit" follows Fonda as she embarks on a road trip through regions heavily impacted by the fossil fuel industry. The film aims to shed light on the real-life consequences of environmental degradation, focusing on the air and water quality issues that plague these communities. By centering the narrative around Fonda, a well-known figure in both the entertainment industry and environmental activism, Camosy hopes to amplify the voices of those often overlooked in discussions about climate change and corporate responsibility.

### The Journey of Katie Camosy

Katie Camosy, who has previously worked on various documentary projects, sees "Gaslit" as a culmination of her passion for storytelling and environmental advocacy. Her decision to collaborate with Fonda was both strategic and inspirational. Camosy stated that having Fonda on board not only brings star power to the film but also aligns with the film's mission to raise awareness about pressing environmental issues. Camosy’s vision for the documentary is to create a narrative that is both informative and emotionally resonant, encouraging viewers to reflect on their relationship with the environment.

### Jane Fonda’s Continued Activism

Fonda's involvement in "Gaslit" is a continuation of her long-standing commitment to environmental causes. Over the years, she has used her platform to advocate for climate justice, often participating in protests and speaking engagements focused on sustainability. Her activism has garnered significant attention, and her collaboration with Camosy is expected to further engage audiences on the topic of environmental degradation.

### The Impact of ‘Gaslit’

As the documentary prepares for its release, anticipation is building around its potential impact. By focusing on the personal stories of individuals living in areas affected by the oil and gas industry, "Gaslit" aims to humanize the statistics often associated with environmental issues. The film seeks to foster a deeper understanding of the challenges these communities face, promoting empathy and action among viewers.

### Conclusion

"Gaslit" represents a significant milestone for both Katie Camosy and Jane Fonda. Through this documentary, they aim to not only entertain but also educate and inspire action on critical environmental issues. As the film prepares to debut, it stands as a testament to the power of storytelling in the fight against climate change, and the importance of highlighting the experiences of those most affected by environmental policies. The collaboration between Camosy and Fonda is a reminder of the role that art can play in advocacy, and the potential for change when influential voices come together for a common cause.
