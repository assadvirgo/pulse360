## Rita Wilson Joins HBO Max Drama Pilot "How To Survive Without Me"

Rita Wilson, an accomplished actress and producer, is set to take on a significant role in the upcoming HBO Max one-hour drama pilot titled "How To Survive Without Me." The project is produced by Berlanti Productions in collaboration with Warner Bros. Television, further solidifying HBO Max's commitment to developing compelling original content.

### Role and Character Overview

In this new series, Wilson will portray the character of Beverly, described as elegant and loving, yet possessing an authority that stems from her wisdom and life experiences. Beverly is characterized as a prolific writer-director and serves as a matriarch, suggesting that her role will be central to the narrative's exploration of family dynamics and personal growth.
