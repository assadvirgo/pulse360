## Ron Perlman Joins Prime Video's 'Cross' for Season 3

Renowned actor Ron Perlman is set to make a significant return to television, having joined the cast of Prime Video's series "Cross" for its upcoming third season. This announcement marks a notable reunion between Perlman and showrunner Ben Watkins, with whom he previously collaborated on the critically acclaimed series "Hand of God."

### Perlman's Role in 'Cross'

In "Cross," Perlman will take on the recurring role of Herschel Zamora, a character characterized as a dedicated police officer who prioritizes his duties above all else. The narrative surrounding Officer Zamora suggests a complex individual grappling with the repercussions of various troubling incidents within his professional life. This role promises to showcase Perlman's extensive acting range, as he navigates the challenges faced by a law enforcement official in a turbulent environment.

### Background on 'Cross'

"Cross" is a series that has garnered attention for its engaging storytelling and character development. The show delves into themes of morality, justice, and the personal struggles of its characters, making it a compelling watch for audiences. The addition of Perlman is expected to enhance the series' depth, as his previous performances have often left a lasting impact on viewers.

### A Reunion with Ben Watkins

The collaboration between Perlman and Watkins is particularly noteworthy, given their history on "Hand of God." This earlier project allowed both the actor and showrunner to explore complex narratives and character arcs, setting a precedent for what audiences can expect in "Cross." Perlman's involvement is anticipated to bring a new dynamic to the series, potentially enriching the storyline and character interactions.

As fans eagerly await the premiere of Season 3, the inclusion of Perlman is likely to generate excitement among viewers. His reputation as a versatile actor, known for roles in iconic productions such as "Sons of Anarchy" and "Hellboy," positions him as a strong addition to the ensemble cast of "Cross." 

The upcoming season is expected to delve deeper into the lives of its characters, with Perlman's Herschel Zamora potentially serving as a pivotal figure in the unfolding drama.
