## Amazon MGM Studios to Release Indian Film ‘Vibe’ in Theatres

Amazon MGM Studios has announced the theatrical release of the Indian action-comedy film **Vibe**, set to hit cinemas on **September 18, 2023**. This film marks a significant addition to the Indian film landscape, showcasing the growing collaboration between global streaming giants and local filmmakers.

### Production Details

**Vibe** is produced by Kunal Kemmu and Chirag Nihalani under their production banner, **Drongo Films**. The film promises to deliver a unique blend of humor and action, focusing on the dynamic relationship between two inseparable friends. As their seemingly ordinary lives take an unexpected turn, the narrative explores themes of friendship, survival, and adventure.

### Plot Overview

The storyline revolves around two friends whose mundane existence is transformed into a high-energy escapade. As they navigate through unforeseen challenges, the film delves into how their bond is tested and ultimately strengthened.
