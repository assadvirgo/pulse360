## Picket Line Forms on Set of Jonathan Majors' Action Film in South Carolina

A picket line has emerged on the set of an untitled action film starring Jonathan Majors, produced by The Daily Wire and Dallas Sonnier's Bonfire Legend, in Gaffney, South Carolina. The situation escalated late last week when crew members walked off the job, prompting the International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees (IATSE) to call for a strike against the production.

### Labor Issues Prompt Walkout

The strike has been attributed to a series of labor-related grievances that have reportedly affected the working conditions of the crew. While specific details regarding the nature of these issues have not been disclosed, the involvement of IATSE suggests that the concerns may relate to standard labor practices, including working hours, pay, and safety conditions on set.

IATSE has a long history of advocating for the rights and welfare of workers in the entertainment industry, and their decision to strike indicates significant dissatisfaction among the crew. The union's actions reflect broader tensions within the film industry, particularly as productions ramp up following pandemic-related slowdowns.

### Production Impact and Response

As the picket line continues, the impact on the film's production schedule remains uncertain. Productions often rely heavily on the cooperation of their crews, and a walkout can lead to significant delays and increased costs. The production team has yet to release a public statement addressing the strike or outlining their plans to resolve the ongoing issues.

The involvement of high-profile talent like Jonathan Majors adds an additional layer of scrutiny to the situation. Majors, known for his work in projects such as "Lovecraft Country" and the Marvel Cinematic Universe, may face questions regarding his stance on labor issues as the situation develops.

This incident is part of a larger narrative concerning labor relations in the film industry, which has seen increased activism and unionization efforts in recent years.
