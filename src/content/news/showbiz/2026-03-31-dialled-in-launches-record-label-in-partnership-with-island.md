## Dialled In Launches Record Label to Promote South Asian Artists

In a significant move for the South Asian music scene, cultural platform Dialled In has officially launched Dialled In Records, a new record label based in London. This initiative aims to support and promote South Asian artists across various musical genres, marking a pivotal moment for representation in the industry.

### Partnership with Island-EMI’s The Collective

The establishment of Dialled In Records comes through a strategic partnership with The Collective, an imprint under Island-EMI, which is part of the Universal Music Group in the United Kingdom. This collaboration is poised to leverage the extensive resources and expertise of one of the world's leading music companies, providing a robust platform for emerging South Asian talent.

### Inaugural Signings

The label's first signings include Excise Dept and Ahadadream, two artists who embody the innovative spirit and diverse sounds that Dialled In Records aims to champion. Ahadadream, in particular, has garnered attention for his unique blend of genres and has been recognized as a promising figure in the contemporary South Asian music landscape.

### A Commitment to Diversity

Dialled In's mission is to create a space where South Asian artists can thrive and reach wider audiences. The label seeks to break down barriers and foster a more inclusive music industry, reflecting the rich tapestry of South Asian culture. By focusing on artists from various backgrounds and musical styles, Dialled In Records aims to curate a diverse roster that resonates with a global audience.

### The Future of South Asian Music

The launch of Dialled In Records is seen as a timely development in the context of growing interest in South Asian music worldwide. As global audiences become increasingly receptive to diverse sounds, the label is positioned to play a crucial role in elevating South Asian artists on international platforms. This initiative not only promotes individual artists but also contributes to the broader recognition of South Asian culture in the global music scene.

### Conclusion

With its commitment to nurturing talent and fostering diversity, Dialled In Records represents a significant step forward for South Asian artists in the UK and beyond. As the label embarks on this journey, it will undoubtedly be watched closely by industry insiders and music enthusiasts alike, eager to see how it shapes the future of South Asian music.
