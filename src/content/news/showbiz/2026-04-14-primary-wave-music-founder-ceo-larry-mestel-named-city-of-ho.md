## Larry Mestel to Receive City of Hope’s Spirit of Life Award

City of Hope, a leading cancer research and treatment organization in the United States, has announced that Larry Mestel, the Founder and CEO of Primary Wave Music, will be honored with the prestigious Spirit of Life Award for the Music, Film, and Entertainment Industry. The award ceremony is scheduled to take place on October 27, 2026, at a gala event that promises to celebrate significant contributions to the arts and philanthropy.

## Recognizing Contributions to Music and Philanthropy

The Spirit of Life Award is a distinguished recognition presented by City of Hope to individuals who have made noteworthy contributions to their respective fields while also demonstrating a commitment to philanthropy and community service. Mestel’s selection for this honor underscores his influential role in the music industry and his dedication to charitable initiatives.

As the head of Primary Wave Music, Mestel has been instrumental in reshaping the landscape of music publishing and artist management. Under his leadership, the company has grown to become a prominent player in the industry, known for its innovative approach to music rights and artist development. Mestel's vision has not only fostered the growth of numerous artists but has also paved the way for new business models in the entertainment sector.

## City of Hope: A Leader in Cancer Research

City of Hope is recognized for its groundbreaking work in cancer research, treatment, and education. The organization has been at the forefront of developing new therapies and improving patient care, making significant strides in the fight against cancer. The Spirit of Life Award gala serves as a vital fundraising event, with proceeds supporting City of Hope’s mission to advance medical research and provide compassionate care to patients and their families.

The selection of Mestel as the honoree reflects City of Hope’s commitment to recognizing individuals who not only excel in their professional endeavors but also contribute positively to society. Mestel’s philanthropic efforts align with the organization’s values, making him a fitting recipient of this honor.

## A Celebration of Music and Hope

The upcoming gala is expected to attract a wide array of attendees from the music, film, and entertainment industries, as well as supporters of City of Hope’s mission. It will serve as a platform to celebrate the intersection of art and philanthropy, highlighting how individuals in the entertainment sector can leverage their influence for the greater good.

In a statement regarding the award, Mestel expressed his gratitude and emphasized the importance of supporting organizations like City of Hope. He noted that the fight against cancer is a cause that resonates deeply with him, and he is honored to contribute to such a vital mission.

As the date of the gala approaches, anticipation builds around the event and the impact it will have on raising awareness and funds for cancer research. Mestel’s recognition as the Spirit of Life Honoree not only celebrates his achievements but also inspires others in the industry to engage in philanthropic efforts that can lead to meaningful change.

In conclusion, Larry Mestel’s upcoming honor at the City of Hope gala represents a significant acknowledgment of his contributions to the music industry and his commitment to philanthropy. The event promises to be a memorable evening, reinforcing the vital role of the arts in supporting critical causes such as cancer research and patient care.
