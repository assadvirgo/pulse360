## Wheelhouse’s Spoke Studios Elevates Gardner Reed to President

In a significant move for the entertainment industry, Wheelhouse's production label, Spoke Studios, has announced the promotion of Gardner Reed to the position of President. This decision comes shortly after the successful launch of their new Netflix reality series, *Calabasas Confidential*, which premiered just last week.

### A Steady Ascent

Gardner Reed has been an integral part of Spoke Studios since its inception in 2018. His journey within the company has been marked by a series of strategic roles that have showcased his leadership and vision. Prior to this recent promotion, Reed held the title of Senior Vice President of Development, a position he ascended to in 2024. His contributions to the studio have been pivotal, particularly in the development of *Calabasas Confidential*, a project that has already begun to capture audience attention.

### The Impact of *Calabasas Confidential*

*Calabasas Confidential* is poised to be a defining moment for both Reed and Spoke Studios. The reality series offers a glimpse into the lives of individuals residing in Calabasas, California, a community known for its celebrity residents and affluent lifestyle. The show's launch has been met with anticipation, and its reception could further solidify Spoke Studios' reputation in the competitive landscape of reality television.

### Future Directions

As President, Reed will be responsible for overseeing the studio's production slate and strategic direction. His promotion signals Wheelhouse's confidence in his ability to lead the company into its next phase of growth. With Reed at the helm, industry observers are keen to see how Spoke Studios will evolve and what new projects will emerge under his leadership.

### Industry Reactions

The promotion has garnered attention within the entertainment sector, with many industry insiders expressing optimism about Reed's capabilities. His track record in developing engaging content aligns with the current trends in television, where innovative storytelling and relatable characters are paramount. 

### Conclusion

Gardner Reed's elevation to President of Spoke Studios marks a significant milestone not only for him but also for the company as it navigates the evolving landscape of entertainment.
