## President Trump Addresses Security Incident at White House Correspondents’ Dinner

In an unexpected turn of events, President Donald Trump addressed the media following a security incident at the White House Correspondents’ Dinner, which took place at the Washington Hilton. The event was disrupted by gunfire, prompting an immediate evacuation of attendees, including the President and First Lady Melania Trump.

### The Incident

The evening began as a celebration of journalism, with numerous prominent figures from the media and political spheres in attendance. However, the atmosphere shifted dramatically when gunshots were reported. The Secret Service swiftly acted to ensure the safety of the President and other guests, leading to a prompt evacuation of the venue.

In a press conference held approximately one hour after the incident, President Trump expressed his surprise at the unfolding situation. “That was unexpected,” he remarked, acknowledging the gravity of the moment. 

### Security Concerns

During his address, Trump emphasized the inherent risks associated with the profession of journalism, stating, “It’s a dangerous profession.” His comments reflect a broader concern regarding the safety of journalists, especially in an era marked by heightened tensions between the media and political figures. The President’s remarks come amidst a climate where threats against journalists have been increasingly reported, raising questions about their safety while performing their duties.

### Response and Reactions
