## Passage Pictures Acquires Rights to ‘In the Blue’ by Delilah Napier and Lucy Powers

Uri Singer's Passage Pictures has announced the acquisition of the rights to the upcoming feature film "In the Blue," directed and written by the filmmaking duo Delilah Napier and Lucy Powers. This marks another significant step in the careers of the two filmmakers, who have been gaining recognition in the independent film circuit.

### Background on the Filmmakers

Delilah Napier and Lucy Powers have made a name for themselves with their unique storytelling and artistic vision. Their previous feature, "Floating Carousel," premiered at the Woodstock Film Festival earlier this year, where it was honored with the Ultra Indie Award for Best Narrative Feature. This accolade not only highlights their talent but also positions them as emerging voices in contemporary cinema.

Their first feature film, "Voyeur," established a foundation for their collaborative work, showcasing their ability to tackle complex themes with sensitivity and depth. With "In the Blue," they aim to continue this trend, exploring new narratives that resonate with audiences.

### About ‘In the Blue’

While specific plot details for "In the Blue" have yet to be disclosed, the film is anticipated to reflect the duo's signature style, which often blends emotional storytelling with visually compelling cinematography. The partnership with Passage Pictures, a company known for supporting innovative and thought-provoking films, is expected to enhance the production quality and reach of their latest project.

Passage Pictures, under the leadership of Uri Singer, has been instrumental in bringing independent films to broader audiences. The company is recognized for its commitment to nurturing creative talent and producing films that challenge the status quo. With "In the Blue," Passage Pictures continues its mission to support diverse voices in filmmaking, providing a platform for Napier and Powers to express their artistic vision.
