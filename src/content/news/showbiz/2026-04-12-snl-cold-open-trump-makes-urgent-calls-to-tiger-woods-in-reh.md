## ‘SNL’ Cold Open Features Trump in Absurd Phone Calls

In the latest episode of "Saturday Night Live" (SNL), comedian James Austin Johnson reprised his role as former President Donald Trump, delivering a humorous cold open that featured a series of increasingly absurd phone calls. The segment, which aired on Saturday, showcased Trump reaching out to various public figures, including professional golfer Tiger Woods, former First Lady Melania Trump, and Fox News personality Pete Hegseth.

### A Humorous Take on Current Events

The cold open began with Trump making an "urgent" call to Woods, portrayed by Kenan Thompson. From the Oval Office, Trump inquired about Woods' well-being and his potential participation in upcoming golf events. The comedic exchange highlighted Trump's characteristic bravado and penchant for blending personal and political matters, setting the tone for the rest of the skit.

As the segment progressed, Johnson's Trump character continued to dial up Melania Trump, played by Chloe Fineman. The interaction was marked by a satirical take on the former First Lady's public persona and her reactions to Trump's antics. This portrayal was particularly relevant given the ongoing discussions surrounding the former couple's relationship in light of recent events.
