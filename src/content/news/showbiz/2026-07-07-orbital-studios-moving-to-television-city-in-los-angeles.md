## Orbital Studios Relocates to Television City in Los Angeles

Virtual production company Orbital Studios has announced its relocation to Television City in Los Angeles, marking a significant milestone in its growth since its establishment in 2020. The company, known for its innovative approach to virtual production, aims to enhance its capabilities and expand its influence in the entertainment industry with this new brick-and-mortar facility.

### A New Era for Orbital Studios

Orbital Studios has made a name for itself in the virtual production landscape, providing art teams, advanced tools, software, and efficient pipelines designed to streamline the production process. The company's portfolio includes notable projects such as Netflix's "Nemesis" and FX's upcoming series "The Drop: A Snowfall Saga." The move to Television City is expected to bolster its production capabilities and facilitate collaboration with a broader range of industry professionals.

### Advantages of the New Location

Television City, a historic studio complex in Los Angeles, has long been a hub for television production and innovation. The relocation offers Orbital Studios access to state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant creative community. This strategic move is anticipated to enhance the company's operational efficiency and foster partnerships with other content creators and production companies in the area.

The new facility will provide Orbital Studios with the necessary infrastructure to support its growing client base and meet the increasing demand for virtual production services. As the industry continues to evolve, the ability to adapt and innovate is crucial for success, and this move positions Orbital Studios to stay ahead of the curve.

### The Future of Virtual Production

Virtual production has gained significant traction in recent years, particularly in response to the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. The technology allows filmmakers to create immersive environments and visual effects in real-time, reducing the need for extensive post-production work. As the demand for high-quality content continues to rise, companies like Orbital Studios are at the forefront of this transformation.

With its new headquarters, Orbital Studios is poised to further develop its offerings and contribute to the evolution of virtual production techniques. The company’s commitment to making production faster and more efficient aligns with the industry's growing emphasis on agility and responsiveness.

### Conclusion

Orbital Studios' move to Television City represents a pivotal moment for the company and the broader virtual production industry. As it settles into its new home, the studio is expected to leverage its resources and expertise to drive innovation and collaboration in the entertainment sector. The relocation not only underscores the company's growth but also highlights the ongoing evolution of production methodologies in an increasingly digital landscape.
