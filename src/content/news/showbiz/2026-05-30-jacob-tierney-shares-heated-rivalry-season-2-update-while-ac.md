## Jacob Tierney Provides Update on 'Heated Rivalry' Season 2 at Critics Choice LGBTQ Showrunner Award Ceremony

Jacob Tierney, the acclaimed creator of the popular series "Heated Rivalry," recently shared insights into the much-anticipated second season of the show while accepting the Critics Choice LGBTQ Showrunner Award. The event took place on Friday at the 3rd Critics Choice Celebration of LGBTQ+ Cinema & Television in Los Angeles, where Tierney was recognized for his contributions to the representation of LGBTQ+ narratives in media.

### Acknowledging Success and Future Endeavors

As "Heated Rivalry" continues to garner accolades for its inaugural season, Tierney expressed gratitude for the recognition and emphasized his commitment to delivering an engaging second season. The show, which streams on Crave and HBO Max, has resonated with audiences and critics alike, earning praise for its authentic portrayal of LGBTQ+ relationships and the complexities of rivalry in the world of sports.

In his acceptance speech, Tierney remarked, “We are working very hard” on the upcoming season, hinting at the dedication and effort being poured into the project. He did not disclose specific plot details or release dates but assured fans that the creative team is focused on maintaining the quality and depth that characterized the first season.

Since its debut, "Heated Rivalry" has not only entertained viewers but has also sparked conversations about representation in the entertainment industry. The series has been lauded for its nuanced storytelling and diverse characters, contributing to a broader understanding of LGBTQ+ experiences in contemporary society.

Tierney's recognition at the Critics Choice Celebration underscores the importance of such narratives in fostering inclusivity and visibility.
