In a stunning conclusion to the season, Ashlee Simpson was unmasked as Galaxy Girl, claiming the coveted golden 'Masked' trophy. This victory marks a significant moment for Simpson, who has been a prominent figure in the entertainment industry for years. Her performance throughout the season captivated audiences, showcasing her vocal talents and stage presence.
