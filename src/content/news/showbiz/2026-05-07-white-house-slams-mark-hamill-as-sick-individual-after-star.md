## White House Responds to Mark Hamill's Controversial Post

In a recent incident that has drawn significant attention, actor Mark Hamill, best known for his role as Luke Skywalker in the "Star Wars" franchise, faced backlash following a controversial post on his social media account. The post, which depicted a graphic image of former President Donald Trump lying in front of a gravestone, was met with strong condemnation from the White House.

### The Controversial Post

The image, which was shared on Hamill's Bluesky account, sparked outrage among various political circles and social media users. Critics quickly labeled the post as inappropriate and offensive, prompting a swift response from the White House. In a statement, officials described Hamill as a "sick individual," emphasizing that such imagery is unacceptable regardless of political affiliation.

### Hamill's Clarification

Following the backlash, Hamill deleted the image and issued a clarification in a subsequent post. He stated, "Accurate Edit for Clarity: 'He should live long enough to… be held accountable for his… crimes.' Actually, I was wishing him the opposite of dead." This attempt to clarify his intentions has not fully mitigated the controversy, as many continue to debate the appropriateness of his original post.

### Public Reaction
