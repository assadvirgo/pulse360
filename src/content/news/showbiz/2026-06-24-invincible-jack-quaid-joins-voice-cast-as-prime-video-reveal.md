## Jack Quaid Joins Voice Cast of "Invincible" as Prime Video Announces Early Season 6 Renewal

In an exciting development for fans of the animated series "Invincible," Prime Video has confirmed that actor Jack Quaid will be joining the voice cast for Season 5. The announcement was made during Prime Video's inaugural panel at the Annecy Animation Festival, where executive producer and co-showrunner Robert Kirkman shared the news alongside a first-look image of Quaid's character.

### Jack Quaid's Role as Gravitator

Jack Quaid, known for his roles in "The Boys" and "Scream," will lend his voice to the character Gravitator, also known as Chris. This addition to the cast is expected to bring a fresh dynamic to the series, which has garnered a dedicated fan base since its debut. The character Gravitator is known for his unique abilities, which are likely to play a significant role in the unfolding narrative of the series.

### Early Renewal for Season 6

In a move that underscores the show's popularity and critical acclaim, Prime Video has also announced an early renewal for a sixth season. This decision reflects the platform's confidence in the series and its ability to engage audiences with compelling storytelling and dynamic character arcs. The early renewal is a testament to the show's success and the anticipation surrounding its upcoming seasons.
