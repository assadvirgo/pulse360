## Sean Baker Secures Major Deal for Follow-Up Film at Warner Bros.

Oscar-winning filmmaker Sean Baker has reached a significant milestone in his career with a lucrative deal for his upcoming project, "Ti Amo!" This follows the critical and commercial success of his previous film, "Anora," which won four Academy Awards in 2025, including Best Picture. The new agreement with Warner Bros. label Clockwork marks a pivotal moment for Baker, both artistically and financially.

### A New Chapter in Independent Filmmaking

Baker, known for his distinctive storytelling and focus on marginalized communities, has carved out a unique niche in the independent film landscape. His previous works, including "Tangerine" and "The Florida Project," have garnered acclaim for their authentic portrayals of life on the fringes of society. With "Anora," Baker not only captivated audiences but also set a new standard for independent filmmaking by achieving unprecedented recognition at the Oscars.

The success of "Anora" has paved the way for Baker's latest project, "Ti Amo!" which is anticipated to explore themes of love and resilience in a contemporary setting. The film is expected to maintain Baker's signature blend of humor and pathos, appealing to both critics and general audiences alike.
