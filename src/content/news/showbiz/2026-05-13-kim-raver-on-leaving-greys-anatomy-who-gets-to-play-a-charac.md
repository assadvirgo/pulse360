## Kim Raver Reflects on Departure from ‘Grey’s Anatomy’

Kim Raver, known for her role as Dr. Teddy Altman on ABC's long-running medical drama "Grey's Anatomy," has announced her departure from the series following the season 22 finale. Alongside co-star Kevin McKidd, Raver's exit marks a significant change for the show, which has captivated audiences for nearly two decades.

### Emotional Farewell

In a recent statement, Raver expressed her deep emotional connection to the character she portrayed. "Letting go of Teddy is a deeply felt and an incredibly emotional journey," she remarked, highlighting the profound impact the role has had on her life and career. Raver's portrayal of Teddy has been a staple of the series since her introduction in season 6, and her character has undergone significant development, navigating complex relationships and personal challenges.

### A Legacy in Television

Raver's tenure on "Grey's Anatomy" is notable not only for its length but also for the evolution of her character. Teddy Altman has been involved in some of the show's most pivotal storylines, earning a dedicated fanbase and contributing to the series' ongoing success. Raver's ability to bring depth and nuance to her role has been widely praised, making her departure a poignant moment for both the actress and the viewers.

The question of longevity in television roles is one that resonates with many actors, and Raver's experience serves as a testament to the unique opportunities afforded by long-running series. "Who gets to play a character for this long?" she pondered, reflecting on the rarity of such experiences in the entertainment industry. Her journey with the show has not only shaped her career but also left an indelible mark on the series itself.
