## Monica Lewinsky to be Honored with Woman of 21st Century Award

Monica Lewinsky, a prominent figure known for her past involvement in a high-profile political scandal, is set to receive the Woman of 21st Century Award at the upcoming Women’s Guild Cedars-Sinai Luncheon. The event will take place on May 7 at the iconic Beverly Hills Hotel and promises to be a significant gathering celebrating women's achievements and contributions.

## Event Details

The luncheon will be hosted by Nischelle Turner, a well-known personality from Entertainment Tonight. Attendees can anticipate a compelling conversation between Lewinsky and Jane Buckingham, an accomplished author and founder of the marketing consultancy Trendera. This dialogue is expected to delve into Lewinsky's experiences and insights, reflecting on her journey and the broader implications of her story in contemporary society.

## Acknowledging Resilience and Advocacy

Lewinsky has transformed her narrative over the years, moving from a figure often defined by scandal to one recognized for her advocacy against cyberbullying and her efforts to empower women. Her candid discussions about mental health, public scrutiny, and the complexities of her past have resonated with many, making her an influential voice in conversations about personal agency and resilience.

The Woman of 21st Century Award is a testament to Lewinsky's evolution and her commitment to social issues, particularly those affecting women. The award aims to honor individuals who have made significant contributions to society and have inspired others through their actions and advocacy.

## Significance of the Luncheon

The Women’s Guild Cedars-Sinai Luncheon is an annual event that celebrates the achievements of women across various fields. It serves as a platform not only to recognize individual accomplishments but also to raise awareness and funds for important causes. Cedars-Sinai, a leading healthcare institution, benefits from the support generated at this event, which contributes to its mission of providing exceptional patient care and advancing medical research.

The upcoming luncheon is expected to attract a diverse audience, including leaders from various sectors, advocates for women's rights, and members of the community who are eager to engage with the topics of empowerment and social change.

Monica Lewinsky's recognition as the Woman of 21st Century at the Women’s Guild Cedars-Sinai Luncheon highlights her transformation into a figure of resilience and advocacy.
