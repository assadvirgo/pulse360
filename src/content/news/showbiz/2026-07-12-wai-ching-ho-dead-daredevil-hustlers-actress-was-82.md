## Wai Ching Ho, Acclaimed Actress Known for Madame Gao, Passes Away at 82

Wai Ching Ho, a distinguished actress celebrated for her role as Madame Gao in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, has passed away at the age of 82. Her contributions to film and television have left a lasting impact, particularly through her work in popular series such as "Daredevil" and "Hustlers."

### A Legacy in the Marvel Cinematic Universe

Wai Ching Ho gained widespread recognition for her portrayal of Madame Gao, a character introduced in the Netflix series "Daredevil," which aired from 2015 to 2016. Madame Gao was a formidable figure within the show's narrative, serving as a co-founder of the nefarious organization known as The Hand. Ho's performance was noted for its depth and complexity, contributing significantly to the series' exploration of morality and power dynamics.

Her co-star, Peter Shinkoda, who played Kagenobu Yoshioka, a fellow member of The Hand, expressed his sorrow over her passing on social media. Shinkoda's tribute reflects the sentiments of many who admired Ho not only for her talent but also for her warm spirit and kindness off-screen.
