## ‘Jimmy Kimmel Live’ Maintains Dominance in Late Night Ratings

In a notable achievement for the ABC network, "Jimmy Kimmel Live" has secured the top position in late-night television for the third consecutive week among adults aged 18 to 49. According to Nielsen live-plus-same-day data for the week of March 9, the show delivered a rating of 0.16 in this key demographic, reinforcing its status as a leading choice for viewers in the competitive late-night landscape.
