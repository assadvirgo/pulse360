Langston Kerman, an emerging talent in the comedy scene, has been cast in NBC's upcoming untitled comedy pilot, which is generating anticipation within the television industry. ### Role and Character Overview

In this new pilot, Kerman will portray Justin Royal, a character described as "a great big teddy bear of a man." Justin works as a private investigator at an agency run by his father, Julius Royal, played by veteran actor Keith David. This father-son dynamic is expected to be a central theme of the show, providing both comedic and heartfelt moments as the characters navigate their personal and professional lives.

Dan Goor and Luke Del Tredici bring a wealth of experience to the project.
