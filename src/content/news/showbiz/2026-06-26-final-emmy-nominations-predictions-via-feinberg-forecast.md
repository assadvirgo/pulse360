## Final Emmy Nominations Predictions via Feinberg Forecast

As the entertainment industry gears up for the upcoming Emmy Awards, anticipation is building around the nominations that will be revealed soon. The Hollywood Reporter's executive editor of awards coverage, Scott Feinberg, has released his predictions for the nominees across 63 categories, providing insights into potential frontrunners, alternates, and surprises.

### Projected Nominees

Feinberg's forecast highlights a range of shows and performances that are expected to earn nominations this year. Among the leading contenders are critically acclaimed series such as "Succession," "The Crown," and "Ted Lasso," which have garnered both audience acclaim and industry recognition. These shows are likely to dominate key categories including Outstanding Drama Series and Outstanding Comedy Series.

In the acting categories, Feinberg identifies several standout performances that could secure nominations. For the Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series, he points to Brian Cox from "Succession" and Jeremy Strong, also from the same series, as strong contenders. In the comedy realm, Jason Sudeikis from "Ted Lasso" remains a favorite for his portrayal of the lovable coach.

### Alternates and Potential Surprises

While Feinberg's predictions focus on frontrunners, he also highlights alternates who could potentially shake up the nominations. Shows like "The White Lotus" and "Yellowjackets" are mentioned as possible dark horses that may surprise audiences and critics alike. These series have gained a significant following and critical praise, making them worthy of consideration in various categories.

Feinberg also notes that the Emmy Awards are known for their unpredictability, and this year may be no different. Viewers can expect a few unexpected nominations that may not align with the mainstream favorites. This unpredictability is part of what makes the Emmy Awards a highly anticipated event in the entertainment calendar.

### "Shoulda Been a Contenda" Selections

In addition to his predictions, Feinberg includes a section dedicated to performances and shows that he believes deserve recognition but may not receive it. These "shoulda been a contenda" selections often spark conversations among fans and critics about the nature of awards recognition and the competitiveness of the industry. Notable mentions in this category may include performances from lesser-known series or actors who have not received the spotlight they deserve.

### Conclusion

As the Emmy nominations approach, Scott Feinberg's forecast serves as a valuable guide for fans and industry insiders alike. His insights into projected nominees, potential surprises, and overlooked performances provide a comprehensive overview of the current landscape of television. The final nominations will undoubtedly spark discussions and debates, reflecting the dynamic and ever-evolving nature of the entertainment industry. As the date draws nearer, all eyes will be on the Emmy Awards to see which shows and performances will ultimately be recognized for their excellence in television.
