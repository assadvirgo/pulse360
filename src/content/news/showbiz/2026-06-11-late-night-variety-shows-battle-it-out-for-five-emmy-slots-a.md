## Late-Night & Variety Shows Compete for Emmy Recognition Following Category Merger

The landscape of television awards is undergoing a significant transformation as the Television Academy has merged two major categories for the upcoming 2026 Emmy Awards. This change has sparked renewed competition among late-night and variety shows, which now vie for a single award in the newly established Outstanding Variety Series category.

### The Merger of Categories

Historically, the Emmy Awards recognized excellence in television through distinct categories such as Outstanding Talk Series and Outstanding Scripted Variety Series. However, in a move aimed at streamlining the awards process and reflecting the evolving nature of television programming, the Academy has decided to combine these categories into one. This decision has been met with mixed reactions from industry insiders, with some expressing concern over the dilution of recognition for individual formats.

### Implications for Nominees

With the merging of categories, the competition for nominations has intensified. Shows that previously competed in their own categories must now contend with a broader range of programming. This shift could potentially alter the dynamics of nominations and wins, as shows that may have been overlooked in the past now have a chance to compete against a wider array of peers.

### Industry Reactions

The decision to merge these categories has prompted discussions among producers, writers, and fans alike. Many industry professionals believe that the change reflects the current state of television, where the lines between talk shows, scripted content, and variety programming are increasingly blurred. Others, however, worry that this consolidation may lead to a lack of recognition for niche shows that cater to specific audiences.

### The Road Ahead

As the nominations for the 2026 Emmy Awards approach, anticipation is building around which shows will stand out in this newly consolidated category. The Outstanding Variety Series category is expected to feature a diverse array of programming, from traditional late-night talk shows to innovative scripted variety formats. This variety not only highlights the creativity within the industry but also underscores the challenges faced by voters tasked with selecting the nominees.

### Conclusion

The merging of the Outstanding Talk Series and Outstanding Scripted Variety Series categories into the Outstanding Variety Series category marks a significant shift in the Emmy Awards landscape. As late-night and variety shows prepare for the upcoming nominations, the industry watches closely to see how this change will impact the recognition of talent and innovation in television. The outcome will likely set the tone for future Emmy Awards and reflect the ongoing evolution of the medium.
