## Jerrold Tarog's Upcoming Horror Film "Knock Three Times" Features Star-Studded Cast

Filmmaker Jerrold Tarog is set to make waves in the horror genre with his latest project, "Knock Three Times." The film, produced by CreaZion Studios, boasts an impressive cast that includes prominent Filipino actors Julia Barretto, Charo Santos-Concio, Christopher de Leon, and Tony Labrusca. The announcement comes just ahead of the Cannes Film Market, heightening anticipation for the film's debut.

### Plot Overview

"Knock Three Times" centers around a young woman who returns home after completing a rehabilitation program. Upon her return, she discovers that her mother has become entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences. The narrative promises to explore themes of familial bonds and personal redemption while delving into the eerie and unsettling aspects of horror.

### A Talented Ensemble

The casting of Barretto, Santos-Concio, De Leon, and Labrusca brings together a mix of established and emerging talents in the Philippine film industry. Julia Barretto, known for her dynamic performances in various romantic dramas, is expected to showcase her versatility in this darker role. Charo Santos-Concio, a veteran actress and former president of ABS-CBN, adds gravitas to the film with her extensive experience in both acting and producing. Christopher de Leon, a stalwart of Philippine cinema, and Tony Labrusca, a rising star, round out the cast, promising a compelling dynamic that will engage audiences.

### Director Jerrold Tarog

Jerrold Tarog has garnered acclaim for his work across various genres, from historical dramas to thrillers. His previous films, such as "Heneral Luna" and "Goyo: Ang Batang Heneral," have showcased his ability to blend storytelling with cultural commentary. With "Knock Three Times," Tarog aims to carve out a niche in the horror landscape, a genre that has seen a resurgence in popularity in recent years.

### The Horror Genre in Philippine Cinema

Horror films have been gaining traction in the Philippines, with audiences increasingly drawn to stories that reflect both local folklore and contemporary anxieties. "Knock Three Times" is poised to contribute to this trend, potentially appealing to both domestic and international viewers. The film's exploration of personal struggles against a backdrop of supernatural elements may resonate with audiences seeking both entertainment and deeper meaning.

### Anticipation for Cannes Film Market

As the Cannes Film Market approaches, the buzz surrounding "Knock Three Times" is expected to grow. The film's participation in this prestigious event could open doors for international distribution and collaboration, further elevating the profile of Philippine cinema on the global stage.

### Conclusion

With a compelling premise and a talented cast led by Jerrold Tarog, "Knock Three Times" is shaping up to be a noteworthy addition to the horror genre in Philippine film. As audiences await its release, the film has the potential to not only entertain but also provoke thought regarding the complexities of family and the human experience in the face of adversity.
