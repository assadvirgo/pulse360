## NBC Announces Season-Finale Dates for Popular Shows

As the spring season unfolds, NBC has revealed the season-finale dates for several of its flagship programs as the 2025-26 television season approaches its conclusion. This announcement comes as fans of the network's popular shows anticipate the climactic moments that will wrap up their favorite storylines.

### Highlights from NBC's Lineup

Among the shows set to conclude their seasons are the well-established dramas from the "Chicago" franchise, including "Chicago Fire," "Chicago P.D.," and "Chicago Med." These series have garnered a loyal following over the years, and their season finales are expected to deliver high-stakes drama and emotional resolutions.

In addition to the "Chicago" series, NBC's long-running "Law & Order" franchise will also air its season finales. The original "Law & Order" series, along with its spin-offs "Law & Order: SVU" and "Law & Order: Organized Crime," have become staples of the network's programming, known for their gripping narratives and timely social commentary.

### Competition and Comedy

The singing competition "The Voice," a mainstay in NBC's lineup, will also be concluding its current season. This show has consistently attracted viewers with its unique format and the opportunity for aspiring artists to showcase their talents. Fans can expect an exciting finale that highlights the journey of the contestants and the performances that have captivated audiences throughout the season.

Additionally, NBC's newer sitcoms, "St. Denis Medical" and "Happy's Place," will be wrapping up their seasons as well. These shows have contributed to NBC's diverse programming slate, offering comedic relief amidst the more dramatic offerings.

### Viewing Schedule

While specific dates for each finale have yet to be detailed in the announcement, fans are encouraged to stay tuned for updates as the network finalizes its schedule. The anticipation surrounding these season finales is palpable, as viewers look forward to the resolutions and cliffhangers that will set the stage for the next season.

### Conclusion

As NBC prepares to close out another successful season, the network's lineup continues to reflect its commitment to delivering engaging content across various genres. The season finales for the "Chicago" and "Law & Order" series, along with "The Voice" and its sitcoms, promise to be significant television events that will keep audiences on the edge of their seats.

As the final episodes approach, viewers are reminded to mark their calendars and prepare for an exciting conclusion to the 2025-26 TV season on NBC.
