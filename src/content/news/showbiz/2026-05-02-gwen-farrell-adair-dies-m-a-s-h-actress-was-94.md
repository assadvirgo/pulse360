## Gwen Farrell Adair, Beloved Actress from M*A*S*H, Passes Away at 94

Gwen Farrell Adair, the talented actress renowned for her roles as various nurses on the iconic television series M*A*S*H, has passed away at the age of 94. Her son confirmed that she died of natural causes on Thursday in Sherman Oaks, California.

Born in Austin, Texas, Adair's journey into the world of acting began with her onscreen debut in the second episode of M*A*S*H, which aired in 1972. Adair's contributions to the series, particularly through her portrayals of various nurses, helped to enrich the show's narrative and provided audiences with a glimpse into the lives of those serving in a war zone.
