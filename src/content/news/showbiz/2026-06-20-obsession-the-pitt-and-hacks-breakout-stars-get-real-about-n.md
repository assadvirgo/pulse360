## Rising Stars Discuss Industry Challenges and Opportunities at SAG-AFTRA Event

A group of emerging talents from popular series such as "Obsession," "The Pitt," and "Euphoria" convened on Thursday for the inaugural First Act: Summer Soiree event hosted by the SAG-AFTRA Foundation. This gathering provided a platform for these actors to share their insights on the evolving landscape of the entertainment industry, particularly as it pertains to the influence of Generation Z.

### Navigating Industry Expectations

The panel featured notable breakout stars including Patrick Ball from "The Pitt," Megan Lawless from "Obsession," and Tonatiuh from "Kiss of the Spider Woman." Each participant spoke candidly about the pressures and expectations they face as young actors in Hollywood. The discussions highlighted the unique challenges posed by an industry that is rapidly changing, especially with the advent of technology and artificial intelligence.

Megan Lawless emphasized the hunger for innovation in storytelling, stating, "We are so hungry for something new." This sentiment resonated throughout the event, as the actors expressed a collective desire for more diverse narratives that reflect their generation's values and experiences.

### The Gen Z Influence

The conversation also touched on the impact of Generation Z on entertainment. As digital natives, this generation approaches content consumption and creation differently than previous generations. The panelists noted that Gen Z's preference for authenticity and relatability is reshaping how stories are told on screen. Patrick Ball remarked on the importance of representation, stating, "We need to see ourselves in the stories we tell."

The influence of social media was also a significant topic during the discussion. The actors acknowledged that platforms like TikTok and Instagram have transformed how they engage with audiences, allowing for more direct interaction and feedback. This shift has not only changed marketing strategies but has also influenced the types of projects that are being greenlit by studios.

### The Role of AI in Entertainment

As the conversation progressed, the role of artificial intelligence in the entertainment industry emerged as a critical point of discussion. The panelists expressed both excitement and concern regarding AI's potential to revolutionize content creation. While AI can streamline certain processes and enhance creativity, there are apprehensions about its implications for job security and the authenticity of artistic expression.

Tonatiuh articulated this duality, stating, "AI can be a tool for creativity, but we must be cautious about how it is implemented." The actors collectively advocated for a balance between leveraging technology and preserving the human element that is central to storytelling.

### Conclusion

The SAG-AFTRA Foundation's First Act: Summer Soiree served as an essential forum for rising stars to voice their perspectives on the future of the entertainment industry. As they navigate the complexities of Hollywood, the insights shared by these actors underscore a broader conversation about the need for innovation, representation, and ethical considerations in an increasingly digital age. Their commitment to pushing boundaries and seeking new narratives reflects a generation eager to redefine the landscape of entertainment.
