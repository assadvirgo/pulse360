## 'Salmokji: Whispering Water' Dominates South Korean Box Office

The local horror-thriller "Salmokji: Whispering Water" has demonstrated remarkable staying power at the South Korean box office over the weekend of April 24–26, 2026. According to the latest figures from KOBIS, the tracking service operated by the Korean Film Council, the film generated approximately $2.4 million from 343,461 admissions, securing a substantial 39.29% share of the weekend's box office revenue.

### Strong Opening and Continued Success

Since its release on April 8, "Salmokji: Whispering Water" has captivated audiences, establishing itself as a significant player in the current cinematic landscape. The film's blend of horror and thriller elements has resonated well with viewers, contributing to its impressive box office performance. The sustained interest in the film indicates a positive reception and word-of-mouth promotion, which are critical factors in the success of any film, particularly in the competitive environment of South Korea's film industry.
