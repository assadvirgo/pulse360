## Australia’s SBS Acquires Icelandic-Portuguese Drama Series ‘Cold Haven’

In a significant development for the Australian television landscape, the public service network SBS has secured the rights to the acclaimed Icelandic-Portuguese crime thriller series, *Cold Haven*. The series is set to premiere on SBS On Demand on July 15, following a distribution agreement with About Premium Content (APC).

### Overview of *Cold Haven*

*Cold Haven* is a gripping narrative that intertwines elements of crime and drama, centering around the character Maria, portrayed by Maria João Bastos. Maria is a Portuguese woman who relocates to Iceland, where she becomes embroiled in a series of chilling events that challenge her sense of safety and belonging in a foreign land. The show has garnered attention for its unique storytelling and atmospheric setting, utilizing Iceland's striking landscapes to enhance the narrative's tension and intrigue.

### Significance of the Acquisition

The acquisition of *Cold Haven* by SBS marks a notable addition to the network's growing portfolio of international content. SBS has a long-standing commitment to showcasing diverse voices and stories from around the world, and this series aligns with that mission. By bringing *Cold Haven* to Australian audiences, SBS not only expands its offering but also provides viewers with a chance to engage with narratives that reflect global perspectives.

### Audience Reception and Expectations

As the premiere date approaches, anticipation is building among Australian viewers who are increasingly seeking out international series that offer fresh and compelling storytelling. The success of similar shows in recent years has demonstrated a growing appetite for foreign-language dramas, particularly those that delve into complex themes and character development. *Cold Haven* promises to deliver on these fronts, with its intricate plot and well-developed characters.

The addition of *Cold Haven* to SBS On Demand exemplifies the network's dedication to providing quality programming that resonates with a diverse audience.
