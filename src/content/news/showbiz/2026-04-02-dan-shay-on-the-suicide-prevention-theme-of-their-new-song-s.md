## Dan + Shay Address Mental Health in New Single "Say So"

Country music duo Dan + Shay, known for their romantic ballads and crossover appeal, have shifted their focus with their latest single, "Say So." This new track not only showcases their musical talents but also addresses a pressing issue: suicide prevention. The duo has dedicated the song to their late mentor, Ben Vaughn, who served as the head of Warner Chappell Music and played a significant role in their careers.

## A Shift in Theme

While Dan + Shay have built their reputation on love songs that often serve as the backdrop for weddings and romantic milestones, "Say So" marks a poignant departure from their usual themes. The duo recognizes the importance of mental health awareness and the impact it can have on individuals and communities. In interviews, they have emphasized that the song is not just about love in the traditional sense but about the love and support that can help someone in crisis.

## Honoring Ben Vaughn

The decision to dedicate "Say So" to Ben Vaughn adds a deeply personal layer to the song. Vaughn was not only a mentor to Dan + Shay but also a champion for many artists in the industry. His unexpected passing has left a void, prompting the duo to reflect on the importance of mental health and the need for open conversations surrounding it. In their tribute, Dan + Shay aim to honor Vaughn's legacy by spreading a message of hope and support through their music.

## The Importance of Mental Health Awareness

In recent years, the music industry has increasingly recognized the significance of mental health, with artists using their platforms to raise awareness and encourage discussions. Dan + Shay's "Say So" fits into this broader movement, aiming to reach listeners who may be struggling with their mental health. The lyrics encourage individuals to speak up and seek help, reinforcing the idea that no one should face their challenges alone.

## Music as a Healing Tool

Dan + Shay believe in the therapeutic power of music. They hope that "Say So" will resonate with fans and provide comfort to those who need it most. The duo has expressed their desire for the song to serve as a reminder that reaching out for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. By addressing such a critical issue, they aim to foster a sense of community and understanding among their listeners.

## Conclusion

With "Say So," Dan + Shay are not only expanding their artistic horizons but also contributing to the vital conversation around mental health. By dedicating the song to Ben Vaughn, they honor his memory while also encouraging others to prioritize their well-being. As they continue to navigate their careers in the music industry, the duo remains committed to using their platform for positive change, reminding us all of the importance of compassion and support in times of need.
