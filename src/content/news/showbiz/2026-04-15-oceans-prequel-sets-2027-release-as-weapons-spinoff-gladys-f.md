## Warner Bros. Expands Release Calendar with New Titles for 2027 and Beyond

Warner Bros. has announced an extensive update to its film release calendar, unveiling a series of highly anticipated titles set to debut in 2027 and beyond. Among the notable additions are a prequel to the popular "Ocean's" franchise and a spinoff titled "Gladys," centered on the character Aunt Gladys from the film "Weapons." The studio's strategic planning reflects its commitment to expanding its cinematic universe while catering to diverse audience interests.

### Key Releases for 2027

The release calendar highlights several significant films scheduled for 2027. One of the most eagerly awaited is the "Ocean's" prequel, which is slated for release on June 25, 2027. This film aims to explore the origins and backstories of characters within the beloved heist franchise, which has garnered a substantial following since its inception.

In addition to the "Ocean's" prequel, Warner Bros. has announced "The Revenge of La Llorona," set to premiere on April 9, 2027. This film is expected to delve into the folklore surrounding the legendary figure of La Llorona, further expanding the studio's repertoire of supernatural thrillers.

### The Expansion of the "Weapons" Franchise

The spinoff "Gladys," which focuses on Aunt Gladys from "Weapons," is another intriguing addition to the lineup. While specific plot details remain under wraps, the film is anticipated to explore new narratives and character dynamics within the established universe. The release date for "Gladys" is set for September 2028, indicating Warner Bros.' confidence in the franchise's ability to attract audiences.

### Other Notable Titles

Warner Bros. has also scheduled "Evil Dead Wrath" for April 7, 2028, further solidifying its commitment to the horror genre. This title is expected to continue the legacy of the "Evil Dead" series, which has been a mainstay in the horror film landscape since the 1980s.

Additionally, the release of "Final Destination 7" has been confirmed, although specific details regarding its plot or release date have not yet been disclosed. The "Final Destination" series is known for its unique approach to horror, often centering around themes of fate and survival.

### Conclusion

Warner Bros.' recent announcements reflect a strategic approach to film production and release, aiming to capitalize on established franchises while introducing new narratives to engage audiences. With a mix of horror, supernatural themes, and heist dramas, the studio's upcoming slate is poised to attract a wide range of viewers. As the entertainment landscape continues to evolve, Warner Bros. appears well-prepared to meet the demands of its audience in the coming years.
