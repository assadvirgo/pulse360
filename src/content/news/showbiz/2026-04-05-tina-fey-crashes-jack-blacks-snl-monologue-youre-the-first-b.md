## Tina Fey Joins Jack Black's 'SNL' Monologue to Celebrate Five-Timer Status

On April 4, 2023, the iconic sketch comedy show "Saturday Night Live" (SNL) celebrated Jack Black's fifth hosting appearance, a milestone that grants him membership in the prestigious "Five-Timers Club." This exclusive group is reserved for a select few who have hosted the show five or more times, and Black's entry was marked by a lively monologue that featured surprise appearances from several comedy legends.

### A Star-Studded Celebration

The episode began with Black taking the stage to a warm reception from the audience. His excitement was palpable as he acknowledged the significance of his fifth hosting gig. Shortly after, he was joined by Jonah Hill, who humorously pointed out that there was "something wrong with the five-timer's club." This comment set the tone for a night filled with laughter and nostalgia, as the show has a rich history of celebrating its recurring hosts.

The highlight of the monologue came when Tina Fey made a surprise entrance. Fey, a former SNL cast member and a beloved figure in the comedy world, added her own flair to the celebration. In a playful jab, she quipped to Black, "You’re the first Black in the Five-Timers Club," which elicited laughter from the audience. This moment not only showcased Fey's sharp wit but also underscored the show's ability to blend humor with cultural commentary.

### The Significance of the Five-Timers Club

The "Five-Timers Club" is a revered tradition within SNL, established in the early 1990s. The concept was popularized by a sketch featuring Steve Martin, Tom Hanks, and other notable hosts, who gathered in a fictional lounge to celebrate their status. Membership in this club is seen as a badge of honor among comedians, reflecting their enduring connection to the show and its legacy.

Jack Black's induction into the club is particularly noteworthy, given his unique comedic style and contributions to film and television. Known for his energetic performances and charismatic presence, Black has become a fan favorite, making his five-time hosting achievement a fitting milestone in his career.
