## Bridget Regan Joins Gina Rodriguez and Justina Machado for 'Matlock' Season 2 Finale

In an exciting development for fans of both *Matlock* and *Jane the Virgin*, it has been announced that Bridget Regan will join Gina Rodriguez and Justina Machado for the Season 2 finale of *Matlock*. The episode is set to air on April 23 at 9 PM on CBS, marking a significant reunion for the three actresses who previously starred in the acclaimed series *Jane the Virgin*.

### A Reunion of Talents

The upcoming episode promises to be a notable event, as it brings together three talented actresses who have made their mark in the television industry. Bridget Regan, known for her versatile roles across various genres, will join Gina Rodriguez, who gained widespread recognition for her portrayal of Jane Villanueva in *Jane the Virgin*, and Justina Machado, celebrated for her work in both *Jane the Virgin* and the critically acclaimed series *One Day at a Time*.

This reunion is particularly special as it reunites the actresses with Jennie Snyder Urman, the executive producer and showrunner of both *Matlock* and *Jane the Virgin*. Urman's ability to craft compelling narratives and develop rich characters has earned her a dedicated following, and her involvement in this project adds an additional layer of anticipation for viewers.

While specific plot details about the *Matlock* Season 2 finale remain under wraps, the inclusion of Regan, Rodriguez, and Machado suggests that the episode will feature strong character dynamics and engaging storytelling. Fans can expect a blend of drama and humor, characteristic of Urman's previous works.

The *Matlock* revival has already garnered attention for its fresh take on the classic legal drama, originally starring Andy Griffith.
