## Sandra Hüller Discusses Hollywood's Attention and Upcoming Projects

In a recent interview at the Cannes Film Festival, German actress Sandra Hüller shared her thoughts on the growing interest from Hollywood in her work, as well as her upcoming projects, including "Hail Mary" and Tom Cruise's "Digger." The conversation took place during an exclusive recording of the 'Awards Chatter' podcast, hosted by Scott Feinberg, the executive editor of awards for The Hollywood Reporter.

## Rising Star in Hollywood

Hüller, who has garnered significant acclaim for her performances in European cinema, is now finding herself in the spotlight of Hollywood. The actress expressed her surprise and gratitude for the attention she has received from the American film industry, stating, "It impresses me beyond anything I’ve ever seen." This statement reflects her astonishment at the scale and reach of Hollywood, a stark contrast to her experiences in the European film scene.

Her rise to prominence can be attributed to her compelling performances and the unique narratives she brings to her roles. Hüller's ability to convey deep emotions and complex characters has resonated with audiences and critics alike, making her a sought-after talent in both European and American cinema.

## Upcoming Projects: "Hail Mary" and "Digger"

During the podcast, Hüller discussed her involvement in "Hail Mary," a project that has generated buzz for its innovative storytelling and strong character arcs. While details about the film remain under wraps, Hüller hinted at its potential to challenge conventional narratives, aligning with her artistic vision.

Additionally, she shared insights into her role in "Digger," a film starring Tom Cruise. Hüller expressed her excitement about collaborating with such a prominent figure in the industry, noting the opportunity to learn from Cruise's extensive experience. "Working with Tom is an incredible opportunity, and I am eager to see how our collaboration unfolds," she remarked.

## The Impact of Cannes

The Cannes Film Festival serves as a pivotal platform for filmmakers and actors to showcase their work, and Hüller’s participation this year underscores her rising status in the film world. The festival not only celebrates cinematic achievements but also facilitates networking opportunities that can lead to future collaborations. Hüller’s presence at Cannes signifies her potential to bridge the gap between European and American cinema, further enriching the global film landscape.

## Conclusion

Sandra Hüller’s reflections on her burgeoning career in Hollywood and her upcoming projects illustrate the dynamic nature of the film industry. As she continues to captivate audiences with her performances, her journey serves as an inspiring example for aspiring actors worldwide. With films like "Hail Mary" and "Digger" on the horizon, Hüller is poised to make a significant impact in both European and American cinema, solidifying her status as a formidable talent in the global film community.
