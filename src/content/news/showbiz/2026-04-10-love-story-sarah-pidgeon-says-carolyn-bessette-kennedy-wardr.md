## Actress Sarah Pidgeon Discusses Impact of Role as Carolyn Bessette Kennedy

In a recent interview, actress Sarah Pidgeon opened up about the physical toll her portrayal of Carolyn Bessette Kennedy in the upcoming series "Love Story" had on her. Pidgeon, who is being touted as a potential Emmy frontrunner for her compelling performance, revealed that the demanding role led to unexpected health issues, including a severe case of psoriasis.

## The Challenges of Portraying a Fashion Icon

Carolyn Bessette Kennedy, known for her iconic style as a publicist for Calvin Klein and her marriage to John F. Kennedy Jr., is a figure synonymous with 1990s fashion. Pidgeon’s commitment to authentically embodying Bessette Kennedy included wearing a wardrobe that was reflective of the late icon’s chic aesthetic. However, the actress noted that the heat of the summer filming conditions exacerbated her skin condition, resulting in "psoriasis all over."

Pidgeon explained that the combination of the intense summer heat and the pressure of maintaining the character's polished appearance contributed to her skin issues. "It was a challenging experience," she stated, emphasizing the lengths actors often go to in order to bring their characters to life. 

## Acknowledging the Physical Toll of Acting

The experience has raised discussions about the physical demands placed on actors, particularly when portraying real-life figures known for their distinctive styles. Pidgeon’s candid remarks shed light on the often-overlooked challenges faced by performers in the entertainment industry. 

Psoriasis, an autoimmune condition that causes red, itchy, scaly patches on the skin, can be triggered by various factors, including stress and environmental conditions. Pidgeon’s case serves as a reminder of the importance of health and well-being in the pursuit of artistic excellence.

## Anticipation for "Love Story"

Despite the personal challenges she faced during filming, Pidgeon remains optimistic about the series. "Love Story" is expected to explore not only Bessette Kennedy's fashion legacy but also her life and the complexities of her relationship with John F. Kennedy Jr. The series has generated significant buzz, with many anticipating a nuanced portrayal of the couple's life in the public eye.
