## Brian Tyree Henry and Spike Fearn to Star in Apple Original Film 'Running'

In a significant development in the film industry, acclaimed actor Brian Tyree Henry has officially joined the cast of the upcoming Apple Original film, "Running," directed by Gavin O'Connor. This announcement comes on the heels of Henry's recent successes, including his performances in Apple's veteran drama "Causeway" and the crime drama series "Dope Thief," both of which have garnered him considerable acclaim and award nominations.

## A Promising Collaboration

"Running" will see Henry sharing the screen with Spike Fearn, known for his role in "Alien: Romulus." The film is anticipated to be a compelling addition to Apple's growing portfolio of original films, which aims to deliver high-quality storytelling and diverse narratives. While specific character details remain under wraps, the collaboration between Henry and Fearn is already generating buzz within the film community.

Gavin O'Connor, the film's director, has a reputation for crafting engaging narratives that resonate with audiences. His previous works, including "Warrior" and "The Accountant," have showcased his ability to blend intense drama with action, making him a fitting choice for this project.

Brian Tyree Henry has emerged as one of Hollywood's most versatile actors.
