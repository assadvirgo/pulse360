## Venice Critics’ Week Unveils Promising Lineup

The Venice Film Festival Critics’ Week has announced its lineup for the upcoming edition, showcasing a diverse array of films from emerging talents across the globe. Among the notable entries is “Our Share of Sand,” the debut feature from London-based Chilean-Indian director Shalini Adnani. This family drama explores the poignant journey of a 12-year-old girl named Maya, who returns to rural India in an effort to reconnect with her estranged father.

### A Glimpse into “Our Share of Sand”

“Our Share of Sand” marks a significant milestone for Adnani, who brings her unique cultural perspective to the screen. The film delves into themes of familial bonds, identity, and the complexities of returning to one’s roots. Maya’s journey is not just a physical return to her homeland but also an emotional exploration of her relationship with her father, set against the backdrop of rural India’s landscapes and traditions.

Adnani’s work is anticipated to resonate with audiences, offering a narrative that bridges cultural divides while highlighting the universal quest for connection and understanding within families.
