## Alisa Bowen Transitioning from Disney+ to Lead Fubo TV

Alisa Bowen, who has held the position of president at Disney+ since September 2022, is set to take on a new role as the chief executive officer of Fubo, an internet TV streaming service that is predominantly owned by Disney. The announcement was made on Thursday, with Fubo's board of directors confirming her appointment, which will take effect on July 10.

### A New Chapter for Bowen

Bowen's transition to Fubo marks a significant shift in her career, as she steps into the role of CEO following the tenure of David Gandler, Fubo's co-founder. Gandler has played a pivotal role in shaping the company since its inception, and Bowen's leadership is anticipated to bring a fresh perspective to the organization.

During her time at Disney+, Bowen was instrumental in the platform's growth and development, overseeing various initiatives aimed at expanding its content library and enhancing user engagement. Her experience in managing a major streaming service is expected to be beneficial as Fubo continues to navigate the competitive landscape of online television.

### Fubo's Position in the Streaming Market

Fubo has carved out a niche in the streaming market by focusing on live sports and entertainment programming. The platform has attracted a dedicated subscriber base, leveraging its unique offerings to differentiate itself from competitors. Under Bowen's leadership, Fubo is likely to explore new strategies to further enhance its service and expand its reach.

The streaming industry has seen rapid evolution in recent years, with numerous platforms vying for viewer attention. As traditional cable subscriptions decline, services like Fubo are positioned to capitalize on the growing demand for flexible and accessible viewing options. Bowen's expertise in digital media and content strategy will be crucial as Fubo seeks to innovate and adapt to changing consumer preferences.

### Looking Ahead

As Bowen prepares to assume her new role, the focus will be on maintaining Fubo's momentum while also driving growth and profitability. Stakeholders will be keenly observing how her leadership style and strategic vision will influence the company's trajectory in the coming months.

Disney's continued investment in Fubo underscores the importance of the streaming service within its broader portfolio. With Bowen at the helm, Fubo is poised to leverage its connection to Disney's vast resources and content library, potentially enhancing its competitive edge in the market.

In summary, Alisa Bowen's appointment as CEO of Fubo represents a strategic move not only for her career but also for the future of the streaming service. As the industry continues to evolve, her leadership will be pivotal in navigating the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
