## Grizz Chapman, Beloved '30 Rock' Actor, Passes Away at 52

Grizzwald "Grizz" Chapman, known for his role as Grizz on the acclaimed NBC sitcom "30 Rock," has passed away at the age of 52. The news was confirmed by his longtime representative, Saideh A. Brown, who shared the information with TMZ. Chapman died on Friday, although the cause of death has not yet been disclosed.

### A Cherished Character on '30 Rock'

Chapman's portrayal of Grizz, a loyal friend and bodyguard to the character Tracy Jordan, played by Tracy Morgan, endeared him to audiences during the show's seven-season run from 2006 to 2013. "30 Rock," created by Tina Fey, received critical acclaim and numerous awards, and Chapman's character was a significant part of its ensemble cast. His performance was characterized by a blend of humor and warmth, contributing to the show's unique charm.

### Early Life and Career

Born on April 16, 1974, Chapman grew up in Los Angeles, California. He began his career in the entertainment industry as a stand-up comedian before transitioning to acting.
