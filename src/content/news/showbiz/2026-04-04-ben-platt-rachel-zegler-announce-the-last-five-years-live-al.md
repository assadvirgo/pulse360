## Ben Platt and Rachel Zegler to Release Live Album for 'The Last Five Years' 25th Anniversary

In a significant celebration of musical theater, Ben Platt and Rachel Zegler have announced the release of a live album commemorating the 25th anniversary of the acclaimed musical "The Last Five Years." The announcement was made during a performance at the Hollywood Bowl on Friday, where the co-stars captivated the audience with their rendition of the beloved show.

### A Milestone Celebration

"The Last Five Years," written by Jason Robert Brown, has been a staple in the musical theater repertoire since its debut in 2001. The narrative, which explores the complexities of a relationship through a unique storytelling structure, has resonated with audiences for decades. As the musical reaches its quarter-century milestone, Platt and Zegler's live album aims to capture the essence of their recent performances, particularly highlighting their run at the London Palladium.

### Details of the Live Album

The live album, which is set to be released on April 20, will feature recordings from the London Palladium performances, showcasing the vocal talents of both Platt and Zegler. Fans of the musical can now pre-order the album through Atlantic Records, ensuring they can experience this special anniversary tribute. The album is expected to include both the iconic songs that have made "The Last Five Years" a favorite among theatergoers and new interpretations that reflect the unique artistry of its current stars.

### The Hollywood Bowl Performance

During the announcement at the Hollywood Bowl, Platt and Zegler expressed their excitement about the project and the opportunity to bring "The Last Five Years" to new audiences. The performance at the Hollywood Bowl was met with enthusiasm, as fans gathered to witness the chemistry between the two performers, which has been a hallmark of their collaboration. Their dedication to the material and the emotional depth they bring to their roles have been praised by critics and fans alike.

### Looking Ahead

As the release date approaches, anticipation is building among fans of both the musical and the artists. The live album not only serves as a celebration of "The Last Five Years" but also highlights the ongoing relevance of its themes in contemporary society. Platt and Zegler's involvement in this project underscores their commitment to honoring the legacy of the musical while also introducing it to a new generation of theater enthusiasts.

In conclusion, the announcement of the live album for "The Last Five Years" marks a significant moment in the celebration of musical theater. With the combined talents of Ben Platt and Rachel Zegler, the album promises to be a heartfelt tribute to a story that continues to resonate with audiences around the world. As fans prepare for the album's release, the legacy of "The Last Five Years" remains firmly in the spotlight, reminding us of the powerful narratives that musical theater can convey.
