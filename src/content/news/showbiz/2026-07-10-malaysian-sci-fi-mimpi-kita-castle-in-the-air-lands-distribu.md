## Malaysian Sci-Fi Film 'Mimpi Kita: Castle in the Air' Secures Distribution Deal

In a significant development for the Malaysian film industry, Abnormal Studios has acquired the theatrical distribution rights for the science-fiction fantasy feature "Mimpi Kita: Castle in the Air." This agreement covers both Malaysia and Brunei, marking a pivotal moment for the film as it prepares to reach audiences in these regions.

### Background of the Film

"Mimpi Kita: Castle in the Air" is a notable entry in the Malaysian cinematic landscape, particularly within the science fiction genre. The film recently garnered attention at the NAFF Project Market's It Project strand during the Bucheon International Fantastic Film Festival. This festival is renowned for showcasing innovative and genre-defining films, providing a platform for filmmakers to connect with potential distributors and audiences.

### Role of Abnormal Studios

Abnormal Studios, a prominent player in the Malaysian entertainment sector, will not only handle the theatrical distribution for Malaysia and Brunei but will also act as the film's worldwide international sales agent, with the exception of certain territories. This dual role positions Abnormal Studios as a crucial partner in the film's journey from production to global distribution.

### Significance of the Acquisition

The acquisition by Abnormal Studios is a promising development for "Mimpi Kita: Castle in the Air." It reflects a growing interest in Malaysian cinema, particularly in genres that blend local storytelling with universal themes. The film's participation in international festivals like Bucheon underscores its potential appeal beyond Malaysian borders, and the backing of a dedicated distributor could facilitate wider recognition.

### Future Prospects

As the film industry continues to evolve, the success of "Mimpi Kita: Castle in the Air" could pave the way for more Malaysian films to explore the science fiction genre. With Abnormal Studios at the helm, there is optimism that the film will not only resonate with local audiences but also capture the interest of international viewers.

In conclusion, the partnership between "Mimpi Kita: Castle in the Air" and Abnormal Studios represents a significant step forward for Malaysian cinema.
