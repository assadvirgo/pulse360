## Eli Manning Addresses Political Involvement of Athletes Following Jaxson Dart's Rally Appearance

In a recent discussion, former New York Giants quarterback Eli Manning shared his perspective on the intersection of sports and politics, particularly in light of current Giants quarterback Jaxson Dart's involvement in a political rally. Dart made headlines after introducing former President Donald Trump at a rally, an action that has sparked a conversation about the role of athletes in political discourse.

Manning, who is widely regarded as one of the most accomplished quarterbacks in NFL history, emphasized the importance of athletes being aware of their influence. He noted that public figures, including athletes, have platforms that can significantly impact their communities and beyond. Manning expressed that while he respects Dart's right to express his political views, he believes athletes should consider the potential repercussions of their actions.
