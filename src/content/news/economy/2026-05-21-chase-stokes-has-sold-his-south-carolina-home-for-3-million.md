## Chase Stokes Sells South Carolina Home Following Conclusion of 'Outer Banks'

Chase Stokes, the actor known for his role in Netflix's hit series "Outer Banks," has recently sold his South Carolina residence for $3 million. This sale comes shortly after the conclusion of the show's final season, marking a significant transition for the actor as he moves on from a project that has greatly influenced his career.

### A New Chapter for Stokes

Stokes, who gained widespread recognition through his portrayal of John B. Routledge in "Outer Banks," has spent the past seven years in South Carolina, where the series was filmed. The home, which has been a backdrop for both his personal and professional life, is now off the market, signaling a new chapter for the actor. 

The decision to sell the property aligns with the end of the series, which has captivated audiences with its adventurous narrative set against the picturesque landscapes of the Outer Banks region. As Stokes transitions from this pivotal role, he is likely to explore new opportunities in his acting career.
