## Son of Mango Founder Detained in Connection with Father's Fatal Hiking Accident

In a shocking development, Jonathan Andic, the son of Isak Andic, the late founder of the renowned fashion chain Mango, has been detained in relation to the circumstances surrounding his father's tragic death during a hiking trip two years ago. Isak Andic, a prominent figure in the fashion industry, died after falling from a mountain while hiking with his son in the Spanish Pyrenees.

### Background of the Incident

The incident occurred in August 2021 when Isak Andic, then 62, and his son were on a hiking expedition. Reports indicate that the pair was exploring a challenging route when the unfortunate accident took place. Initial investigations suggested that the fall was accidental; however, the circumstances surrounding the incident have since come under scrutiny.

### Recent Developments

The Spanish authorities have reopened the investigation into Isak Andic's death, leading to the recent detention of Jonathan Andic. While details regarding the specific charges or allegations against him have not been disclosed, the decision to detain him has raised eyebrows and sparked public interest. Legal experts suggest that the investigation may be focusing on potential negligence or other factors that could have contributed to the fatal fall.
