Authorities have not disclosed the extent of his injuries, nor have they released his identity, citing privacy concerns.

Monaco's police force has launched an extensive investigation into the bombing. The Monaco government has expressed its commitment to ensuring the safety of its residents and visitors, stating that such acts of violence are intolerable in the principality.
