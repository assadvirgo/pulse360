## Dolly Parton's Former Hideaway in Solvang Listed for $2 Million

Solvang, known for its Danish-style architecture and vibrant cultural scene, attracts visitors with its quaint charm and scenic beauty. The town, located in Santa Barbara County, offers a blend of history and modernity, making it a desirable location for both residents and tourists. The property, with its distinctive features, adds to the town's eclectic character.

### The Property's Unique Features

The house, which was Parton's hideaway, showcases a variety of quirky elements that reflect the artist's personality. Inside, the residence is designed to offer both comfort and style, with rooms that are well-appointed and inviting. The blend of rustic charm and modern amenities makes it an attractive option for potential buyers looking for a unique home.
