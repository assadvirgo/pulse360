## Understanding Market Liquidity

Market liquidity refers to the ease with which assets can be bought or sold in the market without affecting their price. Stocks that are sensitive to changes in liquidity often experience greater volatility, making them attractive to investors during periods of economic recovery. When liquidity improves, these stocks can benefit from increased trading activity and investor confidence, leading to potential price appreciation.
