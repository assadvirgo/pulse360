## Elisha Cuthbert Relists Hollywood Hills Home for $2.1 Million

Actress Elisha Cuthbert, known for her roles in popular television series and films, has re-entered the real estate market by relisting her Hollywood Hills home for approximately $2.1 million. This move comes shortly after her return to the acting scene, marking a significant moment in her career following a prolonged hiatus.

### Background on Elisha Cuthbert

Cuthbert first gained widespread recognition for her role in the television series "24," where she portrayed Kim Bauer, the daughter of Kiefer Sutherland's character, Jack Bauer. Over the years, she has appeared in various films and television shows, establishing herself as a prominent figure in Hollywood. However, her recent absence from the screen has led to speculation about her future projects and career trajectory.

### The Hollywood Hills Property

The property in question is located in the coveted Hollywood Hills neighborhood, known for its stunning views and celebrity residents. The home features modern amenities and luxurious finishes, appealing to potential buyers looking for a blend of comfort and style. Originally purchased by Cuthbert in 2014, the actress has made several updates to the property, enhancing its value and aesthetic appeal.

Cuthbert's decision to relist her home comes at a time when the real estate market in Los Angeles is experiencing fluctuations.
