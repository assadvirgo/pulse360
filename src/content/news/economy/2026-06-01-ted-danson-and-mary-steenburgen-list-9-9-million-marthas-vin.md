## Ted Danson and Mary Steenburgen List Iconic Martha’s Vineyard Property for $9.9 Million

In a significant move within the real estate market, actors Ted Danson and Mary Steenburgen have placed their cherished Martha’s Vineyard property on the market for $9.9 million. This home holds sentimental value as the site of their wedding 31 years ago, marking it as a cornerstone in their personal and professional lives.

### A Historic Venue

The property, located in the exclusive enclave of Martha’s Vineyard, has been more than just a residence for the couple; it has served as a gathering place for numerous high-profile guests over the years. Notable figures such as actor Tom Hanks and former President Bill Clinton have graced the home, underscoring its status as a venue of choice for A-list gatherings. The home’s rich history and its connection to the couple's personal milestones add layers of significance to the listing.

### Features of the Property

The estate boasts a variety of luxurious features that appeal to potential buyers. Spanning several acres, the property includes expansive living spaces, beautifully landscaped gardens, and stunning views of the surrounding natural beauty. The design reflects a blend of classic and contemporary styles, creating an inviting atmosphere that has made it a beloved retreat for the couple and their guests.

### Market Context

The decision to sell this iconic property comes amidst a competitive real estate market, particularly in sought-after locations like Martha’s Vineyard. The region has seen a surge in demand for luxury properties, driven in part by an influx of buyers seeking second homes in picturesque settings. As remote work becomes more prevalent, many individuals and families are looking to invest in properties that offer both comfort and a connection to nature.

### Personal Reflections

For Danson and Steenburgen, the sale of their Martha’s Vineyard home represents a poignant transition. The couple, who have been married since 1995, has shared countless memories within its walls, making the decision to sell a deeply personal one. In interviews, both actors have expressed their appreciation for the time spent in the home, reflecting on the joy it has brought them over the years.

### Conclusion

As Ted Danson and Mary Steenburgen prepare to part with their Martha’s Vineyard property, they not only offer a luxurious home to potential buyers but also a piece of their shared history. The listing is expected to attract significant interest, given the property's unique attributes and the couple's celebrity status. Whether it finds a new owner who will cherish it as they have remains to be seen, but the legacy of this home will undoubtedly continue to resonate in the years to come.
