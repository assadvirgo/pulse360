The reluctance to spend can be attributed to various psychological factors. Additionally, the absence of heirs may contribute to a sense of uncertainty about how to allocate wealth. Without the traditional motivations of legacy or familial support, this millionaire may feel unanchored, leading to a more cautious approach to spending.

The narrative of a single millionaire who prefers thrift store shopping challenges conventional notions of wealth and spending.
