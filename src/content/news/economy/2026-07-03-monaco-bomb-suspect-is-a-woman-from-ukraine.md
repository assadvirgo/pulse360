The bombing, which occurred in a public area of Monaco, has raised significant concerns about security and public safety in the region. While details surrounding the incident remain limited, initial reports suggest that it caused considerable damage and led to an urgent response from local law enforcement and emergency services. Fortunately, no fatalities have been reported, but several individuals sustained injuries, prompting a thorough investigation.

Prosecutors have named the Ukrainian woman as a key suspect in the case. While her identity has not been publicly disclosed, authorities have indicated that they believe she played a significant role in orchestrating the bombing. The decision to issue an international arrest warrant highlights the seriousness of the allegations and the urgency of apprehending the suspect.
