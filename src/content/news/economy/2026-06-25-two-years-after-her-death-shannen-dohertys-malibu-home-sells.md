## Shannen Doherty's Malibu Home Sold for $7.7 Million

Two years following the passing of actress Shannen Doherty, her Malibu residence has been sold for $7.7 million. The property, often referred to by Doherty as her personal “sanctuary,” served as a refuge from the demands of Hollywood, particularly during her prolonged battle with cancer.

### A Reflection of Personal Resilience

Doherty, who was widely recognized for her roles in television series such as "Beverly Hills, 90210" and "Charmed," faced significant health challenges over the years, including a highly publicized fight against breast cancer. Her Malibu home was more than just a place to live; it was a space where she found solace and comfort amid her struggles. The sale of the property marks a poignant moment in the ongoing narrative of her legacy.

The Malibu estate, which garnered attention for its stunning ocean views and serene surroundings, was designed to provide a tranquil escape.
