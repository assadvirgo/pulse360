Kalshi, a privately owned company, specializes in prediction markets, allowing users to place bets on the outcomes of various events. This innovative approach to forecasting has garnered attention and investment, particularly as the U.S. government has adopted a relatively lenient regulatory stance towards the sector. The light-touch approach has enabled Kalshi to expand its operations and attract a broader user base, contributing to its soaring valuation.

Prediction markets operate on the principle that collective insights can yield accurate forecasts about future events. By allowing individuals to wager on the outcomes of political, economic, and social events, platforms like Kalshi harness the wisdom of crowds. This model not only provides entertainment but also serves as a tool for information dissemination and analysis.
